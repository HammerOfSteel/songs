# Diolch am Eich Amser

**Labels:** Diolch
**Created:** 2025-12-13
**Edited:** 2025-12-13

---

It’s the final page,
Stepping off the stage.
The last time that we speak,
The strength I have to seek.

The quietness descends upon the room,
A soft, forgiving silence cuts the gloom.
I know you walked this road right by my side,
You had nowhere left to run or to hide.

You heard the shouting and the whispered prayer,
The frantic, heavy truth that hung in the air.
I know the journey wasn’t always bright,
That often we were wrestling with the light.

Thank you for standing by and taking part,
For witnessing the cracking of my heart.

The metaphors were thin, the verses long,
I hope you found some truth within the songs.

I hope you glimpsed the shape of what we lost,
And understood the final, bitter cost.

Why would I hope for you such a crual understanding?
What is it that this heart of the narrator seem to be demanding?

Simply,
to the truth of the matter.
Thank you for taking the time.
Leaving the normal narrative behind.

Getting to know a small little heart,
willing to listen and take part.

Diolch am Eich Amser.
Thank you for your time,
For stepping into this strange,
broken rhyme.

For holding space for all the things unsaid,
For resting all these burdens on my head.

For seeing how the little embers glowed,
Right here on this tumultuous, winding road.

thank you who came before, who made this way,
Who faced the dark and lived to see the day.

I thank the names I whisper in the night,
Who taught me how to seek the smallest light.

I thank the listener who sits and stays,
Who hears these strange and crooked ways.

free form floating fiddle and prayer,
A thread of love I throw up in the air.

This devotional whisper is my bow,
A simple act of letting things go.

Not letting go, goodbye forver,
leting go home, home together.

presence isn't measured by a clock,
It's built into the breaking of the rock.

The way that memory settles in the bone,
The feeling of being utterly, truly known.

I fold the words up neatly, every line,
And tell myself that everything is fine.
fine, not the little calculated lie.
fine as in,
it is okay to try.
try and wake up every single day,
wake up and say.
"Aye, I'll keep going"

It ends not with a victory or an answer,
Just the slow, soft turning of the dancer.
It ends with the presence that is here,
Dispelling every shadow, every fear.
A gentle closing of the heavy door,
We do not need to fight it more.

I leave this here,
right in this sound,
The only honest truth I ever found.
We close the book on this strange, weary year,
And find the quiet strength to hold you near. 

I release the fear,
I release the need,
I plant the final, hopeful little seed.

Diolch am Eich Amser. 

Thank you for your time.
