# Beneath the Surface

**Labels:** Dancing salamanders
**Created:** 2024-08-10
**Edited:** 2024-08-10

---

Two friends in a world so vast,
Lost in thought, as the days rush past,
They speak of life, of hopes that fade,
in the wind's whisper, dreams they made,
But by the shores of Storsjön’s deep,
Jata and Kata's secret keep.

Beneath the surface, something waits,
under the surface, destiny debates,
Two hearts seeking, in the world’s embrace,
To find their truth, to find their place.

Jata stirs the ancient brew,
Kata whispers, “The world is new,”
even in the fire, faith can burn bright,
When the world feels dark, when there’s no light,
But through their words, a spark ignites,
A path unfolds, beneath the nights.

Beneath the surface, something waits,
under the surface, destiny debates,
Two hearts seeking, in the world’s embrace,
To find their truth, to find their place.

Not all that’s hidden is meant to stay,
we must sow seeds of change, they say,
Great lake depths hold shadows old,
But in their hearts, a fire takes hold.

In the depths of the lake, where shadows coil,
Jata and Kata, with ancient toil,
But no fear remains, as they now see,
the truth in the dark, will set them free.

Beneath the surface, something waits,
under the surface, destiny debates,
Two hearts seeking, in the world’s embrace,
To find their truth, to find their place.

As the monster sleeps, so does their fear,
we are the change, they whisper near,
By Storsjön's shore, where the waters break,
They find their path, as the world awakes.


Music Style Description
Acoustic folk with Swedish influences, subtle electronic notes, deep bass, introspective, dynamic, blending tradition and modernity.

Two-Sentence Description
This song intertwines the myth of Storsjön's lake monster and the trolls Jata and Kata with a modern narrative of self-discovery and change. Through a blend of traditional Swedish elements and contemporary acoustic and electronic sounds, it explores the journey of two friends as they realize the power to shape their world lies within them.
