# firefly rosary

**Labels:** Pockets Of Light
**Created:** 2025-08-27
**Edited:** 2025-08-27

---

Counting fireflies as beads in the air,
 stringing them slowly, a rosary prayer.
 Golden confessions in flicker and flight,
 candles that wander the orchard at night.

One for the sorrow, two for the flame,
 three for the moment that whispers your name.

 Pocket of summer, a hymn in the breeze,
 psalm of the grasses that bend to my knees.
 Moon is the chalice, the night is the wine,
 fireflies bead it in merciful line.
 Lanterns of silence, a chapel of dew,
 I kneel in the meadow and count them for you.

Even the shadows say prayers when they hide.

Firefly rosary, bead after bead,
 light in the hollow where nothing can lead.
 Firefly rosary, ember and flame,
 I hold them forever and whisper your name.

Stitch of the twilight, a seam in the sky,
 threaded by wings that can’t ever die.
 Thimble of starlight, needle of rain,
 mending the fabric that’s torn by my pain.
 Salt in the meadow, sugar in air,
 every firefly a bead of my prayer.

How can I walk all the rest of my years
 without your small hand to carry my tears?

Firefly rosary, bead after bead,
 light in the hollow where nothing can lead.
 Firefly rosary, ember and flame,
 I hold them forever and whisper your name.

Glowworm gospel, tiny creed,
 burnt-out sorrow, planted seed.
 Firefly, firefly, counting slow,
 each one a secret I’ll never let go.
 … daffodil, daffodil, shine me through.

Gather the beads till the rosary breaks,
 scatter them skyward, whatever it takes.
 If the meadow forgets me, the night will remain,
 firefly rosary sings out your name.

Firefly rosary, bead after bead,
 light in the hollow where nothing can lead.
 Firefly rosary, ember and flame,
 I hold them forever and whisper your name…
One for the sorrow, two for the flame,
 three for the moment that whispers your name.
