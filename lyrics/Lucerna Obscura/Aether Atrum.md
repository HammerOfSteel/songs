# Aether Atrum

**Labels:** Dancing salamanders, Lucerna Obscura
**Created:** 2024-08-20
**Edited:** 2024-08-21

---

Verse 1
In Alexandria’s twilight, where the flames rise high,
A shadow moves, 'neath the burning sky.
Among the scrolls and secrets deep,
Where ancient knowledge lies asleep.

Through the smoldering ash, where whispers fall,
The Aether’s veil conceals it all.
Edward walks in the fire’s breath,
A figure cloaked in whispered death.

"Umbra profunda," the darkness calls,
As the Library crumbles, as knowledge falls.
He seeks the Aether, where shadows blend,
In the heart of night, where truths descend.

Chorus
Aether Atrum, in the shadow’s hold,
Where secrets burn, and the night grows cold.
In the depths of fire, where the embers glow,
Alexanders shadow begins to grow.

Through the veil of flame, where the past is lost,
He walks the path, whatever the cost.
In the darkened air, where the Aether swirls,
Kelley’s shadow unfurls.

Verse 2
Among the ruins, where the scrolls turn to dust,
He moves through the fire, driven by lust.
For the Aether’s truth, hidden in flame,
Where the ancient voices whisper his name.

"Ignis tenebris," the fire of night,
Guides him deeper, away from light.
In the shadowed corners, where secrets lie,
He seeks the dark truth, as the flames rise high.

The Library burns, its knowledge erased,
But in the dark Aether, shadows are traced.
Edward grasps at the fading light,
Where shadows rule the endless night.

Bridge
In the heart of Alexandria, where the Aether hides,
He finds the truth where the shadow resides.
"Caecus est," the blind eye sees,
As the flames consume the sacred trees.

But in the darkness, a spark remains,
In the Aether’s depth, where the shadow reigns.
Edward’s hand grasps the fleeting breath,
Of the ancient wisdom beyond death.

Outro
As Alexandria falls, the Aether weeps,
In the shadow’s embrace, where silence keeps.
"Obscurum per obscurius," the dark reveals,
the monad truth, the night conceals.

In the ashes cold, where the fires died,
The Aether Atrum is still his guide.
Kelley, in the shadow’s breath,
Walks with the Aether, beyond death.'
