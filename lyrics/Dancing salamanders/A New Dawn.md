# A New Dawn

**Labels:** Dancing salamanders
**Created:** 2024-08-20
**Edited:** 2024-08-20

---

Verse 1 (Elara)
As the first light breaks through the Spire’s embrace,
We stand on the edge of a boundless space.
The portal shimmers, a veil so thin,
Revealing the world where the dreams begin.

Beyond the threshold, where the waters gleam,
An ocean vast, like a waking dream.
Islands of jade in the sapphire sea,
A realm of endless possibility.

Chorus (Elara and Ember)
Through the portal, where the waves gently call,
We see the realm where the old ways fall.
"A’maelamin," (My beloved,) in the light of dawn,
We step forward, where the shadows are gone.

The ocean’s song, a lullaby sweet,
Guides our hearts where the waters meet.
In this new world, where the tide is strong,
We find our path where we both belong.

Verse 2 (Ember)
The sky above, a canvas wide,
With colors that shift like the changing tide.
"Lissenen ar' maska'lalaith," (With sweetness and laughter,) Ember whispers near,
As the portal reveals what we hold dear.

Beyond the shores where the sun does rise,
The mists of the past dissolve in the skies.
Each island a haven, a place to explore,
Where the heart finds peace on a distant shore.

Bridge (Elara and Ember)
"Quel fara," (Good journey,) Elara softly sighs,
As they gaze upon the ocean’s eyes.
The waves they dance, with a whispered plea,
"Na lû e-govaned vîn," (Until our meeting again,) they call to the sea.

The passage opens, wide and true,
A gateway to realms where the heart renews.
"Na lû i’cend," (For the ages,) the dawn whispers low,
As the new world beckons, where the soul must go.

Chorus (Elara and Ember)
Through the portal, where the waves gently call,
We see the realm where the old ways fall.
"A’maelamin," (My beloved,) in the light of dawn,
We step forward, where the shadows are gone.

The ocean’s song, a lullaby sweet,
Guides our hearts where the waters meet.
In this new world, where the tide is strong,
We find our path where we both belong.

Outro (Elara and Ember)
Together they stand, hand in hand,
As the new dawn spreads across the land.
With one last glance at the Spire’s tall height,
They step through the portal, into the light.

"Elen síla lúmenn' omentielvo," (A star shines on the hour of our meeting,) Elara sings,
As the waves embrace them with silver wings.
Through the portal’s glow, they journey on,
Into the ocean, where a new dawn is drawn.



The final song of the album, A New Dawn, captures Elara and Ember stepping into the unknown, symbolizing the beginning of their journey into the next realm. The song describes the dissolution of old beliefs and the emotional purification that prepares them for the vast oceanic realm ahead. It embodies the transition from one journey to the next, filled with hope, renewal, and the promise of infinite possibilities.
