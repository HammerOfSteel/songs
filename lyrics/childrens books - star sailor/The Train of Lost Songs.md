# The Train of Lost Songs

**Labels:** childrens books - star sailor
**Created:** 2024-10-23
**Edited:** 2024-10-23

---

Intro:
In the quiet of night, beneath stars shining far,
A train glides through rivers of light like a scar.
Through the caverns of Afon Lona, it silently weaves,
Carrying echoes that no one believes.

Star Sailor drifts down, her boat glowing bright,
Landing beside Rasmus, guiding his flight.
He looks at her lantern, her light soft and true,
“Come aboard,” he whispers, “there’s work we must do.”

Verse 1:
Through the caverns so deep, where the echoes lie still,
Rasmus guides his train by sheer force of will.
The River Afon Lona, glowing soft in the night,
Carries lost songs drifting out of sight.

Star Sailor arrives, her sailboat aglow,
“Where do these melodies wander and flow?”
Rasmus sighs, lantern heavy in hand,
“They’re searching for peace, for a place to land.”

Chorus:
"Songs drift on Afon Lona’s stream,
Lost in the shadows, carried by dream.
Some find their way, others take time,
But all hold meaning in rhythm or rhyme.

Through stars and rivers, they’ll find where they belong,
In the heart of the Nebula, every soul sings a song."

Verse 2:
Through the caverns of Caerwen’s Veil, cold and deep,
Fragments of songs begin to weep.
Rasmus tries to gather each note in his hand,
But they slip away, like grains of sand.

Star Sailor hums, her light shining near,
“Let them drift, let them steer.”
“It’s not holding tight that will set them free,
It’s the love we guide them with, across this sea.”

Chorus:
"Songs drift on Afon Lona’s stream,
Lost in the shadows, carried by dream.
Some find their way, others take time,
But all hold meaning in rhythm or rhyme.

Through stars and rivers, they’ll find where they belong,
In the heart of the Nebula, every soul sings a song."

Bridge:
Together they hum as the train draws near,
The Nebula of Song, shining crystal clear.
Rasmus’s voice weaves with Star Sailor’s tune,
Lifting melodies high under stars and moon.

“Not every song needs to end in our care,”
Rasmus whispers, releasing the notes in the air.
“Even the lost ones find their own way,
In the light of the Nebula, where memories stay.”

Outro:
As the train disappears into the Nebula bright,
Rasmus smiles softly, his lantern light.
Star Sailor waves from her boat in the stream,
Carrying hope in the heart of a dream.

For even the lost songs find where they belong,
In the endless Nebula, every soul sings a song.
