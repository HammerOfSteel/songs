# Underneath the Hollow Hill

**Labels:** Dancing salamanders, Three drops
**Created:** 2024-09-17
**Edited:** 2024-09-17

---

Verse 1: Beneath the moon’s soft, silvery spill,
She walks alone, to the breathing hill.
The whispers call from deep within,
A door of stone, where dreams begin.
Annwn awaits, through shadowed gate,
The tywysog there, with eyes of fate.
Young feet tread where time stands still,
Underneath the hollow hill.

Chorus: In the dark, where the sidhe sing,
She finds the court of the Hill King.
Lost in dance, in twilight’s grace,
She sees her past in every face.
Gwyn ap Nudd, with crown of bone,
Holds her close, but she’s not alone.
Through the veil, in the midnight chill,
She walks beneath the hollow hill.

Verse 2: In modern garb, but ancient thread,
She follows paths the old ones tread.
The earth it shifts, the world it turns,
As fire within her young heart burns.
Daear quakes with secrets old,
The Hill King’s court, a tale untold.
Her hands reach out to touch the thrill,
Of what lies beneath the hollow hill.

Bridge: The wind it howls, the shadows creep,
But in her heart, a secret keeps.
For in the hill, where old gods reign,
She finds herself, through joy and pain.
The Hill King smiles, his eyes aglow,
As ancient rivers start to flow.
She understands, with a soul to fill,
Her path lies under the hollow hill.

Outro: With dawn’s first light, she leaves the hill,
But in her heart, it lingers still.
A tale of yore, of ancient thrill,
That lives beneath the hollow hill.
