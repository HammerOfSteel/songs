On the edge of the world, where the sea meets sky,
We chase our dreams, as the tides pass by.
Through the mists of dawn, where legends lie,
We found our haven, where the old gods sigh.

From the city’s heart, to the ocean’s edge,
We followed our love, on a whispered pledge.
Through the waves, where the wild winds blow,
We found our home, where the roses grow.
In the hearth’s warm glow, where the firelight’s gleam,
We built our world, we lived our dream.

On the new horizons, we find our place,
In the waves of life, in the sea’s embrace.
Through the storms, through the calm,
We hold our love, like a treasured psalm.

Ar lan y môr, lle mae breuddwydion yn byw,
We found our love, so pure, so true.
In the quiet of the night, in the roar of the sea,
We found our strength, we found our key.
Yn nhawelwch y nos, by the cliffs so high,
We built our dreams, under Cornwall’s sky.

Dan y sêr, lle mae’r enaid yn byw,
We found our place, where the heart is true.
Yn y pellter, where the soul resides,
We found our truth, in the sea’s tides.
Through the whispers of the trees, where the ancients tread,
We carved our life, in the paths they led.

On the new horizons, we find our place,
In the waves of life, in the sea’s embrace.
Through the storms, through the calm,
We hold our love, like a treasured psalm.

In the light of dawn, where the ocean meets sky,
We found our dreams, as the days pass by.
In the heart of the sea, where the waves roll,
We found our love, we found our soul.
In the garden’s bloom, where the wildflowers thrived,
We built our home, where love survived.

On the new horizons, we chase the day,
In the waves of life, where love will stay.
Trwy fryniau Cernyw, lle mae’r gorffennol yn dal i fyw,
We found our peace, as the land believes.
In every stone, in every tree,
We found our place, where we’re meant to be.

"Tides of Change"
In "Tides of Change," Mira and Owen's relationship deepens as they navigate the waves of life together, finding strength and solace in each other’s arms. The song reflects the ebb and flow of love’s journey, set against the romantic backdrop of Paris, where every challenge strengthens their bond.
