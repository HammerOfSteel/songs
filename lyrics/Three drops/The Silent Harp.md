# The Silent Harp

**Labels:** Dancing salamanders, Three drops
**Created:** 2024-09-17
**Edited:** 2024-09-17

---

Verse 1:
In the heart of the coedwig deep,
An old man walks, where shadows creep.
His faithful hound runs far ahead,
As twilight paints the sky in red.
Through paths unknown, he gently treads,
To a grove where ancient magic spreads.
A harp he finds, both still and old,
Its strings lie silent, tales untold.

Chorus:
Dawns y galon, where silence sings,
The harp of memories softly rings.
Each silent note, a story spun,
Of days long past, and battles won.
In quiet strings, his heart does hear,
The echoes of a life held dear.

Verse 2:
He touches strings with trembling hand,
Recalling love and life so grand.
The harp remains in silence still,
Yet his heart begins to fill.
With every thought, a note is played,
By memories that never fade.
The silence hums, a soft lament,
Of time that's passed, and love well spent.

Bridge:
The coedwig whispers through the trees,
A song that only the heart sees.
He smiles as tears begin to fall,
For in the silence, he hears it all.
The laughter of his children’s play,
The voice of love that fades away.
In that grove of ancient time,
The silent harp begins to chime.

Outro:
As evening falls and shadows grow,
He takes his leave, with heart aglow.
For though the harp remains so still,
Its silent song, his heart will fill.
