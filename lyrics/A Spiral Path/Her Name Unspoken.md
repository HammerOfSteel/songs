# Her Name Unspoken

**Labels:** A Spiral Path, Dancing salamanders
**Created:** 2024-09-25
**Edited:** 2024-09-25

---

Verse 1:
They speak in whispers, they walk on glass,
As if her memory’s just a shadow of the past.
But I still see her in the morning light,
A part of me that’s missing, just out of sight.

Pre-Chorus:
They’re afraid to say it, afraid to know,
Like secrets bound in Wydd’s forbidden glow.
But it’s this silence that’s tearing me apart,
Keeping her alive only in my heart.

Chorus:
Say her name, don’t let it fade,
In the quiet, it’s a storm that’s been made.
I need to hear it, to feel she was real,
That love doesn’t vanish just because we can’t heal.
Her name unspoken, it lingers in the air,
A ghost of a memory that no one dares to share.

Verse 2:
I see her in every child’s laugh,
In the autumn leaves, in the photograph.
But they turn away, afraid to recall,
The girl who once was, the light of it all.

Pre-Chorus:
Like the Spider Spire, shrouded and steep,
They hide her name, buried deep.
Just one word, and the weight could lift,
But they bury it deeper, as the years shift.

Chorus:
Say her name, don’t let it fade,
In the quiet, it’s a storm that’s been made.
I need to hear it, to feel she was real,
That love doesn’t vanish just because we can’t heal.
Her name unspoken, it lingers in the air,
A ghost of a memory that no one dares to share.

Bridge:
Is it fear that holds your tongue so tight?
Or the knowledge hidden from Wydd’s forbidden sight?
She’s still here, in the space between,
In the words unsaid, and the silence unseen.

Final Chorus:
Say her name, don’t let it fade,
In the silence, it's a debt unpaid.
I need to hear it, to know she was real,
That love doesn’t fade with the wounds we conceal.
Her name unspoken, it lingers like a prayer,
A shadow cast by the light we still bear.
