# See the Sea

**Labels:** Dancing salamanders, Gills and guts
**Created:** 2024-09-20
**Edited:** 2024-09-20

---

Place: Fistral Beach, Newquay

Verse 1
I walk alone, though voices surround,
Footsteps in the sand, no trace to be found.
The sea roars wild, echoes deep within,
A storm inside, where does it begin?
Faces blur, like waves on the shore,
I feel adrift, but anchored no more.
The crowd moves, yet I stay still,
Lost in the tide, against my will.

Chorus
See the sea, it mirrors me,
Endless depths, where I can't breathe.
Rolling waves, a raging storm,
A restless heart, seeking form.
See the sea, it's never free,
Crashing, pulling, drowning me.
But in the flow, I find a way,
To ride the tide and face the day.

Verse 2
Salt in the air, like tears on my skin,
I dive deeper, searching within.
The waves pull strong, the current’s force,
An ocean of sorrow, without a course.
Among the surfers, the sea’s embrace,
I stand alone in this crowded place.
No shore to land, no sky to soar,
Just the ocean’s grip, forevermore.

Bridge
The water whispers, secrets untold,
Of buried feelings, memories old.
Beneath the surface, far below,
Lies a peace I’ll never know.
But in the swirl, the pull, the fight,
I glimpse the dawn, a coming light.

Outro
Fistral calls, the ocean’s song,
I’ve wandered here for far too long.
But through the storm, I learn to steer,
The sea within becomes so clear.
See the sea, it's part of me,
A wild heart, eternally free.
