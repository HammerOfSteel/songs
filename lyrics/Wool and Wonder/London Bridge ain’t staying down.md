# London Bridge ain’t staying down

**Labels:** Wool and Wonder
**Created:** 2025-05-12
**Edited:** 2025-05-12

---

London Bridge is falling down
Falling down, falling down
London Bridge is falling down.
Ain't gonna stay down though, init!

London Bridge is falling down,
Cold rain soaks this northern town.
Late night calls and heavy sighs,
Pint glass empty, tear-filled eyes.

Iron bars ‘round the heart inside,
Bent and broken where hopes collide.
Falling fast, yet hear the sound:
London Bridge still holds its ground.

Bend and break, twist and quake,
Gold inside the ash we make.
Don’t you quit, don’t you hide,
There’s fire left on the other side.

London Bridge ain’t staying down,
We rebuild stone by stone in this town.
Laugh through tears, dance through pain,
My fair lady’s rising again.

Torn coat, worn shoes, bitter wind,
Dark nights where it all begins.
Therapy steps, slow and small,
Mate by mate to break the fall.

Nicked cuppa, stranger’s grin,
Busker songs where dreams begin.
Falling still, but getting through,
London Bridge holds thanks to you.

Darkest night, ember glow,
From the rubble new roots grow.
Broken things hold beauty too,
Old made new, the gold in you.

London Bridge ain’t staying down,
We rebuild stone by stone in this town.
Laugh through tears, dance through pain,
My fair lady’s rising again.

London Bridge ain’t falling down,
Not with love and mates around.
Hold on tight, start again,
My fair lady’s rising again.

I wander thro’ each charter’d street,
Near where the charter’d Thames does flow.
And mark in every face I meet
Marks of weakness, marks of woe.

In every cry of every Man,
In every Infants cry of fear,
In every voice: in every ban,
The mind-forg’d manacles I hear
