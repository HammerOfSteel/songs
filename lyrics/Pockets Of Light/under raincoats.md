# under raincoats

**Labels:** Pockets Of Light
**Created:** 2025-09-01
**Edited:** 2025-09-01

---

ready? zip, click, go.

Under raincoats, tiny boats, pockets like coves,
drip-drop drumline down sleeve-sewn roads.
Hood strings twang, little catgut chimes,
storm writes stanzas on our forearms’ lines.
Gutter’s a snare, curb’s a bell in blue,
puddles hold halos the streetlights brew.
Lift up the coat like a canvas sky,
we wear the weather and let it reply.

Knees go splash, hearts go clang,
hands make cymbals out of rain.

Under raincoats, carry daylight thin,
puddles raise chalices—sip them in.
Under raincoats, joy unbuttons free,
la-la-la (you) / la-la-la (me).
Blue-stained ink, let the pages sing,
sink into marbled marvellous thing.

Exercise-book letters bloom tide-blue,
where raindrops wander the paper through.
Veins of a river in the notebook grain,
marble of maybe in a spill of rain.
Boots make catechisms—left, right, left,
liturgy of puddles in a bright-wet breath.
Wind flips collars like a gull in flight,
we tuck our grins in the coat’s lamplight.

Are we soaked?
Only on the outside.
Cold?
Only till we laugh.

Under raincoats, clatter into glee,
puddles say “cheers” to our muddy knees.
Under raincoats, rhyme our runaway,
la-la-la lead / la-la-la sway.
Blue-ink bloom on the knuckle bone,
sink into joy like a stepping-stone.


Left step, splash; right step, ring,
boots learn prayers that puddles sing.
Coat-cave, close; sleeve-lanterns, glow,
we bless the street by how we go.

la-la-la-la-la,
la-la-la-la

Under raincoats, hushed and bright,
we pocket thunder, we polish night.
Coins in the lining knock time in two,
tick for the puddle, tock for the blue.
Chalice of sidewalk, baptize our socks,
amen of laughter in the letterbox.
Clouds do cartwheels, gutters agree,
we’re little bells in a watery key.

Hands like cymbals, hearts like tin,
storm says “out,” and we say “in.”

Under raincoats, carry daylight thin,
puddles raise chalices, tip them in.
Under raincoats, joy unbuttons free,
la-la-la you / la-la-la me.
Blue-stained ink, let the pages sing,
sink into marbled, marvellous spring.

shake the sleeves… 
listen: drip-drip-done.
