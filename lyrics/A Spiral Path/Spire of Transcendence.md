# Spire of Transcendence

**Created:** 2024-08-20
**Edited:** 2024-08-20

---

Verse 1
Before the Spire, where the shadows break,
I stand at the threshold, as the earth does quake.
The final veil, where illusions weave,
Whispers of what I must now leave.

The echoes of self, they cling and plead,
But the light of the Spire is all I need.
"Om mani padme hum," the mantra hums,
As the truth of my being gently comes.

Chorus
To let go, to rise, where the ego fades,
In the heart of the Spire, where light pervades.
"Om namah shivaya," I call to the sky,
Releasing the self, I begin to fly.

The veil dissolves, where shadows fall,
In the Spire’s embrace, I hear the call.
"Om mani padme hum," the light within,
As the journey ends, and I begin.

Verse 2
Inside the Spire, where the light is pure,
I face the truth, silent and sure.
The ego’s veil, a shadow thin,
Hides the star that burns within.

"Om namah shivaya," I chant with grace,
As the final illusions lose their place.
In this sacred space, where the dualities die,
I see my true self in the Spire’s eye.

Bridge
In the stillness, where the echoes cease,
I find the light, I find the peace.
"Om mani padme hum," the mantra rings,
In the heart of the Spire, the soul sings.

The last of the shadows fade away,
As the Spire reveals the light of day.
"Om namah shivaya," with every breath,
I transcend the self, I conquer death.

Chorus
To let go, to rise, where the ego fades,
In the heart of the Spire, where light pervades.
"Om namah shivaya," I call to the sky,
Releasing the self, I begin to fly.

The veil dissolves, where shadows fall,
In the Spire’s embrace, I hear the call.
"Om mani padme hum," the light within,
As the journey ends, and I begin.

Outro
At the summit, where the Spire meets the sky,
I stand in the light, where spirits fly.
"Om shanti shanti," the mantra flows,
As the true self in the Spire grows.

In the unity of all, I find my peace,
In the light of the Spire, my soul’s release.
"Om mani padme hum," the journey done,
In the Spire’s heart, we are all one.


Elara reaches the Spire and faces the final test, where she must confront and transcend the last vestiges of her ego. The song reflects her journey toward self-realization and the revelation of her true self as she merges with the light of the Spire. It captures the climactic moment of transformation and the dissolution of the ego.
