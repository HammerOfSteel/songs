# Lines in the Sand - Llinellau yn y Tywod

**Labels:** Blodau yn y Lludw
**Created:** 2025-01-18
**Edited:** 2025-01-18

---

The wind carries whispers, soft and near,
Mae’r gwynt yn canu, but it’s quiet here.
We trace the patterns, your hand in mine,
Drawing stories on the shore, line by line.

"Tima fy seren fach"
"Do you see the waves? They sparkle like jewels on treasure caves"
"Your laugh cuts through the morning mist,
The tide creeps closer, but it’s gently kissed"
“Mae’n edrych fel celf you say with a grin,
And I watch as you carve a world within."

Lines in the sand, washed by the tide,
Stories we tell, where moments collide.
Each wave a reminder, fleeting yet grand,
Mae’r môr yn cofio, the lines in the sand.

“Look, little sun, do you see the shell?
Is it a home? Does it have tales to tell?”
A seashell glimmers, a fragment, a trace,
Of something ancient, time can’t erase.
We talk of the waves, their endless refrain,
But I see your wonder, untouched by pain.

The sea gives and takes, a rhythm so pure,
But our lines remain, in memory secure.

Lines in the sand, swept by the sea,
Moments of love, for you and for me.
Each wave a rhythm, soft on the strand,
Yn y gwynt, mae’r straeon yn sefyll yn wan.
(In the wind, the stories faintly stand.)

“Do you think, Tima, that the sea keeps our marks?
Like a treasure buried deep in its heart?”
The sky turns to amber, the day growing late,
We sit by the shore, and I marvel at fate.
Your small hand draws circles, infinite and true,
And I see eternity reflected in you.

Mae’r tonnau’n codi, a’n hanesion yn parhau,
(The waves rise, and our stories endure,)
Each line a connection, fragile yet sure.
Though the sea may erase, it never forgets,
A legacy written in the tides’ silhouettes.

“Pan oeddwn i’n dy oed di,” I softly confess,
I wrote my own stories, now yours are the best.”
As stars take their places, the tide pulls away,
Leaving the canvas where we chose to stay.
We sit together, your tiny hand in mine,
Marking the moments, tracing time’s line.

Lines in the sand, fleeting but bright,
Marks of our love in the fading light.
The tide carries whispers, over the strand,
Mae’r cariad yn parhau, yn y llinellau’n y tywod.
(Love endures, in the lines in the sand.)

The sea sings softly, its lullaby true,
Mae’r gwynt yn tawelu, calling to you.
Under the stars, on this sacred land,
We live forever, in the lines in the sand.
