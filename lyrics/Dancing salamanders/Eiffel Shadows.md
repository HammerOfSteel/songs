Underneath the Parisian sky so blue,
In the shadow of the Eiffel, it’s me and you.
Strolling hand in hand down cobblestone lanes,
In this city of love, where romance reigns.

Through Montmartre’s whispers, artists in the square,

In the city of lights, your hand in mine.

In Paris, we found who we are.

Cafés and bistros, where stories unfold,

In every corner, we find our place.

Beneath the tower’s glow, dreams take flight,

Eiffel shadows, where our hearts align,

In Paris’s light, our love does show.

Eiffel shadows, where our hearts align,
In the city of lights, your hand in mine.
Through laughter and tears, we’ve come so far,

New Year’s Eve under the Arc’s grand light,

A new chapter starts, our journey’s begun.

Eiffel shadows, where our hearts align,
In the city of lights, your hand in mine.

As the sun sets and the city sleeps,
In Paris, our love we keep.
Eiffel shadows, timeless and true,
In the heart of Paris, I love you.
