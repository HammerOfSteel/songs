# Because I Knew You

**Labels:** The Deep Darkness
**Created:** 2025-04-18
**Edited:** 2025-04-18

---

I don’t know how long I was gone.
I remember the dark, not the dawn.
The dread was like glue — thick, slow, real.
I swung at nothing just to feel.

I heard my name like static hum,
Heard your voice when my strength went numb.
Paula, you didn’t shout — you whispered "stay."
Like a thread pulling me back from gray.

It wasn’t power, it wasn’t light,
It was memory... tight.
A kid in a striped shirt, scared and small,
Heard “we believe in you” and didn’t fall.

I wrote letters you’ll never see.
One for each of you. Just for me.
Jeff — I never told you thanks for that scope,
For aiming with logic but walking with hope.

You fixed more than gadgets, bro, you fixed days.
Made a quiet like math that still found a way.
Paula — fire and prayer, soft and fierce.
You burned through lies like truth with tears.

Poo — man, I didn’t get you at first,
But you taught me stillness in the middle of hurt.
I was swinging fists. You just breathed.
And somehow, that steadied me.

We don’t always say goodbye in games.
The screen fades, the story stays.
I thought I’d see you again one day.
But maybe you were never meant to stay.

You’re not gone — you’re sewn in my walk,
In the pauses, in how I talk.
When I doubt, I hear your laugh.
When I rise, you’re half the path.

I didn’t win. I just didn’t stop.
And I got here ‘cause you stayed through the drop.
You don’t see them again, not ‘cause they’re gone —
But ‘cause you’ve arrived. And you go on.

I held on ‘cause you held me.
I spoke loud ‘cause you believed.
And when I finally saw the sky again,
It was your names in the breeze.

This isn’t magic. This is mine.
This is heart rebuilt in time.
And every step I take from here...
Is because I knew you.
