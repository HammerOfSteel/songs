# The River’s Quiet Flow – Afon Teifi, Ceredigion

**Labels:** Dancing salamanders, Ar Lan y Mor
**Created:** 2024-09-19
**Edited:** 2024-09-19

---

Verse 1:
By the banks of the river, so calm and wide,
I stand alone where the waters glide.
The current whispers soft and low,
Carrying the weight of all I know.

Each ripple tells a tale of days gone by,
Of moments lost beneath the sky.
But here, where the Teifi gently bends,
I find the peace that time transcends.

Chorus:
Flow, river, flow, through the depths of my pain,
Wash away the sorrow, leave only the rain.
In your quiet rhythm, I find my way,
Guided by her spirit at the break of day.

Verse 2:
The Afanc sleeps in your waters deep,
A creature of dreams, of silence and sleep.
And like the river’s steady grace,
I learn to let go, to find my place.

She’s with me still, in the rustling leaves,
In the gentle breeze, in the sighing trees.
Her laughter flows with the river’s song,
A melody that carries me along.

Chorus:
Flow, river, flow, through the depths of my pain,
Wash away the sorrow, leave only the rain.
In your quiet rhythm, I find my way,
Guided by her spirit at the break of day.

Bridge:
The river knows, the river sees,
The quiet strength in the bending trees.
And as it winds through hill and dale,
It carries with it love’s sweet tale.

Chorus:
Flow, river, flow, through the depths of my pain,
Wash away the sorrow, leave only the rain.
In your quiet rhythm, I find my way,
Guided by her spirit at the break of day.

Outro:
So here I stand, where the waters run clear,
With every flow, I feel her near.
The Teifi speaks in a voice so low,
Healing my heart with its gentle flow.

Safaf i’m hun lle mae’r dyfroedd yn llithro’n lan.
Y cerrynt yn sibrwd yn fwyn ac yn isel,
Gan gario’r baich o’r hyn rwy’n ei deimlo’n drysor.

Caf lechu mewn heddwch y tu hwnt i’r gofid mawr.
