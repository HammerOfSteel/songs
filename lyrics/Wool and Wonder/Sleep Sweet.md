# Sleep Sweet

**Labels:** Wool and Wonder
**Created:** 2025-05-20
**Edited:** 2025-05-20

---

Shhh now, love…
One more rhyme, yeah? Then sleep.

Covers off again, feet cold out,
You kick like you got dreams to shout.
Bedtime’s wild, but we’ve got form—
One sock lost, but the heart stays warm.

You blink like stars, wide like skies,
Cheek full of biscuit, half-closed eyes.
I hum what she used to sing to you,
Now it’s bars in the dark and the beat is true.

Snug in this room with the heater low,
I'm holdin' the roof up with toast and flow.
One hand on your back, one eye on the bills,
But I'd carry the lot just to see you still.

They say lads don't cry—lie of the year.
I’ve wept in the loo just to not show fear.
Your nan don’t ring, and the uncles drift,
But your giggle’s enough, that's the holy gift.

You ask where she is—I point to the sky,
Say, "She’s watching you sleep, no need to cry."
She’d braid your hair like a warrior queen,
Now it’s wonky plaits but you say I’m keen.

There’s glitter in shoes, crumbs in the hall,
Drawings of dragons on every wall.
We make our world with sellotape dreams,
And I rhyme you to sleep through busted seams.

Sleep sweet, lil’ fox, moon’s on patrol,
(I got you, I got you, whole of my soul.)

Dream wild, my light, no harm gets near,
(You laugh, I’ll stay. You breathe, I’m here.)

You run the flat like the mayor of joy,
Stealin’ all snacks, claimin’ all toys.
You said once “both Mum and Dad,”
And I folded—half proud, half sad.

Your coat’s too big but you wear it proud,
Like the sky’s your mate and you shout out loud.
In your sleep you mutter full-blown tales,
Of foxes in boots and ships with sails.

I miss her laugh but I see it in you,
That curl in your grin, that fire that grew.
So hush now, love, just close them eyes—
I’m right here always, come wind or rise.

Sleep sweet, starsailor, little sun glow,
(Still here, still yours, through every low.)
Drift light, Elara, my stars and sea,
(The world might shift, but you got me.)
