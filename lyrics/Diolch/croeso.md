# croeso

**Labels:** Diolch
**Created:** 2025-11-27
**Edited:** 2025-12-09

---

The latch is lifted, the door is heavy,
It's made of all the things we couldn't say.
Come out of the rain, the field is muddy,
You don't have to dust the sorrow of today.
The fire's burning low, a quiet ember,
This is the place where we remember,
Here be no dragons, monsters nor foe,
But friend you must know,
though much of you it will require,
Should you so desire,
To look.

Leave the heavy cloak upon the chair,
Let your weary shoulders finally fall.
You don't need a single word or prayer,
Just a listening heart to hear the call.

croeso, the window is still open wide.
croeso, to everything you feel inside.
Lay what you carry down beside the wall,
Let it all splinter, 
let it crumble, 
let it fall.
We are here together,
right beside the flame,
And everything you bring,
we'll call it by its name.
croeso, croeso.

Look at the light that falls across the floor,
friend, no score to settle, no fear,
Just sit down beside me, we're all here.
and friend if you have no words to say,
Such is okay,
There is no one way,
about this.
There is breaking of this bread,

Enough for us all.
For all the grand, the tall and the small,
Smallest stories carried by an old tired heart.
croeso, friend.
Let us start.

croeso, the window is still open wide.
croeso, to everything carried inside.
Lay what you carry down beside the wall,
Let it all splinter,
let it crumble,
let it fall.
We are here together,
right beside the flame,
And everything you bring,
we'll call it by its name.
croeso, croeso.

There's no forgetting,
just a slow return,
To all of it,
all of it as one.
Beneath the shelter,
after all is done.
Come in,
Here is no need to pretend.
croeso,
croeso,
Friend.
