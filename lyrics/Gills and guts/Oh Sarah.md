# Oh Sarah

**Labels:** Dancing salamanders, Gills and guts
**Created:** 2024-09-20
**Edited:** 2024-09-20

---

Place: Porthcurno Beach

Verse 1
The tide rolls in, the sky’s aflame,
I sit alone and speak your name.
Oh Sarah, in your troubled night,
I feel your pain, but can’t set it right.
The waves crash hard, they echo loud,
Your heart’s been lost among the crowd.
But here I stay, just by your side,
Even in the dark, I’ll be your guide.

Pre-Chorus
I don’t have the words to take away your pain,
But I’ll sit with you through every storm and rain.
Oh Sarah, can you feel the light?
I’ll be here with you, through the endless fight.

Chorus
Oh Sarah, when you feel alone,
Know you’ve got a place, a heart, a home.
When the waves crash high, and you’re sinking low,
I’ll stand with you, wherever you go.
Oh Sarah, I can’t ease your mind,
But I’ll walk with you, till peace you find.

Verse 2
The sea is wide, the sky is grey,
I know your fears won’t fade away.
Oh Sarah, in your endless storm,
I see you fighting to keep warm.
The world feels cold, but here I stay,
In silence, love will light our way.
You don’t have to speak or say a word,
Just know your pain has been heard.

Pre-Chorus
I don’t have the words to take away your pain,
But I’ll sit with you through every storm and rain.
Oh Sarah, can you feel the light?
I’ll be here with you, through the endless fight.

Chorus
Oh Sarah, when you feel alone,
Know you’ve got a place, a heart, a home.
When the waves crash high, and you’re sinking low,
I’ll stand with you, wherever you go.
Oh Sarah, I can’t ease your mind,
But I’ll walk with you, till peace you find.

Bridge
And in the darkest hours, I’ll hold the line,
Till the morning sun begins to shine.
No need to face this storm alone,
Together, we will find our way back home.

Final Chorus
Oh Sarah, when you feel alone,
Know you’ve got a place, a heart, a home.
When the waves crash high, and you’re sinking low,
I’ll stand with you, wherever you go.
Oh Sarah, I can’t ease your mind,
But I’ll walk with you, till peace you find.

Outro
The sea may rage, the winds may blow,
But I’ll be here, I’ll let you know.
Oh Sarah, you’re never out of sight,
In the darkest seas, I’ll be your light.
