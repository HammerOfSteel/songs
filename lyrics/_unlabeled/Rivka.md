# Rivka

**Created:** 2024-08-06
**Edited:** 2024-08-06

---

Verse 1
A traveler arrives in a city so grand,
With dreams in her heart and a mission at hand.
She carries a treasure, unseen but bright,
Guided by an inner fire, burning through the night.

Chorus
Rivka, bearer of light,
Through shadows and trials, she holds tight.
Tradition carried, through grit and fire,
In the heart of the storm, she rises higher.

Verse 2
The city looms large, with obstacles tall,
But she stands undaunted, refusing to fall.
In the alchemy of struggle, her spirit refines,
Transforming the base into something divine.

Chorus
Rivka, bearer of light,
Through shadows and trials, she holds tight.
Tradition carried, through grit and fire,
In the heart of the storm, she rises higher.

Bridge
"If you're going through trials," the old wisdom speaks,
"Keep forging ahead, for strength is what it seeks."
In every challenge, in every test,
She finds the courage, to give her best.

Verse 3
Years later, she sits with nieces around,
Their eyes filled with wonder, as her tales resound.
Of a journey long past, of a mission so grand,
Of a treasure within, like gold in the sand.

Chorus
Rivka, bearer of light,
Through shadows and trials, she holds tight.
Tradition carried, through grit and fire,
In the heart of the storm, she rises higher.

Outro
In the warmth of her stories, they find their way,
Guided by Rivka, come what may.
With a treasure unseen, but a legacy clear,
She inspires the hearts that gather near.
