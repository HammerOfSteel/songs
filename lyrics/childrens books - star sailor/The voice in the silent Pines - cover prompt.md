# The voice in the silent Pines - cover prompt

**Labels:** childrens books - star sailor
**Created:** 2024-10-05
**Edited:** 2024-10-06

---

A misty, moonlit forest scene with tall, shadowy pine trees and soft blue and silver light filtering through the branches. In the center, a lone wolf with dark, slightly unkempt fur walks slowly through the forest, his head lowered and eyes filled with sadness. To his side, Star Sailor’s crescent-moon sailboat floats gently beside him, glowing softly. Star Sailor, a tiny girl around 2-3 years old, with short brown hair and bright blue eyes, stands in the boat, looking down at the wolf with a gentle, compassionate expression. The sailboat emits a soft, warm glow, illuminating the mist around them. The overall scene should feel ethereal, filled with a sense of quiet sorrow and a glimmer of hope, capturing the contrast between the wolf’s loneliness and Star Sailor’s comforting presence. The colors should be cool blues, silvers, and soft whites, with the crescent moon hanging high above in the night sky
