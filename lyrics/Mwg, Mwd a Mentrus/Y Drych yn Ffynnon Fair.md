[Spoken Intro – barely above a whisper]
Ffynnon Fair… dwi’n ôl.
St. Mary’s Well… I’m back.

Alla i ddim gofyn dim… ond paid â chadw’n dawel.
I can’t ask for anything… but don’t stay silent.

[Verse 1]
Trodd y llwybr yn dynn, dan frwyn a chwyn,
The path turned narrow, under reeds and weeds,

Fy esgidiau’n cofio’r tir cyn i fi.
My shoes remembered the ground before I did.

Roedd y dŵr mor llonydd, ond roedd rhywbeth yn symud,
The water was so still, but something moved,

Dim gwynt, dim pysgodyn, dim byd i’w weld.
No wind, no fish, nothing to see.

A fy llais, pan siaradais, oedd fel rhywun arall,
And my voice, when I spoke, felt like someone else,

Hen llais heb wyneb, ond yn dal i deimlo.
An old voice with no face, but still feeling.

[Chorus 1]
Y drych yn y ffynnon, ond nid fi oedd yno,
The mirror in the well, but it wasn’t me there,

Ei llygaid yn adnabod, ei gwên yn ifanc.
Her eyes knew me, her smile was young.

Dyma fi… ond hebddo fe.
This is me… but without him.

Dim llaw yn dal, dim sain ei lais,
No hand held, no sound of his voice,

Ond roedd yr atgof yn llaith ar fy mreichiau.
But the memory was damp on my arms.

[Verse 2]
Gwelais ni’n ifanc, ar ochr y dŵr,
I saw us young, beside the water,

Chwerthin mor uchel, roedd adar yn hedfan.
Laughing so loud, birds flew up.

Fe roddodd gusan—dim mwy na eiliad—
He gave a kiss—no more than a second—

Ond mae eiliadau’n byw am flynyddoedd yma.
But seconds live for years here.

Fe drodd, ac fe aeth, fel niwl dros y bryn,
He turned, and he left, like mist over the hill,

A dwi dal yn sefyll… fel carreg.
And I’m still standing… like a stone.

[Bridge – layered whispers, growing softly]
“Wyt ti’n fy nghofio?”… meddais i wrthyf fy hun.
"Do you remember me?"… I said to myself.

Ond dim ateb, dim cilio—ond aros.
But no answer, no retreat—just staying.

Roedd y ddwy fi yno, ar wahan gan dŵr,
Both of me were there, divided by water,

Ac roedd y dŵr yn gwybod mwy na’r ddwy.
And the water knew more than either of us.

[Chorus 2]
Y drych yn y ffynnon, ond nid fi bellach,
The mirror in the well, but not me anymore,

Ei gwên yn dawel, ei gwallt heb wall.
Her smile quiet, her hair untouched.

Dyma fi… ond efallai cof ohonof.
This is me… or maybe the memory of me.

Mae’r dŵr yn cadw’r hyn ni allwn ni,
The water keeps what we could not,

Ac mae’r drych yn dychwelyd dim ond pan wyt ti’n barod.
And the mirror returns only when you’re ready.

[Outro – very soft, fading]
Troais i ffwrdd, ond arhosodd fy adlewyrchiad.
I turned away, but my reflection stayed.

Efallai dyna yw’r gwir…
Maybe that is the truth…

Mae’r atgofion yn aros lle mae’r galon yn gollwng.
Memories stay where the heart lets go.
