# The Wandering Star

**Labels:** Dancing salamanders, Three drops
**Created:** 2024-08-28
**Edited:** 2024-08-28

---

Verse 1
Across the heavens, a light breaks free,
A star that roams, a mystery,
Not fixed in place, nor bound by fate,
A guide for those who hesitate.
From east to west, through endless night,
A wanderer with a burning sight,
A beacon where the shadows are,
An oergriw serch, the wandering star.

Chorus
Lloer yn symud, seren heb ofn,
(Moon that moves, a star without fear)
Yn dangos y ffordd, i’r dyfodol sy’n bod.
(It shows the way, to the future that is)
Through the darkened sky it wends afar,
A light eternal, the wandering star.

Verse 2
The star that falls into the depths,
Where waters kiss the river's breadth,
A flame that meets the ocean’s grace,
Reflects within Elffin’s gaze.
A sign of hope, a voice reborn,
Taliesin’s words from darkness torn.
The blazing star of ancient lore,
Guiding on to distant shores.

Chorus
Lloer yn symud, seren heb ofn,
(Moon that moves, a star without fear)
Yn dangos y ffordd, i’r dyfodol sy’n bod.
(It shows the way, to the future that is)
Through the ever-turning cosmic dance,
A light that dares, a second chance.

Bridge
In every shape, in every form,
The star transforms through calm and storm,
As Taliesin once became,
The salmon, the hare, the newborn flame.
The wise and foolish, the hidden lore,
A star that seeks, yet asks for more,
In every wave, in every wind,
A tale that starts, a truth that bends.

Chorus
Lloer yn symud, seren heb ofn,
(Moon that moves, a star without fear)
Yn dangos y ffordd, i’r dyfodol sy’n bod.
(It shows the way, to the future that is)
A star that whispers from afar,
To seekers lost, the wandering star.

Outro
Oh, star that roams, o seren ddiddiwedd,
(O endless star)
Draw me forth to worlds unknown,
Through shadows deep, through waters wide,
Guide me where the truths abide.
And when my journey’s end is near,
I’ll know you by your blaze so clear,
Seren ar goll, seren o’r nefoedd,
(Lost star, star of the heavens)
The path is found through the wandering star.

Song Description: "The Wandering Star"
"The Wandering Star" weaves a tale of a mysterious celestial guide that appears unexpectedly in the night sky, echoing both the star of Bethlehem and the star that leads to the discovery of the bard Taliesin in Welsh myth. The lyrics are layered with metaphors and references to Taliesin’s legendary transformations, symbolizing the ability to adapt and find wisdom in change. This star, unbound and fearless, becomes a symbol for the Seeker's journey, a light that moves through darkness, guiding the lost towards discovery and renewal.
