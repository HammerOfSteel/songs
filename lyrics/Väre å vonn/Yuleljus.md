# Yuleljus

**Labels:** Väre å vonn
**Created:** 2025-06-03
**Edited:** 2025-06-03

---

Nu tändas tusen juleljus,
In windows frosted thin,
They shine for those who can’t come home,
And those who live within.

A light for hearts that broke in snow,
A flame for nights grown wide,
A whisper through the chimneys low —
We’re still here, side by side.

Backsteps iced, kettle sings on low,
Nain’s old jam jar glows in candle throw.
Risca dark, them terraces hush,
Just frost and the dog and that chimney crush.

Hwn yw'r nos — the long one, still,
But she laughs when I ask if we’re climbing the hill.
No more sleds, not this year love,
Watching stars, down in the garden above just Above.

And in each warm pane across the street,
A thousand ghosts tap tiny feet.
I carry her, barely awake —
To bed, past lights for memory’s sake.

There’s a robin by the gate, just stares me down,
Like he knows what's lost in this mining town.
We light three candles — hum long gone,
And she asks if they still sing when we switch them on.

I say yes, every flame has a song,
Every flake outside hums bout love that belonged.
hand in mine, cold nose and grin,
“you’re warm tad, you glow from within.”

And maybe that’s it — this whole frostbit hymn,
A way to remember while holding it in.
Not joy, not peace, stillness bright —
We’re here, we love, we make the light.

Little light dreams, but her breath still sings,
Like sleigh bells tied to a raven’s wings.
Outside: snow. Inside: spark.
We hold our hearts in the glowing dark.

Papa… look, moon’s out bright,
Cold’s like teeth, but the sky’s alright.
There’s a bloke by the bins, he’s got no hat —
Shall we give him ours?
(…Yeah, we can do that.)
She grins like a spark in the middle of frost,
"Kindness," she says, "don’t get lost."

We walk where the cold’s laid track,
Boots go squish and the sky leans back.
Every step leaves a glow, just a hint,
Like fire lived here, like hearts don’t quit.
So if you’ve got warmth, go pass it on —
It’s the only thing that don’t weigh a ton.
