# Echoes in the garden

**Labels:** Geddon Bird
**Created:** 2024-10-26
**Edited:** 2024-10-26

---

(Verse 1)
We walk beneath branches silver with night,
The moon drapes the garden in quiet light.
Maya hums softly, a tune without end,
She says, "Listen closely—the wind is a friend."

The leaves murmur secrets, the stones hum with grace,
The garden remembers each path and place.
I follow her steps, though I don’t know why,
The answers feel close, just out of my eye.

(Chorus)
Echoes drift through the garden, unseen,
Whispers of all that has ever been.
The moon holds stories in shadows and gleam,
The world is a dream within a dream.

(Verse 2)
The path curves ahead like a thread spun tight,
Every step taken is drawn toward night.
Maya turns with a smile soft and wise,
"Truths hide in silence, not in disguise."

We stop by a tree where the branches entwine,
The roots spread below, like threads that align.
The garden breathes stories too old to tell,
Weaves them through petals where mysteries dwell.

(Chorus)
Echoes drift through the garden, unseen,
Whispers of all that has ever been.
The moon holds stories in shadows and gleam,
The world is a dream within a dream.

(Bridge)
A breeze stirs the flowers, petals take flight,
Maya says, "Nothing is lost in the night."
The garden remembers what words forget,
Each leaf a promise, though not spoken yet.

(Chorus)
Echoes drift through the garden, unseen,
Whispers of all that has ever been.
The moon holds stories in shadows and gleam,
The world is a dream within a dream.

(Outro)
We walk through the night as the garden hums low,
The paths will remain, though we may not know.
And as we leave, the wind carries a song—
The echoes of things we knew all along.
