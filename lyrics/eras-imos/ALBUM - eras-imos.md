# ALBUM - eras-imos

**Labels:** eras-imos
**Created:** 2024-11-27
**Edited:** 2024-11-27

---

1. "The Puzzle's Symphony"
Theme: Celebrating the uniqueness of autism and how every piece of the puzzle contributes to a beautiful whole.
Narrative: A symbolic journey through a world of intricate patterns, where the protagonist discovers that the "puzzle" is not something to solve but a masterpiece to marvel at.
Imagery: Vibrant, shifting landscapes of colors, sounds, and textures that reflect Rasmus's unique way of experiencing the world.

2. "The Silent Thread"
Theme: Nonverbal communication and the depth of connection without words.
Narrative: A parent reflects on the unspoken language between them and their child, finding meaning in gestures, shared looks, and quiet moments.
Imagery: A golden thread connecting hearts, weaving an unbreakable bond across silence.

3. "Constellations of Connection"
Theme: Navigating the unique ways Rasmus expresses love and connection.
Narrative: Each verse represents a "constellation" of moments where love shines: a shared laugh, a breakthrough in communication, or a comforting hug.
Imagery: Stars forming constellations that symbolize milestones and memories.

4. "Blueprint of a Dreamer"
Theme: Celebrating Rasmus’s individuality, imagination, and dreams.
Narrative: A story of a boy building a world from his imagination, shaping it with his unique creativity and inspiring others.
Imagery: A fantastical city rising from blueprints sketched by a young dreamer, filled with impossible structures and magical details.

5. "Rainfall in Reverse"
Theme: Challenging conventional ideas.
Narrative: Rasmus notices the rain falling upward and follows it into the sky, where he discovers a hidden world that defies all logic.
Imagery: Raindrops rising into glowing clouds, lifting the boy into a surreal, dreamlike realm.

6. "The Harmony Machine"
Theme: Understanding emotions through music.
Narrative: A boy builds a machine that turns feelings into music, creating symphonies that express what words cannot.
Imagery: A room filled with swirling, colorful notes that dance in rhythm with emotions.

7. "Kaleidoscope Tree"
Theme: Growth and individuality.
Narrative: A magical tree grows in a boy’s backyard, with branches that reflect his ever-changing emotions and thoughts.
Imagery: A kaleidoscope of shifting colors and patterns on the tree’s leaves, each representing a unique feeling.

8. "The Wind’s Forgotten Name"
Theme: Memory and identity.
Narrative: The boy befriends the wind, which has forgotten its name. Together, they search for its true identity.
Imagery: Whirling breezes carrying whispers and fragments of names across a sunlit plain.

9. "The Color That Didn’t Exist"
Theme: Discovering the unimaginable.
Narrative: The boy sees a color no one else can perceive, and he sets out to show the world the beauty of what they’re missing.
Imagery: A landscape painted in surreal, otherworldly hues, dominated by an indescribable new color.

10. "Echoes in the Key of Blue"
Theme: Emotional resonance and sound.
Narrative: A boy discovers that emotions have musical notes, and he learns to play his feelings to create harmony.
Imagery: Vibrant waves of sound, each a different shade of blue, rippling through the air.

11. "The Well of Many Voices"
Lore Inspiration: The well of wisdom, Mímisbrunnr, from Norse mythology.
Theme: A journey of understanding and communication.
Narrative: A boy visits a well where the waters echo with countless voices. Each voice holds a piece of wisdom, and he learns to listen and find his own among them.
Imagery: A shimmering well in an ancient forest, surrounded by runestones that glow softly as they speak.

12. "Runes of the Rain"
Lore Inspiration: The magical nature of runes in Norse and Swedish folklore.
Theme: Hidden messages and understanding.
Narrative: Raindrops form runes on a window, and the boy deciphers their message to unlock a forgotten story.
Imagery: A rainy scene where drops spell glowing runes on glass, forming patterns of ancient language.

13. "Loke’s Little Trick"
Lore Inspiration: Loki, the Norse trickster god.
Theme: Playfulness and adaptation.
Narrative: Loki plays a harmless prank on the boy, but the boy outsmarts him, teaching the god a lesson about empathy and kindness.
Imagery: A mischievous Loki, shapeshifting in a glowing, shifting landscape while the boy stands calmly, holding the answer to the riddle.
