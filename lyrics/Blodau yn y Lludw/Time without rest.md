# Time without rest

**Labels:** Blodau yn y Lludw
**Created:** 2025-01-16
**Edited:** 2025-01-16

---

The path winds steep, through shadow and stone,
A climb that feels less like leaving, more like home.
The mountain hums with an ancient tune,
A lullaby cradled beneath the moon.
And in the breeze, her voice takes flight,
A whisper that warms the coldest night.

She drifts on the edge of a dream half-seen,
Through a doorway carved in twilight’s gleam.
Nain calls softly, her arms open wide,
A fire’s glow, a love that never died.
Her hands are warm, not a tear,
“Come, my star, right here.”

Time without rest, it pulls and it sways,
Through silent nights and endless days.
A thread unbroken, though miles apart,
Love lingers still in the quiet heart.

The pool reflects a father’s face,
Lines of sorrow time cannot erase.
Tima watches, her hand outstretched,
A bond untethered, yet still it connects.
She kneels by the water, whispers his name,
Her heart a beacon, a steady flame.

The stars in the water ripple and gleam,
A bridge to the world that lies between.

Time without rest, it ebbs and it flows,
Through valley of marrow, in to the rows .
A light in the shadow, a silver in night,
Her love still burns, an eternal light.

The veil thins; he feels her near,
A presence soft, her voice right there.
“Dad I'm right here, you're not alone"
Through the night, he holds her close.
No words, just the rhythm of hearts aligned,
A fleeting moment, beyond all time.

The dawn awakes in briliant light,
Her essence lingers, in his sight.
In his heart a secret known,
In the soul the sunflower seed was sown.

Time without rest, the circle turn,
In the heart the darkness burn
Through the silence of stars, her voice still sings,
A melody carried on timeless wings.

The summit speaks, the winds reply,
Her spirit lives in the earth and sky.
Dad, I'm with you every day ,
Through good and bad, come what may.
