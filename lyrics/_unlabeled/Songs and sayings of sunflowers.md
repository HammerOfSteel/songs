# Songs and sayings of sunflowers

**Created:** 2025-04-21
**Edited:** 2025-04-21

---

Hi Tad, it’s me, your little star,
I want to ask you how you are.
But then, I see, I know.. you know.
Hoppy sleeps by my side each night,
She’s scared of the dark, but I hold her tight.

I made you a pancake but it floated away,
So I ate the corner, hope that’s okay.
Boe still smells like marmalade toast,
He says you’re the one he misses the most.

Sunflower star, I’m still turned your way,
Even if clouds make you cry in the day.
I’m just up high, where hugs don’t land —
But I hold your hand with my not-hand hand.

Remember the morning we both got wet,
We laughed and you said, “Best walk yet.”
Stomped in puddles, you made tea,
let me stir — it splashed on your knee.

I remember Cwmcarn and the path to the top,
“Little legs, don’t ever stop.”
Saw a fox and a sleepy crow,
Told you I’d never ever grow.
Grow from your cutsh and arms,
Sheepwool Sox and old Welsh charms.

Sunflower seed, grow through time,
Up the twmp and down in rhyme.
You can’t see me but I’m not gone,
I’m curled in your quilt when the night feels long.

Mam is here, and she sings by the light,
She hums like she did on the coldest night.
Nain bakes clouds with jam inside,
And Taid reads books where dragons hide.

I wake up early and play in the sky,
Me and Boe and the foxes fly.
Hoppy rests and says she fine,
Cloud tea's here at noon and nine

Your sunflower, shine your name,
A little star, all the same.
I know it's hard to hug me now,
In where's and why's and somehow.
Even though you take the long way
All you need to know is
"Jag älskar dig"

Tad.
It's okay to bee
Bee bee buzz, a little lost.
like the sheep aye.

I know you'll be home,
saving a cutch, 
for you and me
Everything will be okay dad,
You'll see.

Love, little star
