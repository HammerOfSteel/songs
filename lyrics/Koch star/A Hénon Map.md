# A Hénon Map

**Labels:** Koch star
**Created:** 2024-12-03
**Edited:** 2024-12-04

---

Walls that move, a shifting maze,
Paths dissolve in the morning haze.
Voices whisper, “Turn around,”
But the answer’s lost, nowhere found.

The Labyrinth Keeper waits unseen,
Behind the walls, they weave the scheme.
Steps I take are wiped away,
The map I follow leads astray.

In chaos lies a hidden thread,
A pattern where the lost are led.
Each turn I make, a story told,
In fractured paths, I find my hold.

Time slips through like grains of sand,
Memories blurred by unseen hands.
Who am I when the mirror breaks?
A thousand faces the silence takes.

In chaos lies a hidden thread,
A pattern where the lost are led.
Each turn I make, a story told,
In fractured paths, I find my hold.

"You are more than scattered lines,
The Keeper guards but cannot bind.
The maze you walk is not your foe,
Each twist and turn helps you grow."

I see the Keeper’s shadow fade,
Their taunts are echoes, unafraid.
The shifting walls no longer lie,
They show me how, they tell me why.

In chaos lies a hidden thread,
A pattern where the lost are led.
Each turn I make, a story told,
In fractured paths, I find my hold.

The maze was built to shield my heart,
Its shifting paths, a work of art.
I walk these lines with clearer sight,
A map of shadows, born of light.

Walls no longer rise to bind,
Their shifting grace was always kind.
In the map of chaos, I see me,
A fractal whole, unbroken, free.
