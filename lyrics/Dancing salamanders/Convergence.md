"Convergence"
Set against the backdrop of Paris, "Convergence" narrates the fateful meeting of Mira and Owen, where their souls connect amidst the city’s ancient streets and hidden secrets. The song explores the idea of destiny through poetic symbolism and alchemical references, blending their lives in a dance of fate.
