Verse 1
In the mist, the world is veiled,
A tapestry of grey and green,
Between the breaths of dawn and dusk,
Where the seen and unseen convene.
Each step a question, each turn a prayer,
A whisper in the ancient air,
Through paths that twist like serpent’s scales,
Past y gorsedd of lost tales.

Chorus
Dyma’r ffordd, yn gudd o’r byd,
(The path is here, hidden from the world)
Yn llawn o hud, yn llawn o hyfryd.
(Filled with magic, filled with delight)
Where shadows dance with the morning dew,
And truth wears a cloak of the moon's cold hue.

Verse 2
An oaken door of roots and stone,
Beckons with a silent tone,
A gateway to the hollow hill,
Where time stands still, and dreams distill.
Each step an echo of an age-old rhyme,
A riddle spun from the threads of time,
Eyes of wyrdd like mirrored pools,
Reflect the wisdom of forgotten fools.

Chorus
Dyma’r ffordd, yn cudd o’r byd,
(The path is here, hidden from the world)
Yn llawn o hud, yn llawn o hyfryd.
(Filled with magic, filled with delight)
Where the boughs bend low to whisper truth,
And rivers run with eternal youth.

Bridge
In the silence where shadows sleep,
The secrets of the earth run deep.
From the cradle of oak to the sea’s refrain,
Everything is lost, yet all remains.
Underneath the stars' distant light,
There is no wrong; there is no right,
Only paths that twist and intertwine,
In the tapestry of space and time.

Chorus
Dyma’r ffordd, yn gudd o’r byd,
(The path is here, hidden from the world)
Yn llawn o hud, yn llawn o hyfryd.
(Filled with magic, filled with delight)
Where fate and fortune weave and wind,
And the mist reveals what was left behind.

Outro
Beneath the mists, the truth lies bare,
In every breath, in every prayer.
A step into the unknown deep,
Where the ancient oaths forever keep.
Dyma’r ffordd, fy nhaith, fy fflam,
(This is the path, my journey, my flame)
Through shadowed woods to an endless calm.

Song Description: "The Mist-Laden Path"
The song "The Mist-Laden Path" begins the Seeker's journey with a step into a mystical landscape shrouded in mist. Drawing on the Celtic idea of a hidden, liminal path — dyma’r ffordd — the lyrics explore themes of uncertainty, spiritual awakening, and the quest for deeper meaning. Welsh phrases add layers of mystery and heritage, emphasizing the connection between the natural and supernatural, the seen and unseen, and the eternal search for truth and wisdom. This song captures the essence of a journey that is both literal and metaphorical, leading deeper into the unknown realms where answers are hidden among the mists.
