# Little chime

**Labels:** Dancing salamanders
**Created:** 2024-08-05
**Edited:** 2024-08-05

---

Verse 1
In a village quaint, by the forest's edge,
A young girl wandered, on the courage she pledged.
With a little bell, that softly chimed,
Invisible to the world, but her heart shined.

Chorus
Little chime, in the silent night,
Guiding hearts, with a gentle light.
Though unseen, your spirit's bright,
In the shadows, you find your might.

Verse 2
Her cousin, a witch, with magic profound,
Traveled the world, where wonders abound.
But she, with no magic, no spells to cast,
Found her strength, in the quiet, at last.

Chorus
Little chime, in the silent night,
Guiding hearts, with a gentle light.
Though unseen, your spirit's bright,
In the shadows, you find your might.

Bridge
In the village, she moved unseen,
Helping hands, where she'd been.
A whisper of courage, a silent cheer,
In her quiet way, she drew near.

Verse 3
From fixing a wheel to mending a heart,
Her gentle touch, a silent art.
With every chime, her bravery grew,
In the village's heart, her spirit flew.

Chorus
Little chime, in the silent night,
Guiding hearts, with a gentle light.
Though unseen, your spirit's bright,
In the shadows, you find your might.

Outro
The curse remains, but so does her light,
A girl unseen, yet shining bright.
With a heart of gold and courage untold,
In the village's tale, her story's rolled.
