# Beneath the Rowan’s Shade

**Labels:** Dancing salamanders, Drink deeply, and dream
**Created:** 2024-09-07
**Edited:** 2024-09-07

---

This song captures the tension of standing at a crossroads, where the traveler must choose between paths of healing or darkness. The ancient rowan tree, a silent guardian, witnesses this pivotal moment, embodying the folklore’s deep connection to protection and the unseen forces that guide our lives.

Verse 1
Through the whispering woods, a traveler roams,
Guided by whispers, led far from home.
Where shadows twist and secrets keep,
The rowan tree stands tall and steep.
No sun can pierce its ancient leaves,
No moonlight there, the traveler perceives.
In twilight’s hush, they pause and wait,
Before the tree that guards the gate.

Chorus
Oh, rowan tree, with berries red,
A silent sentinel for the dread.
No words you speak, no truth you share,
But shelter me from despair.
In your shade, I’ll find my way,
Through darkened night or dawning day.

Verse 2
One path is cloaked in forest deep,
Where echoes of the lost souls weep.
The other leads to a distant glow,
A village light, where warm winds blow.
The traveler’s heart, it pounds and aches,
For the choice that every soul must make.
The rowan watches, silent, still,
As fate bends to the traveler’s will.

Chorus
Oh, rowan tree, with berries bright,
Guide me through this endless night.
In your shade, the winds may howl,
But in my heart, I won’t let fear prowl.
In your embrace, I’ll find my way,
Through paths unknown, come what may.

Bridge
In the rustling leaves, a whisper calls,
A voice that echoes through ancient halls.
“Choose your path, the time is nigh,
For in your choice, your fate shall lie.”
The traveler breathes, then takes a step,
Into the shade where secrets are kept.

Chorus
Oh, rowan tree, with berries red,
A silent sentinel for the dread.
No words you speak, no truth you share,
But shelter me from despair.
In your shade, I’ll find my way,
Through darkened night or dawning day.

Outro
Beneath the rowan, the traveler’s choice,
Echoes softly, like a forgotten voice.
And as they walk, through light or gloom,
The rowan stands, a guardian of doom.
For every soul must find its way,
Beneath the rowan’s shade, come what may.
