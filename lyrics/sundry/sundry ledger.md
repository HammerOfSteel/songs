# sundry ledger

**Labels:** sundry
**Created:** 2026-01-12
**Edited:** 2026-01-12

---

Four-fifteen,
tis' tiniest of an arm,
sweet little moreeng alarm.

slow, strech of a spine.
perfectly sundry existence of mine.

aye, it was long ago now,
still awake to the feeling somehow.

minutes, hours, day, years...
tears...
counless and bright.
wash away the sleepy sand of night.

wake up,
wake up again.
write it all down in this ledger,
with this ballpoint pen.

hi little ledger,
aye, am doing the work..
a small perk..
for later that is.

columns and lines of what I note down,
adjectives, verbs,
every now and then a noun.

Accounting for every breath.

The ink is running low and the pages wear thin,
noting down all the trouble I am in.

A receipt for a perfect coffee, a list for the store,
Lists of such things I would be wishing for.

No filters,
every now and then a white lie
Aye, little ledger I promise I try.

Found that this is good,
a good way to go..
what shall it in the days comming now bestow.

Oh sweet little ledger
I’ve started a task,
yet of you I do solomly nothing ask.

charts of decisions,
recursive tiny trees,
all of what I spot,
what the little eye sees. 

There’s a white space at the bottom of the very last page.
An unkept margine for a "Sundry" age.

It doesn't need anything at all.
So I let it stay like that,
all nothin and small.

I’ve done the accounting.
the great work is done.
Stare right at the setting sun.

The Law is just Love...
Escape from that smirk...
All this,
heaps of love.
Is just the perk.

scatter the proverb,
simple and true,
let it take me home to you.

let the lines be a reminder,
let it make this heart kinder.

pour it all into a lesson or two,
settle it straight,
bend it askew.

little ledger,
no nothing inside.
parts of my history,
piece of my pride.

heavy, whole,
sweet and almost divine,
This little sundry life of mine.

Oh, I’ve finished the ledger,
I’ve honored the call,
I've nothing to account for at all!

It's all in there,
you can read me like a book.
all it takes for you,
is to take a look..

No, it ain't nothin small...
The bits ain't really sundry at all..
