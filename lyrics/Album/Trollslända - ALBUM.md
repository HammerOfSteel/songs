# Trollslända - ALBUM

**Labels:** Album, trollslända
**Created:** 2026-01-23
**Edited:** 2026-02-08

---

Instead of a simple "life after death" story, we frame the surface of the water as a perceptual barrier. *

The Mud (The Pond): Represents stagnation, shared trauma, and the pressure to stay "one of us."

The Reed (The Climb): The agonizing process of trying to change or heal, often met with resentment from those left behind.

The Air (The Transformation): Not a "heaven," but a different state of being where you are fundamentally changed. You can see the pond, but you can no longer breathe its water.
