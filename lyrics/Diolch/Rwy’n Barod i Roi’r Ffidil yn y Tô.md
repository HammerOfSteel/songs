# Rwy’n Barod i Roi’r Ffidil yn y Tô

**Labels:** Diolch
**Created:** 2025-12-09
**Edited:** 2025-12-09

---

I walked until the tarmac turned to mud. I walked until the silence thickened the blood. Carrying this wooden box. This dead weight.

I met him where the heavy roads collide, 
Nowhere left for me to run or hide. 
The air was thick, the sky was turning black, 
I felt the wooden case upon my back. 
He stood there waiting in the fading light, 
To see if I would put up any fight. 
He didn't have a contract or a pen, 
Just watched me like a broken, tired men. 
I didn't come to bargain for a skill, 
I didn't come to climb another hill. 
I came to lay the instrument down, 
And leave it in the dirt of this old town.

I’m done with the song, I’m done with the bow, 
I’m ready to cash in the chips right now. 
The melody is heavy, it’s hurting my head, 
I’d rather be silent, I’d rather be dead. 

Rwy’n Barod i Roi’r Ffidil yn y Tô. 
(I'm ready to put the fiddle in the roof) 

Put it in the rafters, let the dust grow, 

Rwy’n Barod i Roi’r Ffidil yn y Tô. 
There’s nothing left in here for me to show.

He asked me to play him a tune for the cost, 
A hymn for the people that I have lost. 
I lifted the fiddle, I tightened the string, 
But my hands were too frozen to make the thing sing. 
The gravity pulled me right down to the floor, 
I couldn't pretend to be strong anymore. 
The wood started cracking, the bridge started snapping, 
I heard the dark wings of the birds started flapping. 

I told him, 
"Take it, I don't want the gift," 
"I just need the weight of the sorrow to lift." 
"I don't want the fame, and I don't want the spark," 
"I just want to sleep in the quiet and dark."

It’s folding in. 
The roof is coming down. 
The walls are folding in. 
The timeline is bending back. 
I can't hold the note. 
I can't hold the line. 
Take it. 
Just take it.

I climbed up the ladder to the attic space, 
Tears running cold all over my face.
I laid the old fiddle down under the slate,
Resigning myself to the silent fate.
The devil just nodded and walked down the lane,
Leaving me there in the wind and the rain.
No deal was made, no soul was sold,
Just a story of ending that had to be told.

It’s in the roof. It’s done. I give up.
