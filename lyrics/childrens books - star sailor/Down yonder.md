# Down yonder

**Labels:** childrens books - star sailor
**Created:** 2024-11-11
**Edited:** 2024-11-11

---

Intro:
Through the cosmic woods where the stars softly glow,
Star Sailor drifts down, where the strange flowers grow.
A girl stands alone at the forest’s far edge,
Eyes bright with wonder, yet lost on the ledge.

“Come with me,” Star Sailor calls, her voice light,
“Let’s wander together through this curious night.”

Verse 1:
Down yonder she walks, where the paths twist and spin,
Where trees sway and beckon, inviting her in.
Alice takes a step, her heart skips a beat,
As colors swirl softly beneath her small feet.

“What’s down there?” she asks, eyes wide and bright,
“It feels like a dream, in the pale starlight.”

Chorus:
“Down yonder, dear Alice, where wonder meets wise,
The paths may feel strange, like a game of disguise.
But wander, don’t worry, let your heart lead the way,
For sometimes it’s mystery that teaches us grace.”

“Not all paths are clear, nor the journey straight through,
But they each hold a lesson, a whisper of truth.”

Verse 2:
She finds a strange path that loops back on itself,
With flowers that shimmer, like stars on a shelf.
The creatures around her all blink and stare,
As if they know secrets they’re unwilling to share.

“Why do these roads wind without end in sight?
Do they lead somewhere real, or just twist left and right?”

Bridge:
Star Sailor smiles, her gaze soft and kind,
“Some paths are not meant for the map or the mind.
They wind and they wander, they lead us astray,
But sometimes it’s those paths that light up the way.”

“So listen, dear Alice, to the trees as they sway,
They whisper of wisdom in their own quiet way.”

Verse 3:
As dawn starts to break, she turns to look back,
The trees stretch their arms, the sky fades to black.
Alice smiles softly, a peace in her heart,
Realizing each step was its own work of art.

With Star Sailor beside her, she turns to the night,
Knowing each twist was a piece of the light.
