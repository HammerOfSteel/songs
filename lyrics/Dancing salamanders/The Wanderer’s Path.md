# The Wanderer’s Path

**Labels:** A Spiral Path, Dancing salamanders
**Created:** 2024-09-27
**Edited:** 2024-09-27

---

(Verse 1)
Through lands unknown, with skies so wide,
I walk alone, with shadows as my guide.
Artinia calls with whispers of the past,
The secrets of Spirit, the power that will last.
In cities old, and forests deep,
I search for knowledge that makes the soul weep.
Spirit Arts, they sing through the air,
A toll on the soul, a burden to bear.

(Pre-Chorus)
But I wander still, for strength untold,
To wield the arts, and break fate’s hold.

(Chorus)
On the wanderer’s path, I search for the key,
Through hope and despair, what will set me free?
In Artinia’s winds, I chase the unknown,
Where spirit and soul are bound and sown.

(Verse 2)
Her voice in dreams, my wife’s soft plea,
“Go to Artinia, seek what you need to be free.”
The marks on her skin, a sign of the way,
Spirit Arts waiting, for those who stray.
I meet the attuned, with eyes so bright,
Masters of elements, shrouded in light.
But failure clings like the weight of time,
Still, I search for the power, beyond the climb.

(Pre-Chorus)
And I wander still, through lands so vast,
Chasing her voice, chasing the past.

(Chorus)
On the wanderer’s path, I search for the key,
Through hope and despair, what will set me free?
In Artinia’s winds, I chase the unknown,
Where spirit and soul are bound and sown.

(Bridge)
The cost of Spirit, it takes its toll,
A piece of my soul, as I lose control.
But I press forward, through fire and stone,
In the silence of failure, I stand alone.

(Chorus)
On the wanderer’s path, I search for the key,
Through hope and despair, what will set me free?
In Artinia’s winds, I chase the unknown,
Where spirit and soul are bound and sown.

(Outro)
Years may pass, but I carry on,
With her memory guiding me, long after she's gone.
For in the journey, I may find my way,
The power, the knowledge, to face the day.
