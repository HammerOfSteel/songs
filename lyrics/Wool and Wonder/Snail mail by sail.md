# Snail mail by sail

**Labels:** Wool and Wonder
**Created:** 2025-05-10
**Edited:** 2025-05-10

---

Så länge skutan kan gå, så länge hjärtat kan slå, Så länge solen, den glittrar på böljorna blå

Om blott endag eller två, så håll tillgodo ändå
För det finns många som aldrig en ljusglimt kan få.

Up the mossy trail where the pine trees sway,
Breath clouds hanging in the break of day.
Left my scarf on a twisted birch like you used to do,
“Don’t let the cold catch ya!”—I swear I heard you.
Boots crunch soft on the frostbitten stone,
Winding through woods where the elk have roamed.
There’s your flask on the stump by the lake,
I talk to the wind like you’re there when I wake.
Even now, stars peek out between fir and sky,
I swear, love, you never said goodbye.

I sat by the dark lake, threw a stone for luck,
Watched ripples dance where the loons glide and duck.
Remember? We called ‘em “long-necked lads” back then,
We’d laugh till the dusk pulled us home again.
Now I carry them echoes in me pocket tight,
Tiny pebbles of memory, warm in the night.
I spin ‘em like runes under me sleeve,
Soft whispers of you I still half-believe.
You were my compass, my North Star in sky,
Now I walk with your song at me side.

Vem kan segla förutan vind?
Vem kan ro utan åror?
Hur ska minnet av dig leva än,
I hjärtat djupa fåror.

Jag kan segla för kraft av barm,
Ror hela vägen hem,
men aldrig kommer jag att glömma,
från dig mitt hela hjärta gömma.

There’s a trail past the marsh where the mist hugs low,
Where the wind writes secrets in powdery snow.
I see ya there, boots in the mud by the pine,
Ragged old hat, still pretending you’re fine.
The air smells of smoke, like the fires we lit,
Bara bröd on a stick by the fire pit.
I whisper out loud, and the echoes reply,
The lake says nothing—but neither do I.
We speak in silence, like we always knew,
Love dont leave, the sunflower grew.

Night falls soft over branches bare,
A moon halo glows in the frozen air.
Your shadow’s behind me, close as skin,
I close me eyes, feel you pulling me in.
Not gone. Not ever. Just slipped through the seam,
You visit me still in the folds of me dream.
I walk on the path where you used to stand,
You’ve just gone ahead—I’ll catch up, hand in hand.
A final breath, a nod to the sky,
Still orbitin’, love… never goodbye.

Vem vill segla förutan vind?
Vem vill ro utan åror, för hand?
Vem vill skiljas från vännen sin
Utan att någonsin komma i land.

Här seglar vi förutan vind,
Vi stampar vatten, ror utan åror,
vi minns tillsammans vännen min,
I hjärtats fagra fåror.

How still,
How strangely still
The water is today,
It is not good
For water
To be so still that way
