# Seren fechan

**Created:** 2025-05-17
**Edited:** 2025-05-17

---

You know, love,
Some nights the stars feel like old mates watchin’ from a distance—
bit nosy, bit kind.
And that one there… I reckon it's got your giggle.

Twinkle up there, cheeky star,
Oi—who d’you think you are?
Winkin’ like you’re in the know,
Proper show-off, full of glow.
Up you float, cool as can be,
Like you’re watchin’ over me.

Shine on, shine on, little light,
You’ve got her laughter in the night.
Up where the hush and wild things are—
That’s my girl, my brightest star.
Shine on, shine on, keep her near,
She’s the reason I’m still here.

When the sky turns bruise and blue,
And even the fox has called it through,
You come out, no fuss, no fight,
Pullin’ stories out the night.
Not a word, but loud with glow—
You say more than you could know.

And every blink feels just like her—
Bit of wild, bit of wonder.

Shine on, shine on, little flame,
Callin’ softly out her name.
Up where dreams go wanderin’ far—
That’s my girl, my heart, my star.
Shine on, shine on, through the sky,
Tell her Daddy said hi.

She’s tucked tight with her teddy crew,
Socks mismatched, one slipper too.
Talks to sleep and snores a song,
Even her dreams don’t stay still long.
You catch it all, don’t miss a beat—
That girl’s got chaos in her feet.

And me, I’m sippin’ tea gone cold,
Thinkin’ ‘bout the days I’ll hold—
When she’s taller, bold and free,
Still my moonbeam, endlessly.
So give her dreams that dance and spin,
With stars like you, she’ll always win.

If she ever feels alone,
Wanders far, away from home—
Be her signpost, bold and true,
A bit of me lookin’ out for you.

(acoustic guitar with soft whistle or fiddle, like a lullaby hummed into a breeze)

So, if you see her up there someday,
spinnin’ through the sky on her own starlight

you tell her I said:
“Go on, my love. The world’s still yours.”
