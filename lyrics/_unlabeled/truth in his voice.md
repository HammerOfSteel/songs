# truth in his voice

**Created:** 2024-10-04
**Edited:** 2024-10-04

---

Verse 1
On a windswept hill, in the whispering glen,
I met a soul who felt like a long-lost friend.
His eyes held stories, both sorrow and grace,
And he offered me wisdom, no mask on his face.
We shared our fears, like shadows in flight,
But the truth in his voice lit a fire in the night.
With each word spoken, a thread of our tales,
Wove a bond that stood, though the daylight pales.

Chorus
And we laughed like old comrades, though we’d only just met,
With the weight of our troubles, no need to forget.
A real smile shared, a brief spark in the dark,
Two hearts finding light, two flames finding their mark.
So let the winds howl and let the rains fall,
For the truth we exchanged is worth more than it all.

Verse 2
In a quiet pub, as the mist rolled in,
We spoke of love lost and battles of kin.
Each sip of the ale, each crack of the hearth,
Was a verse of our lives, a tune in the dark.
He told me of hope that crumbled to dust,
Of promises broken, of ashes and rust.
Yet his words, like stars through a fog-laden night,
Held a whisper of courage, a glimmer of light.

Chorus
And we laughed like old comrades, though we’d only just met,
With the weight of our troubles, no need to forget.
A real smile shared, a brief spark in the dark,
Two hearts finding light, two flames finding their mark.
So let the winds howl and let the rains fall,
For the truth we exchanged is worth more than it all.

Bridge
But like fog on the moors, some things must part,
Two paths converged, two wandering hearts.
And as dawn broke the sky, with the first touch of day,
He rose with a nod, and then drifted away.
But I’ll carry that night, like a charm ‘round my neck,
A keepsake of truth, of friendship, of depth.

Final Chorus
For we laughed like old comrades, though we’d only just met,
With the weight of our troubles, no need to forget.
A real smile shared, a brief spark in the dark,
Two hearts finding light, two flames finding their mark.
So let the winds howl and let the rains fall,
For the truths that we shared were worth more than it all.

Outro
Now I wander alone, with the night at my back,
But his voice in the mist still whispers of track.
And I know that somewhere, as paths cross and wind,
I’ll meet others like him, and new truths we’ll find.
For a real smile shared, a word pure and true,
Is a bond that endures, like morning’s first dew.
