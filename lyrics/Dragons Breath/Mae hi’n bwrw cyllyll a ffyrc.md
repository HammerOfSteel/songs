# Mae hi’n bwrw cyllyll a ffyrc

**Labels:** Dragons Breath
**Created:** 2025-07-17
**Edited:** 2025-07-17

---

Drip, drop, slip, stop,
Snap, tap, gasp, knock.
Breath, chest, mess, press,
Sink, blink, tightness, guess.

It’s rainin’ knives, forks in my lungs,
Feels like thunder’s got a forked tongue.
Walls bend in, like they know me too well,
Like the kettle’s been snitchin’ my fears to the bell.
Keys in the bowl but I’m locked in place,
Brain’s in a maze with a treadmill pace.
I count the tiles, then I count ‘em again,
Tryna pin down time but it slips in my den.
Window’s a mouth full of fog and bite,
And I ain’t slept since Tuesday night.
Shadows got elbows, makin’ tea with dread,
And the floorboards twitch with the words I’ve not said.
Breathe in deep — no signal, just fuzz,
Like a party gone quiet, and no one was.
The fork hits plate, and I flinch too fast,
Like the world cracked open but forgot to ask.

Clink, clank, thunk, thud
It ain’t the fall, it’s the flood
Gasp, rasp, hush, hum
Countin’ my breaths til the quiet comes

Room feels miles wide and three steps deep,
My socks are dry but I’m neck-high steep.
Can’t find ground with my toes or head,
Just floatin’ like crumbs in a cup of dread.
Mug on the edge like my nerves and speech,
Words come out like they’ve gotta breach.
Tried callin’ someone but forgot my voice,
Screen just blinked like it had a choice.
My coat still hangs like I might go out,
But the door breathes heavy like it’s full of doubt.
Bin bag full of air, that's me today,
Can’t lift a spoon but I think I prayed.
Not to God, just to calm, or moss,
Or the corner of the rug that don’t feel like loss.
I curl like steam, vanish mid-track,
Too many thoughts, and they all shout back.

Tap, snap, drip, drop
Tryna hold on but it won’t stop
Crash, clash, mash, dash
Breathe real slow and ride the flash

What do you call the fear of no name?
When you pace the rug just to feel the same?
When a clock ticks wrong and the air gets cold,
And your name feels secondhand, or sold.
I wrote a list of “I’m still here”
Stuck it to the fridge like a silent cheer.
Tea’s gone cold but I’m still in it,
Cup got cracks but I sip each minute.
Held by threads, but that’s still held,
Even panic gets quiet if you’ve ever yelled.
So I hum to the sink, talk soft to the light,
It ain’t alright, but it ain’t all night.

Clink, clank, thunk, thud
It ain’t the fall, it’s the flood
Gasp, rasp, hush, hum
Countin’ my breaths til the quiet comes

Grit, pit, knit, sit,
Whirl, pearl, curl, flit.
Pause, claws, draw, hush,
Rush, crush, flush, trust...
