Music Style Description
Korean folk-inspired, acoustic guitar, violin, subtle electronic accents, deep bass, mystical, dynamic, blending traditional and modern.

Two-Sentence Description
This song tells the story of a woman’s journey from conformity to empowerment, inspired by the myth of Pulgasari. Blending traditional Korean elements with modern acoustic and electronic instrumentation, it symbolizes resilience and the forging of one's own path, even when faced with seemingly insurmountable challenges.
