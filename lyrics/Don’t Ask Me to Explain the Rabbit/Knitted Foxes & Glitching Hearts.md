# Knitted Foxes & Glitching Hearts

**Labels:** Don’t Ask Me to Explain the Rabbit
**Created:** 2025-04-19
**Edited:** 2025-04-19

Hey, Alice…
Remember that fox with the half-tail, right?
The one you said glitched on purpose —
So no one could pull him back?

Yeah. I liked him.
He looked like us.

Guess what?
We’re still here.

I stitched my coat from old save files,
hugs on loop and Teema’s smiles.
Foxes ran through RAM and snow,
left little prints we didn’t know.

My hoodie smells like yesterday,
and cocoa mugs we hid away.
She said 
“don’t cry, you’re buffering,”
then kissed my cheek — rebooted spring.

Knitted foxes, glitching hearts,
Patched-up days and pixel parts.
Hold this thread and don’t let go —
Love like this was stitched to show.

Her voice lives in my folder tree,
marked “adventure_v1”
Tima drew stars in the HTML,
and Alice laughed — “we cast a spell.”

My mandolin’s outta tune again,
like old dogs, or Mum, or rain.
But joy’s a bug we let run wild,
and grief’s the fox that hugged our child.

We stitched our story on the moon,
with tea-stained threads and 8-bit tunes.
If I glitch out, just pull me near —
Your fox-tail heart still keeps me here.

She said —
"Some things come unraveled slow,
not ‘cause they're broken —
but ‘cause they grow.”

I held the thread, and didn’t speak.
It pulsed like stars beneath my cheek.

We walked through Sveg on echo feet,
where snow fell back in soft repeat.
Tima’s laugh played in the pines,
looped in foxcode, line by line.

Alice waved to the sky and grinned,
"Let’s patch the world — from end to end.”

We’re glitched, sure, but built to mend.
And every stitch says “begin again.”

Patched-up days and pixel parts.
Hold this thread and don’t let go —
Love like this was stitched to show.

with tea-stained threads and 8-bit tunes.

You know…
knitted foxes never really go.
They just… recompile in dreams.
