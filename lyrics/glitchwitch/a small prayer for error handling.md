# a small prayer for error handling

**Labels:** glitchwitch
**Created:** 2025-09-26
**Edited:** 2025-09-26

---

Count in the breath.
Count out the ache.
Tune every string the dusk will take.

My heart threw exceptions at quarter to two,
stack trace glowing in a ribcage blue.
I catch to my chest then release to the air,
tiny try again in a whispered repair.
I write guard clauses at the edge of my name,
preconditions kindness, postconditions same.
Invariant simple as water and bread,
fail fast anger before it is fed.
I lint my voice for the splinters and blame,
format the truth till it fits in the frame.
I seed a test for the gentler part,
mock a safe morning for a shaking heart.

Small prayer for error handling, hear me when I fall,
route my wild output to a shoulder not a wall.
Let the night be logging but the stars decide the level,
warn not fatal, mercy middleware the devil.
Pipe my heavy stderr to the tide and tree,
tee a quiet copy to a friend who knows me.

Recursion of regret keeps calling me back,
base case resting, unwind the stack.
Tail call the sorrow till the loop runs light,
optimize grieving to sleep by night.
Retry with a patience that does not bruise,
exponential spacing on the storms I choose.
Idempotent apology, same soft phrase,
safe to repeat on the troubled days.
I write a spec for the lines I will cross,
sacred guardrails when I carry loss.
I mark a commit with a clean clear note,
scope what I fix and the parts I do not.

Small prayer for error handling, keep the channel clear,
trace the hidden currents that carry me to here.
Let the warnings sing but the panic never lead,
roll back the harshness, roll forward what I need.
Route my heavy stderr to the rain and reed,
tee a quiet copy to the hand that reads.

An engine of learning looks over my code,
finds latent branches on an old hard road.
Attention on tokens of thorn and fire,
weights on the parts that ask desire.
Will an artificial mind refactor my art,
or write scripture lore from the shards of a heart
Diff of a doctrine and a warm redesign,
clean small functions where the grief can align.
Model the days with a gentler loss,
regularize edges in a cradle of moss.
Train on the kindness that people gave,
serve that model when the pulses crave.

I will not send my sorrow to a null sink void,
I will not drop the packet of a joy employed.
Let the holy handler be a human ear,
let the harp be circuit, let the reel steer.

Small prayer for error handling, shape the falling sound,
buffer me in breathing till my feet find ground.
Let the logger listen and the limits bend,
grace on the exit, soft on the end.
Route my heavy stderr to the mouth of day,
tee a quiet copy to the ones who stay.

Try again softly.
Finally, rest.
All clauses guarded.
Heart learns best.
