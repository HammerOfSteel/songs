# Julia Set

**Labels:** Koch star
**Created:** 2024-12-04
**Edited:** 2024-12-04

---

"We’ve cleared the path, but what’s ahead?"
"Another trial to twist the thread.
Each step we take, the spire schemes,
Testing bonds, unraveling dreams."

"Will we face monsters, or shadowed fears?"
"The spire whispers, ‘Step through here.’"

The door swings wide, the air turns thin,
Each of us drawn to lives within.
Through his eyes, I take my place,
A borrowed step, a stranger’s face.

Julia set, a fragile design,
Shards of a self that intertwine.
Each fragment speaks, but none complete,
A puzzle formed where shadows meet.

The rogue’s sneer cuts through the haze,
His words like edges, sharp and dazed.
I see his past through fractured light,
The rogue and I, both bound by night.

Julia set, a maze of lines,
A fractured mind where shadow shines.
Each thread connects, though frayed and torn,
In every break, new light is born.

Through his steps, I see his scars,
Moments fleeting, broken stars.
What can I fix? What can I mend?
In his rhythm, I comprehend.

His voice is mine, his steps my own,
A mirrored path I’ve never known.
Each piece I see is not to heal,
But to hold the truth of what is real.

Julia set, no longer torn,
A map of truths where hope is born.
Each shard remains, none to erase,
A fragile whole we learn to embrace.

The trial fades, the mirror’s glow,
Through his lens, I’ve come to know.
Not broken lines, but patterns true,
In every shard, a clearer view.'
