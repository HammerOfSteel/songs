# Beneath the Tyrant’s Gaze

**Labels:** A Spiral Path, Dancing salamanders
**Created:** 2024-09-24
**Edited:** 2024-09-24

---

Style: Acoustic strings with high-paced intensity, blending rapid guitar strumming and haunting violin crescendos to build tension and emotion.

Verse 1
Under the gilded sky, a shadow falls,
The Tyrant’s heir, his curse it calls.
Generations past, the bloodline’s feud,
Now, fate has claimed what love once knew.

Pre-Chorus
In the golden halls where power reigns,
The cost of vengeance courses through my veins.
Your light was stolen, torn from life,
Beneath their gaze, I’ve lost my wife.

Chorus
Under the Tyrant’s cruel decree,
You were taken, ripped from me.
Now I stand, with vengeance cold,
In my heart, the tale unfolds.

Verse 2
Their grudge ran deep, their hatred old,
A story etched in blood and gold.
Your life, the price they came to claim,
Now all that’s left is burning flame.

Pre-Chorus
I see their eyes, those burning coals,
Their legacy of shattered souls.
Your smile, now a distant star,
And I’m left with scars that never scar.

Chorus

Now I stand, with vengeance cold,
In my heart, the tale unfolds.

Bridge
The cycle turns, I cannot see,
An end to pain, no way to flee.
Beneath the gaze of the golden throne,
I vow to strike, to die alone.

Chorus

You were taken, ripped from me.

Outro
Your voice still whispers through the air,
But vengeance binds me to this snare.
Under the Tyrant’s endless gaze,
I’m lost within this endless maze.

Narrative Explanation:
The song chronicles the devastating moment when the protagonist's love is taken from him by one of the children of the Tyrant in Gold, due to a generations-old feud. The tragedy ignites a fire of revenge within the protagonist, as he wrestles with the bitter cycle of hatred passed down through families. The high pace of the strings and intense acoustic elements reflect the protagonist’s inner turmoil, the growing rage, and his loss of hope. His sorrow transforms into a desire for vengeance, setting up the next stage of his journey.
