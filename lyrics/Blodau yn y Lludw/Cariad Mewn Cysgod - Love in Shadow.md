# Cariad Mewn Cysgod - Love in Shadow

**Labels:** Blodau yn y Lludw
**Created:** 2025-02-07
**Edited:** 2025-02-07

---

Cariad Mewn Cysgod

"She was here again last night... laughing, spinning in the sunlight..."
"Eighteen. Today should have been eighteen."

The morning drifts in, slow and pale,
A hush of gold on the window’s veil.
A whisper lingers where dreams still lay,
Her laughter fading with the break of day.

The scent of books, a coat still hung,
A drawing she made when she was young.
The dust has settled, but love still clings,
Like echoes caught on silent wings.

"You’re still here, aren’t you?"

Love in shadow, light that stays,
A name unspoken, yet never fades.
Time moves forward, but never through,
Somewhere in the quiet, I still find you.

Tredegar’s trees in autumn’s light,
Leaves like embers burning bright.
Children running, voices free,
And in their laughter, you find me.

"You always ran ahead, little sun… too fast for my heart to catch."

Through every street, through every door,
I trace the path we walked before.
Your footprints gone, but here they stay,
Etched in gold where shadows play.

Love in shadow, light unseen,
Soft as breath, strong as dream.
Time won’t bring you back to me,
But love outlives eternity.

Back at the cabin, candle’s glow,
Two cups of tea, the fire low.
One for me, and one for you,
A quiet toast to what was true.

"Penblwydd hapus, fy seren fach..."

The night exhales, the candle sways,
A shadow moves, the silence stays.
Was that the wind? Or something more?
A love that lingers, ever sure.

No words remain, yet none are lost,
Love stays warm through winter’s frost.
Not in stone, nor time’s embrace,
But in the quiet, in this place.

Love in shadow, light that stays,
A name unspoken, yet never fades.
Time moves forward, but never through,
Somewhere in the quiet, I still find you.

"Goodnight, cariad bach…"
(Goodnight, little love…)
