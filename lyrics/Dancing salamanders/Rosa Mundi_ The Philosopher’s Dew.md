# Rosa Mundi: The Philosopher’s Dew

**Labels:** Dancing salamanders, Lucerna Obscura
**Created:** 2024-08-21
**Edited:** 2024-08-22

---

"In the world’s rose, the morning’s dew reveals the soul’s pure essence. Distillation brings forth the hidden light, drawn from the heart of night."

Verse 1
In the hour before the dawn's first breath,
The Rosa Mundi stirs from sleep's caress.
Upon the leaves, where the night’s tears fall,
The Aqua Vitae begins its silent call.

"Aurora’s veil," where shadows part,
The Solis gemma touches the heart.
In the dew, the essence lies,
The soul’s reflection in the Mundi’s eyes.

Chorus
Rosa Mundi, in the dew's embrace,
Where Anima pura reveals its face.
Through the Distillatio of the night’s refrain,
The spirit ascends in Verbum’s name.

In the Flos aetheris, the truth is known,
The Philosophia of the soul’s own.
The morning dew, the Opus starts,
In Rosa Mundi, the world imparts.

Verse 2
Through the garden’s mist, where silence sings,
The Flamma divina takes to wings.
In the Ros marinus, the light refracts,
A dance of Lunae and Sol’s pacts.

"Aqua caelestis," in droplets pure,
Carries the truth, the soul’s allure.
In every bead, the cosmos spins,
The Dew of Wisdom where life begins.

Bridge
In the Gutta prima, where the dawn arrives,
The Rosa breathes, the Vitae thrives.
"Viae lactea," the milky way,
Guides the spirit to the day.

The Aqua Sapientia, in morning’s light,
Reflects the Veritas in purest sight.
The dew distills, the soul is free,
In the Rosa Mundi, eternity.

Outro
In the first light’s touch, the dew does fade,
Yet the Rosa blooms where shadows played.
"Lux in tenebris," the light remains,
In Rosa Mundi, the soul's domain.
