# Here, Now, and Blues

**Labels:** Geddon Bird
**Created:** 2024-11-14
**Edited:** 2024-11-14

---

(Intro)
“Feels like life’s been running away, doesn’t it?”
“Yeah, like the present’s always slipping past.”
“And just when you think you’re caught up, you’re already behind again.”

(Verse 1)
Been chasing time like a runaway train,
Feels like I’m holding sand in the rain.
Every minute slips, every hour flies,
Trying to catch the here and now before it dies.

They say be present, live each day,
But the “here and now” keeps drifting away.

(Chorus)
Here, now, in the midst of it all,
Life’s a song too short, a story too tall.
With coffee in hand, we breathe and try,
To be in this moment as it rushes by.

(Verse 2)
Got a little girl who needs me near,
I’m her world, her joy, her biggest fear.
Can’t let her see the weight I hold,
The blues of a heart that’s tired and old.

Some days I’m strong, some days I break,
But I keep on, for both our sakes.

(Verse 3)
We all wear masks to make it through,
Holding dreams, holding fears, balancing the two.
Feels like juggling fire, spinning plates,
In a dance with time that never waits.

If only presence was something you could buy,
I’d save up enough to finally try.

(Chorus )
Here, now, in the midst of it all,
Life’s a song too short, a story too tall.
With coffee in hand, we breathe and try,
To be in this moment as it rushes by.

(Verse 4 )
Parenthood ain’t no fairytale,
It’s midnight cries and prayers gone stale.
I try to stay here, try to stay true,
But some nights, man, I sing the blues.

One step forward, two steps back,
Yet love’s the only thing I never lack.

(Bridge)
Funny how the world keeps moving fast,
While we’re here tryin' to make each moment last.
Sometimes it’s heavy, sometimes it’s light—
Being here now’s a long, long fight.

(Verse 5 )
When it’s all too much, I just breathe deep,
Let the weight go, let the worries sleep.
Life’s a dance, a give and take,
And every dawn’s a fresh mistake.

Here and now, I try to see,
This messy, beautiful life for me.

(Outro)
“I guess the here and now is all we’ve got, huh?”
“Yeah, might as well hold it close, for as long as it lasts.”
“And share it with those who get it, even for a minute.”
