# southbound somehow

**Labels:** The Alchemists Estate
**Created:** 2025-11-24
**Edited:** 2025-11-28

---

Packed the boot with optimism 
and a crumpled paper map
The sat-nav’s telling lies again,
taking a digital nap
We’re chasing the grey
out of the skyline, shifting down gears
Leaving the postcode wars
and the city fears

You’ve got your feet
on the dashboard,
painting toe-nails red
While a thousand unspoken futures 
dance around inside your head
The hedgerows are high here,
scraping the side of the door
We’re not really sure what we’re looking for.

But we’re Southbound, somehow
Chasing the cliff-edge,
wiping the sweat from the brow
The lanes are getting tighter,
the air is getting sweet
And youth sits laughing
in the empty back seat
Yeah, the road bends blind
but we don’t slow down
Lost in the logic of a seaside town
Southbound, somehow.

St Just is a rumour,
Polperro’s a maze of stone
We’re out of the signal,
we’re strictly on our own
You asked for ice cream at ten in the morning, a curious craving
I’m watching the way that
you’re smiling, the way that you’re waving
At strangers in tractors
at ghosts in the mist
The steering wheel’s shaking,
vibrating the wrist
There’s a glow on your skin
that isn’t just from the sun
A secret arithmetic,
one plus one equals... 

And we’re Southbound, somehow
Tracing the coastline,
making a silent vow
The ocean is angry,
the pasty is hot
We’re happy with exactly
the little we’ve got
And youth sits laughing
in the empty back seat
Keeping the time with a phantom beat
Southbound, somehow.

"Do you smell that?
It’s the salt.
Or maybe it’s the future. I think...
I think we’re gonna need a bigger car."

Sunburn on the shoulder,
salt crystals in the hair
A tiny vibration,
a hum in the air
We park on the verge
where the land drops away
Into the violent blue and the sea-spray grey
You rest your hand on your stomach, unconscious and light
Protecting a spark from the chill of the night
No need for the map now
no need for the plan
Just the girl, and the road,
and the sea, and the man.

See, you say and gently sway,
Pulling all the threads of my fear away.
Soft silent in Hebrew you say

"Temima", all is okay.
Tima, our Tima I repeat,
slow and sweet,
As I lay my head down,
To hear her sleep.
Perfect, this moment.
Complete.

Yeah, we’re Southbound, somehow!
Right here in the glory of the eternal now
The lanes were all borrowed,
the cliffs were all steep
But we’ve got a promise
that we’re gonna keep
And it sits laughing,
but it’s fading away
To make room for the
life that is coming to stay
Southbound, somehow.
Yeah, we’re southbound, somehow.

St Just...
Polperro...
Just drive.
