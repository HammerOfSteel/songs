1. The Journey of Grief: A Symbolic Transformation (Inspired by Suaság Na Machaire)
Theme: Discuss how grief is a transformative journey, much like the phases of the moonstone. The song’s Elvish lyrics can symbolize the universal nature of grief, transcending language and culture.
Coping Strategy: Offer guidance on embracing grief as a necessary process for personal growth, suggesting practices like journaling or creating personal rituals to honor each stage of grief.

2. Illuminating the Darkness: Finding Hope in Grief (Inspired by Rēdōm Sunonum)

Coping Strategy: Encourage readers to find small sources of light in their lives—whether through nature, art, or relationships—and use these to guide them through the darkness.
