# Aqua Lethe: The Mystic’s Reflection

**Labels:** Dancing salamanders, Lucerna Obscura
**Created:** 2024-08-21
**Edited:** 2024-08-22

---

"In the waters of forgetting, the soul is unmade and reborn. The river winds through shadows, where ego dissolves and spirit ascends."

In the twilight’s hush where shadows bow,
A Pilgrim weeps where time does plow.
His heart, once whole, now split and bare,
As the stars dissolve in the silent air.

"Nox Abdita," where echoes dwell,
In the deepened void, the sorrows swell.
Through the Vallis Tacita, where phantoms glide,
The Wanderer treads where dreams reside.

Aqua Lethe, where currents weave,
In the Noctis hold, the burdens leave.
Through the veils, the stream does wind,
Loosening chains that once confined.

From the Cineres cold, the essence born,
In Lethes Aquis, where grief is worn.
As Tenebrae lifts, a new light grows,
In the dissolution, the spirit flows.

He gazes upon the mirror's veil,
Seeking the trace in the empty tale.
The loss, an abyss both vast and deep,
Where the echoes of silence softly seep.

"Flamma Occulta," the embers fade,
In the Nox Ampla, where truths invade.
Through the ashes, the river sighs,
In the release, the freedom lies.

The Seeker drinks from the shadowed rill.

Recast in light where dark was laid.
From sorrow's chasm, the soul ascends,
In the Aqua Lethe, the journey bends.
