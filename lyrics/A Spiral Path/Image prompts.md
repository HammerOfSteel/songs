Judgement of the hydra:

Medieval universe, set in the heart of a dark, mystical forest clearing surrounded by towering ancient trees with twisted roots. A young man in his early 20s stands at the base of a colossal stone Hydra statue. He is dressed in simple but rugged clothes, a dark green tunic with subtle magical symbols embroidered along the sleeves, signifying his role as a novice sorcerer. His short, brown hair is tousled, and his expression is one of determination mixed with apprehension as he faces the Hydra's many serpentine heads, each carved with intricate details, their eyes glowing faintly with ethereal energy. The Hydra’s shadow seems to almost come alive, twisting around the young man, as if testing his resolve. A soft, eerie blue mist swirls around the ground, and the scene is illuminated by a ghostly pale moonlight, casting long shadows. Magical symbols and runes are etched into the ground around the statue, glowing faintly. The young man holds a staff with a small crystal embedded at the top, its light wavering as if reflecting his inner turmoil. The background shows dense forest foliage, with faint, ghostly shapes lurking in the darkness, adding to the sense of foreboding. A single, brilliant beam of moonlight pierces through the trees, focusing on the young man and the Hydra, symbolizing his moment of judgment. Art style: a blend of Disney and Studio Ghibli, with dark fantasy elements, capturing the tension, mysticism, and emotional intensity of the scene.
