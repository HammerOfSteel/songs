# terminal

**Created:** 2025-12-30
**Edited:** 2025-12-30

---

The concourse is a cathedral of linoleum floors, 
The traveler stepping through the automated doors. 
We’re sitting on the edge bolted to the ground, 
Waiting for the static of the intercom to make a sound. 

Our suitcases are packed with what we’re trying to leave, 
running out of places, the last trick up our sleeve
There’s silence, a movement in the still, 
As we’re climbing up, through this long weathered hill

No names, no needs, no deeds,
worthy of any praise, 
just the flickering of the long days.

Stutter the shutter,
flicker and flutter,
These vessels all waiting on a steady old grating.
The diesel is dripping, the gravity’s abating

In the Cauda Pavonis.
This puddle set ablaze,
never seeks to amaze,
A million colors, a single dream, in a silver-blue haze.

Look down, right here, this is where it used to stand,
Iridescent kingdom in a oily, dark and sunless land.
The Peacock’s Tail is blooming in a spill of toxic, neon flight,
Showing every color that was hidden by the coming of the night.

"volatile" state, soul unfixed and free,
Our mercury sliding toward a vast and graying sea.
Nothing but the friction between the flask and the hand.
slowly rising, climbing through the land.

Terminal,
distil,
break anew,
fill the vessel,
as it boils the brew.
concoction of,
something new.

Clock on a wall, forgotten how to move,
spining caught, nothing to prove.
potential,
potentially no place left to go,
the free out of the "Sunyata" grow.

keeps on kindling even when the well is dry.
keeps on giving out the corner of the eye.
shutter the stutter and flutter the flicker once more,
Don't let go of what came before.

A million colors, a single dream... 
A million colors, a single dream...
