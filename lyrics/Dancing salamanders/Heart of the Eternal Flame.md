# Heart of the Eternal Flame

**Labels:** Dancing salamanders, heart is where the home is
**Created:** 2024-08-27
**Edited:** 2024-08-27

Verse 1
In the quiet of the morning light,
Where the first dew gently clings,
I hear your voice in whispered breeze,
In every song the dawnbird sings.
Grandmother, your love was my guiding star,
A flame that never dies,
In every shadow, I see your light,
In every tear, your soft replies.

Chorus
Aisha, قلب النار الأبدية (Qalb al-nar al-abadiya),
Your warmth keeps the cold at bay.
In the circle of our family’s name,
You light our path, you lead the way.
Grandmother, your love transcends the veil,
In every prayer, in every tale.

Verse 2
You taught me إيمان (iman) in simple things,
In the moon’s rise and the ocean’s sway,
In the way the earth renews itself,
In the light of each new day.
Grandmother, your hands were the weavers of peace,
Your words, the seeds of grace,
In the garden of my wandering soul,
Your spirit holds its place.

Bridge
And when the stars fade from the sky,
And the world feels vast and wide,
I find you in the quiet space,
Where time and love collide.
Your wisdom like the ancient trees,
Your strength in the rolling tide,
Grandmother, في الليل الهادئ (fi al-layl al-hadi’),
You’re forever by my side.

Outro
So I walk with you in the golden fields,
Where the harvest meets the sun,
Grandmother, شكرًا (shukran) for all the moments,
For every lesson, every one.
Aisha, your legacy lives on,
In every step I take, every song I’ve sung.
Grateful for the memories, for all I’ve become,
Because of you, Grandmother, I’m never alone.
