# Cornish Rain

**Labels:** The Nature of Things
**Created:** 2024-11-19
**Edited:** 2025-02-12

---

The morning whispers, the gray clouds stay,
The world feels quiet, washed in gray.
Raindrops dance on the windowpane,
A melody soft, this Cornish rain.

"The rain’s here, love. What shall we do?"

"Watch it fall, Papa, just me and you."

The drops form rivers, they race, they glide,
Tiny streams on the glass outside.
She traces shapes with her little hand,
A castle, a cloud, a far-off land.

"What do you see, love, out in the mist?"

"A dragon’s lair, where the rain persists."

Cornish rain, so soft, so still,
A blanket of peace on the windowsill.
Through gentle streams and whispered skies,
A world of wonder in your eyes.

The kettle sings, the teacups wait,
The rain plays on, an endless state.
She cuddles close with her teddy tight,
Her laughter a glow in the dim gray light.

"What story today, my little sun?"

"The one where star sailor saves everyone!"

The crayons bloom in a rainbow array,
On paper fields, her dreams will play.
A ship on waves, a sky so blue,
Her world unfolds in every hue.

"You’ve painted the sea, the cliffs, the shore,
What happens next? What’s in store?"

"A pirate’s treasure, lost in the sand,
Waiting for me to find it with my hand!"

Cornish rain, a gentle refrain,
A soothing song on the windowpane.
Through tiny rivers and cloudy skies,
We weave our dreams as the day goes by.

The rain slows down, a quiet hum,
The afternoon fades, the twilight comes.
She leans on my arm, her eyes grow wide,
"Do you think the rain feels warm inside?"

"It feels like a hug, soft and true,
Just like the love I feel for you."

The rain picks up as the night draws near,
The sound a comfort, the world unclear.
We light the lamp, its golden glow,
A haven of peace where the moments flow.

"Papa, why does the rain never stay?"

"It comes and goes, but it’s never far away."

The stories end, the crayons sleep,
Her dreams now waiting, soft and deep.
The rain still taps a gentle song,
Its rhythm steady, all night long.

"Goodnight, my love, my rainy day star,
No matter the weather, I’m never far."

Cornish rain, so soft and true,
A timeless gift, just me and you.
Through cloudy skies and rivers that play,
Your love will warm each rainy day.

The rain hums low, the night turns still,
A quiet peace on the windowsill.
With dreams of treasure and skies so wide,
We find our world in the rain outside.

"Cornish rain, my little sun,
Our day together has just begun."
