# Veil of the Labyrinth

**Labels:** Dancing salamanders
**Created:** 2024-08-19
**Edited:** 2024-08-19

---

Verse 1
Through corridors woven with argent threads,
I walk where the serpent’s breath softly spreads.
The vaporous veil, where the nimble dance,
Obscures the gold in its silent trance.

The Lion’s heart beats in the shadow’s fold,
As Mercury whispers secrets untold.
In the corridors where the lapis hides,
I seek the path where the Phoenix resides.

Chorus
In the smoke where Luna’s tears descend,
I trace the arcane, where truths bend.
Through the labyrinth’s twisted, spiraled guise,
I search for Sol in the dragon’s eyes.

Where shadows speak in tongues of lead,
I seek the flame where the dawn is bred.
In the veil of mist where the shadows play,
The star of the Magi lights my way.

Verse 2
The stone’s reflection in the darkened pool,
Turns the wise to folly, the sage to fool.
The alchemical mirror, where images confound,
Reveals the path where no light is found.

The echoes murmur in the blackened well,
Where the green lion’s breath begins to swell.
In the circle’s heart, where time stands still,
I hold to the spark that bends to will.

Bridge
In the athanor where the smoke does rise,
The salt of Saturn dims the skies.
Yet within the mist, the dragon coils,
Guarding the truth through endless toils.

The lamp of Hermes guides the hand,
Through the paths where few can stand.
In the maze where the sulphur’s song is sung,
The chain of stars has just begun.

Outro
In the smoke’s embrace, the truth is spun,
The veils lift, as the trial is won.
With the flame in hand and shadows torn,
We leave the labyrinth where the light is born.

The albedo’s path now clearer still,
In the void where the echoes fill.
Through the smoke, where illusions fall,
The light within reveals it all.



Elara and Ember navigate the treacherous Labyrinth of Smoke, a place where illusions and shadows test their resolve. Through cryptic visions and deceptive paths, Elara must trust her inner light to see through the veil and find her way.
