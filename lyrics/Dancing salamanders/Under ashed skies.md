# Under ashed skies

**Labels:** Dancing salamanders
**Created:** 2024-08-14
**Edited:** 2024-08-14

---

Under the ashen sky, where the earth meets fire,
In the shadows, I found my desire.

Father’s voice like thunder, demanding I obey,
But my spirit’s like the lava, carving its own way.
Bound by tradition, yet yearning to be free,
In the heart of the mountains, I found me.

In the darkness, I found my light,
A rebel’s heart burning bright.
From the coldest stones, I’ll rise,
Under the ashen sky, I’ll claim my prize.

Hvísl í nóttinni, draumar fjarlægra landa,
I’ll break these chains with my own hands.
Frostið bítur, but I fear it not,
My fire burns hot, in every thought.

In the darkness, I found my light,
A rebel’s heart burning bright.
From the coldest stones, I’ll rise,
Under the ashen sky, I’ll claim my prize.

Through the winds that howl and skies that weep,
I find my strength, in caverns deep.
Jörðin skelfur, but still I stand,
With fire in my heart, and stone in my hand.

Under the aurora’s dance, in the icy night,
I see my future, burning bright.
In fjarlægðinni, I hear the call,
I’ll rise above, I’ll conquer all.

In the darkness, I found my light,
A rebel’s heart burning bright.
From the coldest stones, I’ll rise,
Under the ashen sky, I’ll claim my prize.

Under the ashen sky, my spirit flies,
Through the fire, through the ice, I rise.

"Ashen Sky"
This song captures the tumultuous journey of Mira's youth, as she wrestles with her father's expectations and the fierce independence that Iceland's harsh, volcanic landscape inspires in her. It’s a story of finding light in the darkness, rising from the coldest stones to carve a path of her own.
