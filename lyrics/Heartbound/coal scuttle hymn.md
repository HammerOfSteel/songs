# coal scuttle hymn

**Labels:** Heartbound
**Created:** 2025-08-19
**Edited:** 2025-08-19

---

As the rain gets heavier,
The drumming echoes harder,
The murmur swallowed by them,
Not a single one is free.

Don't they know?
Peace and quiet under the rain is
Flattering, for they now could pause.
From the ever busy and stressful life.

Ash and ember, crack and glow,
iron rhythm, ebb and flow.
Soot and shimmer, grain and gin,
roll it back, the coal scuttle hymn.
Smoke and silver, black to white,
day to dust, and night to light.
Flesh and furnace, bone and skin,
beat it down, the coal scuttle hymn.

Rattle and ring, shimmer and spin,
echo the pulse of the coal scuttle hymn.
Clatter and glow, scatter within,
carry the spark of the coal scuttle hymn.

Stone and cinder, flame and breath,
song of labour, song of rest.
Salt and sorrow, ale and grin,
turn it round, the coal scuttle hymn.
Crack and kindle, burn and bind,
heart of matter, soul of mind.
Gold from ashes, loss to win,
spell it out, the coal scuttle hymn.

Rattle and ring, shimmer and spin,
whisper the word of the coal scuttle hymn.
Clatter and glow, scatter within,
dance with the fire of the coal scuttle hymn.

Soot to silver, ash to gold,
heat remembers what hands once hold.
Breath to ember, bone to flame,
all is altered, all the same.

Rattle and ring, shimmer and spin,
life resounds in the coal scuttle hymn.
Clatter and glow, scatter within,
world remade in the coal scuttle hymn.

Clink and scatter, spark and spin,
hush now… the coal scuttle hymn.

Is freeing a crime?
Rain only wants us to shine,
By giving a break from all the rush,
And a rainbow at last
