# 404_Dream_Not_Found

**Labels:** Don’t Ask Me to Explain the Rabbit
**Created:** 2025-04-20
**Edited:** 2025-04-20

Roll for initiative,
Two...
SYSTEM ERROR,
The installation wizard casts fireball!
You take 3 D2s nostalgia damage,
Ugh.. I need a long rest..

Woke up in grayscale, socks on wrong,
lost all my playlists, lost my song.
The sky was beige, the toast was bland,
I scrolled so long, I lost my hands.

Work clocks screamed, joy took flight,
I left my crayons in the night.
My dreams? Unplugged. Expired. Out.
I whispered fun but heard a doubt.

All the pretty pixel pastel dreams,
blasting out at the glitchy seams.
Sugar, spice, and soft delight —
too big to fit this growing fight.

King of cozy late-night cuddle,
rainbows crystallized from puddles.
Stolen apples, baked in pies —
nostalgia held in warm disguise.

Overfrozen nice jacuzzi,
laughter sealed in soft confusie.
Midnightsun and herbs of gold —
some love like that just won't grow old.

I rebooted all my wonder,
crashed the silence, broke the thunder.
Joy came back in pixel swirl —
a candy-colored, dreaming world.

I thought I outgrew all this light,
but turns out grief just dims the bright.
The fox returned with patched-up ear,
and in my tea, the stars drew near.

Blanket forts, no plan, no clue,
a to-do list scrawled in baby blue.
The spell was small: a dance, a snack,
and just like that, my colors cracked.

I thought the dreams were gone for good —
but they respawned in spilled applewood.
A giggle loop, a bedtime beep —
the joy I lost was just… asleep.

All the pretty pixel pastel dreams,
glitching through my grayscale seams.
Soft explosions, glowing thread —
love in colors I thought were dead.

King of cozy, cloud parade,
whispers caught in marmalade.
Stolen apples, tongue-tied truth —
reboots taste like toasted youth.

“You can’t delete this joy.”
“We hid it in the soup.”
“The password was… bedtime.”
“Found it under ‘favorites’.”

Now my dreams wear knitted socks,
hang with foxes, break the clocks.
They climb out windows, draw on walls,
and play guitar in bathroom stalls.

They don’t ask if they belong —
they sing in off-key, candy song.
This, this now, this messed-up grace —
is where I found my dreaming place.

All the pretty pixel pastel dreams,
patched with glitter, stitched in streams.
I play them now, I keep them near —
they glitch with love, and that's sincere.

King of cozy, code and calm,
a sugar-frosted healing psalm.
Midnightsun and herbs of gold —
this weird, weird love will never grow old.

The installation wizard found a present,
Inside there was a cookie.
