# The Star Sailor’s Lullaby

**Created:** 2024-08-23
**Edited:** 2024-08-23

---

Verse 1
Underneath a velvet sky, where the stars begin to sing,
You sailed among the constellations, like a child’s dream with wings.
Each star a note, each moon a rhyme, in the lullaby you knew,
You whispered tales of far-off worlds, where the sky is ever blue.
With every twinkle, every light, you guided me to see,
That even in the darkest night, your love would anchor me.

Chorus
Sing me the Star Sailor’s lullaby,
Where dreams drift on the Milky Way.
Your voice a beacon in the sky,
Guiding me where shadows sway.
Through the galaxies afar,
I hear your song, my northern star.
Sing me the Star Sailor’s lullaby,
And in your arms, let my spirit lie.

Verse 2
You charted maps of the unknown, with a compass made of light,
Navigating through the night, with a heart so pure and bright.
The sea of stars was yours to roam, your ship a silver beam,
And in your wake, you left a trail, of every precious dream.
Now as I gaze upon the sky, I feel you by my side,
The Star Sailor’s lullaby, a song that will not hide.

Bridge

For in your song, I find my home,
A place where love abides,
The Star Sailor’s lullaby,
Forever by my side.
