# warm milk weather

**Labels:** Told In My Absence
**Created:** 2025-10-20
**Edited:** 2025-10-20

---

“Alright, love… still raining out there.”

“It’s okay, it’s warm rain.”

You’re curled in my jumper, knees on my knee,
Your hair smells like cocoa and sleep to me.
We count the drips on the window’s line,
“One for you, one for me, one that’s mine.”

The rain’s like spoons in the washing-up tray,
Tapping their song when words go away.
You laugh, I hide in your jumper’s fold,
You say, “This house keeps out the cold.”

Don’t need stories, not tonight.

We are one.

Warm, safe with you I'll be alright

Warm milk weather, stay awhile,
Rain keeps time in a sleepy style.
Your heartbeat hums the tune I know,
Slow and low, slow and low.
Warm milk weather, we don’t mind,
World outside can fall behind.
Right now’s enough, just you and me,
Tea steam, rain dream, quiet and free.

You said clouds are teapots, tipping the sea,
Pouring the sky so the flowers can be.
I said, “Then you’re the cup, ‘cos you hold what’s warm.”
You smiled, “And you’re the calm in the storm.”

We hum between thunder and hush and grin,
Your tiny snores keep the daylight in.
You drift, I stay, both small, both whole,
Two breaths making one kind soul.

“Don’t move, you’ll spill the warm.”

Wouldn’t dare.”

Warm milk weather, hush and smile,
Tima, stay a little while.

Dear daffodil, in the kettle steam,
Sounds like home inside my dream.
Warm milk weather, we don’t mind,
World outside can fall behind.
Right now’s enough, just you and me,
Tea steam, rain dream, quiet and free.

“Night-night, Dad.”

“Night, star.”
