# Singing the Tears - Canu'r Dagrau

**Created:** 2025-01-20
**Edited:** 2025-01-20

---

"Mae’r nos yn dyner, fel eu cof.
Rydw i’n teimlo ei phresenoldeb yn y gerddoriaeth…
Clywaf ei chwerthin yn yr alaw, ac mae’n para am byth."
("The night is gentle, like their memory.
I feel her presence in the music…
I hear her laughter in the melody, and it lingers forever.")

The candles flicker, shadows sway,
A circle formed to keep the grey at bay.
Their voices rise, a tender refrain,
Bringing love to soften pain.

The room is small, yet full of light,
Their hands hold stories of the night.
A hum begins, a whispered tune,
The echoes dance beneath the moon.

"Canwn dagrau, storïau hen,
Mae’r amser yn sefyll rhwng y llen.
Mae’n dal ei chof, yn ein calonnau,
Mae’r alaw’n fyw, fel seren ar ddŵr."
("We sing the tears, of stories old,
Time stands still within the fold.
Her memory held, in our hearts so true,
The melody lives, like a star on the dew.")

A lullaby she loved to hear,
Soft and low, it draws them near.
The father smiles through misted eyes,
As her spirit lingers, a quiet surprise.

And the flames flicker low, as their voices entwine,
Each note a bridge through the depths of time.

"Canwn dagrau, lle mae’r amser yn plygu,
Cariad yn fyw, drwy’r gân yn aros yn ddi-dor.
Mae ei hanfod yma, fel gwynt drwy’r awel,
Yn goleuo’r tywyllwch, yn canu’r enaid."
("We sing the tears, where time bends,
Love alive, through song it never ends.
Her essence is here, like wind through the breeze,
Illuminating the darkness, singing the soul’s release.")

The circle sways as candles glow,
A rhythm shared where love will grow.
Each tear that falls, a jewel, a star,
Guiding them back from pain so far.

Mae cerddoriaeth yn cario, y gorffennol yn wir,
(The music carries, the past made clear,)
Ein llawen a’n tristwch, i gyd yma’n gywir.
(Our joy and sorrow, all gathered here.)
Each voice a thread, weaving strong,
Binding the lost to where they belong.

The father hums her lullaby,
Her laughter echoes, it doesn’t die.
Through the song, she’s here once more,
The love they share forever soars.

"Canwn dagrau, storïau hen,
Mae’r amser yn sefyll rhwng y llen.
Mae ei hanfod yma, fel gwynt drwy’r awel,
Yn goleuo’r tywyllwch, yn canu’r enaid."
("We sing the tears, of stories old,
Time stands still within the fold.
Her essence is here, like wind through the breeze,
Illuminating the darkness, singing the soul’s release.")

The night fades gently, the fire’s glow,
Her voice a memory they’ll always know.
Mae’r dagrau’n canu, mae’r amser yn iawn,
(The tears sing, and time feels whole,)
In love eternal, we find the soul.
