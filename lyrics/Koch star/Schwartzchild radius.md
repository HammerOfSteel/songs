# Schwartzchild radius

**Labels:** Koch star
**Created:** 2024-12-05
**Edited:** 2024-12-05

---

The crowd’s a distant echo,
Voices spinning, pulling tight.
Gravity presses harder,
The light begins to fight.

The room is filled with laughter,
But I can’t quite find my place.
Each word feels like a tether,
Pulling me through endless space.
Their smiles blur together,
Reflections I can’t trace.

I’m caught in the greedy malestrom,
Where light bends, and shadows spin.
The pull is fierce, the night insidious,
But even here, I’ll begin.

I feel the Warden’s whisper,
A shadow in my mind.
“Don’t let them see your weakness,
Leave the truth behind.”
The crowd keeps pressing closer,
Each word a twisting bind.

Through the noise, a voice calls gently,
A thread of light, a quiet plea.

I rage through our plotted patterns,
Where light bends, and shadows spin.
The pull is fierce, the night insidious,
But even here, I’ll begin.

A laugh cuts through the static,
A glance too long, too near.
I feel myself unravel,
Swallowed whole by fear.
The room becomes a vortex,
The edge pulls me clear.

I see the others’ shadows,
Each carrying their own storms.
A dance of light and darkness,
Where pain takes different forms.
I feel the smallest flicker,
A warmth inside the cold.

I tear through a black hole sun,
Where light bends, and shadows spin.
The pull is fierce, the night insidious,
But even here, I’ll begin.

These violent walls will fade,
Its light will find a way to break.
Through the endless pull of shadows deep,
A spark remains, and it will keep.

I step into the cool night air,
The stars above, a quiet sea.
The gravity begins to loosen,
And I feel the breath of me.
The darkness isn’t empty,
It holds infinity.

Through the looking glass I go,
Where shadows cling, and time unwinds.
But even here, I’ve found my anchor,
A light persists, and it’s mine.

Soft, fading tones, like distant starlight piercing the void.
Through the endless pull of shadows,
A spark remains, steady and true.
Even the darkest horizons,
Can shed their light to you.
