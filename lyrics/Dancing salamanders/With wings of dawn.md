# With wings of dawn

**Labels:** Dancing salamanders
**Created:** 2024-08-14
**Edited:** 2024-08-14

---

In the dawn of a new life, where the city meets the sky,
With wings of dawn, I learn to fly.

From the ice and fire, to the city’s light,
I chase the dreams that take their flight.
Far from the cold, far from the pain,
I find my path, in the light of the Seine.

With wings of dawn, I spread my wings,
In the heart of the city, where freedom sings.
In every step, in every stride,
I find my place, I cannot hide.

Sous les étoiles, I find my way,
Through the streets of Paris, where dreams sway.
In every shadow, in every light,
I find my strength, I claim my right.

Au cœur de la ville, où la lumière brille,
I find my soul, I find my will.
In fjarlægðinni, where the heart resides,
I find my peace, in the city’s tides.

With wings of dawn, I spread my wings,
In the heart of the city, where freedom sings.
In every step, in every stride,
I find my place, I cannot hide.

Through the streets, where shadows dance,
I find my strength, I take my chance.
In every word, in every thought,
I find the dreams that can’t be bought.

With wings of dawn, my spirit flies,
Through the city, through the skies.

As Mira leaves Iceland behind for the vibrant, unfamiliar streets of Paris, "Wings of Dawn" captures the mix of fear and exhilaration that comes with chasing one’s dreams. This song is an anthem of transformation, where new horizons bring both challenge and the promise of rebirth.
