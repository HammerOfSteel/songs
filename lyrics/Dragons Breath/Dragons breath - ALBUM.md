# Dragons breath - ALBUM

**Labels:** Dragons Breath, Album
**Created:** 2025-07-04
**Edited:** 2025-07-19

---

Anadl y ddraig dros y bryniau’n dawnsio,
Yn cuddio’r cofnodion, yn codi'r hanes.
Yn y niwl, mae’r galon yn canu’n araf,
Gyda chwerthin, gyda chariad, gyda cholli’n dawel.

Cwtsh ar y sill, llais mewn glaw,
Cân dros fryniau lle’r haul unwaith oedd.
O de, o banad, o hanesion Taid,
Dyma lle mae’r enaid yn aros — mewn niwl, mewn cân.

The dragon’s breath dances across the hills,
Hiding memories, lifting stories.
In the mist, the heart sings slow,
With laughter, with love, with quiet loss.

A cwtsh on the sill, a voice in the rain,
A song over hills where the sun once lay.
With tea, with tales, with Taid’s old truths,
This is where the soul stays — in mist, in song.

🎵 Music Style (overall):

Genres: acoustic folk, UK rap/spoken word, ambient textures, post-rock, chamber folk
Instruments: fingerpicked guitar, Welsh harp, harmonium, field recordings (rivers, birds), upright piano, subtle beats, fiddle, soft brass
Vocals: warm, raw male voice (yours), sometimes female harmonies, no autotune
Feeling: atmospheric, grounded, deeply emotional with moments of playful banter
Pace: mostly slow to mid-tempo, but varied
