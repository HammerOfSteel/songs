# The Crystal’s Curse

**Labels:** A Spiral Path, Dancing salamanders
**Created:** 2024-09-27
**Edited:** 2024-09-27

---

Verse 1:
I remember when you were born under stars so bright,
A legacy passed, but with shadows in the light.
Your mother’s curse, her mana’s weight you bore,
But I swore I’d protect you, now I’m fighting even more.
The healers speak of magic, of crystals with hope,
But this crystal’s curse has bound us in rope.
Orochi’s eyes watching from the Hydra's throne,
Kerivas, where are you? Your law, our cornerstone.

Chorus:
The crystal’s curse, a shimmering lie,
Once held promise, now it's why she’ll die.
Law of the spire, of Hydra’s descent,
I can’t save her, I can’t prevent.
This crystal I hold, now shrouded in grief,
Once a symbol of hope, now a thief.

Verse 2:
Her breath grows shallow, her mana fades away,
I chase every healer, every path, every day.
I scream to the skies, where is Orochi’s might?
Kerivas, where are you? Give us the right!
This spire, once a place of my rise,
Now haunts me, with every tear in her eyes.
Law and resilience, the judgment I passed,
But the crystal’s curse? Too much to outlast.

Bridge:
In the depths of the spire, the crystal called bright,
But now it’s a shadow, consuming her light.
Orochi coils tight in the depths of her soul,
Kerivas, I beg, please make her whole.
But law is unbending, fate holds no sway,
And I watch as her breath slips further away.

The crystal, now dust in my hands so cold,
A relic of failure, a story untold.
Kerivas, Orochi, the law takes its toll,
I’ve lost her, my heart, my soul.
