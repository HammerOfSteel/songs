# Cloud and Light - Cwmwl a Goleuni

**Labels:** Blodau yn y Lludw
**Created:** 2025-01-19
**Edited:** 2025-01-19

---

The rain falls softly, a whisper on the street,
Umbrella held between us, where the clouds and sunlight meet.
The pavement gleams, a mirror for our fears,
But your hand in mine wipes away the tears.

Through the clinic doors, we step into the grey,
A storm of questions brewing, yet words drift away.
The sky splits open, letting slivers of light through,
And I think of all the storms that brought me here to you.

Cwmwl a goleuni, y ddau’n dawnsio’n dawel,
(Cloud and light, the two dance quietly,)
Between the shadows, love’s flame does swell.
Though the path is steep, we’ll make it anew,
For every cloud, a light shines through.

We pause beneath the park’s old chestnut tree,
A lantern from Peter glows softly, guiding me.
The leaves above are whispers, the wind carries dreams,
And we speak of what’s ahead, fragile as it seems.

Each moment feels heavy, yet light breaks the weight,
Through love, we rewrite what seemed sealed by fate.

Cwmwl a goleuni, yn ein calon ni,
(Cloud and light, within our hearts,)
Through the darkest nights, love always starts.
Though the waves crash high, and the sky is torn,
Every storm will end, and hope is reborn.

The rain has faded, the moon begins to rise,
Reflections of our journey glimmer in your eyes.
We light the lantern in the window, warm and clear,
A beacon for tomorrow, for all we hold dear.

When the shadows call, and the past feels near,
Remember, love’s flame burns bright, my dear.
Mae’r bywyd hwn yn ein dwylo’n fflam,
(This life in our hands, a flame,)
Guiding us forward, unbroken by pain.

The dawn breaks gently, painting skies in gold,
A new day emerges, a story to be told.
Peter’s voice lingers, his kindness in our chest,
As we hold onto hope and let the rest rest.

Cwmwl a goleuni, yn y gwynt mae cân,
(Cloud and light, in the wind there’s a song,)
Through every trial, love carries us on.
Though the road winds far, and shadows remain,
We’ll follow the light and cherish the flame.

The lantern glows softly, a promise to the night,
In the dance of cloud and light, we find our flight.
Rydym yn creu bywyd, ac mae bywyd yn creu ni,
(We create life, and life creates us,)
Through cloud and light, we will always be.
