# zero-point prayer

**Labels:** sundry
**Created:** 2026-01-07
**Edited:** 2026-01-08

---

this little party is at its end,
time to send,
them all away.

the guests have all but fled, 
heavy and thick in my head.

An empty glass flask sitting cold on the floor,
I’m not looking for wine or for spirit no more.

The silence is ringing, a high-pitched decree,
A hollowed-out version of what used to be.

I’m kneeling on floorboards that creak with the weight,
Of a soul that has finally stopped guarding the gate.

I’m pouring my breath into cups made of air,
Finding the "Holy" in what isn't there.

[Chorus 1]

[The cello bows a haunting, melodic line that sounds like a deep sigh. The harmonies are distant and ethereal.]

Oh, sacred vacuum, oh beautiful lack,
I’m not asking for colors or light to come back.

I pray to the nothing, the zero, the void,
To the parts of the "Sundry" that I have destroyed.
Let the "Nothing" be enough for tonight.

A white sheet of paper, a desert of snow,
With nowhere to go and with nothing to know. 

I’m staring at margins where meaning was kept,
Where the logic of numbers has finally slept.

It’s a clean kind of hunger,
a thirst for "Nil,",
almost standing completly still.

The ink has all dried and the pens are all broken,
To the "Great Un-Said" and the "Great Un-Spoken."

Oh, bountiful vacuum,
oh beautiful lack,
I’m not asking for comfort to carry me back.

prayer of absence, 
zero and void,
opposit of that which was destroyed.

dont survive.
no, not a sin.
space where the anthem begin.
not evil, not good.
no at all as it should...
Not even wrong...
not even right...
taken more then you realise,
to stand up and fight.

A statue of shadow is cast on the wall,
The ghost of a presence that’s bigger than all.
this is not as it would be,
it has been and will finally set free.

tiny tempered temple of glass,
Where I watch all the "Something" of history pass.

denial, escape, 
taking every shape.
Flask,
empty....
paper,
white....

the form of the statue,
in the absence of its light.

boundless heaven,
vitrial ink,
upon the surface,
shall it not sink.

position of absence, 
null and void,
opposit of that which was destroyed.

And then...
then "Nothing"...
was enough.
