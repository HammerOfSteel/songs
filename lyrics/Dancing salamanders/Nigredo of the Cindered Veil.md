# Nigredo of the Cindered Veil

**Labels:** Dancing salamanders
**Created:** 2024-08-16
**Edited:** 2024-08-16

---

Verse 1
Through the gate of ebony, where shadows curl,
I tread the barren path where the night pearls unfurl.
The raven's wing sweeps over the ashen land,
Where the serpent coils in the grip of sand.

Chorus
In the blackened soil where the dragon’s blood seeps,
The crucible holds the secret that weeps.
Beneath the dark veil, where the black sun stands,
I follow the whisper of the hidden lands.

Verse 2
The scorpion’s sting marks the way unseen,
Through the vitriol’s breath where the earth turns green.
In the marrow of night, where the shadow lies,
The peacock’s tail unfurls in disguise.

Bridge
A relic of lead, born of mercury’s dance,
Guides me through the labyrinth, in a trance.
The stone awakens in the heart of the night,
As the phoenix ascends in the sulphuric flight.

Chorus
In the blackened soil where the dragon’s blood seeps,
The crucible holds the secret that weeps.
Beneath the dark veil, where the black sun stands,
I follow the whisper of the hidden lands.

Outro
Through the furnace where the raven’s caw resounds,
The flame reveals what the blackness surrounds.
In the cauldron of shadows, truth takes form,
Where the light is born from the gathering storm.
