# Starlight Keepsakes

**Labels:** Dancing salamanders, Elara
**Created:** 2024-08-23
**Edited:** 2024-08-23

---

"Starlight Keepsakes" is a tender and reflective song that captures the idea of collecting memories like stardust, each one a precious keepsake that shines in the darkness. The song uses the metaphor of starlight to represent the lasting impact of Elara’s love, guiding and comforting those she left behind. It is a beautiful reminder that even in the vastness of space, love remains a constant and eternal light.

Verse 1
Underneath the velvet sky, where the stars so softly gleam,
I gather up the stardust, to weave you a dream.
Each tiny light a keepsake, from the nights we used to share,
A shimmering reminder that you’re still everywhere.
Your laughter echoes in the night, a melody so pure,
In every twinkling star, I feel your presence sure.

Chorus
Starlight keepsakes, in the dark they shine,
Reminders of your love, forever entwined.
In the night’s embrace, I see your face,
In every star that falls, in every cosmic trace.
Starlight keepsakes, glowing soft and true,
A map of all the memories that lead me back to you.

I walk beneath the heavens, where your spirit loves to roam,

I hold them close, these keepsakes, in the quiet of the night,
A treasure trove of love, that guides me by their light.
