# Aurea of the Luminous Field

**Labels:** Dancing salamanders
**Created:** 2024-08-16
**Edited:** 2024-08-16

---

Verse 1
In the dusk where the dew of the heavens drips,
The maiden’s breath lingers, sweet on my lips.
Fields of verdant serpents, where shadows play,
Under the wing of the phoenix’s sway.

Chorus
Here in the heart of the viriditas bloom,
The secret fire dances in the silent gloom.
Beneath the virgin sky, where the stars unfold,
The dragon’s vein flows, in threads of gold.

Verse 2
Moonlit trees whisper the secrets of the green,
The lion’s heart stirs in the sylvan sheen.
Waters still, reflecting what the soul cannot grasp,
In the silent pool, where the past and future clasp.

Bridge
A message distilled from the winged serpent’s flight,
Crosses the threshold of day into night.
To the heart that knows, a silent guide,
In the fields where the mysteries confide.

Chorus
Here in the heart of the viriditas bloom,
The secret fire dances in the silent gloom.
Beneath the virgin sky, where the stars unfold,
The dragon’s vein flows, in threads of gold.

Outro
In the land where the eternal rose is sown,
I seek the stone that turns bone into throne.
In the shadow of the unseen light, I roam,
For the albedo’s kiss, in this sacred home.
