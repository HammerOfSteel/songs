# Halfpenny Dreams (Heno, Little One)

**Labels:** Wool and Wonder
**Created:** 2025-05-22
**Edited:** 2025-05-22

---

Tonight, tonight, little ones crash,
Halfpenny dreams in a firefly flash.
Bed, bed, lil' sleepy crew,
Tomorrow's a rocket, and it starts with you.

Oi, socks on the toaster, jam on the cat,
One’s in the wardrobe wearin’ me hat.
Toothbrush in the teapot, cereal in the sink,
Other one’s sayin’ the moon can blink.

Can I ride a fox? Like, real slow?

If I wear four coats, will I never feel snow?

I say “yes” to most, “maybe” to some—
But bedtime’s comin’ and the stars won’t run.

PJs? Nah. Just vibes and biscuit.
One’s tryin’ spells, the other's gone mystic.
They're all full tilt till they trip on the rug—
And land in a cwtch like a bear in a hug.

Tonight, tonight, little stars go crash,
Halfpenny dreams in a moonlight dash.
Hush now, hush now, run and catch—
Dime dime dime, lil' hearts all hatch.

Bed bed, little noisy crew,
Dream of rivers and sky so blue.
Sleep, sleep, you mad wee sparks—
Tomorrow you chase the sun in the dark.

One asked me: “Why don’t clouds fall down?”
I said: “They do. That’s rain, love—brown town.”
Another said: “What’s inside a wish?”
And I said: “A whisper, wrapped in a dish.”

Are ghosts just dreams that don’t know they’re gone?

Can I have cake in the bath at dawn?

These are the gospels of bedtime chat,
Big thoughts in a room with a dragon mat.

Their teeth not brushed, but minds all lit,
Little philosophers throwin’ a fit.
And me? I just rhyme to keep ‘em still,
Spittin’ spells while the house turns chill.

Tonight, tonight, little stars go zoom,
Halfpenny dreams in the bedroom bloom.
Hush now, hush now, time ticks slow,
Dime dime dime, let the night wind blow.

Sleep sleep, little gobby gang,
Snorin’ like wolves in a bedtime slang.
Tomorrow's wild with toast and plans,
And riddles that only your crew understands.

I’ve walked this floor since before you spoke,
Since nappies and nonsense and dreams half broke.
Now you all chatter like gods on the rise,
Makin’ stars in your minds, paintin' clouds in the skies.

I’m just here with the cocoa, the mop, and the beat,
Countin’ you in, tuckin’ dreams in your feet.
One day you’ll climb where I can’t reach,
But tonight, I’ve still got the right to teach—

That love ain’t loud, and peace ain’t fake,
And bedtime’s holy, make no mistake.
So I whisper my bars like a myth half-told,
And wrap you in warmth while the fire gets cold.

Tonight, tonight, little ones hush,
Halfpenny dreams in a cwtchy rush.
Dime dime dime, the stars all wait,
For your next sunrise through the garden gate.

Bed bed, where the magic breathes,
Sleep soft beneath story leaves.
Sleep, sleep, you brilliant crew,
Tomorrow’s mad—and I’m proud of you.
