# Here, there, where

**Labels:** Dragons Breath
**Created:** 2025-07-15
**Edited:** 2025-07-15

---

we sit,
letting latte sugar
harden,
in coffee-less
cups,
chit chatting,
feeling “alchemistry.”
we darken dusk,
Light
out of glowing “open”
signs,  
then blow galaxies
out the sunroof
a tasseled red heart,
dangles
from the rear view
angled, just right.

Tarp over tree, rain in the beat,
You brought the crisps, I brought the heat.
Daffodil vine, chips from Dai’s,
Fog in the woods, stars in your eyes.

Trail is gone, gone home, awry,
Chaos I spy with my little eye.
You twirled round, slipped, caught you fast ,
Laughed so hard we woke the past.

Where is over there?
Past the gap in the hedge, where the daffodils dare.
dare to bloom all full in ebbwvale
we were here, wrote this tale.

That night I wore your hoodie wrong way ‘round,
Smelled of bonfire, socked the ground.
tall tingly tales of sweet little ghosts,
Like matter of fact, said I like em the most.

We made vows with twig and rhyme,
Swapped secrets like “see you next time.”
I kissed your cheek, you missed the cue,
But your hand held mine like it always knew.

Where is over there?
Through the brambles and half a dare.
Past the fence, up the stile, hit the x after a mile
looking for what, treasure or truth?
Just a glance of forgotten youth.

Ran outta phone but charged on tunes,

Stepped on your toe under the moon.

“what if love is just a fire?”

“then let’s burn like broken lyres.”

Hid in the thicket to dodge the rain,

But danced like fools through the soaking grain.

Home by four,  that'll do
tangle true, me and you.

Where is over there?
Where the fern unfurls and your curls caught air.
Where we laughed too much, and time let go,
If you’re askin’ now, then you already know.

You still got that song on your playlist?

Course I do, it’s track seventeen... best one
