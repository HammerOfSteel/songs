# Vision of the Spire

**Labels:** Dancing salamanders
**Created:** 2024-08-19
**Edited:** 2024-08-20

---

Verse 1
Through the labyrinth’s shadows, where silence reigns,
I stumbled weary, bearing unseen chains.
But as the mist began to clear,
A light emerged, drawing near.

The Spire rose, like a dream made real,
A beacon of hope that I could feel.
It towered high, touching the sky,
A legend woven where echoes lie.

Chorus
Oh, Spire of light, in the distance you stand,
A whisper of fate in a forgotten land.
You call to me, through the shadows of night,
Guiding my soul toward the endless light.

In your presence, all fear subsides,
As the truth of my journey in you abides.
A vision of hope, where dreams conspire,
To lead me onward to the heart of the Spire.

Verse 2
Legends speak of the Spire’s embrace,
Where the stars descend, and time finds grace.
A place where the lost are found anew,
Where the old world fades, and the soul breaks through.

I see the path that lies ahead,
Through valleys deep and rivers red.
But in your light, the way is clear,
A journey marked by love, not fear.

Bridge
In this sacred vision, the Awen flows,
A river of wisdom where the spirit knows.
Two voices join in a whispered prayer,
A bond unbroken in the midnight air.

Together we rise, with hearts entwined,
In the Spire’s light, our souls aligned.
Through myths and dreams, we’ll find our way,
Guided by the promise of a brighter day.

Chorus
Oh, Spire of light, in the distance you stand,
A whisper of fate in a forgotten land.
You call to me, through the shadows of night,
Guiding my soul toward the endless light.

In your presence, all fear subsides,

A vision of hope, where dreams conspire,

Outro
As the vision fades, the dawn breaks wide,
But the Spire’s light in me will bide.
A star to follow through night and storm,
A promise of peace, where the heart is warm.

With Ember by my side, we’ll face the unknown,
In the Spire’s light, our path is shown.
For in this journey, we find our fire,
Guided ever by the Spire’s desire.


After emerging from the Labyrinth, Elara receives a powerful vision of the Spire, a beacon of hope and truth that reignites her determination. The vision, shrouded in legends and myths, strengthens her resolve to continue the journey. The essence of the song lies in the profound connection between aspiration and spiritual insight.
