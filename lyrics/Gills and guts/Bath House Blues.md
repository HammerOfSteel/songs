# Bath House Blues

**Labels:** Dancing salamanders, Gills and guts
**Created:** 2024-09-20
**Edited:** 2024-09-20

---

Place: Jubilee Pool, Penzance

Verse 1
I went down to Jubilee, thought I’d find some peace,
The waters warm but the weight don’t cease.
Art Deco walls, they shine so bright,
But inside me, baby, it’s still cold as night.
The waves they lap, gentle and slow,
But the past keeps pullin’, won’t let me go.

Chorus
I got the bath house blues, deep in my soul,
Sittin’ in the water but I’m feelin’ so cold.
Jubilee’s callin’, but I can’t let it be,
Lost in the memories, they keep drownin’ me.
Bath house blues, they follow me still,
Can’t shake the echoes, can’t climb that hill.

Verse 2
I see reflections, but they ain’t mine,
Faces from yesterday, caught in time.
The pool’s a mirror, but it’s cracked in two,
Half of it’s here, the rest is what I knew.
Water’s risin’, but I just float,
Feelin’ the weight of every word I wrote.

Bridge
I came here hopin’ to ease my mind,
But all I found was the weight of time.
Every ripple’s a reminder, every breeze a sigh,
Of the days we let slip, of the reasons why.
The water’s warm, but I can’t seem to mend,
Nostalgia’s got me, like an old lost friend.

So I’ll sit here, till the sun goes down,
Feelin’ the waves, hearin’ that lonesome sound.
Maybe tomorrow, I’ll find some peace,
But today, these blues just won’t release.
