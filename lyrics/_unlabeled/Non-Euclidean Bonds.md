# Non-Euclidean Bonds

**Created:** 2024-12-11
**Edited:** 2024-12-11

---

[Intro: Soft and Reflective]
The rain taps light on the windowpane,
A quiet echo of endless refrain.
Why does trust feel like shifting sand,
A map I hold but can’t understand?

[Verse 1: The Beginning of the Weekend]
The coffee brews, the morning slow,
Familiar faces, yet I don’t know.
Their smiles are warm, their words are kind,
But shadows linger in my mind.
I ask myself, “What’s wrong with me?”
Why can’t I let this tension be?

[Chorus 1: The Questioning]
They say, “Just trust, let go of doubt,”
But the answers fade when I reach out.
I ask “Why?”—the bonds unwind,
Twisting threads I’ll never find.
Non-Euclidean, these ties I see,
Curves that bend but won’t set me free.

[Verse 2: The Afternoon Stroll]
We walk through fields, the skies turn gray,
Their laughter feels a world away.
The trees lean close, their roots entwined,
As if they hold what I can’t find.
The questions rise, I push them back,
The path ahead a winding track.

[Chorus 2: The Excuses]

They say, “It’s fine, just let it be,”
But their words don’t align with what I see.
I ask “Why?”—the answers blur,
A quiet ache without a cure.
Non-Euclidean, these ties I hold,
A labyrinth carved in stories untold.

[Pre-Chorus: The Evening Reflection]
The moonlight spills through fractured glass,
Shadows stretch, and questions amass.
Why can’t I let this silence stay?
Why does trust keep slipping away?

[Verse 3: The Quiet Night]
The fire burns low, the room turns still,
The weight of doubts bends time and will.
Their voices soft, they drift to sleep,
But in my chest, the questions keep.
Am I the one who breaks these ties?
Or do their walls conceal their lies?

[Musical Solo: Ethereal and Expansive]
[Resolve]
The dawn breaks soft, the colors fade,
A fragile calm, decisions made.
I trace the threads of what we’ve shared,
The bonds we’ve built, the love we’ve dared.
Though twisted paths may not align,
I’ll hold the map and call it mine.

[Chorus 3: The Acceptance]
They say, “Just trust, let go of doubt,”
And maybe they’re right—I’ll figure it out.
I ask “Why?”—the answers fade,
But in this space, a truce is made.

[Outro: Gentle and Resolute]
The rain returns, the weekend done,
The ties remain, though threads may run.
Non-Euclidean, but mine to hold,
A fragile map of stories untold.
