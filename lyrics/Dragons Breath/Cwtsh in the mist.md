# Cwtsh in the mist

**Labels:** Dragons Breath
**Created:** 2025-07-04
**Edited:** 2025-07-04

---

“Be’ sy? Ti 'di gweld gôst, neu rwbeth?”

“Nah. Just thought I saw her for a sec.
You know... the way she used to sit there — head tucked under, knees up... proper koala vibes.”

“Still feel her sometimes. Not scary or nothin’.
Just warm. Like… mist with a heartbeat.”


Blanket round my shoulders, brew in hand,
You used to fit right in — knees, curls, tiny hands.
Window steamed up, fog thick as bread,
You’d draw hearts in it, leave ‘em floatin’ instead.

We didn’t talk — we just were,
Your head on my chest, world a blur.
Cutshin’ through storms we couldn’t explain,
I’d hum you to sleep while it bucketed rain.

Ghosts don’t haunt — they hold, they hum,
Sit beside you when the hard days come.
She’s not gone — just slightly mislaid,
In the steam of the glass and the dent in the braid.

Cutsh in the mist, love never missed,
You’re here in the hush where the window kissed.
Close in the quiet, warm in the grey,
I feel you drift in with the start of day.

Some nights I still talk out loud,
Say “you alright, love?” to the gathering cloud.
Laugh at myself — feel daft as hell,
But what’s grief but a story you still wanna tell?

Your blanket’s folded by the window ledge,
Still smells like raspberries and blue felt pens.
I ain’t washed it — that’d be rude,
Like scrubbin’ away your attitude.

They say “move on”, but I walk with you,
One step behind where the fog rolls through.
Your giggle’s stitched in the shape of the chair,
And the warmth? The warmth never left the air.

Cutsh in the mist, breath and a name,
You’re laughter in fog, you’re candle and flame.
Stay in the hush, don’t need to be seen,
Love don’t vanish — it lives in between.

“You still cold, love?”

“Nah... I’m good now.”

“You remember the day it rained sideways?”
“You tucked me in and said the clouds were just grumpy sheep.”

Sometimes I swear I see your shape in steam,
Not scary, just like you drifted from a dream.
Feet curled under, nose in a book,
Eyes meet mine, and then it’s just a look.

Not there, not gone. Just mist in the bend,
Where the past don’t stop, it just finds a friend.

So I leave the kettle on for two, still,
Let the window fog, let the silence fill.

'Cause some cwtshes never end, see.
They just hum quiet when the world can't speak.

Cwtsh in the mist, time takes its turn,
But I hold you close in the way stars burn.
Right here, love — in the steam and grey,
Wrapped in the morning, just one breath away.

“It’s liftin’ now... look.”

“Still here, innit?”
