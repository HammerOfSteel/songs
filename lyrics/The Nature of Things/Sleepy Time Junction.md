# Sleepy Time Junction

**Labels:** The Nature of Things
**Created:** 2024-11-24
**Edited:** 2024-11-24

---

The house grows still, the lights turn low,
The moonlight casts a silver glow.
Her tiny hands hold a favorite book,
As bedtime whispers find their nook.

"Are you ready, my little sun?"
"One more story, then I’m done."
"Which shall it be, the stars or sea?"
"The stars tonight, they sing to me."

He turns the page with a gentle hand,
A tale of dreams, of distant lands.
Her eyelids flutter, her breath grows slow,
Her tiny world begins to glow.

"Do you see the ship on the starlit tide?"
"It’s sailing far where dreams abide.
Will it take me there, where the clouds can play?"
"It will, my love, till the break of day."

Sleepy Time Junction, soft and sweet,
A quiet train where dreams will meet.
Through silver skies and whispered streams,
We journey far on moonlit dreams.

He hums a tune as the clock ticks slow,
A melody soft, a gentle glow.
Her head leans close, her eyes grow dim,
The stars align in her evening hymn.

"Hush, my love, the night is near,
The stars will keep you safe, my dear.
"Will the moon watch too, from the sky so wide?"
"Always, my love, it’ll light your tide."

Her tiny hand falls still in mine,
Her breathing soft, her heart divine.
The stories fade, the dreams take flight,
A sleepy star in the quiet night.

"How small she feels, how vast her glow,
A piece of heaven I’ll always know."

Sleepy Time Junction, calm and bright,
A train that rides through the quiet night.
Through fields of stars and rivers wide,
It carries love where dreams reside.

The world feels still, the moment true,
A bond of love in the softest hue.
The years may pass, the nights may change,
But these quiet whispers will remain.

"She’ll grow, she’ll soar, she’ll find her way,
But in my heart, she’ll always stay."

He tucks her close, her blanket tight,
A little cocoon in the softest light.
The room feels warm, the stars shine clear,
Her dreams take shape, her heart sincere.

"Sleep well, my love, my guiding star,
Wherever you dream, I’ll never be far."

Sleepy Time Junction, soft and sweet,
A train of love where dreams will meet.
Through silver skies and whispered streams,
We journey far on moonlit dreams.

The house grows still, the stars hold sway,
Her dreams take her where the clouds can play.
A father’s love, a whispered song,
In Sleepy Time Junction, where hearts belong.


"Goodnight, my love, my little star,
Wherever you are, I’ll never be far."
