# Osiris and the Monster Parade - Fear is Fun, If You’re Fast Enough

**Created:** 2025-04-14
**Edited:** 2025-04-14

---

It started with a bump.
Then a growl.
Then… a knock on my closet.
And a note that said:
“Midnight. Be ready. Wear boots.”

I got a werewolf who’s allergic to fleas,
A swamp witch who knits with haunted cheese.
A cyclops DJ with a boom-box hat,
And a ghost who’s scared of his own doormat.

There’s a mummy who raps (but only in rhyme),
A vampire late to everything—all the time.
They showed up outside in a neon van,
Said: “Osiris, you're in — you're the Monster Man!”

Monsters, myth, and mayhem too,
We stomp through puddles in monster shoes!
Shadow snacks and giggle growls,
Marchin' past the midnight owls.
Fear is fun (if you're fast enough),
So grab your torch and monster fluff!
We're weird, we’re wild, we’re not that tough—
We’re just a little spooky... with birthday stuff.

The vampire’s vegan, he sips beet juice,
The banshee sings with auto-tune loose.
The bog-beast cries in every movie,
He sobbed for hours at Shrek 2, truly.

A shadow tried to sneak up slow,
But tripped on candy and yelled, “Oh no!”
And Zim the zombie — the legend himself —
Wears rubber duck socks on his top shelf.

So if you hear a bump in the night,
It might just be a monster who’s lost his light.
They’re clumsy, kind, and just like you...
And tonight they’re marching straight on through—

Monsters, myth, and mayhem crew,
Now there’s dragons dancing in party shoes!
Pajama ghosts and giggle bats,
Telling jokes with pumpkin hats.
Fear is fun (if you laugh enough),
So bring your socks and monster fluff!
We’re weird, we’re loud, we’re full of guff—
And Osiris leads when the trail gets rough.

We snuck to the kitchen past a sleepy troll,
Who guards the jellybeans in a cereal bowl.
Zim did a dance (it looked like math),
And the ghost moonwalked down the bubble bath path.

The fridge was haunted — it moaned and creaked,
But we grabbed the cookies and nobody shrieked.
We toasted with slime punch, raspberry red,
And threw confetti made of monster bread.

Osiris stood tall in his cake-crumb crown,
With monsters howlin’ all through town.
He said, “Tonight, we’re more than fears—
We’re birthday beasts with monster cheers!”

The moon was high, the stars did swing,
And the marshmallow bats began to sing.
From basement caves to towered trees,
They all came out — the shy and the beasties.

We marched till dawn through shadows and dew,
With spider lights and party glue.
And as we reached the morning gate,
They all yelled loud:
"Osiris is GREAT!"

Monsters, myth, and midnight friends,
The Monster Parade? It never ends!
From cozy closets to attic shelves,
The scariest ones are just like ourselves.
Fear is fun (if you're brave enough),
And love’s what makes the wild stuff tough.
So stomp your boots and puff your fluff—
'Cause monsters need birthdays too... and us.

And when he curled up under the blanket that night,
Zim tucked in his socks.
The ghost whispered “good job.”
And the last line in the sky said:
"Monsters come and go… but Osiris stays awesome."
