# Starlight

**Labels:** Elara
**Created:** 2025-06-18
**Edited:** 2025-06-18

---

Far away,
This old ship's carrying me far away,
Miles off from the echoes and the days
Of the ones who knew if I stayed or strayed.

Starlight,
Still chasing your starlight,
Till the edge of whatever comes next—
Don’t know if it matters, if I’m honest.

Hold you in my arms—
That’s all I ever wanted, love,
To hold you in my arms,
My little light, my moonchild dove.

My life—
You lit up all my life,
Let’s set fire to the grey
And warm the souls who’ve lost their way.

I won’t let you go,
If you promise not to drift,
Don’t vanish like the morning mist,
Not while your name still curls in my breath,
Not while I walk these hills with your steps.

Our hopes and quiet dreams,
Cracked skies and silver seams,
Our hopes and quiet dreams,
Black holes and soft routines.

Far away,
This ship’s still pulling me far away,
But your voice—it hums in the wind,
In the kettle’s song, in the warmth within.

I won’t let you go,
If you promise not to fade,
Stay tucked in the stars,
In the woods we made,
In the light by the old back gate.

Our hopes and quiet dreams,
Tea steam and old moonbeams,
Our hopes and quiet dreams,
Black holes and memory streams.

Hold you in my arms—
That’s all I ever wanted, love,
To hold you in my arms.
I just wanted to hold—
You in my arms.
