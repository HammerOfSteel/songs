A map of stars, a quiet night,
Each one a spark, a point of light.
They guide the way through time and space,
A constellation of embrace.

A tiny hand that reaches mine,
A fleeting smile, a precious sign.
A laugh escapes, the world feels near,
A moment held, crystal clear.

Each star ignites a sacred thread,
A memory stitched, a word unsaid.
A quiet hug beneath the moon,
A love that always feels in tune.

Constellations in his eyes,
Moments woven through the skies.
A map of love, a glowing chart,
Each point a piece of his bright heart.

A sudden laugh, a joyful sound,
He shows me stars upon the ground.
“Look, Dad, they’re there!” he softly says,
Tracing patterns in his gaze.

We follow shapes the stars have drawn,
Through quiet nights into the dawn.
Each touch, each sign, a whispered line,
That binds his soul and heart to mine.

Constellations in his eyes,
Moments glowing, vast and wise.
Each touch, each glance, a sacred art,
A universe within his heart.

Through milestones carved in fleeting light,
We navigate the quiet night.
A look exchanged, a silent cheer,
A breakthrough felt, a love sincere.

Each step we take, a thread appears,
A tapestry of fleeting years.
The stars align, their meanings clear,
Each one a beacon, drawing near.

Constellations in his eyes,
Stories written in the skies.
Each star, a bond, a light we share,
A love that grows beyond compare.

A summer night, a distant glow,
The fireflies dance, the breezes flow.
We trace the stars, his laughter soars,
A universe behind closed doors.

His love shines bright in little ways,
Through smallest acts, through quiet days.
Each star he leaves, a sacred sign,
A constant thread, a love divine.

Constellations in his eyes,
Dreams that span the endless skies.

The night grows still, the stars remain,
A quiet bond through joy and pain.
Each star we’ve found, a thread we’ve spun,
A constellation never done.

And as I watch him softly sleep,
The universe feels calm and deep.
For in his love, I see the skies,
A constellation through his eyes.
