# A Room full of stars

**Labels:** Its good to look homeward
**Created:** 2024-11-28
**Edited:** 2024-11-28

---

The room is dim, the light low and soft,
Her brush poised like a feather aloft.
The city hums, a distant refrain,
But here, the world feels calm, contained.

"Do you think the stars are watching us paint?
Will they envy our hands, our mortal constraint?"

"If they could see, I think they’d agree,
We’re crafting a sky for eternity."

Her brush traces orbs, a planetary dance,
Mercury’s arc, a fleeting chance.
The ouroboros curls, its tail in its jaw,
An eternal cycle without a flaw.

"The stars we paint—they’re like us, you see,
Both fractured and whole, impossibly free."

"And yet they shine, no matter the break,
A lesson in light, in what we make."

A room full of stars, where dreams ignite,
A tapestry spun from shadow and light.
Each stroke a wish, each mark a flame,
A cosmos whispered with love’s true name.

She paints a crescent, then stops mid-flow,
"Do you think the moon ever feels alone?
Bound to the Earth, yet yearning away,
Chasing a sun that will never stay."

He takes her hand, his voice a guide,
"The moon finds strength in the shifting tide.
And you, my love, you’re a star complete,
A map of worlds, where hope and fear meet."

A room full of stars, where courage is spun,
From leaden nights to a golden sun.
Each stroke a bond, each mark a key,
Unlocking the skies where dreams run free.

Her brush turns soft, a comet’s trail,
"Do you think our stars will tell a tale?
Of hands that painted the heavens wide,
And a child who laughed at the world inside?"

"They’ll tell of a love that spanned the sky,
Of stars that never said goodbye."
She nods, her voice a quiet refrain,
"Then let’s leave her a map of where we’ve been."

The stars above, the stars within,
Each light a battle she dares to win.
"Alchemy’s secret," she softly sighs,
"Is turning tears into what survives."

"And you’re the flame that never dies,
A star that burns through shadowed skies."

The ceiling glows with their cosmic art,
Each painted star a piece of their heart.
She leans on him, her voice serene,
"Do you think they’ll remember what we’ve seen?"

"They’ll remember the love we stitched in light,
A legacy born in the quiet of night."
She smiles, her struggle set aside,
"Then let’s let the stars be our guide."

A room full of stars, where love resides,
A universe painted with dreams as guides.
Each stroke a vow, each mark a flame,
A cosmos lasting beyond the name.

The brushes rest, the night turns still,
The stars remain, their light fulfilled.
And in this room, they’ve made a sky,
Where love burns bright and never dies.

"A room full of stars, a universe near,
Where every fragment becomes sincere."
