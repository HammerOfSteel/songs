# Anima Aeterna: The Breath of the Phoenix

**Labels:** Dancing salamanders, Lucerna Obscura
**Created:** 2024-08-21
**Edited:** 2024-08-22

---

"Eternal breath rises from the ashes, where fire renews the soul. In the phoenix’s flight, the timeless flame flickers in the night."

Verse 1
In the city’s pulse, where the night’s alive,
A Young Man walks, where shadows thrive.
With a smirk and a glance, he finds his prey,
At the edge of the world, where the lost souls play.

"Fulgur nova," in his eyes it gleams,
The spark of ages, beyond all dreams.
In the crowd’s embrace, where youth collide,
He catches your gaze, there’s nowhere to hide.

Chorus
Anima Aeterna, the flame unseen,
In the night’s rebellion, the soul is keen.
Through the dance of fire, the Phoenix flies,
In the breath of youth, where truth lies.

In the city’s heart, where shadows blend,
He shows you the flame that has no end.
Anima Aeterna, the eternal fight,
In the burning soul of the endless night.

Verse 2
Through the house of noise, where chaos reigns,
He speaks in riddles, where nothing’s plain.
With a drink in hand and a laugh so light,
He draws you closer, to the edge of night.

"Flamma perpetua," he whispers low,
In your ear, where secrets flow.
He’s not just a boy, but something more,
A soul reborn from ancient lore.

You see the world in a different light,
In his eyes, the dawn of endless night.
He’s not just a dream, but something real,

Outro
As the night fades into the dawn’s first kiss,
He vanishes like a fleeting mist.
"Phoenix surge," in your soul it burns,
The eternal flame, as the world turns.

He’s a whisper now, in the city’s hum,
But the fire he lit will never succumb.
Anima Aeterna, forever bright,
In the soul of the Phoenix, the endless night.
