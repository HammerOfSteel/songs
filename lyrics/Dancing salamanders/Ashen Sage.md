# Ashen Sage

**Labels:** Dancing salamanders
**Created:** 2024-08-16
**Edited:** 2024-08-16

---

Verse 1
In the wasteland of thought, where echoes fade,
I walked with a heart that had barely stayed.
Through the barren expanse, where silence reigns,
I met the Ashen Sage, in the land of pains.

Chorus
He said, “In the quiet of the mind, where shadows part,
You’ll find the truth that lies within the heart.
In the dust of your journey, where despair finds its mark,
The light you seek is born from the dark.”

Verse 2
With eyes deep as the night, he spoke with care,
“Your path is not easy, but it leads somewhere.
In the ruins of the self, where old dreams die,
You’ll find the seed of who you truly are, by and by.”

Bridge
“The pain you carry, like a burdened stone,
Is the weight that shapes you, when you’re alone.
But within that heaviness, there lies a grace,
A truth unspoken, in the void you face.”

Chorus
He said, “In the quiet of the mind, where shadows part,
You’ll find the truth that lies within the heart.
In the dust of your journey, where despair finds its mark,
The light you seek is born from the dark.”

Verse 3
As I listened, weary, to his solemn tone,
The world around seemed to turn to bone.
Yet his words were like water, in a desert wide,
They quenched the thirst of the soul inside.

Outro
With his guidance, I walked, though my strength was thin,
To the nomad’s settlement, where my journey would begin.
And as I collapsed, in the dust-strewn land,
I knew his wisdom would help me stand.
