# Eleusinion

**Labels:** Dancing salamanders
**Created:** 2024-08-11
**Edited:** 2024-08-11

---

Wandering through ancient streets, beneath the moon’s soft light,
A whisper calls, a distant song, pulling me into the night,
Among the ruins, shadows dance, secrets in the air,
I stumble on a path unknown, drawn by something rare.

Eleusinion, in the dark you hide,
Mysteries unfold, with no one as my guide,
Lost and found, in the sacred ground,
What truths await, when the night comes round?

Eleusinion, nei tuoi misteri mi perdo,
La verità sussurra, ma io non comprendo,
Perso e trovato, nel terreno sacro,
Cosa svelerà la notte, con il suo abbraccio?

Figures move in silent grace, cloaked in ancient lore,
Their eyes, they see beyond the veil, what have I come here for?
The fire burns, the shadows grow, as questions fill my mind,
What is real, what is the dream, what’s hidden in the blind?

I see the light, but is it real, or shadows on the wall?
The cave, it calls, the truth, it waits, beyond the ancient hall,
Awake or sleep, I cannot tell, as mysteries unfold,
The journey’s end, or just the start, the story yet untold.

Vedo la luce, ma è reale o un’ombra nella mente?
La grotta chiama, la verità aspetta, in questo luogo ancestrale,
Sveglio o addormentato, non lo so, mentre i misteri si svelano,
È l’inizio o la fine, il racconto ancora non detto?

As dawn breaks, I find myself, on familiar ground once more,
Yet in my heart, a fire burns, ignited by the lore,
The truth I seek, it still eludes, but now I understand,
The journey’s path is ever long, and I hold it in my hand.

Il giorno sorge, e sono di nuovo qui,
Ma nel cuore una fiamma arde, accesa dal mistero antico,
La verità che cerco sfugge ancora, ma ora comprendo,
Il sentiero è lungo, e io lo stringo nella mano.

These lyrics narrate the young tourist's mysterious and transformative experience, blending the ancient with the modern and the mystical with the philosophical. The references to the Eleusinian Mysteries and the allegory of the cave are interwoven to create a journey of discovery and awakening.

Music Style:
Upbeat acoustic guitar with rhythmic percussion, layered with ambient synths, airy flute, and haunting vocals for a modern, mystical vibe.
