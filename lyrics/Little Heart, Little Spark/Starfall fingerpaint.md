Aiii… aiiiooo… oooooohhh

Socks on the ceiling, toast in me shoe
Daisy on a biscuit, sky turns blue

Awwweeee… eeeiiinnn… boom

Peanut moon, jelly jam floor,
Spinning round twice, then fall through the door.
Bop to the left, drop to the tree,
I saw a butterfly wink at me.

Puddle got a secret, bin got a name,
My shoe's a rocket but it’s not the same.
Baba said “hurry,” I said “cheese!”
My finger drew clouds on the breeze.

Ooooh na-na, boo-bah, bang,
Twinkle tap with a jelly jam clang.
Awnnnnnn… eeeiii… zzzahhh
Lick the sky with a la-la laugh.

Why’s the rain feel like socks?
Can a giraffe wear building blocks?
Do bananas sleep in pairs?
Can worms do plaits in teddy bears' hair?

My belly’s got a button for dreams,
Tried to post a letter to a bus with beams.
Bumblebuzz twang and my hair went swish,
Now my lunch is a flying fish.

Ai ai ai aiiiiooouu
Tickle the day with a finger blue
Whip snap flip flap whuuuuum
Draw the sun with a jelly drum

Sock sock tick tick, bop the pot,
Crayon crown and a teabag spot.
Mug’s got legs and the clouds say “hey,”
Skippin’ down dreams in a jam tart tray.

Tickle me elbow, thunder goes pop,
Slipper full of buttons and a lollipop drop.
I’m not wrong if I say it twice,
Cabbage in a hat makes the sky taste nice.

Awen, awennn, ohhh ai ai,
Paint with stars ‘til the sun says bye.
Bum bum bap and a buttercup bloom,
Swirl the world with a spoonful tune.
