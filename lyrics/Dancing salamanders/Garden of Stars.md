# Garden of Stars

**Labels:** Dancing salamanders, Elara
**Created:** 2024-08-23
**Edited:** 2024-08-23

---

"Garden of Stars" paints a celestial landscape where Elara’s spirit resides among the stars, her light shining eternally in the night sky. The song reflects on the connection between the earthly and the divine, using the imagery of a garden in the heavens where dreams and memories bloom forever. It is a poetic tribute to the enduring bond between Owen and Elara, celebrating her as a guiding light even from afar.

Verse 1
In the twilight of the fading day,
Where the evening shadows grow,
I walk among the roses red,
In the garden where we sowed.
Each petal holds a memory,
Each thorn, a whisper faint,
Of laughter shared and secrets kept,
In a world that we would paint.

Chorus
In the garden of stars, where your dreams reside,
I see your light, though you’re far and wide.
You’re the bloom that never fades away,
The guiding star at the close of day.
In the garden of stars, where the heavens meet,
I hear your voice in the night so sweet.
You’re the light that shines through every scar,
In the garden of stars, that's where you are.

To the garden where you wander,
A testament to our love.
