# Cozy, Scary, Dark

**Labels:** Little Heart, Little Spark
**Created:** 2025-06-17
**Edited:** 2025-06-17

---

Hush now, love, the wind's come knockin'
Tucked up tight while the dark things talkin'
Sleepin’ deep where the brave don’t bark
It’s cozy, scary, cold and stark

Under the quilt with me torchlight flickin’
Monsters marchin’, but I ain't kickin’
Got one eye open, head in me hands
Tryna count sheep but they just dance
Walls go creak and the floorboard groans
Every shadow got a name, every closet moans
Got teddy on guard with a stitch for a scar
He's tougher than he looks — lil' soft war star
Mum said the night just tells long tales
But some of ‘em whisper through keyholes and nails
Still, I lie still, while the cold wind snarks
Proper snug in this kingdom of cozy, scary, dark

Kettle still warm, but the silence bites
Like frost on the tongue of the hallway lights
I breathe out ghosts, they float to the roof
They know I see ‘em, that’s the truth
Old dreams drip like taps from the sink
Each drop’s a worry, makes you think
I dreamt of you, in the woods that talk
With a wolf that sings and a doll that walks
But I weren’t scared, not proper shook
Just held my breath and never looked
Still here tucked with my tiger’s mark
Brave lil' knight in the cozy, scary, dark

Cozy, scary, dark — where stories spark
Under the duvet with the world gone stark
Breathe in whispers, exhale the spark
Little heart beats loud in the cozy, scary, dark

Clock strikes three — witching hour on the nose
Me slippers stare back with buttoned-up woes
The moon’s got a grin like it knows me fears
And I swear the coat rack just moved near
But listen — not all things creepy are cruel
Some just hide 'cause the world’s too cruel
They gather in corners, hum old tunes
Of crickets, crooks, and skeleton spoons
I says to ‘em, “Oi — I’m one of you”
I get scared too when the world’s askew
But we hold the night like a beating heart
Stitchin’ brave with thread in the cozy, scary, dark

Growin’ up's like a hallway long
Full of echoes that sing your song
With a flick of the light you’re back at the start
Socks on the stairs and a flutterin’ heart
But I learned from me dreams and the hushin’ walls
You don’t need to shout when the silence calls
Some fears are friends, just dressed all wrong
With shadow coats and a hum for a song
So I whisper goodnight to the ghosts and the gloom
Leave the door cracked for the stars and the moon
And I tuck in deep with me beatin’ spark
Still safe, still small, in the cozy, scary, dark

Cozy, scary, dark — we’re made of that part
From blanket forts to a runaway heart
Where dreams grow teeth and lullabies bark
Still we sleep sound, still we hold our spark
In the cozy, scary, dark

Night don’t bite if you greet it nice
Some monsters just cold, not made of vice
So sleep with your fears, let ‘em hum their tune
You’re braver than most beneath that moon
