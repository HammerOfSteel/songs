# The Weeping Yggdrasil

**Labels:** Dancing salamanders, Drink deeply, and dream
**Created:** 2024-09-09
**Edited:** 2024-09-09

---

In the shadow of the World Tree, Yggdrasil, the narrator grieves for the loved ones lost to time, finding solace in the ancient roots that connect all life and death. The song intertwines Norse mythology with a reflection on the enduring cycle of grief and renewal.

Verse 1: In the heart of a forest, where shadows dwell,
Yggdrasil weeps, its sorrow to tell.
Its roots run deep, through earth and sky,
Tears fall like leaves, from branches high.
For souls that wander, lost in the night,
It mourns their passage, from life to light.
The world turns on, in endless flight,
Yet here, beneath, all feels right.

Chorus: Weep, oh Yggdrasil, tears of the wise,
For in your sorrow, the truth lies.
Life and death, in circles spin,
One journey ends, another begins.
Through grief's veil, we forge our way,
In the twilight’s glow, we will stay.

Verse 2: I sit in silence, beneath your boughs,
A heart heavy, with forgotten vows.
The wind whispers tales of those now gone,
Yet in your shade, I am not alone.
Your branches cradle, my aching soul,
In your ancient presence, I feel whole.
No easy path, no simple cure,
But in your roots, I find the strength to endure.

Bridge: The world tree stands, tall and strong,
Through endless night, through timeless song.
In its bark, the stories trace,
Of every soul, every face.
A reminder that in death, there’s life anew,
In every tear, a morning dew.

Outro: Beneath the Yggdrasil, I rise once more,
With the strength of those who walked before.
Their echoes guide, through night and day,
In this life, I’ll find my way.
For in the weeping of the ancient tree,
I see the path that sets me free.
