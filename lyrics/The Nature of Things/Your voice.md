# Your voice

**Labels:** The Nature of Things
**Created:** 2024-11-23
**Edited:** 2024-11-23

---

The sun dips low, the air turns sweet,
The summer hums where the rivers meet.
I carry her close, her tiny frame,
The world feels soft, never the same.

"Do you see, my love, how the river glows?"

*"Ba-da... ee..." as the evening flows.
"What do you see, my little sun?"
"A world of wonders, just begun."

We sit by the water, the ripples play,
A gentle rhythm to end the day.
She grabs my thumb, her hand so small,
Her laughter echoes, the sweetest call.

"Do you feel the breeze, soft and kind?"
Her eyes light up as if to find,
A sound, a word, that’s waiting there,
In the quiet hum of the evening air.

Your voice, your light, your world so new,
A song that starts, a path to you.
Through tiny sounds and whispers near,
Your heart speaks loud; I’m here, I hear.

The river bends, the trees stand tall,
The sun fades slow, a golden sprawl.
She looks at me, her lips apart,
A tiny sound, a blooming start.

*"Eef... leef..." her voice so clear,
A melody pure, I pause to hear.
My breath holds tight, my heart takes flight,
Her first true word in the fading light.

I laugh, I cry, the moment glows,
Her voice a gift the river knows.
"Say it again, my little star,"
She smiles, repeats, her world ajar.

"Do you know, my love, what you’ve just done?"
Her giggle says the world’s begun.
"A voice that’s yours, a sound so true,
The world will listen, just like I do."

Your voice, your light, a sound so free,
A whisper small, a symphony.
Through tiny waves and skies so near,
You found your song; I’m here, I hear.

The world feels wide, yet small tonight,
Her voice a lantern, a guiding light.
I hold her close, my heart so full,
Her tiny sound, a moment beautiful.

"How quickly time will take her wings,
But tonight, I’ll hold the song she brings."

The stars appear as we head away,
The river hums a gentle sway.
Her tiny hand still holds my own,
Her voice, her light, a world I’ve known.

*"Eef... leef..." she says once more,
Her words a key, an open door.
"Papa, do stars have words to say?"
"They sing through the night and light your way."

Your voice, your light, a gift, a start,
A sound that blooms within my heart.
Through whispers soft and skies so clear,
Your voice is love; I’m here, I hear.

The cabin glows as the night takes hold,
Her voice a story, a thread of gold.
Her eyes grow heavy, her dreams take flight,
Her words a lantern in the quiet night.

"Your voice, my love, will light your way,
Forever strong, come what may.
