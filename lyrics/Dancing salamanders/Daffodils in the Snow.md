# Daffodils in the Snow

**Labels:** Dancing salamanders, Elara
**Created:** 2024-08-23
**Edited:** 2024-08-23

---

"Daffodils in the Snow" is a song of resilience and hope, where the daffodil serves as a symbol of life’s enduring beauty even in the harshest conditions. Through the imagery of a flower blooming against the odds, the song honors Elara’s spirit and the promise of renewal that she embodies. It is a touching reminder that love and memory can thrive even in the coldest, darkest times.

Verse 1
In the quiet of a winter’s morn,
Where the world is draped in white,
I wander through the frozen fields,
Searching for your light.
Among the snow, a golden bloom,
A daffodil stands tall,
A symbol of the life we shared,
Through every rise and fall.

Chorus
Daffodils in the snow, where hope takes root,
A reminder of the spring that’s yet to bloom.
In the coldest days, your warmth remains,
A flower in the frost, through joy and pain.
Daffodils in the snow, a promise kept,
Of the love we hold, though tears are wept.
You’re the bloom that breaks through winter’s grip,
A daffodil in the snow, on our frozen trip.

Verse 2
Each petal holds a whisper soft,

A memory of your gentle touch,
That time cannot dissuade.
The snow may fall, the wind may howl,
But in this bloom I see,
The strength of your enduring love,
That still lives on in me.
