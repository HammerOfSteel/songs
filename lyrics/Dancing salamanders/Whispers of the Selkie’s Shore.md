# Whispers of the Selkie’s Shore

**Labels:** Dancing salamanders, Drink deeply, and dream
**Created:** 2024-09-07
**Edited:** 2024-09-07

---

Inspired by Cornish folklore, this song delves into the struggle of finding one’s identity and place in a world that feels both familiar and foreign. The narrative draws on the legend of the Selkie, weaving a tale of longing and belonging, where the search for true selfhood is mirrored in the Selkie’s dual existence between sea and land.

Verse 1
By the rugged cliffs where the wild waves cry,
The mist rolls in, and the Selkie sighs.
A figure stands on the twilight sand,
With feet in the water, heart in the land.
The ocean’s breath, a haunting sound,
Carries tales of love long drowned.
In the selkie’s gaze, they see their own,
A life between, forever alone.

Chorus
Whispers of the Selkie’s shore,
Of lives we’ve lived, of loves no more.
In the sea’s embrace, we lose our way,
Longing to find where our true selves stay.
The seal and human, both and none,
Where do I belong when the day is done?

Verse 2
Shedding skins in the moon’s pale light,
The Selkie dances on the edge of night.
In every step, a story’s told,
Of hearts that long for what they can’t hold.
The sea calls out, a mother’s plea,
“Return to me, where you’re meant to be.”
But the land holds tight, with whispers sweet,
Of human warmth and earthen heat.

Chorus
Whispers of the Selkie’s shore,
Of lives we’ve lived, of loves no more.
In the sea’s embrace, we lose our way,

The seal and human, both and none,

Bridge
In the depths of night, when the tide is low,
The Selkie’s eyes in the starlight glow.
A choice to make, a life to weave,
Between the sea’s call and land’s reprieve.
But the ocean’s song is bittersweet,
In every wave, a past to greet.

Chorus
Whispers of the Selkie’s shore,

Outro
As dawn breaks over the silver sea,
The Selkie returns, but where shall they be?
On the shore, or in the deep,
Where souls like mine forever sleep.
For in the Selkie’s tale, I see,
The endless search for where I’m free.
