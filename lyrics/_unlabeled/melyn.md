# melyn

**Created:** 2025-12-26
**Edited:** 2025-12-26

---

Tempo: Relaxed, swaying 2-step rhythm
Instrumentation: A shimmering, syncopated harp arpeggio, ukulele, and a lo-fi hip-hop beat with plenty of vinyl crackle.

Vocals: Rhythmic, male conversational British delivery; melodic and hushed during the chorus.

[Intro]

[The sound of a window opening to a quiet city night.]

[The harp begins—a delicate, repeating pattern that feels like starlight.]

[A soft, muffled kick-drum starts a steady heartbeat.]

[Verse 1]

[Delivery is a rhythmic, spoken-word flow]

Just look up at the stars, 
see how they’re glistening for you,
And every single little thing,
We've been through.

Yeah, it’s all yellow,
like the petals in the spring,
Like the way the morning light,
starts to make the kitchen sing.

Daffodil you can along,
I hum this little tune for you,
For the messy bits,
and all the things you do.

It’s all "Yellow".

It’s the flicker in the hallway,
it’s the steady kind of flame.

Took sometime, aye
Yet I stepped into the light,
And everything was golden,
everything was bright.

[Chorus 1]

[The beat opens up; vocals become melodic and layered]

And your skin, oh aye,
your skin and bones,

you know I love you so,
Oh Daffodil,
I’ll never let you go.

[Verse 2]

[More energy in the flow; sharp and direct]

I’d jump across the channel, aye,
I’d swim across the sea,
Just to have you breath here,
Here next to me.

‘Cause you’re all yellow,
you’re the gold in the gray
The best part of my day.

It’s all yellow.

Still blooming by the hedge.

Stubborn little things,
pushing through the winter dirt,
aye, one leans out from the pocket,

[Bridge]

[The beat drops out; just the harp and a warm, low cello]

And your skin, oh aye,
your skin and bones,

It’s true.

Look how they shine for you.
Look how they shine for you.

[Outro]

[The 2-step beat returns, but softer. The harp is the lead.]

Look at the stars,
look how they’re shimmering for you,
In everything we still will do.

Aye Daffodil,
blooming in the rain,
The brightest yellow,
Does my heart sustain.

[Spoken]
Just look at them.
Shining.
For you.
