# sanguine song

**Labels:** Sunny side
**Created:** 2025-12-20
**Edited:** 2025-12-20

---

Style:
indie with British garage undertones; self harm, warm, and slightly hazy. Narrative, novel-like lyricism with flowing internal rhyme, youth hearts start apart

Tempo:
Fast, syncopated UK garage beat (approx. 132 BPM).

Instrumentation:
A heavy, driving bassline, "skippy" high-hats, and a melancholic, repeating synth-bell melody. The vocals are rhythmic and dry, sitting right at the front of the mix.

[Intro]

[The sound of a keyboard typing rapidly, then the 'ping' of a sent message.]

[The garage beat kicks in instantly—sharp, urban, and restless.]

[A distorted, low-end bass pulse begins to throb.]

[Verse 1]

[Delivery is breathless and rhythmic; a fast-flowing internal rhyme]

I’m staring at the blue light,
hoping just to see you there,
In the static of the evening,
in the cold and biting air.

I couldn’t bear the speaking,
couldn’t bear to show my heart,

So I hide behind this rhythm,
try to find a place to start.

It’s a small little beginning,
just a spark inside the dark,

But we’re binary and distant,
leaves us searching for a mark.

We are so far apart,
love,
separated by the glass,
Watching all the hours,
and the minutes slowly pass.

I’ve known you for a second,
or a decade,
or a day,
Just here in this now,
Would you not just come and play.

Play those little tunes,
That settles in my veins,
That hush little sound,
Marked by all these stains.

You write poems,
My heart wants to hear.
Wants to tear through,
This screen and just..
Hold you..

[Chorus 1]
[The beat opens up—high-energy, "sunny" but with a hazy, emotional weight]

What do we believe in?
Do you believe in us?

Is there something in the hunger bitter to the bone?

When we are here together,
Are you still alone?

So star, little vampire,
won’t you sing me your song?
Two rights after all,
doesn't have to make it wrong.

Let’s hold this together,
in the middle of this night,

Neither of us is ready for the fire or the fight.
We walk upright,
Upright in the sunlight.
I know it,
In the marrow growing in my bone.
The closest I have ever felt to finally being home.

[Verse 2]

[The beat gets grittier, a bit of "self-harm" imagery in the lyrical tension]

I’ve already said "I love you," and you’ve said the same thing too,
So we're just waiting,
Waiting for all,
That things we’re meant to do.

There is a proof right here,
beyond the doubt and all the fear,
A new little heartbeat, Crystal Clear.

This is what we do, love,
We do it time again,
I've seen it one hundred times,
Then a thousand times again.

If I told you the truth,
would you really understand the cost?
Could you bear this fate with me,
No matter what we lost.

No matter,
Matter what we'd lose,
Would you still chose,
Me.


[Bridge]

[Music drops to just the bass and a flickering digital sound. The vocal becomes more cerebral.]

I’m a particle,
a photon,
Devoid of density and mass,
I came here,
Escaping through the looking glass.

Again and again,
This little recursive tree,
Live through the best,
Of you,
And of me.

Fear and fright and flight,
I knew you might,
just turn and run.

[Chorus 2]

[The music swells to its peak—maximalist garage indie. Loud and euphoric.]

What do we believe in?
Is it us?
Is it true?

I’m trading every version of myself just to get to you.

So star,
little vampire,
won’t you sing me your song?

Two rights after everything,
doesn't have to make it wrong.

Let us hold this together,
through this terrible night,

Neither of us,
Set to ignite the fight.
Let this what is,
Be alright.

[Musical Section]

[A heavy, melodic bass solo played with a pick. The tin flute from 'Harmony' makes a brief, distorted cameo, sounding like a siren in the distance. The garage beat is relentless.]

[Outro]

[The beat drops away. Just a slow, warm acoustic guitar strumming.]

I don't really know you.
Or maybe I truly do.
Maybe I'm just standing here,
Completely see-through.

Would you turn away?
Or just tighten your grip on my hand?

Fae or friend...
Where do we stand...
I'll let you know when I land.

[Whispered]
Star...
Hold me tight.

[Fade Out]
[The sound of a phone locking—a single 'click.' Silence.]
