# Indeterminate Number

**Labels:** sundry
**Created:** 2026-01-04
**Edited:** 2026-01-04

---

Check the calendar, circle the date, 
I’m five minutes early, 
so I’m ten minutes late. 

Milk on the counter and bread on the list, 
I’m counting the "Sundry" things that I’ve missed. 

Tuesday is yellow and Wednesday is blue, 
I’ve got forty-four things that I’m meant to go do. 

Categorize the way that I breathe, 
holy, heavy, holesome, heave

I’ve got homework in piles and a plan for the week, 
A logical structure for the truth that I seek.

grasping at digits and clinging to lines, 
Trying to read all the intricate signs. 
suits of sundry pride, 
I’m keeping the tally of the "static" inside. 

desperate geometry, logic of stone, 
something I take for my self and own.

numbers, shifting, 
sifhing throu in a motion of blur, 
given gold,
given insence,
given myrrh.

Category Books on how to sleep
Category B unknown integers I keep 

days where my head is wrappen up in foils of tin
blocking the noise, little creases on my skin
If I add up the minutes and divide by the day, 
will the heavy just seap away?

I’m marking the margins of a notebook of lead,
With all of the "Sundry" thoughts in my head.
The grocery list is a holy decree,
A tether to keep me from floating out to sea.

Oh, I’m grasping at digits and clinging to lines,
Trying to read all the intricate signs.
One for the sorrow and two for the pride,
I’m keeping the tally of the "static" inside.

It’s a desperate geometry, a logic of stone,
Trying to build me a house I can own.

But the numbers are shifting, 
the math is a blur,
not really sure,
anymore...
what came before.

One, two, three... 
Six, seven, nine...

Does it add up?
Does it add up?
