# LunaLamb and Her Sheep of the Silver Clouds - Song

**Labels:** childrens books - star sailor
**Created:** 2024-10-17
**Edited:** 2024-10-17

---

Verse 1:

Star Sailor drifts through skies soft and bright,
To the Silver Meadow glowing in twilight.
LunaLamb leads with a voice calm and clear,
Her flock of clouds drifting far and near.

She calls to Bryn who dances on air,
And Seren shines bright, beyond compare.
They wander through Awel’s Crossing’s breeze,
And rest beneath Lleuad’s Hollow’s trees.

Chorus:

“Gentle words guide, like winds through the night,
With kindness and care, everything turns right.
Let your heart lead, and the path will unfold,
For even the softest touch can be bold.”

“Through storms or calm skies, the clouds always know,
That patience will guide where they need to go.
Be gentle, be kind, let trust fill the air,
And soon you will see, love leads everywhere.”

Verse 2:

The flock drifts close to Rhaeadr Aur’s fall,
But Aeron floats far, not hearing the call.
Star Sailor worries, her heart full of fear,
But LunaLamb whispers, “Stay calm, my dear.”

They hum a soft tune, a lullaby light,
Calling Eira to bring him back through the night.
With patience and love, the sheep find their way,
Returning to Mynydd Glas at break of day.

Bridge:

In the quiet, Star Sailor learns what’s true,
That leading with love lets others break through.
Through LunaLamb’s eyes, she finally sees,
That guiding with grace sets hearts at ease.

Outro:

As the dawn rises, the meadow aglow,
The flock drifts onward, soft winds in tow.
LunaLamb smiles as Star Sailor departs,
Their lesson in kindness now close to her heart.

Star Sailor sails on, her light steady and clear,
Knowing that love will always steer.
And beneath the soft clouds that drift through the night,
The sheep hum their song till morning light.
