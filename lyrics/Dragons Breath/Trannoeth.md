# Trannoeth

**Labels:** Dragons Breath
**Created:** 2025-07-10
**Edited:** 2025-07-10

---

We hold,
We hug,
With loving arms.
It makes us feel warm,
It's what we all need,
When we're feeling down,
It's the best comfort around.
Sometimes we all need a big cwtch,
To let us know we're loved so much.

pretty puddles, muddy socks,
Bottle Bed time grumpy rock.
Waking little tiny steps to start,
a marmelade mumbling heart.
Eggy soldiers stood in a line,
Marched them straight to your mouth, said, “Mine.”

Gloop of honey, drip-drop song,
Said the bees hum “I love you” all day long.
Left a crumb trail up the stair,
Cute ghosts can follow if they dare.

Trannoeth, where the daisies speak,
And the puddles, which dreams to keep.
“Don’t cry, it’s just pretend - not pretend.”
Let's keep playing to the end.

Pete's pockets full of stars,
Steph's a “Busy Witch”, with a spell to untap jars.
Crayons chewed and names for rain:
“Misty-Jones” and “Slippy Jane.”

perfect pixel pirate ship,
Raisin crew and butter drip.
clouds are sheep in sleepy school,
Sky the field, the sun to rule.

Looks like the wind’s got somewhere to be,
Tied its laces on the tree.
daft diligent daffodil,
Pretty hearts in morning chill.

Trannoeth, still wearing your grin,
Sticky-fingered, jam on chin.
Hardly heard, all so still.
Brightest light, my daffodil.

We’re waiting for you, little pearl,
not that we need you to rush
take your time as the arms to catch you
will cwtch forever

Your mama has laid layer and layer
of love on you,
egg-shell cautious love

Just be little pearl
come to us little girl

As the basking warmth of the sun
Comes cascading through the blinds
It finds itself cast on still, rested souls
Serene and calm, no rest disturbed

Cwtch, a word from a wondrous place
An intimate moment, two’s safest space
To hold, and be held
Seldom seen, but always shared

Now she wakes and opens her eyes
A greyish blue
With a sparkling hue
They look back into mine
Transfixed, I smile
I say something nice,
"Morning daffodil"
Holden true, love too much
I pull you into a cwtch.

Trannoeth, where your giggles hide,
In mismatched shoes and countryside.
If time forgets to tell me when,
I’ll be there inside your “nows and thens.”

At some point I must go
Another day
Another time
We’ll be back there again
Cwtching till the light
Comes back through the blinds
