# The Dew of Dawn

**Labels:** Dancing salamanders, Risca
**Created:** 2024-09-04
**Edited:** 2024-09-04

---

Verse 1
In the hush of morning, the world awakes,
Every droplet glistens, as the night breaks.
Mewn pob defnyn (In every drop), your love is clear,
Lingering softly, ever near.
Här i gryningen (Here in the dawn), I feel you close,
In every shimmer, in every rose.

Chorus
The dew of dawn, a kiss from the night,
Whispers of love in the morning light.
Renewed by the sun, a brand new day,
Carrying your warmth in every ray.
Though darkness fades, your light stays true,
In every drop, I find you.

Verse 2
The cabin hums with a gentle peace,
As the night’s memories quietly cease.
Every blade of grass, every leaf,
Holds your touch, beyond belief.
Daggens pärlor (The pearls of dew), so pure, so bright,
Hold the echoes of your light.

Bridge
As the world awakens, I stand in awe,
Of the beauty in the simplest law.
Each new dawn brings your grace,
Här i morgonljuset (Here in the morning light), I see your face.
The dew that clings, so fragile, so pure,
Is the love that will always endure.

Outro
So, as the day begins anew,
I’ll walk this path, always with you.
In every droplet, in every dawn,
Your love, my light, will carry on.
