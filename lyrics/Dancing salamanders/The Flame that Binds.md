**Edited:** 2024-09-23

Style: Acoustic guitar with delicate string picking, interwoven with somber violin melodies. The song reflects the depth of struggle, personal growth, and the blossoming love born in the darkest of trials.

Narrative Explanation:

The song follows the protagonist and their partner after they fall through a trap within the Phoenix Spire in Edria, away from their group. They are tested by Melkyr, the Visage of Resilience, and must face a dark night of the soul—a test of despair and loss. As they endure the seemingly endless trials, they grow closer, finding strength in each other. This shared suffering forges a deep love between them, their bond becoming the light that guides them back from the depths. Their combined attunements—fire and resilience—symbolize their growth as individuals and as a pair.
