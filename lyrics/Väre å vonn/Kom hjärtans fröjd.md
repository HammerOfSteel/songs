# Kom hjärtans fröjd

**Labels:** Väre å vonn
**Created:** 2025-05-29
**Edited:** 2025-05-29

---

Buskar bär bland blomster går,
Plocka bär där vinden rår.
Krans i håret, sommarvind,
stickad stolt så grand och trind

dub me to summer with stick as sword,
Midsummer knighted, like you were the lord.

No swearing says mum! — but you swore at the sun,
“Papa, it’s hot, but summer’s real fun.”

You danced in meadow, little big
This is the bees “flower wiggle jig.”

Flowers of sun, bells of the blue
Rainbows and puddles and hugs from you.

I laughed so hard I near forgot,
That grownups ain’t always supposed to talk a lot.

Tre dagar sol utan regn på sin höjd
Kom nu lilla hjärtans fröjd!
Vill du mig någe’? 
Du må va’ snäääll!
En midsommarnatts, en midsommar kväll.

Kom liljor å… a-quil… le-ja!
Kom rosor å salva… via!
Kom ljuva krusmynta, 
kom hjärtans fröööjd!

You asked if bumblebees go to school,
Rain like drums, cold and cool

You made a potion of mud and joy,
“Drink this, Papa — tastes like oboy!”

We walked past the lake,
Sat down for squash and toast
You said, “I think, this is what I love the most”
You picked every flower like they owed you rent,
And I followed behind, not knowing where time went.

Fagra små blommor, de dansar så bra!
Och alla söta myggor de vill jag också ha.

Kom nu lilla hjärtans fröjd!
En dag för kort, hjärtat förnöjd

Kom liljor å aquileja,
Kom rosor å salvia,
Kom ljuva krusmynta, 
kom hjärtans fröjd!

"Papa... do flowers fall in love?"

"Only the lucky ones."

"You’re silly."

"You’re brilliant."

"I know."

Now it’s quiet, but your echoes stay,
Carved in stone, won’t wash away.
The reef you made’s still on my shelf,
Wilted, maybe — just like myself.

I see your grin in the wild blue sea,
hear your heartbeat inside of me.

And if anyone asks what summer is like —
Like you little star, warm and bright.

Kransen den sätter i ditt hår…
Solen sakta över himmelen går

Kom lilla hjärtans fröjd…
Kom liljor å aquileja,
Kom rosor å salvia,
Kom ljuva krusmynta, 
kom hjärtans fröjd…

“Goodnight, Papa.”

Gömda blad i ljustens vrå,
Sov nu hjärtat,
Under alla stjärnor små.
