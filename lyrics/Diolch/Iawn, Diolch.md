A tiny little drama I must beat.

It’s just a pattern, a verbal little trap,
So I quickly put my pleasant face,
check off the map.

aye, this is past the surface to my soul.

About ten seconds before the whole thing starts to show,
So I give them the answer that they know.
I keep the smile glued onto my face,
perfect little safe simple place.

No need for any falling out of this need to expand,
Just walk on with what it shall demand.
