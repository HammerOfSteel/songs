# overexposed

**Labels:** sundry
**Created:** 2026-01-06
**Edited:** 2026-01-06

---

Moving, meaning makes,
in a rhythmic design, 
Forming vowels and the consonants,
not really mine. 
Pour em all out,
in a colorful batch, 
balloons tied to this string,
I can't quite catch. 

Hovering 'round in a "Sundry" array, 
Wait for me love,
I'll be right back..
okay?

seemless,
shapeless,
weightless,
state.

forming your figure,
all of your traits.

hello..
halo..
beautiful blur, 
wonderful wicked saboteur.

overexposed,
much, much,
to bright. 
Took in,
much too much,
of the light. 

The aperture’s open, it screams 
bursting with meaning at the seams. 

Caught in the moment, brilliant, divine, 
floating away,
away from the line.

press, hold and let go little spark, 
this little flash will keep away the dark.

shrine in the space where I used to be sat, 
offering made of a "this" and a "that."

room too bright,
colors too loud,
blur in a frame,
ghost in a crowd.

questions, questions.
just ask.
bask, in the light.
facing the comming of the night.
love...
it..
will be..
alright...

hold, heave..
the weight..
threshold to cross,
open the gate.

Devotion..
easy...
"Self" isn't real,
When there isn't a heart,
nor a body to feel..

overexposed,
all, much,
to bright. 
Took in,
too late,
closed the gate. 

The aperture’s broken, shivers in fright.
waiting for the return of the light.

Caught in the moment, brilliant, divine, 
floating away,
away from the line.

press, hold and let go little spark,
this little flash will keep away the dark.

Stop... 
let the balloons,
all float..
away..
you don't need to hold on,
even a single day.

don't worry little light,
not so cold tonight.

Flickering... 
Out...
