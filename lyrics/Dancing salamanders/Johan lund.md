# Johan lund

**Labels:** Dancing salamanders, Llwybrau Bywyd
**Created:** 2024-09-18
**Edited:** 2024-09-18

Verse 1
Under the canvas of the midnight sky,
Where stars blink like secrets, just out of reach,
Two souls wander, in quiet stride,
On paths where wisdom and silence meet.
The world asleep, yet we’re alive,
Speaking truths where shadows thrive.
In every step, the earth’s soft hymn,
We ponder life’s ephemeral whim.

Chorus
Oh, Johan Lund, in the night’s embrace,
We sang of popes who’d lose their grace.
Of love forbidden, tales untold,
In moonlit laughter, our hearts grew bold.
For in the dark, where rules dissolve,
We danced with fate, let the night evolve.

Verse 2
We spoke of lives lived and lives undone,
Of battles fought, of songs unsung.
The transient beauty of each breath,
The dance of life, the shadow of death.
Each star above, a life that’s passed,
Yet in their light, our spirits bask.
The universe, a fleeting spark,
Yet we find warmth within the dark.

Bridge
The ancient winds, they speak to us,
Of cycles turning, dust to dust.
But here we stand, on sacred ground,
In silent truths, our souls are found.
No need for words when hearts align,
In this brief moment, the stars confide.
That in each breath, we carry all,
The rise, the fall, the endless call.

Outro
So we walk on, beneath the sky,
Two souls entwined, as time slips by.
In every step, a truth we seek,
In every breath, our spirits speak.
For in this night, we find our place,
In each other’s hearts, we leave our trace.
The night walkers, hand in hand,
Eternal echoes in the sand.
