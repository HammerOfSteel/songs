# magpie logging

**Labels:** glitchwitch
**Created:** 2025-09-22
**Edited:** 2025-09-22

---

Two black coats on the fence in a row
click click code in the shine they stole
tidy the timeline beak by beak
they log our mornings cheek to cheek

Clack on the rail like a dotted tick
note every crumb every clink every nick
flip of a feather writes date and time
audit in echo and rhyme on rhyme

Clack if it’s clear, clack clack if it’s late
one clean click for an open gate
feather prints scatter like a little parade
log the love we almost said

Click for a start, click click for a wait
three soft taps for a turned back plate
tie it with wire where the shadows braid
save the smile before it fades

They pinch a ringlet of foil and light
stash it as proof that the dawn was right
save to a cache in the hazel shade
checksum glimmers in the twig arcade

Page of the day with a glossy sheen
pushed to the cloud of the hedgerow green
branch on a breeze where the records wait
fence as a ledger for a saved state

When rain writes static across the lane
they buffer the thunder and trim the rain
tell me the line where the light comes through
tick it with beaks in a bright tattoo

When dusk gets heavy and clocks feel slow
they file the hush in a steady row
sidelong shimmer in the paling slate
signed with a glint at the garden gate

O hey bright thieves of the silver thread
leave me a log for the loaf and the shed
note my laugh on the sill by noon
tag my tea with a tin-cap rune

O keep our secrets but keep our truth
pin them in pairs on the paling smooth
when the hour trips twice and drifts a beat
write it in clicks with your clever feet

Clack for the promise we nearly spoke
click for the gentle unknotted yoke
two bright thieves with a book of sky
write our yes where the breezes fly

Diff all the hours with a beady eye
merge our mistakes where the fence lines lie
roll back worry when the sky goes slate
restore the warmth to a golden state

Log our living

Line by line

Pin our giving

Shine to shine

Hold our home in a fence-top slate

Saved by the click of a magpie state
