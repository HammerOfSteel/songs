# Garden of Stars

**Labels:** Tides In Memories Arbor V2
**Created:** 2025-10-11
**Edited:** 2025-10-11

---

acoustic folk, celestial ballad, chamber pop, ambient indie, ethereal storytelling, cinematic warmth, slow waltz rhythm, brushed percussion, finger-picked guitar, upright bass, soft piano, atmospheric strings, choral harmonies, wind-chime ambience, lo-fi intimacy, poetic lyricism, alchemical symbolism, lullaby undertones, tender nostalgia, hearth-light tone

[Verse 1]
[footsteps on gravel]
In the last blue sip of evening light,
Where hedges breathe and shadows grow,
I prune the rose we trained by hand,
Along the fence we sowed.
Each petal keeps a laughing spark,
Each thorn a quiet vow,
Blackened soil beneath my knees,
I’m faithful to it now.

[Pre-Chorus]
[kettle click in the distance]
The world turns slow as prayer and spade,
And breath becomes my guide;
I name the stars like garden beds,
You glow on every side.

[Chorus]
In the garden of stars where your dreaming stays,
You’re the lamp of love at the end of days.
You’re the bloom that time can’t take apart,
The north that stitches up my heart.
In the garden of stars where the heavens meet,
I hear your voice in the night so sweet.
You’re the light that shines bright from afar,
In the garden of stars, that’s where you are.

[Verse 2]
[soft inhale]
The lilies wake in albedo white,
Their incense warms the air;
I feel your small hand fold in mine,
A memory said in prayer.
I tie the stakes with twine we saved,
Moon silver on the leaves,
Rubedo blush within the rose,
Citrine along the eaves.

[Pre-Chorus]
[wind through chimes]
If time’s a wheel and love the hub,
Then loss is turning’s art,
Your laughter moves the constellations,
And steadies my old heart.

[Chorus]
In the garden of stars where your dreaming stays,
You’re the lamp of love at the end of days.
You’re the bloom that time can’t take apart,
The north that stitches up my heart.
In the garden of stars where the heavens meet,
I hear your voice in the night so sweet.
You’re the light that shines through from afar,
In the garden of stars, that’s where you are.

[Bridge]
[whisper]
I trace the chalk of Orion’s bones,
A trellis drawn in light;
Retort of sky, the heart’s alembic,
Distilling grief to bright.
If sorrow is the crucible,
Then mercy is the flame,
Your star writes home across the dark,
And answers to your name.

[Breakdown – hush]
[pages turn / a small laugh far away]
I set one lantern by the gate,
And let the night be kind;
I breathe you in, one living thread,
The stitch that mends my mind.

[Final Chorus]
In the garden of stars where your dreaming stays,
You’re the lamp of love at the end of days.
You’re the bloom that time can’t take apart,
The north that stitches up my heart.
In the garden of stars where the heavens meet,
I hear your voice in the night so sweet.
You’re the light that shines through from afar,
In the garden of stars, that’s who you are.

[Outro]
[match strike, candle warm]
So I’ll tend this little acre of sky,
Through midnight into morn;
When constellations touch the hedge,
We’ll walk the path once worn.
In the garden of stars, hand in hand, ever,

[Childs voice]
[soft laugh]
“Look, Papa, flowers of forever.”
