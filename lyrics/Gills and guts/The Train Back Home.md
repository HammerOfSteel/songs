# The Train Back Home

**Labels:** Dancing salamanders, Gills and guts
**Created:** 2024-09-20
**Edited:** 2024-09-20

---

Place: Land’s End

Verse 1
At the edge of the world, where the sea meets the sky,
I stand in awe, watching time fly by.
The cliffs rise high, the waves roll in,
Carrying whispers of where we’ve been.
Once we walked, side by side,
Now I cherish those moments, deep inside.
The wind it sings, the sea it calls,
And through it all, I feel it all.

Pre-Chorus
The tracks lead on, through hill and dale,
But every step holds a sacred tale.
Beyond the cliffs, beyond the shore,
Your love guides me forevermore.

Chorus
On the train back home, I carry the light,
Through valleys and mountains, through day and night.
The moments we shared, they shine like stars,
Guiding me onward, no matter how far.
On the train back home, with a heart full of grace,
I carry your love, in every place.

Verse 2
The waves crash soft against the land,
Reminding me of every hand-in-hand.
Your voice still dances in the breeze,
Like a melody carried by the trees.
The tracks ahead stretch long and wide,
In the home of my soul, our love cannot hide.
The time we’ve spent, the lessons learned,
In my soul, they are forever burned.

Pre-Chorus
As the train rolls down the line,
I find your heart still intertwined.
In every corner, in every way,
Your love still guides me, come what may.

Chorus
On the train back home, I carry the light,
Through valleys and mountains, through day and night.
The moments we shared, they shine like stars,
Guiding me onward, no matter how far.
On the train back home, with a heart full of grace,
I carry your love, in every place.

Bridge
In every mile, in every bend,
I feel the love that will never end.
For time and distance cannot erase,
The light you’ve left, the love, the grace.

Final Chorus
On the train back home, I ride with peace,
Knowing the love will never cease.
Though the journey continues, the road goes on,
Your light within me will always dawn.
On the train back home, through all I’ve known,
I carry you with me, I’m never alone.

Outro
At Land’s End, where the cliffs touch the sky,
I thank the stars for you and I.
The waves still crash, the cliffs still stand,
But love will guide us, hand in hand.
