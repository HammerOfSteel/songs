# Ar Lan y Mor

**Labels:** Dancing salamanders, Ar Lan y Mor
**Created:** 2024-09-17
**Edited:** 2024-09-17

---

"Ar Lan y Môr" is a deeply reflective journey through the Welsh coastline, where a grieving father walks under the starry sky, seeking solace in the memories of his lost daughter. As he communes with her spirit in the moonlight, the song weaves a narrative of love, loss, and the enduring connection between them, offering a sense of peace and hope as the dawn breaks, symbolizing a new beginning.

Verse 1
On the shores where the whispers meet the tide,
Underneath a sky where the stars confide,
I wander alone, where we once walked,
In the night’s embrace, where the spirits talked.

The sea sings low, a lullaby of old,
Its rhythm echoes, a story yet untold.
And in the moon’s soft silver light,
I feel your presence, warm and bright.

Chorus 1
Fy Seren Forwr, my little sun,
Through the veil, we are still as one.
In the silent night, your voice I hear,
Whispering softly, you are near.

Verse 2
The waves caress the sands so pure,
A place where love and loss endure.
I close my eyes, and there you stand,
A gentle touch, a guiding hand.

Lleuad's light, a beacon in the dark,
Illuminates the path, a sacred mark.
We speak in silence, words so clear,
Your voice, in Welsh, draws me near.

Chorus 2 (Conversation)
Her:
"Byddaf gyda thi bob amser, Dad,"
I hear your words, so soft and sad.
"Mam a Mamgu yn disgwyl amdanaf,"
But in your heart, I’ll always stand.

Him:
"Rydw i’n dy garu di am byth, fy seren,"
With every breath, you are my prayer.
"Yn fy nghalon, byddi di'n aros,"
Forever here, through every tear.

Verse 3
As the first light kisses the sea,
I rise with a heart more light, more free.
For I know in every dawn’s embrace,
I carry you with me, your light, your grace.

The path ahead, once shrouded in night,
Now gleams with your love, a guiding light.
And as I walk this life’s long shore,
I know we’ll meet again, once more.

Chorus 3 (Conversation)
Him:
"Rydw i’n dy gario di ym mhob golau dydd,"
In every dawn, I find you here.
"Byddwn ni'n cyfarfod eto, fy seren,"
In the light, where love conquers fear.

Her:
"Rydw i gyda ti bob cam o’r ffordd,"
In every step, I walk with thee.
"Byddi di’n fy nghalon am byth,"
Forever in my heart, you’ll be.

Outro
In every wave, in every star,
You are with me, never far.
Mae'n newydd ddydd, a new day begins,
With you in my heart, where love never ends.
