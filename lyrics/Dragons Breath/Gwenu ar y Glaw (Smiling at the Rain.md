# Gwenu ar y Glaw (Smiling at the Rain

**Labels:** Dragons Breath
**Created:** 2025-07-18
**Edited:** 2025-07-18

---

Plask, mask, fjäril, glas
Solen gömmer sig, underbar grimas
Dröm, göm, vän, hem
Dansar i regnet, jag ler igen

Bucket on the doorstep, socks soaked through
She’s stompin’ in puddles like she’s got a clue
Rain hits her fringe, hoodie way too big
She spins, says “Papa, I’m a fish! Do a wiggle and jig”

No punchline needed, this right here’s the joke
A life so small, but my god it spoke
Through boots full of splashes, thunder in play
The rainbow I see when it’s all gone grey

Drip, drop, splash, sway
We smile at the rain, come what may
Skip, hop, giggle, sing
The sky don't hold back a single thing

brittle butterfly, dance, string and jab
storm-footed light, hands open to grab
A second of time, like a coin in a jar
Moments don’t last, but they carry you far

Asked once if the clouds feel pain
They let it fall, to paint the stain
Nods like she gets it, dance ain’t done
“Don’t cry too hard, Mr. Rain, we’re just havin’ fun!”

Plip, plap, tip, tap
Rain’s just rhythm for feet that clap
Drop, dribble, hum, sway
laughs with the storm, all in a day

Brused and batters, sung and said
Aye such peace in me cluttered head
There she goes, breakin’ old lore
Aye she’s been here before, and wants more

No camera, verse could wrap this sight
Trailing rain, stitched in light
You’d think joy comes from sun or spring
It blooms from hand to hold, a heart to cling.

Pat, pat, fall, flow
Every storm brings room to grow
Tread, twirl, rain, shine
sweet daffodil, still mine.

Rain on the clothesline, shirts doin’ a waltz
She hums to the sky like it’s all her fault
“Maybe I dreamt this,” she grins with a skip,
“Maybe the clouds just needed a drip.”

Steam on the window, fog on the breath
Her fingers draw hearts like she’s spellin’ out depth
No grand truth, just the puddles we cross
But we’re here, and we’re dancing, that’s gain, stones to toss.

Swish, swash, spin, sway
Hearts don’t break when we play this way
Blip, bop, bounce, beam
Love rides the drops in every stream

We sang to the bins, we blessed the drain
Ordained every puddle in her silly rain reign
Swore the snails had secrets, gave the worms a cheer
Her kingdom of damp, and I’m happy here

Aye, and if grief’s a guest, let it pour some tea
We'll toast the drizzle, both wild and free
Not all’s fixed, and that’s alright
Some things shine best in the middle of night

Flick, flash, drop, glide
You and the rain, side by side
Tap, tiptoe, twirl, grin
The world comes back, and lets you in

Cwtsh, gwlyb, cannwyll, llygad
Glaw yn dawnsio, dim ots am y gwadd
Un, dau, tri, pedwar, tro
Rwy’n gwenu ar y glaw, fy nghariad fach o’r fro
