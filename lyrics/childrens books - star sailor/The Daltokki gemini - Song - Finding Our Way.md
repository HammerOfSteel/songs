# The Daltokki gemini - Song - Finding Our Way

Song Title: “Finding Our Way”
(A Lullaby for the Daltokki Family)

Intro:
On a night full of stars, in the forest of leaves,
Two little Daltokkis wandered through the trees.
With a wish in their hearts and the shadows so deep,
They wandered so far, they forgot how to sleep.

Verse 1:
In the Moon’s silver forest, where the shadows lay deep,
Two little Daltokkis wandered without sleep.
“Just one little wish,” the young brother cried,
But the dark closed in, and the sister sighed.

With a light so gentle, Star Sailor appeared,
Her glow pushed back every shadow they feared.
“Take my hand,” she whispered, “I’ll show you the way,
Through the dark, little ones, till the break of day.”

Chorus:
“Trust in each other, for courage will grow,
When you walk side by side through the paths you don’t know.”
“Hold tight to your hope, let love be your guide,
And together, dear ones, there’s no need to hide.”

Verse 2:
Through thorny mazes and tangled roots,
The siblings found courage, their bond taking root.
The sister stepped forward, her voice clear and true,
And the brother followed, their trust shining through.

With Star Sailor guiding, they pushed through the night,
Till home’s soft glow filled their hearts with light.
“Even the smallest stars can show us the way,
And guide those who wander to the light of day.”

Bridge 1:
“There are shadows that whisper, and doubts that will creep,
But you’re never alone when your hearts make the leap.”
“So take each step bravely, with faith in your stride,
And remember that love walks right by your side.”

Chorus (Reprise):
“Trust in each other, for courage will grow,
When you walk side by side through the paths you don’t know.”
“Hold tight to your hope, let love be your guide,
And together, dear ones, there’s no need to hide.”

Bridge 2:
“For even in darkness, there’s light in a friend,
And the road that is tangled can still reach its end.”
“With each step you take, and each hand that you hold,
You’ll find that your story is brighter than gold.”

Final Chorus:
“Trust in each other, for courage will grow,
When you walk side by side through the paths you don’t know.”
“Hold tight to your hope, let love be your guide,
And together, dear ones, there’s no need to hide.”

Outro:
The Moon’s silver forest is still and bright,
With the Daltokki family safe for the night.
And up in the sky, Star Sailor’s light,
Shines down to guide those lost in the night.

Song Title: “길을 찾아서”
(달토끼 가족을 위한 자장가)

Intro:
별이 가득한 밤, 은빛 숲 사이,
두 작은 달토끼가 길을 떠났네.
소원 하나 품고 어둠 속을 걸어,
멀리 떠나서 잠을 잊었네.

Verse 1:
달빛 은빛 숲 속, 그림자 깊은 곳,
두 작은 달토끼, 잠들지 못했네.
“소원 하나 빌자,” 동생이 말했지만,
어둠이 다가와, 누나는 한숨 쉬었네.

부드러운 빛과 함께, 별빛 소녀가 와,
모든 두려운 그림자 물리쳤네.
“내 손 잡아,” 속삭였네, “길을 찾도록,
어둠 속에서도, 빛이 너희를 비출 거야.”

Chorus:
“서로를 믿어야, 용기가 자라,
낯선 길을 가도, 함께라면 괜찮아.”
“희망을 꼭 잡고, 사랑을 의지해,
함께라면 두려워할 필요 없지.”

Verse 2:
가시 덤불 사이, 얽힌 뿌리 지나,
둘은 용기를 찾고, 서로를 믿었네.
누나는 먼저 나서서, 목소리는 뚜렷하고,
동생은 따라가며, 신뢰가 빛났네.

별빛 소녀가 길을 비추며 나아가,
집의 불빛이 둘의 가슴을 채웠네.
“작은 별빛도 길을 보여줄 수 있어,
방황하던 길도 결국 밝아지네.”

Bridge 1:
“어둠 속 속삭이는, 의심이 올 때,
함께 걷는다면 두려움은 멀리 사라져.”
“한 걸음씩 용감하게, 신뢰와 함께,
사랑이 곁에 있음을 기억해야 해.”

Chorus (Reprise):
“서로를 믿어야, 용기가 자라,
낯선 길을 가도, 함께라면 괜찮아.”
“희망을 꼭 잡고, 사랑을 의지해,
함께라면 두려워할 필요 없지.”

Bridge 2:
“어둠 속에서도, 친구가 있으면,
복잡한 길도 결국엔 끝에 다다를 거야.”
“한 걸음씩 나아가, 손을 꼭 잡으면,
너의 이야기는 밝게 빛날 거야.”

Final Chorus:
“서로를 믿어야, 용기가 자라,
낯선 길을 가도, 함께라면 괜찮아.”
“희망을 꼭 잡고, 사랑을 의지해,
함께라면 두려워할 필요 없지.”

Outro:
달빛 은빛 숲은 고요하고 밝아,
달토끼 가족은 밤 속에서 평안하네.
하늘 높은 곳, 별빛 소녀의 빛이,
길을 잃은 이들에게 빛을 비추네.
