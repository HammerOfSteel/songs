# Mindful Meddling - song

**Labels:** Geddon Bird, Dancing salamanders
**Created:** 2024-10-23
**Edited:** 2024-10-23

---

(Verse 1)
Strange little whispers ride the sea breeze,
A key that was lost lands back at our feet.
Streetlights flicker in a rhythm unknown,
And life keeps nudging where it doesn’t belong.

Lucas laughs at the stars in the night,
He says, “It’s all patterns, hidden in sight.”
I roll my eyes but take one more glance,
Maybe there’s meaning behind every chance.

(Chorus)
Oh, life’s got a hand in the strings that it’s pulling,
Tugging and twisting when we aren’t looking.
Chaos, the teacher, with lessons disguised,
In meddlesome moments, we learn to be wise.

(Verse 2)
We walk through the town, the tide at its low,
Tracing old maps we don’t even know.
The clock strikes twelve, the seagulls sing,
And Lucas says, “See? It’s a curious thing.”

A missed bus leads us to strangers and songs,
Each little misstep where we belong.
I sigh, but his grin pulls me along,
Maybe this meddling's been right all along.

(Chorus)
Oh, life’s got a hand in the strings that it’s pulling,
Tugging and twisting when we aren’t looking.
Chaos, the teacher, with lessons disguised,
In meddlesome moments, we learn to be wise.

(Bridge)
In every wrong turn, there’s a story that waits,
In the tumble of plans, we discover our fates.
Not every answer is neat and refined,
Sometimes it’s chaos that sharpens the mind.

(Chorus)
Oh, life’s got a hand in the strings that it’s pulling,
Tugging and twisting when we aren’t looking.
Chaos, the teacher, with lessons disguised,
In meddlesome moments, we learn to be wise.

(Outro)
So here we stand, the night fading slow,
A lesson or two from the winds that blow.
Lucas smiles, “It’s not always clear,”
But meddling moments brought us right here.
