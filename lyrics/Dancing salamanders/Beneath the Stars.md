# Beneath the Stars

**Labels:** A Spiral Path, Dancing salamanders
**Created:** 2024-09-27
**Edited:** 2024-09-27

---

(Verse 1)
Beneath the stars, I stand so still,
The night so vast, yet I feel the thrill.
A whisper in the wind, a voice so near,
It calls to me, soft and clear.
My heart, once dark, begins to glow,
In her presence, I finally know.
Though she’s gone, she’s not far away,
Her light will guide me, show me the way.

(Pre-Chorus)
Through the silence, I hear her plea,
"Don’t seek revenge, come back to me."

(Chorus)
Beneath the stars, she whispers my name,
A love beyond death, never the same.
She asks me to live, to carry her light,
And in that moment, I let go of the fight.

(Verse 2)
The winds, they carry her gentle song,
In this stillness, I’ve searched for so long.
Her voice a melody, soothing the pain,
A reminder that love still remains.
I thought I’d lost her, but here she stays,
In the stars, in the wind, in all the days.
The path of revenge seems so far behind,
Her memory, now the peace I find.

(Pre-Chorus)
Through the silence, I hear her plea,
"Let go of the past, come back to me."

(Chorus)
Beneath the stars, she whispers my name,
A love beyond death, never the same.
She asks me to live, to carry her light,
And in that moment, I let go of the fight.

(Bridge)
I close my eyes and feel her near,
In the gentle breeze, she wipes my tear.
Her spirit wraps around my soul,
In her warmth, I am whole.
Though vengeance called, I’ll heed her grace,
I’ll live for her, and find my place.

(Chorus)
Beneath the stars, she whispers my name,
A love beyond death, never the same.
She asks me to live, to carry her light,
And in that moment, I let go of the fight.

(Outro)
Beneath the stars, I know she’s here,
A love beyond death, forever clear.
In her memory, I will rise,
Guided by the stars, through endless skies.
