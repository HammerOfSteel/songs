# glitchwitch

**Labels:** glitchwitch, Album
**Created:** 2025-09-21
**Edited:** 2025-09-26

---

Salt and star,
thread and flame,
time may stutter,
love stays the same.

a hearth-witch tends the “parameters” of a small world. bread rises, seasons tick, the kettle sings—each ritual is both spell and system call. glitches appear (a bird repeating its flight, a shadow that lags), but the cure is kindness, pattern, and play. the album blends everyday tenderness with philosophical wonder: maybe we’re simulated, maybe we’re storied—either way, love is the runtime.

narrative arc (3 acts)

act i — setup: “house as cosmos”
we meet the cottage (our control room), the tools (kettle, chalk, thread), and the rules (hospitality, reciprocity, small offerings). everything feels stable, handmade.

act ii — seams & glitches
odd loops, time hiccups, déjà vu. silly meets sacred: the magpies “log” events, the sourdough “remembers.” questions arise without answering them outright.

act iii — consent to the pattern
we don’t “solve” the sim; we befriend it. acceptance through care, humor, and craft. the homely ritual is the metaphysic.

track concepts (titles • mini-concept • style tags)

1. Hearthglitch — the first stutter in the wallpaper of reality; the kettle whistles twice at once. we smile and set two cups anyway.
acoustic folk, lo-fi textures, brushed kit, tape flutter, female lead, whispered harmonies


2. Bless This Mess (and All Its Variables) — house blessing as patch file: naming each sticky note, spoon, and shoe as if they’re sacred constants.
alt-folk, hand percussion, room mics, hummed choir, subtle drones


3. Magpie Logging — birds on the fence keep the audit trail; each shiny theft a saved state. call-and-response with clacks and clicks.
indie pop, woodblocks, clacks, field recordings, playful vocals


4. Lintel Chalk, Lintel Code — chalk sigils = if/then wards; thresholds as APIs for luck, rest, return.
minimal harp, bodhrán pulse, spoken-song cadence, airy pads


5. Leaven, Rise — sourdough starter as memory engine; it “learns” us. rising as a soft metaphysical proof, alchemical cycle of refinement - heart knod, raise and rise, slow wait and wonder, bake asunder quintessential compilation, gestation to a seen saltation.
warm piano, upright bass, nylon guitar, breathy hh, intimate vox


6. Kettle Logic — steam divination; we read patterns in fog on the pane and choose mercy.
trip-hop brushes, glassy keys, sampled kettle, soft glitch fx


7. Moss Matrix — the simulation prefers slow algorithms; patience wins. moss and lichen as the real rendering pipeline. Yielding to the simulation or where are the cheatcode to reload, reload that saved state if its not too late and rewrote fate, or.. do we simply learn to live and love again
ambient folk, sustained strings, field forest, slow 3/4 sway


8. Patch Day at the Cottage — laundry, mending, software updates, sweeping the stoop; the sacred rhythm of upkeep. Special days, anniversaries, equinoxes, holidays for remembrance
jangly indie, claps, toy piano, light whistle, upbeat groove


9. Half hymn whole hashmap — kitchen logic as cosmos logic, correctly storing data - how to hold it all / how to help yourself.
folk-rnb blend, gentle 808, harp arpeggios, close harmonies


10. Kindly Demons of the Pantry — silly guardian sprites who hide the nutmeg until you say please and beg (because it's two factor authentication after all). comic reverence.
skiffle swing, upright bass, kazoo-ish reed, foot taps, smiles


11. Breadcrumb Cosmology — crumbs as constellations, design pattern of destiny. Knowing ones self getting to know one self or multiple selfs, the fates weave and weave but can you know their design pattern (is it a big monolithic story, is it a sea of microservices etc..)
chamber folk, pizz strings, bell chimes, stereo whispers


12. A Small Prayer for Error Handling — when hearts throw exceptions, we wrap them in try/again, do you recursively regret that you weren't more cautious, or do you sacredly guard your clauses (guard clauses etc), who listene to the error output (STDERR) or where do is go? Does an Artificial intelligence refine the structure of your hearts code or does it write into scripture like lore.
solo voice, Welsh harp, reel like and mid beat wild


13. meandering migration - bird come back in spring, I write myself out from my winter hibernation and into spring, I remember you daffodil still.

Ukulele and silver flute sweet and fun



recurring symbols & how to use them

kettle: oracle / timer. (short whistle motif threads the album)

chalk: boundary & blessing. (tiny shaker mimics chalk scratch)

thread: continuity, repair. (bowed-string glissandi = thread pull)

moss/leaven: slow intelligence. (very slow crescendos)

magpies: observability, logs. (percussive clacks, stereo pans)

pantry demons: comic relief; nudge toward humility. (toy instruments)


sonic palette (grounded, warm, handmade)

Welsh harp, nylon guitar, zither, harmonium, bodhrán, brushed kit, upright bass, toy piano, spoons and teacup clinks, tape flutter, window-rain, magpie calls, kettle steam, room creaks.
Vocals: clean, raw, mostly female lead; whispered doubles; small hummed choir. No heavy tuning; intimacy over polish.

structural glue

micro-motif: a three-note whistle (kettle) appears in multiple songs in new guises.

lyric device: gentle “if / then / else” phrasing in ordinary language (“if the loaf falls, then we share jam anyway”).

patch-notes interludes: 10–20s voice memos titled v1.0.3—Swept the stoop, v1.0.4—Fed the starter between tracks.

language sprinkles: a little Welsh (“trugarha,” “hiraeth”) and Swedish (“lagom,” “fika”) used sparingly as texture.


two tiny lyric seeds (to set tone)

Hearthglitch
If the sky stutters at three seventeen, don’t panic, love—
we’ve biscuits in the blue tin, blankets by the stove.
If time double-clicks, we’ll pour two cups of steam,
name one for grief, one mercy, and drink until they’re one.

Leaven, Rise
Starter in a jar, an old soul breathing,
keeps our days from sinking when they’re leaving.
Fold, turn, rest—our patient creed,
love proves warm at baking speed.

visual world (for cover & stage)

cover: a wooden table at dusk; chalk circle around a jar of starter; a mossed stone, a pair of magpie feathers, steam sketching a faint sigil in the window.

typography: handwritten/calligraphed track titles like chalk on slate; Celtic-knot accents as circuit-trace patterns.

stage: lamp-lit “kitchen” set—kettle on a safe fogger, clothesline of paper “patch notes,” small table mic’d for tea-cup percussion.
