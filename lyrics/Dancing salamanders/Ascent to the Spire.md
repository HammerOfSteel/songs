# Ascent to the Spire

**Labels:** Dancing salamanders
**Created:** 2024-08-20
**Edited:** 2024-08-20

---

Verse 1
At the foot of the Spire, where shadows grow tall,
We start our ascent, heeding the call.
The path is steep, the winds they moan,
But with Ember’s light, we are not alone.

"Sabr," whispers the wind, in the cool night air,
Patience guides us as we climb with care.
Each step is heavy, yet our spirits rise,
The fire within keeps the flame alive.

Chorus
To the summit we reach, where the Spire awaits,
Through trials we climb, through the shifting fates.
With each breath and each step, the world fades away,
"Shukran," we say, for the light of the day.

The Spire calls, like a star in the night,
Guiding our journey, where shadows and light.
With Ember by my side, and the wisdom’s grace,
We climb the heights with unwavering pace.

Verse 2
The path winds and bends, through stone and mist,
Yet the way is clear where hope persists.
The stars above in the sky’s deep blue,
Watch over us as we pass through.

"Hayat," I whisper, as the climb grows steep,
Life is the gift that we strive to keep.
Through the challenge and strain, the summit near,
The Spire in sight, we conquer our fear.

Bridge
We pause in the stillness, where the world lies low,
The journey has shown us what we need to know.
Each trial a lesson, each step a prayer,
"Amal," we say, as we breathe the rare air.

Chorus
To the summit we reach, where the Spire awaits,
Through trials we climb, through the shifting fates.
With each breath and each step, the world fades away,
"Shukran," we say, for the light of the day.

The Spire calls, like a star in the night,
Guiding our journey, where shadows and light.
With Ember by my side, and the wisdom’s grace,
We climb the heights with unwavering pace.

Outro
As we near the top, with the Spire so close,
"Qalb," I whisper, for the heart that knows.
The journey is long, but the goal is clear,
With the Spire ahead, we cast aside fear.

Through the night and the day, with our spirits high,
We ascend to the Spire, where the truth does lie.
"Sabr wa Amal," patience and hope,
Guide us through the climb, as we rise and cope.


With the trials behind her, Elara begins the arduous journey to the Spire, accompanied by Ember and guided by the wisdom of the Ashen Sage. The ascent symbolizes both the physical climb and the spiritual elevation necessary to reach the Spire’s summit. This song embodies the unity and determination required to continue toward enlightenment.
