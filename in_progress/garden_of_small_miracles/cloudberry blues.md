# Cloudberry Blues

**Created:** 2026-02-21

Instrumentation
- Slide guitar or soft electric lead, upright bass, brushed snare, harmonium/organ swell, subtle marimba for shimmer.

Focus
- Nordic folk melancholy; the rare fruit (cloudberry) as metaphor for scarce joy and the ache of savoring small sweetnesses.

Hook
- Lyrical line: "One cloudberry in the palm, one small sweet for two — taste and know the blue." Repeated with harmony.

Structure
- Haiku intro (northern landscape image)
- Verse (memory of picking/tasting, scarcity imagery)
- Chorus (melancholic but tender refrain)
- Bridge (instrumental slide guitar solo)
- Outro (soft hum + organ pad)

Lyrical seed

Haiku:
Bog smoke under sky,
one gold berry holds a sun,
we split the small light.

Verse sketch:
We stole the last ripe jewel from the mossy edge of ground,
we cupped it like a secret, felt the sugar soft and round.
A taste and all the winters turned to honey in our mouth,
we kept the map of sweetness quiet, passing it south to south.

Production note
- Keep vocal warm and slightly reedy; harmonies should be close and intimate.
