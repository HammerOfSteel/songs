# The Anchor of Memories

**Labels:** Dancing salamanders, Risca
**Created:** 2024-08-30
**Edited:** 2024-08-30

---

Verse 1
In the quiet of your room, where shadows softly play,
I’m standing at the threshold, unsure if I should stay.
This space, it holds your laughter, your whispers in the air,
A sacred place of memories, where time just isn’t fair.

Chorus
Oh, can I ever leave again, and take you with me too?
Or am I bound to this place, where every breath is you?
The world outside is calling, but here I feel you near,
Oh, can I ever leave again, when all I hold is here?

Verse 2
I lay upon your bed, where your dreams still linger on,
The echoes of your heartbeat, in the silence of the dawn.
This room, it is a haven, a bridge to what was lost,
But stepping out without you, feels like too great a cost.

Chorus
Oh, can I ever leave again, and take you with me too?
Or am I bound to this place, where every breath is you?
The world outside is calling, but here I feel you near,
Oh, can I ever leave again, when all I hold is here?

Bridge
Every memory is an anchor, holding me so tight,
In the walls that knew your presence, in the softest morning light.
But to move beyond these echoes, and carry you with grace,
Is the battle that I wrestle, as I stand here in this place.

Chorus
Oh, can I ever leave again, and take you with me too?
Or am I bound to this place, where every breath is you?
The world outside is calling, but here I feel you near,
Oh, can I ever leave again, when all I hold is here?

Outro
I’m grateful for these moments, for the memories we’ve made,
But stepping out this doorway feels like a choice delayed.
So I linger in your room, where our worlds are intertwined,
Unsure if I can leave, and leave our past behind.
