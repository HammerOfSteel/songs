# The quiet dark

**Labels:** Dragons Breath
**Created:** 2025-07-19
**Edited:** 2025-07-19

---

The Devil
Doesn’t tear you down
He builds you up
Until

You believe you can
Do it alone

No more OKs, no more brave face
Don’t want your light, I like this place
Curtains drawn in me chest for days
Heart beatin’ quiet in blackout grays

Yeh, I cried in the fruit aisle, so what?
Laughed when they said “chin up, toughen that gut”
Ain’t the storm, I’m the fog that stays
The thing you hush when you ain’t got praise

Soft spell, still fell
Dark don’t bite but it do dwell
Heart cold, hands still
Don’t fix me, just feel

Stared at a wall like it owed me a word
Wrote nothin’ down but the silence I heard
"How you holdin’ up?", like a paper plate
Thin, flappin’ fake in a twist of fate

Sleep with the phone on moon mode now
They say call if I need, I don’t know how
It’s not for them, not their weight to wear
Let ‘em send prayers, I don’t care

No flame, no flare
Still breathin’ in the heavy air
Night hum, heart spell
This is mine, I dwell

Old jumpers smell like November
Don’t say smile, I remember
Teeth clench, soft knuckles sore
Want the hug I had before

Sky’s too wide, sun too bright
Clouds feel safer, hold me tight
Want tea and no chat, just be
Don’t need fixin’, just let me be

Breath in, breath stay
Don’t wish the ache away
There’s peace in not pretending
There’s light that comes from ending

Still spell, still real
Heavy don’t mean I won’t heal
Soft fight, small flame
I ain’t gone,  just not the same

I've heard it said
That everyone
Has at least one someone
Then being
The one someone
That has no one...
