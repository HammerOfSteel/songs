# Whispers of Vengeance

**Labels:** A Spiral Path, Dancing salamanders
**Created:** 2024-09-27
**Edited:** 2024-09-27

---

(Verse 1)
I hear the call of shadows, where the world meets night,
A heart once gentle, now burns with spite.
The children of the Tyrant, they took all I loved,
Now I rise from the ashes, with no mercy above.

(Pre-Chorus)
I'll find the edge, I’ll make them see,
What a heart betrayed can be.

(Chorus)
Whispers of vengeance, carried by the wind,
Rage in my soul, let the hunt begin.
I’ll follow the thorns, through the forest of night,
To face the ones who shattered my light.

(Verse 2)
Vaelien, King of Thorns, with gold draped cruel,
Your children weave fate, but I’m no fool.
I’ll tear through the shadows, break free from your chain,
For the blood of the innocent, they will know my pain.

(Pre-Chorus)
I’ll find the edge, I’ll make them see,
What a soul unbound can be.

(Chorus)
Whispers of vengeance, carried by the wind,
Rage in my soul, let the hunt begin.
I’ll follow the thorns, through the forest of night,
To face the ones who shattered my light.

(Bridge)
I walk the path of ruin, I carve my own way,
With shadows beside me, in the night I stay.
I swear on the blood, I’ll make them fall,
I’ve nothing to lose, I’ll give it my all.
