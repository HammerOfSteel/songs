# Hyperbolic Manifold

**Labels:** Koch star
**Created:** 2024-12-06
**Edited:** 2024-12-06

---

The moonlight filters gently,
Through the leaves of Cwmcarn’s trees.
I carry her in silence,
Her weight a comfort, her warmth a peace.

The woods are quiet, the air is still,
Her tiny hand rests soft, tranquil.
Each coo she makes, a melody,
Her world so small, yet wild and free.
I feel the pull of threads inside,
Each one unravels, yet here they bind.

I’m a web of stars, a tangled line,
But her light weaves through, a thread divine.
Through the dark, she’s my guiding trace,
My little star, my heart’s embrace.

The Keeper’s voice still echoes near,
“Are you enough? Do you belong here?”
But her small sigh breaks through the night,
A tether pulling my chaos tight.
Her coos like answers I cannot say,
Her tiny world lights up my way.

I’m a web of stars, a tangled line,
But her light weaves through, a thread divine.
Through the dark, she’s my guiding trace,
My little star, my heart’s embrace.

Her eyes are moons they face,
In her gaze, I find my place.
The worries scatter, the doubts fall still,
Her love’s the pattern I’ll always fill.

I hum to her as the night grows deep,
Her tiny yawns lull me to keep.
The woods fade out, the stars shine through,
A love so vast, it feels brand new.
Her coos are fading, her breath is slow,
Her world at peace, as mine lets go.

I’m a web of stars, a tangled line,
But her light weaves through, a thread divine.
Through the dark, she’s my guiding trace,
My little star, my heart’s embrace.

The moonlight filters gently,
Through the leaves of Cwmcarn’s trees.
I carry her in silence,
Her weight a comfort, her warmth a peace.
