# Dancing salamanders

Three hearts gather 'neath the moon's soft glow,
Seeking solace where the wildflowers grow.
In the whispering woods, their mother's call,
A year has passed, yet they recall.

Youngest sister, dreams so bright,
Middle brother, steadfast knight.
Eldest sister, wise and kind,
In nature's arms, peace they find.

Dancing salamanders, flames flicker high,
A jazzy club beneath the starry sky.
In the mountain’s heart, shadows sway,
Memories of love, guiding their way.

Around the fire, tales unfold,
Of laughter and lessons, hearts consoled.
In the distance, a light begins to play,
A siren song, leading them away.

Through the forest, secrets unfurl,
A hidden path, another world.
Trolls’ feast, with sights so rare,
A nightclub’s pulse fills the air.

Dancing salamanders, flames flicker high,
A jazzy club beneath the starry sky.
In the mountain’s heart, shadows sway,
Memories of love, guiding their way.

Music echoes in the cavern’s core,
Salamanders swing, as spirits soar.
Notes of fire, haunting and clear,
Awakening memories, drawing them near.

In the melodies, her presence felt,
A mother’s love, in every note she dealt.
Her touch in the rhythm, her voice in the song,
In the dance of flames, they belong.

Dancing salamanders, flames flicker high,
A jazzy club beneath the starry sky.
In the mountain’s heart, shadows sway,
Memories of love, guiding their way.

Morning comes, the fire's ember,
Around the campfire, they remember.
Eyes meet in silent reverie,
Through the night's dance, they found harmony.
