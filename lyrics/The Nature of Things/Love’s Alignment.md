# Love’s Alignment

**Labels:** The Nature of Things
**Created:** 2024-12-11
**Edited:** 2024-12-11

---

"Which one, Papa?" you point and say,
"That one there, the one that sways."
I smile and nod, "It’s standing strong,
Perfect for our star before too long."

We wander through the frosted grove,
Bare branches whisper as we rove.
Your mittened hand tugs gently tight,
"Papa, this one feels just right."

I nod, "Alright, my little sun,
Let’s bring it home before day’s done."
You giggle, skipping through the snow,
Our footprints weaving where winds blow.

Jupiter’s glow, Venus near,
Love aligns in skies so clear.
Through winter’s veil, the star will guide,
A beacon bright where hearts reside.
Oh, my little star so true,
The universe shines because of you.

The scent of pine fills up the air,
As lights and ribbons spiral there.
"Is this too much?" you softly tease,
"Never," I grin, "it lights the freeze."

You pause, a silver bauble shines,
"This one’s for Mama, her love entwined."
I kneel and whisper, "She’s with us still,
Her light, like yours, forever will."

Jupiter’s glow, Venus near,
Love aligns in skies so clear.
The star atop burns ever bright,
Guiding us through the longest night.

"Papa, what makes the stars so high?"
You point beyond, to winter’s sky.
"They’re dreams that shine through endless space,
A light for love no time can erase."

"The tree will stand till the frost has gone?"
You ask as shadows dance till dawn.
"It’s rooted strong, like us, my dear,
A cycle turns, but love stays near."

The fire crackles, warmth surrounds,
Your laughter echoes, soft yet profound.
The tree’s soft glow reflects your face,
A spark of joy, a timeless grace.

"Up you go, the final touch,"
I lift you gently—your smile’s too much.
The star ascends, you cheer in awe,
A glimmer of hope without a flaw.

"Will Mama see it from afar?"
Your voice so soft beneath the star.
I nod, "She’s watching, she always will,
Her love’s the glow that guides us still."

The stars above hold all we’ve known,
Their light a thread through time they’ve sewn.
But here with you, my little one,
The darkest night is filled with sun.

"Goodnight, Papa," you softly say,
The tree stands tall as dreams replay.
I tuck you close, your breathing slows,
Through frosty panes, the starlight glows.

Jupiter’s glow, Venus near,
Love aligns in skies so clear.
The star atop burns ever bright,
Guiding us through the longest night.
Oh, my little star so true,
The universe shines because of you.

The tree stands tall, its star aglow,
A light of love through frost and snow.
