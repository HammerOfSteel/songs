# Forgetmeknot

**Labels:** Dancing salamanders
**Created:** 2024-08-01
**Edited:** 2024-08-01

---

Verse 1
In the twilight of our first-year dreams,
Two hearts entwined like whispered streams.
Promises spoken in the night's embrace,
A knot of love, a fragile space.

Pre-Chorus
But shadows fall where light does not,
Our silent battles, a tangled knot.
Your eyes held storms I couldn't see,
Now I'm lost in the memory.

Chorus
Forgetmeknot, our promise tight,
In the garden of sorrow, under moonlight.
One heart lingers, one slips away,
In the boundless night, where shadows play.

Verse 2
Desperation's whisper stole you away,
In fear's cruel grasp, you couldn't stay.
Now the notes and dreams we used to share,
Haunt the empty spaces where you once cared.

Pre-Chorus
Books and pages, our stories told,
Now echoes of love in the night's cold.
Your laughter, a ghost in the silent room,
I mourn in the stillness, in the gloom.

Chorus
Forgetmeknot, our promise tight,
In the garden of sorrow, under moonlight.
One heart lingers, one slips away,
In the boundless night, where shadows play.

Bridge
In every petal, a tale of us,
Of nights of dreams, of whispered trust.
Your spirit dances in the evening breeze,
A reminder of loss, a love that frees.

Chorus
Forgetmeknot, our promise tight,
In the garden of sorrow, under moonlight.
One heart lingers, one slips away,
In the boundless night, where shadows play.

Outro
Under starlit skies, I kneel alone,
A promise to remember, my heart a stone.
Forgetmeknot, forever bound,
In the garden of memories, where love is found.
