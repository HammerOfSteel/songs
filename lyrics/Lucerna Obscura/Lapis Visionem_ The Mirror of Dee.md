# Lapis Visionem: The Mirror of Dee

**Labels:** Dancing salamanders, Lucerna Obscura
**Created:** 2024-08-20
**Edited:** 2024-08-21

---

Verse 1
In the shadows deep where the Veil is thin,
A Seer gazes, where dreams begin.
Through the Lapis, where visions churn,
In the glass, the hidden ones burn.

"Speculum tenebris," whispers of light,
Reflected truth in the endless night.
The Stone of Sight, in the Seer’s hand,
Reveals the path to the hidden land.

Chorus
Lapis Visionem, where shadows play,
In the mirror’s depths, the spirits sway.
Through the eye of the Stone, the world unfolds,
Where light and dark in silence mold.

The scryer’s gaze, where time unwinds,
Seeks the secret that none may find.
In the depths of glass, the truth refrains,
The Stone of Vision, where all remains.

Verse 2
Through the smoke and flame, where the twilight gleams,
The Seer searches for the bridge of dreams.
In the mirror’s heart, where shadows fall,
The Stone reveals the hidden call.

"Caelum apertum," the heavens part,
In the Seer’s mind, the visions start.
Through the glass, where the shadows merge,
The truth of worlds begins to surge.

Bridge
"Umbra lucis," the shadowed light,
Guides the Seer through the endless night.
In the mirror’s depths, the veil is torn,
Revealing secrets yet unborn.

The Stone of Sight, where the past is drawn,
Echoes of dawn in the mirror’s pawn.
Through the crystal’s eye, where the future weaves,
The Seer finds what the night deceives.

Outro
In the silence deep, where the Lapis lies,
The Seer whispers, as the vision dies.
"Finis silentii," the end of sight,
As the mirror fades into the night.

Through the glassy veil, where the shadows cease,
The Seer rests in quiet peace.
But the Lapis gleams, in the darkened air,
A beacon of truth, beyond despair.


"Through the stone of sight, reflections reveal the hidden realms. Visions dance in the dark glass, where the veil of the world thins."
