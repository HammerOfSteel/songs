# Sea side Treasures

**Labels:** Dancing salamanders, Risca
**Created:** 2024-09-02
**Edited:** 2024-09-02

---

"Seaside Treasures": This song evokes the playful and joyful moments of a father and daughter walking along the beach, collecting small treasures like shells and stones. The beach serves as a metaphor for discovery and the treasures symbolize the precious bond they shared.

Verse 1
By the shore where the waves collide,
We walk with footprints side by side.
The sand is soft beneath our feet,
A treasure trove where dreams and sea meet.
Each shell a story, each stone a tale,
In your hands, the world sets sail.
The ocean’s whisper in your ear,
A secret only you can hear.

Chorus
Ar lan y môr (By the sea), where the tides embrace,
We gather treasures, time cannot erase.
Every shell, a memory bright,
Every stone, a piece of light.
In this dance of sand and sea,
You’re with me, always free.

Verse 2
The gulls above, they sing our song,
As we wander the shore, where we belong.
In the cool breeze, your laughter flows,
Like a river where the wild grass grows.
Yn y gwynt (In the wind), your joy takes flight,
A kite that soars in the fading light.
The day is ours, the world so wide,
In this moment, time’s on our side.

Chorus
Ar lan y môr (By the sea), where the tides embrace,
We gather treasures, time cannot erase.
Every shell, a memory bright,
Every stone, a piece of light.
In this dance of sand and sea,
You’re with me, always free.

Bridge
As the sun dips low and the shadows grow,
We find our peace where the wild winds blow.
The ocean’s breath, a lullaby,
That sings of love, that cannot die.
With each wave that gently rolls,
The sea reflects our kindred souls.

Chorus
Ar lan y môr (By the sea), where the tides embrace,
We gather treasures, time cannot erase.
Every shell, a memory bright,

Outro
So here we stand, where the sea meets land,
With treasures gathered in your hand.
Each find a keepsake, each step a sign,
Of the love we share, pure and divine.
Cariad bach (Little love), you’re my seaside joy,
Forever cherished, my heart’s envoy.
