# PSI: Melancholy

**Labels:** The Deep Darkness
**Created:** 2025-04-17
**Edited:** 2025-04-17

---

PSI: Cry. Alpha. Initiate.
Source: loss.
Target: all.

Begin.

It started with a tremble, like rain unsure it wants to fall,
A weight behind my eyes that never made the call.
The world kept asking for fire,
But all I had was static and a flicker of desire.

Paula whispered, “It’s okay to not be brave,”
While I stared down shadows I couldn't save.
My hands don’t glow. My feet still drag.
But inside, something sparked — soft as a tag.
You remember that, right? When names still meant home?

Now the tears come sharp, like glass that sings,
Each memory swings like it’s pulling strings.
I held that bat like it could block the storm,
But this grief? It shapeshifts, soft and warm.

I wanted to break. I wanted to scream.
But PSI won’t cast if the heart can’t dream.
So I stitched the spell from what I had:
Half a smile, one broken pad,
And the hum of something not quite sad.

Then came the push — not force, but flood,
The kind that knocks but leaves you stood.
A voice inside said, “This is yours. Let it hurt.”
So I carved each tear into ancient dirt.

And when I cast, the room got still.
Not rage, not fire, but unfiltered will.
No flash, no flame — just light that bent,
And the scent of everything you meant.
That was PSI: Cry, but this… this is lament.

PSI: Melancholy.
Ω tier.
Casts once per soul.
Never forgotten.
Always near.

Paula said, “Next time, I’m casting PSI: Hug.”
Ness said, “Is that Omega tier yet?”

Jeff from the back: “Please cry responsibly. Circuits were fried last time.”

Poo, eyes closed: “Water nourishes. Even fire.”
