# pockets of light

**Labels:** Pockets Of Light
**Created:** 2025-08-22
**Edited:** 2025-08-22

---

Crumb-constellations on a plate, little late-night satellites,
butter-moon bloom on the knife in slice-thin candlelight.
Steam is incense, sink is choir, kettle’s throat goes lullaby,
mug rim, hymn rim, warm breath rising like a firefly.
Window’s a lens on a rain-tremble street, drip-script, tick-beat,
sock-feet patter where the night and the hallway meet.
I stitch my hush to the hush of the house, soft vow, no fright,
pocket my glow like a coin of the sun: bright, slight, tight.

I keep little lanterns. I keep them close.

Pockets of light, pockets of light,
folded like notes in the coat of the night.
Pockets of light, pockets of light,
hold me, old me, home me, right.

Crack of a door is a silver seam, seam sings, beam brings,
dust in the stairwell dances its small gold wings.
Toast coast, crumb drum, table a drum I tap,
mouth of the room goes mmm like a hum-wrapped lap.
Tea-leaf galaxy turns in the cup, swirl, curl, pearl, twirl,
I spin my wish like a skipping-rope girl.
Fridge-light midnight, gospel of milk and jam,
I write with a spoon in the fog of the glass, “I am.”

And maybe “I am” is already enough.

Little light, little rite, little right-now prayer,
tuck it in the pocket where the palms feel air.

Pockets of light, pockets of light,
folded like notes in the coat of the night.
Pockets of light, pockets of light,
hold me, old me, home me, right.

Radiator psalm in a rib-bone click,
clock-tongue talks in a tick after tick.
Soap halo, halo, bowl of warm hands,
moon on the tap makes a coin where it stands.
Alphabet magnets, prayer on the fridge,
bridge to a bridge to a shimmering bridge.
Floorboard remembers the feet that I’ve been,
shadow-me dances in soft violin.
I rhyme the ordinary till it burns white,
alchemy of tea towels, linen into light.

We bless the mess by seeing it shine.

Pocket-lantern, pocket-lantern, click,
matchhead star on a cotton wick.
Carry, carry, little spark,
window-marigold in the dark.
Pocket-lantern, pocket-lantern, stay,
show me the yes in the edges of gray.

Pockets of light, pockets of light,
folded like notes in the coat of the night.
Pockets of light, pockets of light,
hold me, old me, home me, right… right… right.

Crumbs are constellations, steam is incense,
love is the lantern I keep in my pocket.
