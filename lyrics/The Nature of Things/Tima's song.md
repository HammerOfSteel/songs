# Tima's song

**Labels:** The Nature of Things
**Created:** 2024-11-25
**Edited:** 2024-11-25

---

The morning light spills soft and slow,
Through fields where tender breezes flow.
The world feels wide, yet calm, serene,
A canvas painted in gold and green.

"Can you feel it, this quiet air?
It feels like waiting, like hope laid bare."

"The world seems to hold its breath for her,
A whisper of love that time can’t deter."

The path winds gently through the hills,
The morning hums, the air stands still.
Her hand on her belly, a quiet pride,
She dreams of the life her heart will guide.

"Will she see the world as a place of light?"

"With us, my love, she’ll always fight.
She’ll find her way through the clouds and rain,
With love to guide her through joy and pain."

Tima’s name, a bridge, a flame,
A song of hope, a tender claim.
Through fields of green and skies of blue,
Her light will shine in all we do.

The breeze feels warm, yet doubts remain,
A mother’s heart holds joy and pain.
Her thoughts drift wide, her fears run deep,
But hope takes root where love can keep.

"Can I give her more than the shadows I hold?
Can I guide her through this world so bold?"

"You’re stronger than you know, my star,
And she’ll see the strength that brought us this far."

They sit by the stream where the waters gleam,
Her laughter soft, like a half-formed dream.
"Will she laugh like you, will she smile so wide?"
"She’ll carry your heart, like a tide inside."

"Will she love the stars, the stories we weave?"

"She’ll find her light in the lives she’ll leave.
A piece of you, a piece of me,
Her name a song for eternity."

Tima’s name, a light, a guide,
A spark of hope where dreams reside.
Through rivers clear and skies of blue,
Her love will shine in all we do.

The sun dips low, the skies burn red,
Their hopes and dreams like threads are spread.
Her voice turns soft, her heart feels light,
"She’ll carry us forward into the night."

"She’ll find her strength, her song, her way,
And through her light, we’ll always stay."

The stars come out as the evening falls,
Their tiny light a mother’s call.
"Do you think she’ll see these same bright skies?"
"She’ll wonder, love, as the world complies."

"I’ll tell her these stories, these dreams of ours,
Of rivers and fields and skies of stars.
She’ll know her name is more than a sound,
It’s love, a light, forever bound."

Tima’s name, a bridge, a flame,
A song of hope, a tender claim.
Through skies so vast and rivers true,
Her light will shine in all we do.

The night grows still, the fields grow deep,
Their hearts hold close the love they keep.
Her hand on her belly, his hand in hers,
A future written in whispers and murmurs.

"Tima’s name, a light, a guide,
Through time and space, where love resides."
