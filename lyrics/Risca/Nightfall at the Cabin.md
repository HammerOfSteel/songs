# Nightfall at the Cabin

**Labels:** Dancing salamanders, Risca
**Created:** 2024-09-02
**Edited:** 2024-09-02

---

"Nightfall at the Cabin": This mystical song describes the transformation of the cabin at night, where the father feels the presence of his lost loved ones. The veil between worlds thins, and the cabin becomes a place of connection, warmth, and comfort, dispelling all fear.

Verse 1
As the fire softly crackles low,
I hold you close, mor dyner, mor araf (so tender, so slow).
Your tiny hand wrapped around mine,
In this quiet, sacred time.
The cabin hums with warmth and light,
The day gives way to nos dawel (quiet night).
I hum a tune, so soft, so dear,
And feel your mother’s presence near.

Chorus
In the glow of the hearth, shadows dance,
The veil between worlds in this quiet trance.
Cariad Mam (Mother’s love), it lingers still,
In every breath, in every thrill.
No fear can stay within this light,
Her spirit holds us through the night.

Verse 2
Your eyelids flutter, closing tight,
As I sing to you in the dimming light.
The night outside, it softly calls,
But here within, no shadow falls.
I feel her warmth, mor agos, mor go iawn (so close, so real),
A love so strong, it’s all we feel.
Her whisper mingles with my song,
In this cabin, lle’r ydym yn perthyn (where we belong).

Chorus

Bridge
The night is deep, the world is still,
Yet in this space, mae’n calonau’n llawn (our hearts fulfill).
Her touch, it wraps us, safe and sound,
In this moment, we are bound.
The cabin walls, they hold her grace,
In every corner, pobman (every place).

Outro
So as you drift to sleep so deep,
I know in dreams, her love you’ll keep.
And in this cabin, twym a gwir (warm and true),
Her love surrounds both me and you.
The night may come, the world may turn,
But here in love, rydym yn dychwelyd (we always return).
