# Blueprint of a Dreamer

**Labels:** eras-imos
**Created:** 2024-12-01
**Edited:** 2024-12-01

---

The hum of keys, the glow of screens,
Our world awaits through pixel streams.
A doorway opens, a sky takes form,
Imagination’s quiet storm.

He maps the ground with steady hands,
A world of blocks, a sea of plans.
“This will be the tallest tower,”
He whispers soft, his finest hour.

Each shape aligns, a vision grows,
With every move, his magic flows.
The blueprints hum, the world takes flight,
A dreamer’s touch in the endless night.

Brick by brick, the skyline starts,
A world alive from one small heart.
A castle here, a bridge of light,
His dreams take shape beneath the night.

“Look here, Dad,” he calls me near,
“This mountain’s where the stars appear.”
He carves a cave, its walls aglow,
A quiet wonder starts to grow.

The city rises, bold and bright,
Impossible shapes defy the night.
A floating tree, a river bends,
A masterpiece that never ends.

Brick by brick, the skyline grows,
A dreamer’s art in every row.
Each block a story, each turn a rhyme,
A place where dreams outrun the time.

A staircase spirals to the stars,
A library with glowing jars.
He builds a clock that doesn’t tick,
Time moves forward, slow and quick.

“This bridge,” he says, “connects the lands,
A way for everyone to understand.”
His voice is light, his world is whole,
A dreamer’s heart, a boundless soul.

Brick by brick, the skyline glows,
A world alive, a heart exposed.
His hands, the architects of skies,
A place where dreams refuse to die.

The sea unfolds, a ship takes form,
Its sails are fire, the mast the storm.
“It’ll sail forever,” he softly says,
“Through endless nights and brighter days.”

He carves a harbor, lines the shore,
With lanterns lit and open doors.
“Here they’ll come, the lost, the free,
This city’s built for all to see.”

Brick by brick, the skyline soars,
A world alive with open doors.
A harbor safe, the stars nearby,
His dreams now sail across the sky.

The night grows late, the stars grow dim,
But still his world is bright within.
“Tomorrow, Dad, we’ll build the sky,
A place where every dream can fly.”

And as we log off, close the screens,
I hold the weight of all his dreams.
For in his heart, a world is drawn,
A dreamer’s light that will live on.

The hum of keys, the glow of screens,
A quiet end to vibrant scenes.
But in my mind, the skyline beams,
The blueprint of a dreamer’s dreams.
