# The bells of Lyonesse

**Labels:** Dancing salamanders
**Created:** 2024-08-05
**Edited:** 2024-08-05

---

Verse 1
In a small Cornish home, as the twilight falls,
A father whispers tales within ancient walls.
To his young daughter, eyes wide with dreams,
Of the magic and lore, where the Awen streams.

Chorus
The bells of Lyonesse, ringing through the night,
Echoes of a realm, bathed in silver light.
Where the Awen flows, through hearts so near,
Binding us together, love sincere.

Verse 2
He speaks of Lyonesse, beneath the ocean's veil,
Where spirits dance, and the old myths prevail.
Of cauldrons that brew, with the Awen's grace,
Connecting every soul, in time and space.

Chorus
The bells of Lyonesse, ringing through the night,
Echoes of a realm, bathed in silver light.
Where the Awen flows, through hearts so near,
Binding us together, love sincere.

Bridge
"Awen," he whispers, "is the poet's fire,
The spark of inspiration, lifting hearts higher.
It flows through you, my dear, and through me,
A river of wisdom, like the endless sea."

Verse 3
The story deepens, the bells softly chime,
Of legends and love, woven through time.
Her eyes grow heavy, in his tender embrace,
As he hums a lullaby, in the quiet space.

Chorus
The bells of Lyonesse, ringing through the night,
Echoes of a realm, bathed in silver light.
Where the Awen flows, through hearts so near,
Binding us together, love sincere.

Outro
As she drifts to sleep, in her father’s arms,
Safe and warm, away from harm.
He hums a gentle lullaby, as dreams begin to seep,
And the bells of Lyonesse, ever chiming in the deep.
