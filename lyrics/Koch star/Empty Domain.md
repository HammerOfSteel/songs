# Empty Domain

**Labels:** Koch star
**Created:** 2024-12-05
**Edited:** 2024-12-06

---

In the space where nothing lingers,
No truths, no lies, no names.
An empty domain holds nothing,
Yet somehow, I remain.

You said it didn’t happen,
That the past was just a phase.
Erase the marks, the echoes,
Like scribbles on a page.
But the lines, they still exist here,
Even hidden in the haze.

Do you need these lies?,
I can write them in the sky.
Say the stars never burned,
Say my voice is a disguise.
But if you want the truth,
It’s a chaos I can’t deny.
The empty domain’s still spinning,
And I’m trapped inside.

Every mirror holds a fragment,
A reflection I can’t claim.
Each memory shifts like shadows,
A face without a name.
I tried to hold the pieces,
But the void swallows them away.

Okay, let's pretend
I can bury what you see.
Let the journal tear its pages,
Let the truth fall silently.
But if you want the truth,
It’s a storm that breaks the sea.
Even an empty domain radiates,
Its light escapes from me.

No universal promise,
No truth to bind it all.
The void doesn’t mean nothing,
It’s where shattered pieces fall.
You said “let it be forgotten,”
But forgetting means I’m gone.
Even the emptiest space,
Holds echoes lingering on.

I am not a single answer,
Not a silence to erase.
I’m the echoes and the fractures,
I’m the void, I’m the space.
And in the places nothing lingers,
There’s still a light that waits.

little late lies
But I won’t write them anymore.
The stars still burn within me,
Even as the shadows roar.
Because I’ve found the truth,
It’s the chaos I’ve explored.
The empty domain still spinning,
But it’s mine to restore.

In the space where nothing lingers,
No truths, no lies, no names.
The empty domain still turning,
And I remain.
