# Seedless

**Labels:** Dancing salamanders
**Created:** 2024-08-09
**Edited:** 2024-08-09

---

In the city’s pulse, where steel meets sky,
A man walks a path, but wonders why,
The rhythm of the day, a ceaseless scroll,
Yet whispers in the wind awaken his soul.


何してる calls in the night,
In the maze of thoughts, a hidden light,
まだまだ echoes from afar,
Guiding steps, like a falling star.


Through crowded streets, where shadows play,
A sudden shift, where the mundane sways,
Faint traces linger, unseen but near,
Pulling him inward, through the atmosphere.


何してる calls in the night,
In the maze of thoughts, a hidden light,
まだまだ  echoes from afar,
Guiding steps, like a falling star.


A question without words, stirring deep,
A presence like the dawn, waking from sleep,
Through the corridors of thought, the path unfolds,
In the maze of life, the secret’s told.


The city fades, as echoes grow clear,
A dance with the unseen, in twilight's sphere,
Steps led by signs, seen but never known,
In silent spaces, where the seed is sown.


何してる calls in the night,
In the maze of thoughts, a hidden light,
まだまだ echoes from afar,
Guiding steps, like a falling star.


Through tangled streets, he walks anew,
Guided by whispers, as the city withdrew,
In a world once mundane, he finds the trace,
A seedless journey, to a timeless place.

Style:
Acoustic folk, ambient layers, Japanese influences, subtle strings, haunting flute, mysterious, introspective, poetic blend.
