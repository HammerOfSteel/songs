# Golden sand

**Labels:** Dancing salamanders
**Created:** 2024-08-06
**Edited:** 2024-08-06

---

Verse 1
In the garden of our childhood, where memories bloom,
There was always a presence, a light in the room.
More than just a friend, a guiding hand,
A touch of gold, like philosopher's sand.

Chorus
Golden sand, moments refined,
Grains of wisdom, in the heart's design.
Through her prism, the light expands,
Turning our moments into golden sand.

Verse 2
In times of joy, in times of need,
She was there, a constant, with a heart that leads.
A nurse by trade, but so much more,
Her compassion endless, her spirit pure.

Chorus
Golden sand, moments refined,
Grains of wisdom, in the heart's design.
Through her prism, the light expands,
Turning our moments into golden sand.

Bridge
Philosophers speak of time's endless flow,
But she taught us to cherish, to truly know.
Each grain of sand, each fleeting day,
Turns to gold in her loving way.

Verse 3
Through every milestone, every life event,
Her presence a gift, heaven-sent.
With selfless love, she helped us stand,
From base to noble, through her gentle hand.

Chorus
Golden sand, moments refined,
Grains of wisdom, in the heart's design.
Through her prism, the light expands,
Turning our moments into golden sand.

Outro
As we grow and journey on,
Her lessons linger, her light has shone.
In our hearts, forever she'll stand,
The essence of change, our golden sand.
