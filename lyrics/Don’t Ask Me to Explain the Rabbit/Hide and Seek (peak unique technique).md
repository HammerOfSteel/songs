# Hide and Seek (peak unique technique)

**Labels:** Don’t Ask Me to Explain the Rabbit
**Created:** 2025-04-19
**Edited:** 2025-04-19

---

“Eight… nine… ten… ready or not…”

Hide behind the berry bush,
breath held like a secret crush.
Socks soaked through from dew and fear,
do they even know I’m here?

Sunlight’s thick like melted gold,
the tree knows secrets never told.
I laugh into my knees, then hush —
peak unique technique, no rush.

Peak unique technique, I ghosted sweet,
but now I miss the shuffle of seeking feet.
Love’s a game that counts us in —
I hope they trip. I hope they grin.

I hid so well I nearly lost me too,
but if they call my name… I might come through.

Hid inside the scroll and swipe,
in memes and reels and half-saved hype.
Said "I’m just tired" like a code —
but stayed online to watch them load.

I wore my hoodie like a shell,
a fox in glitch, a broken spell.
Ignored the knocks, declined the ping —
but deep inside, I missed the ring.

Peak unique technique, a hide so deep,
I dreamed my seeker fell asleep.
But still I wished, beneath my coat,
to hear my name float like a note.

Love’s not loud — it climbs, it creeps.
It waits while we forget we weep.

They called my name.
Not loud. Not strong.
Just once — like dusk, like half a song.

I didn’t move.
But still, I knew —
someone
still
was seeking too.

Family’s not just blood and skin,
it’s who still knocks to let you in.
Who sees the spot you love to hide,
and sits there, gentle, by your side.

So when I moved, it wasn’t grand —
no speech, no plan, no raised hand.
Just one step out, one breath begun,
into the sun, the spring, the run.

Peak unique technique, I left the game,
but you still sought me — soft by name.
You found me folded in the green,
a seeker too, behind the screen.

You saw my shadow, called it light —
you didn’t say “you’re wrong” — just “right.”

Now every step is hide-and-seek —
and I still hide, but not so deep.

“One… two… three…”
“Found you.”
