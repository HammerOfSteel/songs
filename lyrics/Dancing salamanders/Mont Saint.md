# Mont Saint

**Labels:** Dancing salamanders
**Created:** 2024-08-11
**Edited:** 2024-08-11

---

In the quiet of the night, a vision came to me,
A light so pure, an angel’s voice, calling from the sea,
“Build it high, on sacred ground, where the waters rise,”
But doubt crept in, my weary heart, questioned heaven’s skies.

Mont Saint, Mont Saint, in the dreams you call,
A voice that burns, a whisper’s flame, echoing through the hall,
Build it, build it, though the shadows fight,
Faith will rise, in the darkest night.

Mont Saint, Mont Saint, des songes tu m’appelles,
Une voix brûlante, une flamme murmure, comme un écho éternel,
Construis-le, construis-le, malgré l’obscurité,
La foi s’élèvera, même dans la nuit.

The mornings came, the nights returned, his voice would not relent,
With every prayer, the doubts grew strong, but my soul was bent,
To stone and earth, to hands and faith, I turned with trembling hands,
For in the fire of his command, I’d build on shifting sands.

Mont Saint, Mont Saint, in the dreams you call,
A voice that burns, a whisper’s flame, echoing through the hall,
Build it, build it, though the shadows fight,
Faith will rise, in the darkest night.

Mont Saint, Mont Saint, des songes tu m’appelles,
Une voix brûlante, une flamme murmure, comme un écho éternel,
Construis-le, construis-le, malgré l’obscurité,
La foi s’élèvera, même dans la nuit.

They doubted me, they questioned all, but still I held the line,
A hole was burned into my skull, a mark from the divine,
With every stone, with every prayer, I built toward the sky,
A beacon bright, a testament, to faith that cannot die.

À chaque pierre, à chaque prière, je construis vers le ciel,
Un phare lumineux, un testament, à la foi éternelle.

Mont Saint, Mont Saint, in the dreams you call,
A voice that burns, a whisper’s flame, echoing through the hall,
Build it, build it, though the shadows fight,
Faith will rise, in the darkest night.

Mont Saint, Mont Saint, des songes tu m’appelles,
Une voix brûlante, une flamme murmure, comme un écho éternel,
Construis-le, construis-le, malgré l’obscurité,
La foi s’élèvera, même dans la nuit.

The abbey stands, the waters flow, and still his voice remains,
In every stone, in every wind, the archangel’s refrain,
I built it high, upon this rock, where heaven meets the land,
Mont Saint, Mont Saint, by faith alone, it forever stands.

Mont Saint, Mont Saint, par la foi seule, il se dresse à jamais.


Music Style:
Epic orchestral with acoustic elements, haunting vocals, deep strings, and organ tones, blending sacred and mystical atmospheres.
