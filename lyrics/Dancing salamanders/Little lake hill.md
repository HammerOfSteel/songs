# Little lake hill

**Labels:** Dancing salamanders
**Created:** 2024-08-05
**Edited:** 2024-08-05

---

Dusty photo album, pages worn and thin,
Memories of a summer, where it all begins.
In a town by the mountains, where the tall grass sways,
We were young and free, in those golden days.

Little Lake Hill, where memories are made,
Beneath the summer sun, in the light and shade.
Through the years, our hearts still fill,
With the echoes of joy from Little Lake Hill.

Ayla and Rune, Mira and Finn,
Liora and Bram, as close as kin.
Bicycle rides down winding trails so fast,
Laughter and banter, moments meant to last.

Little Lake Hill, where memories are made,
Beneath the summer sun, in the light and shade.
Through the years, our hearts still fill,
With the echoes of joy from Little Lake Hill.

"Remember the time," Rune would always say,
"When Finn fell in the lake that day!"
"Or when Mira's kite got stuck in the tree,"
Those simple moments, still so close to me.

We built secret forts in the fields of gold,
Told tales of adventures, brave and bold.
Jumping in the lake, water cool and bright,
In those endless days, everything felt right.

Little Lake Hill, where memories are made,
Beneath the summer sun, in the light and shade.
Through the years, our hearts still fill,
With the echoes of joy from Little Lake Hill.

The blue sky bus, just for us, we'd ride,
To the top of the hill, where dreams collide.
My inner child whispers, calling my name,
In every joy, in every game.

Little Lake Hill, where memories are made,
Beneath the summer sun, in the light and shade.
Through the years, our hearts still fill,
With the echoes of joy from Little Lake Hill.

Though time has flown and we've traveled far,
In our hearts, Little Lake Hill is never far.
With every step, in every thrill,
We carry the love from Little Lake Hill.
