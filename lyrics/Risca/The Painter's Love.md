# The Painter's Love

**Labels:** Risca
**Created:** 2024-09-02
**Edited:** 2024-09-02

---

"The Painter's Love": A dreamlike lullaby that tells the story of a mother painting her love for her unborn child, weaving her emotions into every brushstroke. The song reflects the depth of a mother’s love and how it becomes a guiding force in her child’s life.

Verse 1
In a quiet room, where dreams take flight,
She paints a world bathed in soft moonlight.
Her brush, it moves with gentle care,
Creating a story she longs to share.
For you, my love, who soon will be,
A life painted in love’s decree.
Each stroke, a promise, warm and bright,
A future wrapped in colors light.

Chorus
Through every shade, through every hue,
She paints her love, pure and true.
In every brushstroke, a tale unfolds,
A mother’s heart in colors bold.
Cariad (Love), this world she weaves,
A gift for you, her heart believes.

Verse 2
She paints the stars to light your way,
A sky where dreams and hopes will stay.
The sun she paints with golden rays,
To greet you on your coming days.
In every bloom and every tree,
She paints the world for you to see.
Her love, a canvas wide and deep,
For you, my child, in dreams you sleep.

Chorus
Through every shade, through every hue,
She paints her love, pure and true.
In every brushstroke, a tale unfolds,
A mother’s heart in colors bold.
Cariad (Love), this world she weaves,
A gift for you, her heart believes.

Bridge
And as the night begins to fall,
She paints a lullaby, soft and small.
A story told in colors bright,
To cradle you through the silent night.
With every stroke, her love will grow,
In the world she paints for you to know.

Chorus
Through every shade, through every hue,
She paints her love, pure and true.
In every brushstroke, a tale unfolds,
A mother’s heart in colors bold.
Cariad (Love), this world she weaves,
A gift for you, her heart believes.

Outro
So as you sleep within her heart,
Know this world is just the start.
Her love, a painting rich and wide,
A canvas where you’ll always reside.
In every color, in every line,
The Painter’s love forever shines.
