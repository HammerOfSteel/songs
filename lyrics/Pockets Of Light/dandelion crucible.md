# dandelion crucible

**Labels:** Pockets Of Light
**Created:** 2025-08-30
**Edited:** 2025-08-30

---

one breath two, blow through 
Puff-pulse, bellows-breast, meadow-nest hush,
 gold-dust gospel in a wind-soft rush.
 Seed-bead, bead-seed, beadled in light,
 snow of the summer on a noon-blue night.
 Lip-sip, wish-swish, fizz in the air,
 petal-metal crucible, bright as a prayer.
 Halo-halo over thistle and toe,
 I load my lungs like a lantern and let go.

 Dandelion crucible, blow, slow, sow;
 little bellows, let the tiny suns go.
 Dandelion crucible, hush, hush, glow;
 hope rides the airstream, row by row.

 Stem-stem serif, serif of green,
 ink-spun aether in a typeface of sheen.
 Pollen-psalm syllables, syllabic snow,
 tickle my tongue with a soft halo.
 Fluff-loft, loft-fluff, off you sail,
 whirligig hymn on a thistledown trail.
 My mouth is a kiln for a whisper-hot spark,
 I temper a wish and I temper the dark.

 Field-breath, field-bread, fed by the breeze,
 knees in the clover, elbows of ease.
 Vane-vein weather, feathered refrain,
 clocks of the meadow keep merciful time.
 Sieve me the sunlight, sift me the blue,
 sow me a somewhere I never yet knew.
 Round little roundels, aureate snow,
 drift into maybe, drift into grow.

 Dandelion crucible, blow, slow, sow;
 let go the glitter of the yes I don’t know.
 Dandelion crucible, hush, hush, glow;
 carry my quiet where the wild seeds go.

 One breath, bead. Two breath, bead.
 Three breath, need. Four, proceed.

 Alembic of lungs, a liturgy of air;
 what I can’t carry, I cradle in prayer.

 Furled into furloughs of feather and shine,
 mercury meadow on a sunshine line.
 Sulfur of laughter, salt of the skin,
 elemental alphabet writ on the wind.
 Wish-dish, swish-sash, sash of the sky,
 holiness hiding in a blink of an eye.
 I pocket the nothing that glitters like gold,
 and blow it again just to see it unfold.

 Dandelion crucible, blow, slow, sow;
 little bellows, let the tiny suns go.
 Dandelion crucible, hush, hush, glow;
 hope on a hairline, row by row.

Enough to release; enough to keep
