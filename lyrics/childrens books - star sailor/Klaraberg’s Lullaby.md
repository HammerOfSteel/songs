# Klaraberg’s Lullaby

**Labels:** childrens books - star sailor
**Created:** 2024-10-06
**Edited:** 2024-10-06

---

Intro:
Through the moonlit streets of a city so still,
Where shadows play softly on windowsills,
A small crescent boat sails quiet and slow,
With Star Sailor’s light casting a gentle glow.

She drifts above rooftops, her gaze warm and bright,
And spots a gray cat in the soft silver light.
The cat pauses briefly, her eyes filled with tears,
“For a moment I thought… my brother was near.”

Star Sailor smiles gently, a glow in her eyes,
“I’m here to guide you through these empty skies.”

Verse 1:
With two little kittens trailing behind,
The City Cat walks, her thoughts far in her mind.
The places they pass, once full of cheer,
Now echo with memories she holds so dear.

Her steps are uncertain, but her gaze is strong,
She leads them onward, though the night feels long.
Star Sailor floats down, her light glowing clear,
“Maybe there’s hope, even here, even here.”

Chorus:
“Take a step, take a breath, let the past fade away,
For new paths can brighten with each passing day.”
“New streets, new parks, new places to see,
Where laughter can echo, where dreams can run free.”

“Don’t fear the changes or the roads yet unknown,
For sometimes it’s courage that finds us a home.”
“Take a step, take a breath, let the past fade away,
For new paths can brighten, like dawn’s early ray.”

Verse 2:
They cross narrow bridges and alleys so small,
Through gardens where moonlight paints shadows tall.
The kittens run forward, their paws light and quick,
While the City Cat watches, her heart feeling thick.

But when they reach the park, hidden between,
The kittens start playing, their joy evergreen.
Star Sailor whispers, her voice soft and true,
“New places can have new memories, too.”

Bridge:
“It’s okay to remember, it’s okay to sigh,
But don’t let old heartaches keep dreams locked inside.”
“For the past will remain, but the future can grow,
With each step that you take, there’s more to bestow.”

The City Cat sighs, her gaze turning bright,
Watching her kittens in the cool, gentle night.
“Maybe you’re right,” she murmurs at last,
“We can build something new, not bound by the past.”

Chorus (Reprise):
“Take a step, take a breath, let the past fade away,
For new paths can brighten with each passing day.”
“New streets, new parks, new places to see,
Where laughter can echo, where dreams can run free.”

“Don’t fear the changes or the roads yet unknown,
For sometimes it’s courage that finds us a home.”
“Take a step, take a breath, let the past fade away,
For new paths can brighten, like dawn’s early ray.”

Outro:
As dawn’s light breaks softly, painting gold through the skies,
The City Cat smiles, a warmth in her eyes.
“Thank you,” she whispers, her gaze lifting high,
And Star Sailor drifts on, like a star in the sky.

The kittens curl close, on a soft patch of grass,
Their mother beside them, watching time pass.
“New paths,” she murmurs, “new dreams to unfold,”
And the city awakes, bathed in soft hues of gold.
