# The Struggle of Release

**Labels:** Dancing salamanders
**Created:** 2024-08-19
**Edited:** 2024-08-19

---

Verse 1
In the quiet aftermath, where the echoes lay,
I feel the weight of all I tried to say.
The cavern's dust still clings to my soul,
Even after forging a bond that makes me whole.

But letting go is harder than it seems,
Even when the light is born from dreams.
The past whispers like a breeze in the night,
Reminding me that the shadows still hold tight.

Chorus
Between the past and what’s yet to be,
I walk a line where the heart can't see.
Holding on to what’s already gone,
Struggling to find where I belong.

Ember, you are the light I seek,
In the darkness where my soul feels weak.
But even in your warmth, the cold remains,
As I wrestle with these unseen chains.

Verse 2
Your flame is a comfort, a guiding star,
Yet the journey’s weight is never far.
You remind me that strength is found within,
But the struggle to release is where I begin.

In this new world, where nothing is the same,
I carry the scars of an old, familiar name.
The stories of my past still echo deep,
And in my heart, their voices weep.

Bridge
I stand at the river's edge, where the currents flow,
Watching the memories I must let go.
The water carries them, but they resist the tide,
Lingering still, where the shadows hide.

"Release," you whisper, as the flames dance low,
"Let the river take what it must know."
Yet in the letting go, I find new pain,
For the heart clings to what it can’t regain.

Chorus
Between the past and what’s yet to be,
I walk a line where the heart can't see.
Holding on to what’s already gone,
Struggling to find where I belong.

Ember, you are the light I seek,
In the darkness where my soul feels weak.
But even in your warmth, the cold remains,

Outro
In the silence after, where the shadows cease,
I find a moment’s fragile peace.
With Ember by my side, the night feels less long,
Yet the struggle of release still lingers in my song.

The path ahead is shrouded in mist,
But in the struggle, I find the twist.
For in the letting go, I slowly learn,
That the fire within will always burn.



Elara grapples with the emotional toll of letting go, finding herself caught between the memories of the past and the challenges of the present. With Ember’s guidance, she begins to navigate the complex process of release, searching for the strength to move forward.
