# Echoes of Eternity

**Labels:** Dancing salamanders, Risca
**Created:** 2024-09-04
**Edited:** 2024-09-04

---

Verse 1
In the quiet of the evening’s fall,
I hear your voices, faint yet tall.
They echo through the halls of time,
A melody, a gentle rhyme.
Yr alwad dragwyddol (The eternal call), it draws me near,
A whisper soft, yet crystal clear.

Chorus
Echoes of eternity, they linger on,
In every breath, in every dawn.
Through the shadows, through the light,
You’re with me still, in every night.
Your love, a song that never dies,
In the echoes, your spirit flies.

Verse 2
In the wind that moves the ancient trees,
In the sigh of the ocean’s breeze,
I feel you there, beside me still,
In every moment, in every thrill.
Ysbrydion sy'n tywys (Spirits that guide), they lead me on,
Through the night, into the dawn.

Chorus
Echoes of eternity, they linger on,

Through the shadows, through the light,

Bridge
Though time may pass, and worlds may turn,
The flame of love will always burn.
In every star, in every sky,
I see your light, I hear your cry.
For in my heart, you’ll always be,
An echo of eternity.

Chorus
Echoes of eternity, they linger on,

Through the shadows, through the light,

Outro
So as I walk this path ahead,
With every step, with every thread,
I carry you, in all I see,
In the echoes of eternity.
Forever bound, forever free,
In the echoes, you’re here with me.
