# Wrap It Up  - ALBUM

**Labels:** Album, Wrap it up
**Created:** 2025-02-16
**Edited:** 2025-03-05

---

Description:

This album is a heartfelt acoustic rap project that weaves themes of memory, loss, and love through vivid storytelling and poetic imagery. Each song reflects on moments, places, and things connected to Tima’s life—the places you went, the things she loved, and the imprint of her presence in time. The lyrics blend spoken-word elements with rhythmic verses, creating deeply personal and evocative tributes.

Tracklist

1. Only moments - D

2. Her Favorite Song (Still Playing) - D

A tribute to the music she loved, weaving her favorite melodies into an evolving chorus. The song transitions from playfulness to reflection, as if her favorite tune still echoes in the air.

3. Little Sun, Big Sky - D

An uplifting, heartfelt piece about how she brought warmth into every space she entered. The lyrics compare her to the sun—small in size, but capable of lighting up even the darkest days.

4. The Long Way Home - D

A song about walks taken slowly, holding hands, pointing out birds, streetlights glowing, and the quiet peace of just being together.

5. Counting Stars, Counting Time - D

A deeper reflection on how time moves differently when you're watching the sky together. The song explores how counting stars feels endless, just like love, even when time insists on moving forward.

6. Bara Brith and Buttered Scones - D

A warm, nostalgic song about shared tea-time moments—eating Welsh Bara Brith, Cornish buttered scones, and other familiar comforts. The song captures small, quiet joys that remain long after the table is cleared.

7. Train to Somewhere - D

A rhythmic, slightly upbeat track about taking train rides together through the countryside—watching landscapes blur past, sharing snacks, and making up stories about the places you passed.

8. Snowdonia in the Rain - D

A song about hiking trails together, mist-covered peaks, and the way rain never felt cold when you were walking side by side. The melody follows the steady rhythm of footsteps on wet stone.

9. Crumbling Castles and Hidden Doors - D

An adventurous, playful song about exploring old castle ruins, finding hidden doorways, imagining knights and dragons, and how the magic of those days still lingers in the stones.

10. Foxgloves and Fairy Rings - D

A whimsical, folklore-inspired song about searching for fairy rings, running through fields of wildflowers, and believing in the unseen magic of the world.
