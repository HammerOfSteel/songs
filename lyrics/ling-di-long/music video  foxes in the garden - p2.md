🎬 11. “Story by Candlelight”
Pixar 3D cinematic realism, soft filmic lens and shallow depth of field.
Natural Welsh lighting — mist, rain, candle glow, and golden-hour tones.
Textures of wool, wood, and wet stone rendered with tactile realism.
Subtle camera drift, natural breathing motion, and emotional focus on faces and light.
Color grading in warm desaturated autumn palette — amber, moss green, slate blue.
Composition inspired by live-action cinematography (Luca × The Secret of Roan Inish × A Ghost Story).
Mood-driven realism with poetic surreal accents in night and dream sequences.
Fine cinematic grain, lens flare, and environmental particles (dust, rain mist, candle flicker). 
Setting: Interior, cabin living room at night, soft candlelight flickering across wooden walls.
 Characters:
 – The seventeen-year-old boy, with brown hair and a high hairline, blue eyes, faint freckles, wearing a cream wool jumper, sits cross-legged on the rug.
 – Steph, a woman in her late twenties with long red hair cascading over her shoulders, green-gray eyes, and light freckles, dressed in a dark green jumper and corduroy trousers, leans against a wooden post, holding a mug of tea.
 – Mam, a woman around forty-seven years old with dark-brown hair streaked with gray, blue eyes, and a kind face, wearing a navy shawl and long brown skirt, sits in an old rocking chair.
 – Eira, a five-year-old girl with short curly brown hair and blue eyes, in a red jumper and striped leggings, sits near the candle.
 – Tima, a two-year-old girl with short oak-brown hair and blue eyes, in a yellow cardigan and denim dungarees, curls up against Mam’s lap holding a plush hippo.
 Scene: Mam gestures slowly with her hands as if weaving invisible thread while telling a story. Eira’s eyes widen; Tima’s head tilts upward in wonder. Candlelight paints their faces in gold.
 Mood: enchantment, generational warmth, spoken magic.
