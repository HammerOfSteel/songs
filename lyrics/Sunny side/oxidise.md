# oxidise

**Labels:** Sunny side
**Created:** 2025-12-21
**Edited:** 2025-12-22

---

I burnt almost all of it,
the letters and the thread,
Thinking I could empty,
out the ghosts inside my head.

Was taught I should forget you,
like a script I could reset,
As if the "you and I was just a debt I hadn't met.

I haven't seen your bright smile for a long and lonely time,
Treating every memory like a witness to a crime.

The thought of truly living,
should have brought me to my tears.
Instead, I chose the silence,
and I fed it through the years.

I let myself be severed,
put my heart upon a shelf,
Turned into a stranger, into everything but myself.

Rise, rise,
let the smoke start to crawl,
I’m tired of staring at the writing on the wall.
Phoenix from the ashes of a heart's desire,
See if all,
in the middle of the pyre.

My heart, rise,
rise and oxidise,

Let the rust turn red,
like the truth in your eyes.

Bend the weary metal
 let the jagged edges start,
Let me be close,
let me be apart,
close to your heart.

The world shouldn't go on,
no, it can't go on like this,
Wise men say oh ignorance is bliss.
Oh that is vengeful little lie,
It eats you from the inside,
and it hangs your heart to dry.

I need you, like a fire needs air to live. 
So please,
my Daffodil,
help me to forgive.
Help me find the reasons,
that I should live.

I won't let myself forget,
for another single day,
I’m done with the burning,
I’m done with turning away.

Forgive me for the choosing,
for the way I tried to hide,
For all of the us I keept inside.

The metal’s getting older
but it’s stronger for the stain,
There is something in the anger,
the love and the pain.

It’s a chemical reaction,
it’s a change in the bone,
It is the longest, god-damn path,
I’ve ever really known.

Rise, rise,
let the smoke start to crawl,
I’m tired of staring at the writing on the wall.
Phoenix from the ashes of a heart's desire,
Start again and set free in the fire.
My heart, rise,
rise and oxidise.

Let the rust turn red,
like the truth in your eyes.

Bend the weary metal
let the jagged edges start,
Let me be close,
let me be apart,
close to your heart.
