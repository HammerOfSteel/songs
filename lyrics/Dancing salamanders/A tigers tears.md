# A tigers tears

**Labels:** A Spiral Path, Dancing salamanders
**Created:** 2024-09-27
**Edited:** 2024-09-27

---

(Verse 1)
I sit by the window, the night calm and still,
The weight of the world, but I bend to my will.
A letter to write, with trembling hands,
Of the love we shared, across distant lands.
Her laughter, her light, forever will stay,
Though she's gone, she guides my way.
With each word I write, the pain turns to grace,
And I see her smile in the lines that I trace.

(Pre-Chorus)
The weight of loss, the shadows fade,
As Ferras stands, in light displayed.

(Chorus)
Tears on the page, falling like rain,
Each drop a piece of the endless pain.
But through the words, a path is clear,
With Ferras beside, I know she’s near.

(Verse 2)
Ferras stands tall, his eyes full of light,
He’s seen my rage, my endless fight.
He speaks without words, a presence so near,
Guiding my heart through sorrow and fear.
"Creation is yours," he whispers with grace,
"With every step, you reclaim your place."
The Tiger Spire rises, a beacon so high,
I take the first step, beneath the sky.

(Bridge)
In this moment, I find my way,
Through the tears, I start to pray.
For her memory, for her light,
I take her love into the night.
Ferras watches, silent, strong,
With his presence, I belong.
He’s seen my rage, but now he knows,
That in her name, my heart still grows.
