# Spiritus Viridis: The Emerald Breath

**Labels:** Dancing salamanders, Lucerna Obscura
**Created:** 2024-08-20
**Edited:** 2024-08-21

Verse 1
In the heart of Paris, where the Seine flows deep,
A young soul walks, where secrets sleep.
Nicolas, with a mind so bright,
Wanders the night, drawn to ancient light.

"L'esprit vert," the Green Spirit calls,
In whispered tones through the ancient halls.
Beneath the city, where the waters glide,
A dragon stirs, with flames inside.

The Seine’s reflection, a mirror dark,
Hides the beast that bears the mark.
From under the waves, where shadows creep,
The dragon wakes from its endless sleep.

Chorus
Spiritus Viridis, the breath of green,
In the dragon’s heart, where flames are seen.
Through fire and water, in the alchemist’s hand,
Nicolas wields the ancient brand.

Verse 2
With courage born of wisdom’s flame,
Nicolas faces the beast untamed.
In the night’s deep veil, where shadows twine,
He draws the line with symbols divine.

"Feu et eau, terre et vent,"
He chants the words, the ancient chant.
The dragon roars with fire and might,
But the Green Spirit holds the light.

The battle fierce beneath the moon,
Paris sleeps, unaware of doom.
But in the silence of the Seine,
Nicolas fights, where few have been.

Bridge
In the final clash, where light meets dark,
Nicolas strikes the dragon’s heart.
"L'œuf du dragon," the dragon’s egg,
In the Seine’s depths, where shadows beg.

But as the beast falls to the night,
The Green Spirit breathes new life.
From the dragon’s ash, an egg does bloom,
A secret kept in Paris’s gloom.

Outro
The Seine flows on, with secrets deep,
Where dragons stir in eternal sleep.
Nicolas walks with wisdom's grace,
But in his heart, the dragon’s trace.

"La légende naît de la vérité," (Legend is born from truth,)
The people say, in Paris’s light.
But beneath the streets, where shadows cling,
The Green Spirit still whispers in the spring.
