# The North Wind Doth Blow (Winter Warm)

**Labels:** Wool and Wonder
**Created:** 2025-05-14
**Edited:** 2025-05-15

---

"The north wind's here, lil’ miss... better prep, it’s serious now."

"You always say that, Taid."

The north wind doth blow, I know, I know,
Time to stack up the blankets, go go go.
Mittens by the door, dog jumper out,
Fluffy socks, bobble hat, no doubt.

Candles lit up, windows tight shut,
Hot water bottle for my cold lil’ gut.
Mug o’ cocoa, slippers on feet,
Let it snow outside, in here we got heat.

The north wind doth blow, and we shall have snow,
Best layer up now or you’ll turn into crow.
No scarf? You’re doomed, I’ve warned you twice,
No jumper? That’s it. Human block of ice!

Silly rule one: two jumpers or bust,
Rule two: tea first, in tea we trust.
Triple wrap, double sock, bobble hat high,
North wind’s no match, mate. We dry.

North wind doth blow, let the cold winds spin,
But we’re safe in here with the warm tucked in.
North wind doth blow, outside it may bite,
But inside this house we’re golden tonight.

Dad you’re soft, you’re peak dramatic,
You got two hats? That’s not pragmatic!
Scarf round your neck, scarf round your knees,
Three jumpers deep and still sayin’ “freeze!”

You’ve got tea, you’ve got heat, you’ve got fluffy socks too,
Dog’s got a jumper and so do you.
Window’s steamed, kettle’s singin’ low,
Honestly Taid... it’s barely snow.

Oi, survival’s an art, young missy mind,
Old school wisdom of the warmest kind.
Best to over-prepare, never underdress,
North wind doth test you, nothing less.

Now hush your sass, it’s blanket time innit,
Storm’s out there, but we’re warm within it.
Come closer, lil’ miss, under this quilt,
Tea in my hand, let the cold feel guilt.

North wind doth blow, let the cold winds spin,
But we’re safe in here with the warm tucked in.
North wind doth blow, outside it may bite,
But inside this house we’re golden tonight.

"Told ya, not that cold."

"Yeah yeah… wait ‘til tomorrow."
