# The Fire beather

**Labels:** Dancing salamanders, Llwybrau Bywyd
**Created:** 2024-09-18
**Edited:** 2024-09-18

---

Verse 1
On a train to London, where the city hums,
We carried dreams in tattered drums.
A canvas wide, a stage so bright,
We called the world to paint in light.
From the colors spilled, to laughter’s sway,
In every stroke, a new story lay.
The streets alive, the night ahead,
Where art and chaos softly wed.

Pre-Chorus
Underneath the city’s gaze,
We wandered through a painted haze.
Like circus tents, the sky unfurled,
And we danced through the vibrant world.

Chorus
In the lights of London, where shadows played,
Fire breaths danced in the flames we made.
With brush in hand, we made our mark,
A canvas shared, igniting sparks.
Through circus lights and dancing feet,
Our hearts kept time with every beat.
In every flame and every stroke,
We wrote our past in plumes of smoke.

Verse 2
The tents we left, the circus rings,
The fire breaths and the songs we'd sing.
Now in the city's crowded stream,
We chased the echoes of our dream.
From Camden's streets to the river’s side,
We found a place where art could hide.
A hundred hands, a thousand hues,
Each soul we met, their colors used.

Pre-Chorus
The lights above, the river’s gleam,
The night was young, a painter’s dream.
We gathered strangers, side by side,
And watched the canvas come alive.

Bridge
The canvas grew with every soul,
A tapestry of life, now whole.
The circus of our youth remained,
In every laugh, in every name.
We stood there, still, as dawn awoke,
Among the colors, the words we spoke.
