# Chasm of Molten Rock

**Labels:** Dancing salamanders
**Created:** 2024-08-20
**Edited:** 2024-08-20

---

Verse 1
At the edge of the chasm, where the earth’s breath sears,
I stand in the fire, stripped of my fears.
The molten flow, it calls my name,
A trial of soul, through the burning flame.

I descend into the crucible, where shadows bow,
In the heart of the forge, where the ancients vow.
To break or to bloom, in this final test,
I seek the truth in the heat’s unrest.

Chorus
Through the fire, the essence remains,
In the ashes, the truth unchains.
Three gems emerge, where the darkness once lay,
Guiding me forth on my final way.

Verse 2
In the heart of the flame, where illusions melt,
I find the soul of what I’ve felt.
An elven whisper, soft and true,
Elena naur, calad thalion. (Glimmer of fire, steadfast light.)

Azure blue, the wonder of skies,
The garden’s breath, where innocence lies.
Ruby red, the sea’s deep call,
The pulse of life, where emotions fall.

Bridge
Emerald green, the heart of the earth,
Where creativity springs, giving birth.
Elen silia, palúrien cuil, (A star shines, nourishing life,)
In these gems, my soul is revealed.

Through the crucible’s fire, I see my way,
Through the heat and the light of day.
Lá taure si cuileth, (No forest without life,)
These gems, my guide, as night turns to grey.

Chorus
Through the fire, the essence remains,
In the ashes, the truth unchains.
Three gems emerge, where the darkness once lay,
Guiding me forth on my final way.

Outro
As I rise from the depths, the gems in hand,
I stand reborn on this sacred land.
With the Spire ahead, I walk unafraid,
For in these gems, my soul is laid.

Elenion silme, naur ná calad, (The starlight, the fire is bright,)
With wonder, emotion, and creation's spark,
I journey on, through the light and dark,
Toward the Spire, where my truth embarks.



Elara faces the final trial in the Chasm of Molten Rock, where she endures intense heat and pressure, symbolizing her transformation. Within the crucible, she discovers the core essences of her soul, distilled into three precious gems that represent her wonder, emotion, and creativity. This song captures the intense process of purification and the emergence of Elara’s true self.
