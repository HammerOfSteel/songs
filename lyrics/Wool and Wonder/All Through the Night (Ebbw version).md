# All Through the Night (Ebbw version)

**Labels:** Wool and Wonder
**Created:** 2025-05-21
**Edited:** 2025-05-21

---

That one looks like a spoon.

That’s the Crumpled Biscuit Constellation—classic.

Blanket on the bench, socks on wrong, 
You brought three biscuits, ate one gone. Thermos lid’s cracked but tea still steams, 
Sky full of stars, sky full of dreams.
That one’s the dragon, tail to the hill, 
And there’s old Orion just standin’ still. 
Fox walked by like he owns the road, 
We whispered like the moon was owed.
Your gloves don’t match, and mine don’t either, But love’s warm-blooded, not knit by fever. You lean your head right on my coat, And hum in time with the river’s throat.

All through the night, the sky holds low, 
And the stars wink soft, like they know. 
All through the night, the tea stays sweet, 

And your muddy boots don’t fit your feet.
All through the night, the world’s stood still, We saw a heron, swear we will.
Me and you, and the sky's own light—
We walk, we talk, all through the night.

That star’s a snail, that one’s a sock, 
And that one there? 
That’s TikTok Rock. 
You said “Ursa Major,” 
I said “Nah,” 
That’s clearly a unicorn, eatin’ a car.

Fair play, I’ll give you that.

You always do, ‘cause you’re old and kind— And I’m the brains in this dad-daughter bind.

You never stop talkin’, but that’s alright, 
You’re the best soundtrack to a slow Welsh night. Your mum’d say I talk too much too, 
But she’d grin at the way you talk me through.
I see her in you when you count stars wrong, When you laugh off cold with a cookie song. You said once I’m like the sky so wide— I said, “Only 'cause I keep you inside.”

All through the night, our tea runs cold,
But our stories stay fresh, never old. 
All through the night, we stitch new names, For every star that plays our games.

All through the night, the sky holds tight, 
Like the arms that tuck me every night. 
We drift, we sit, we hold, we write— 
A whole new world all through the night.

You’ll grow past me, light years ahead, 
But I’ll walk in your pockets, sleep in your stead. You’ll find your own stars, maps, 
and skies—
But know this love never says goodbyes.
For now you yawn like you mean the moon, And I pack up crumbs with a clumsy tune. Your head goes soft on my shoulder crease, And I rhyme this night into perfect peace.

All through the night, you dream so loud, 
Stars wrap me like a sleepy cloud. 
All through the night, you breathe so deep, 
I float in tea and nighttime sleep.
All through the night, I stay close, still, 
'Til dreams roll in like Cwmcarn chill. 
You shine, you laugh, you hold my light— My little sun, all through the night.
