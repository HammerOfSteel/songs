# La Lune de Verre" (The Glass Moon)

**Labels:** The Nature of Things
**Created:** 2024-10-24
**Edited:** 2024-10-24

---

[Verse 1 | Première Strophe]
Beneath the moon, fragile and bright,
(Sous la lune fragile et claire)
We danced through shadows and silver light.
Every crack upon its face,
(Chaque fissure une douce trace)
Told stories time could not erase.

Your hands in mine, a gentle touch,
(Tes mains dans les miennes, tendres et douces)
We held on tight, though not too much.
Through nights of rain, and days of snow,
(Sous la pluie et dans le flocon)
Our love would bend but never bow.

[Chorus | Refrain]
La lune de verre, delicate, true,
(La lune de verre, fragile et pure)
Reflects the cracks between me and you.
Beauty blooms where flaws reside,
(La beauté vit là où les failles se cachent)
In every flaw, love will abide.

[Verse 2 | Deuxième Strophe]
A glass moon’s light will shift and sway,
(Sa lumière danse et parfois s’égare)
Just as hearts can lose their way.
But every scar the moon might bear,
(Chaque cicatrice que la lune éclaire)
Is a vow that love will still be there.

Through every fault, we learn anew,
(Dans chaque faille, on trouve le vrai)
That broken things can still be true.
Love’s not perfect, nor is it whole—
(L’amour n’est jamais parfait ni sans faille)
It’s the cracks that let it touch the soul.

[Chorus | Refrain]
La lune de verre, delicate, true,
(La lune de verre, fragile et pure)
Reflects the cracks between me and you.
Beauty blooms where flaws reside,
(La beauté vit là où les failles se cachent)
In every flaw, love will abide.

[Bridge | Pont]
C'est dans l’ombre que brille la lumière,
(In shadows, light begins to shine)
Chaque fissure devient une prière.
(Every crack becomes a sign.)
Les cœurs brisés trouvent leur voie,
(Broken hearts find paths anew)
Dans l’imparfait, l’amour rend tout doux.
(In imperfection, love rings true.)

[Verse 3 | Troisième Strophe]
Even glass that shatters bright,
(Même le verre brisé scintille clair)
Holds fragments of the moon’s pale light.
Though fragile things may fall apart,
(Les choses fragiles peuvent se briser)
They leave their mark upon the heart.

Together, we are not unscarred,
(Ensemble, nous portons nos cicatrices)
But love was never meant to guard.
It opens wide, it lets things through—
(Elle s’ouvre large et laisse entrer)
The light, the dark, the me, the you.

[Chorus | Refrain]
La lune de verre, delicate, true,
(La lune de verre, fragile et pure)
Reflects the cracks between me and you.
Beauty blooms where flaws reside,
(La beauté vit là où les failles se cachent)
In every flaw, love will abide.

[Outro | Outro]
So here we stand beneath the glass,
(Ici, sous la lune fragile, on reste)
Every crack a love that lasts.
The moon may break, but love will stay—
(Même brisée, l’amour ne s’efface jamais)
In every shard, we find our way.

[Verse 1]

Que les choses brisées peuvent s’ouvrir.
L’amour n’est jamais parfait, c’est sûr,
Mais c’est la faille qui touche le cœur.
