# The Douady Rabbit

**Labels:** Koch star
**Created:** 2024-12-03
**Edited:** 2024-12-04

---

A flash of fur, a shadowed ear,
Through the warren, whispers near.
"Follow me," the rabbit calls,
But shadows stretch across the halls.

Through the warren, twisting deep,
Echoes wake from where they sleep.
Each room a story left untold,
A memory bright, a fear grown cold.

Douady rabbit, lead the way,
Through paths where light and shadows play.
Each step reveals a hidden spark,
In every room, a piece of my heart.

The Hollow Hare stands at the gate,
Whispers warnings steeped in fate.
“Turn away, don’t let them see,
The fragments locked inside of me.”

Douady rabbit, lead the way,
Through paths where light and shadows play.
Each step reveals a hidden spark,
In every room, a piece of my heart.

"I see the child I used to be,
Her laughter rings, wild and free.
I see the walls I’ve built so high,
To hide the tears I’d never cry."

Through painted doors and mirrored halls,
The rabbit bounds; I hear its calls.
Each fear I face, each joy I find,
A thread that mends this fractured mind.

Douady rabbit, lead the way,
Through paths where light and shadows play.
Each step reveals a hidden spark,
In every room, a piece of my heart.

The Hollow Hare no longer speaks,
Its shadows fold, the silence peaks.
The child I was, the self I hide,
Stand hand in hand, now unified.

A flash of fur, a shadowed ear,
Through the warren, all is clear.
The Douady rabbit leaps away,
But leaves its spark to light my way.
