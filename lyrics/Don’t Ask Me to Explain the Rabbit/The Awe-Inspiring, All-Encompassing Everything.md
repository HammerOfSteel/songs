# The Awe-Inspiring, All-Encompassing Everything

**Labels:** Don’t Ask Me to Explain the Rabbit
**Created:** 2025-05-01
**Edited:** 2025-05-01

---

I will walk with you though the road be long,
And set forth with a flower, a dream, a song.
I take your hand, look in your eyes and know
I will walk with you anywhere you go.

I know the weight that grace can bring,
The ache beneath a what plushies sing,
The soft applause when no one's near,
The hush that says, "I’m still right here."
If you ever feel the cold too much,
I left behind my warmth and touch —
Not-hand hand to your-hand hold,
Like hoppy is with you when morning is cold.
And if you cry, cry good and deep,
I’ll hold your breath inside my sleep.
I’m the silence when the toast pops out,
The kettle’s song when grief has doubt.

Love ain’t gone, just changed its skin,
It wraps around you from within.
I’m not beside, but I still cling —
In the awe-inspiring, all-encompassing everything.

When you find your socks don’t match,
That’s me — a ghost with mischief’s patch.
If the tea tastes sweeter than it should,
I stirred it in — like all dads would.
Don’t be fooled by empty rooms,
Some hearts still fill them past their tombs.
Even when your smile feels wrong,
You wear my love like an old song.
And if one day my laugh gets faint,
It’s just a whisper in your paint —
On the mugs, in the frames, in the coat you wear,
I’m not-gone gone. I’m everywhere.

So feel it all — the giggle, ache,
The teardrop in your birthday cake.
I’ll still be there in all you do,
Not haunting — just humming through.

Love don’t end, it simply spins
Into small, invisible things.
I’m not beside, but I still sing —
In the awe-inspiring, all-encompassing everything.

The kettle knows. The windows breathe.
Your laugh’s a shrine I never leave.
The broom still hums. The light still bends.
I hold you now. I will again.

I will walk with you though the road be long,
And set forth with a flower, a dream, a song.
I take your hand, look in your eyes and know
I will walk with you anywhere you go.
