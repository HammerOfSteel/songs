# Little heart, little spark - ALBUM

**Labels:** Little Heart, Little Spark, Album
**Created:** 2025-06-17
**Edited:** 2025-08-12

---

🌙 ALBUM: Little Heart, Little Spark

1. "Cozy, Scary, Dark"

Rhyme Base: Rock-a-bye Baby

Style: Jazz chillhop + subtle horrorcore instrumentation (eerie lullaby piano loops, soft static)

Theme: The comfort of fear when it’s known — like hiding under a blanket. A child’s room at night as a metaphor for adult anxiety.

Chorus Example:
"Cozy, scary, dark / quiet beats in my heart / monsters hum lullabies / under my bed of stars"



---

2. "Pocket Full of Dust"

Rhyme Base: Ring Around the Rosy

Style: Cute-core + trip-hop + dusty boom bap

Theme: Grief disguised as play — catchy, innocent words with a heavy history. Exploring how children cope.

Verse Concepts: References to hiding, spinning, laughing until you fall. Real themes of cycles, pandemic, loss.



---

3. "Lamb on the Moon"

Rhyme Base: Mary Had a Little Lamb

Style: Lo-fi Irish fiddle + 8bit chiptune fusion + soft rap

Theme: Missing the you that you loved when you held little star and believed in. the lamb’s gone, The memory plays on loop.
Seeing her everywhere everyday, seeing other families, children etc. She among the flock on the moon now just out of reach from my hugs

Line Ideas:
"Pixel sky, wool dreams / I rewind old moon beams"



---

4. "Hushheart"

Rhyme Base: Hush, Little Baby

Style: RnB vocal hook + minimalist synth-hop

Theme: A rap from an older sibling or young parent trying to soothe — but they’re breaking too. 

Vibe: Raw. Gentle. Honest.



---

5. "This Little Light"

Rhyme Base: This Little Light of Mine

Style: Gospel blues + boom bap + ukulele + harmonic layers

Theme: A tiny light in a heavy world. Childlike hope held tight in an adult’s chest.
Vocals are child's voice, beat is sweet yet hopeful and cool Grove style mixed bit lullaby

Lyrics are non-native, cool rhymes dwelling into childlike wonder and alchemical undertones blending into quotes etc going out and growing out of the original lyrics of "this little light of mine"


---

6. "Jack Fell Down"

Rhyme Base: Jack and Jill

Style: Jungle drums + glitch-hop + orchestral swells

Theme: Cycles of failure and resilience. Jack falls, again and again. But the well keeps calling. Non-narative, rhyming words mixed with prose's rhyming well, banter, sweet

Tune: UK british Rap fun and sweet acoustic beat



---

7. "Peep"

Rhyme Base: Little Bo Peep

Style: Vaporwave jazz-hop + ghostly violin

Theme: Letting go. Peep’s sheep are not lost. They left. And that’s okay.



---

8. "One Crow Sorrow"

Rhyme Base: One for Sorrow (magpie rhyme)

Style: uk rap, Folk-grime hybrid (string samples + heavy beat)

Theme: Superstition, signs, paranoia. Childish patterns that become adult beliefs.
Non-narative

Lyrics Explore: Counting birds, fate, whispers in the wind.



---

9. "Humpty"

Rhyme Base: Humpty Dumpty

Style: Industrial glitch-hop + dark brass + broken toy sounds

Theme: Fragility. The cracks that never got fixed. But also, maybe, goldleaf repair. (Kintsugi reference)



---

10. "Starfall Fingerpaint"

Rhyme Base: Twinkle Twinkle Little Star

Style: Chillhop + celestial synth pop + downtempo rap

Theme: Wonder and awe from a small soul. Cosmic questions in crayon colors.

Lyric Tone: Thoughtful, poetic. Like a child who sees the universe but doesn’t yet fear it. A child's written rap rhyming well barely coherent at points, questions, no narrative



---

11. "Little Heart, Little Spark" (Title Track)

Rhyme Base: Original, referencing multiple nursery lines

Style: 
UK british RAP, slang
ukulele lead → slow-building layered rap + soft lo-fi jazzhop

Theme: A gentle anthem. Addressing the inner child, the young listener, or a child who’s gone.

Chorus Example:
"Little heart, little spark / lit my world in the dark / still carry thr flame / always always, in your name"

Non-narative, rhyming words like a spell almost, banter but sweet and cozy rap
