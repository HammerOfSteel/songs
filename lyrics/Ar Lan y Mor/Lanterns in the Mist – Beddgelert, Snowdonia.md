# Lanterns in the Mist – Beddgelert, Snowdonia

**Labels:** Dancing salamanders, Ar Lan y Mor
**Created:** 2024-09-19
**Edited:** 2024-09-19

---

Verse 1:
Through the fog, I wander slow,
A lantern in hand, its gentle glow.
Each step I take, the mist draws near,
Grief in my heart, yet hope so clear.

The night is thick, but I press on,
Seeking the light where shadows have gone.
In this haze, I feel her close,
A guiding star, a presence most.

Chorus:
Lanterns in the mist, shining bright,
Guiding us through the darkest night.
Each flame a soul, still burning high,
Leading us where love cannot die.

Verse 2:
As I move, the fog starts to fade,
And I see the lights, a soft parade.
Other lanterns, flickering true,
Held by hands that have lost loved ones too.

Among them, hers, it shines so bold,
A beacon of warmth in the evening cold.
She lights the way, her flame so pure,
In her glow, I find the cure.

Chorus:
Lanterns in the mist, shining bright,
Guiding us through the darkest night.
Each flame a soul, still burning high,
Leading us where love cannot die.

Bridge:
Through the veil, we walk as one,
The lanterns glow like the rising sun.
A path of hope, lit by the lost,
Guiding us through grief’s heavy cost.

Chorus:
Lanterns in the mist, shining bright,
Guiding us through the darkest night.
Each flame a soul, still burning high,
Leading us where love cannot die.

Outro:
And as the mist begins to clear,
Her lantern whispers, soft and near.
I follow now, with hope restored,
Together, we walk toward the shore.
