Verse 1:
Under the Elan skies, so vast and clear,
I gaze up high, and feel you near.
Among the stars, you softly shine,
A light eternal, forever mine.

Each constellation whispers your name,
In every spark, it’s all the same.
Though you're far in the distant night,
You guide me still, with gentle light.

Chorus:
Between the stars, you watch and wait,
In every twinkle, I feel our fate.
Though time may pull us far apart,
You're forever here within my heart.

Verse 2:
Elan’s waters reflect the skies above,
A mirror of our endless love.
In the silent night, I hear your voice,
A celestial song that makes me rejoice.

Through the dark, I feel your hand,
Leading me through this starry land.
Each light that flickers, a sign from you,
Reminding me our bond is true.

Chorus:
Between the stars, you watch and wait,
In every twinkle, I feel our fate.
Though time may pull us far apart,
You're forever here within my heart.

Bridge:
From the valleys below to the heavens high,
Our love stretches far across the sky.
No distance too great, no time too long,
In the stars, I find where you belong.

Final Chorus:
Between the stars, you watch and wait,
In every twinkle, I feel our fate.
Though time may pull us far apart,
You're forever here within my heart.

Outro:
Elan Valley’s night so wide,
Where the stars and my love collide.
I’ll look to the skies and always know,
You’re with me, wherever I go.
