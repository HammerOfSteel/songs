# You Feel Tired but Determined

**Labels:** The Deep Darkness
**Created:** 2025-04-17
**Edited:** 2025-04-17

---

You know that feeling when you think you’re dreaming
but everything's too sharp to be?
It’s late. Or early.
The road doesn’t end — it just keeps curving.
So I walk.

Every mailbox looks like mine.
Same red flag, same warped spine.
Streetlight buzz like a broken song,
I’ve been out here walking way too long.

My backpack’s heavier than before,
Not with things — just ghosts and lore.
A pencil case, a pan, some pins,
A bat that hums where it begins.

[Refrain – soft layered whisper]
You feel tired… but determined.
You feel tired… but you move.
There’s a road. There’s a night.
There’s a beat that says: prove.

Mr. Saturn Ribbon, folded neat,
Still smells like fire and Paula’s heat.
Jess’s Holmes hat, fraying at the brim,
Taped it once — still think of him.

My old cap’s stitched on the side,
From when we thought the sky could hide.
And Poo… well, nothing to show —
No hair, no keepsake. Just that Mu glow.
I smiled. That’s so Poo.

You feel tired… but determined.
You feel tired… but you breathe.
One more block. One more bend.
No one said you had to leave.

I’ve saved the world, but not myself.
Left joy on every other shelf.
I keep swinging even when it’s hollow —
The bat don’t fly, but the feet still follow.

I remember you. And not just your face.
The way you stirred soup. The way you made space.
So I walk. With your weight. With mine.
The sky don't talk, but the stars align.

If I stop, I dream.
If I dream, I rest.
But not yet.
There’s still a bit of road left.

You feel tired.
But you keep going.
