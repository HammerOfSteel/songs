# Jupiters grace - song

**Labels:** The Nature of Things
**Created:** 2024-10-20
**Edited:** 2024-10-24

The little sun, a center so bright,
Pulled me through the endless night.
Orbit tight, no drift, no sway,
We moved through space, come what may.
Your laughter lit the darkest skies,
Constellations drawn from your eyes.
But stars burn out, they don’t delay,
And gravity pulls the heart astray.

Now time drags slow, each second a mile,
Breath by breath, it’s been a while.
Heartbeat distant, one pulse, two—
Every moment, I fall back to you.

Elara, little sun, still shining bright,
Across the void, you bring me light.
Though time pulls back, I move ahead,
Carrying your sun through the night we tread.

Elara, in this endless sky,
You’re still my star, though worlds pass by.
In every echo, I hear you say,
"Love’s not lost, it just finds new ways."

The laws of space don’t yield to love,
Even when sorrow fits tight as a shroud.
Gravity pulls from a time now gone,
While I drift forward, still holding on.
Time’s cruel joke, a broken stream—
Backwards pull, like a half-formed dream.
The past a place I can't let go,
A sunlit fragment in the undertow.

But stars, they shine from years before,
Even light lost still finds the shore.
Your glow, it reaches me from afar,
A distant pulse, my guiding star.

But your gravity, love, will never fade.
Even as time pulls back like a tide,
Your sun still burns, deep inside.

Lost in space, I drift, I roam,
But your light will always guide me home.

Elara, my little sun, you remain,
A cosmic echo through joy and pain.
Though stars may flare and moons may fall,
Your love, your light, outshines them all.
