# Bridges to the Shadows - Pontydd i’r Cysgodion

**Labels:** Blodau yn y Lludw
**Created:** 2025-01-15
**Edited:** 2025-01-15

---

“You know, she was a puzzle I never solved.
A bridge half-built, stretching to a shadow.
And her silence... it said so much,
but it never spoke her name.”

The mist clings heavy, the air feels still,
Each step on the path bends to memory’s will.
The bridge ahead creaks, a quiet refrain,
Each plank a fragment of love and pain.

I see her hand in the lines of the wood,
Splintered and aged, misunderstood.
The river below hums a low, mournful tune,
Carrying whispers beneath the moon.

Bridges to the shadows, where truth hides away,
Where echoes linger, and regrets still play.
She built them in silence, never looking back,
But I feel her love in the spaces of lack.

Her chair sat empty, her gaze fixed low,
A garden of secrets she’d never let grow.
Her laughter once bright, now a phantom of light,
Casting long shadows on this autumn night.

And the dreams she once shared, scattered like leaves,
Holding a truth that no one believes.

Bridges to the shadows, where truth hides away,
Where echoes linger, and memories stay.
The weight of her silence, a language unspoken,
Yet the ties of her love remain unbroken.

Through her letters and trinkets, a map appears,
A journey of grief traced through the years.
Each token a bridge, fragile and thin,
Leading me back to where it begins.

The wind speaks her name in the rustling leaves,
A language of loss only the heart perceives.
I step forward, the shadows recede,
Finding her love in the space I still need.

The moonlight breaks through, a gentle guide,
Illuminating the path where memories abide.
Each bridge I cross, I carry her still,
In the shadows of silence, her love fulfills.

Bridges to the shadows, where truth hides away,
Where echoes linger, and memories stay.
The silence is heavy, but her love is bright,
A bridge to the shadows, now bathed in light.

The river flows on, the bridges endure,
Her shadow beside me, steady and sure.
Though her silence remains, her love still thrives,
In the bridges to shadows, she keeps us alive.
