# The Passage Between Realms

**Labels:** Dancing salamanders
**Created:** 2024-08-20
**Edited:** 2024-08-20

---

Verse 1 (Elara)
In the heart of the Spire, where the light bends low,
I stand at the threshold, not sure where to go.
The passage before me shimmers with light,
"Amin n'rangwa edanea," (I don’t understand this path,)
The journey’s end or the start of the night.

Verse 1 (Ember)
"Na ethuil, ú-'erin râd," (In spring, I do not have a path,)
Ember whispers soft, with a guiding flame.
"I lû toll ú-'uren," (The time has come, without a heart,)
In the dance of shadows, none are the same.

Chorus (Elara and Ember)
Do we step forward, where the realms divide?
Or remain in the Ashen Lands, where shadows hide?
"Tolo, i 'aladh na gaim," (Come, the tree is adorned with leaves,)
The choice is ours, in the twilight’s tide.
"Avo 'osto, ennas estel," (Do not fear, there is hope,)
For in the passage, our fates reside.

Verse 2 (Elara)
I see the world beyond, where the stars still burn,
"Man cenich?" (What do you see?) I ask, as the tides turn.
The unknown calls, like a song in the wind,
Yet the Ashen Lands hold what I’ve learned.

Verse 2 (Ember)
"Gurth goth, amar ú-bedin," (The shadowed land, I do not await,)
Ember replies, with a flame that’s bright.
"Aiya Eldalótë," (Behold, Elven flower,)
We have faced the trials, we’ve earned the right.

Bridge (Elara and Ember)
"Man iest lin?" (What is your wish?) Elara asks with care,
Ember’s light flickers in the silent air.
"Lá erthad nin cuil," (To renew life is not my wish,)
We’ve walked this path, through shadow and fire,
But the choice is yours, at the heart of the Spire.

Chorus (Elara and Ember)
Do we step forward, where the realms divide?
Or remain in the Ashen Lands, where shadows hide?
"Tolo, i 'aladh na gaim," (Come, the tree is adorned with leaves,)
The choice is ours, in the twilight’s tide.
"Avo 'osto, ennas estel," (Do not fear, there is hope,)
For in the passage, our fates reside.

Outro (Elara and Ember)
"Amin n'rangwa edanea," (I don’t understand this path,) Elara softly sighs,
"Nae saian luume," (It has been too long,) Ember replies.
Together they stand, where the worlds collide,
In the passage of light, their futures bide.

With hands entwined, they face the dawn,
For whatever they choose, the journey goes on.
"Amin hiraetha," (I am sorry,) Elara whispers low,
But in the passage between, they both shall grow.


After passing the test, Elara discovers the gateway between realms within the Spire and must decide whether to move forward into the unknown or remain in the Ashen Lands. The song is a dialogue between Elara and Ember, exploring the tension between the comfort of the familiar and the call to embrace the unknown. It embodies the pivotal choice that will define the next stage of Elara’s journey.
