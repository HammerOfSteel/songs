# Ace of Spades

Place: Tintagel Castle

Verse 1
At the edge of Tintagel, where the waves collide,
The wind whispers truths I’ve kept inside.
These stones, they’ve seen a thousand years,
But today they crumble my hidden fears.
In the ruins, I find what’s been lost,
Breaking through the fog, no matter the cost.
The cliffs beneath, like thoughts set free,
I see the world, but clearer, like me.

Chorus
Ace of Spades in my hand tonight,
Cutting through shadows to find the light.
Every step on this sacred ground,
Brings me closer to the truth I’ve found.
Ace of Spades, the mind’s own blade,
Through the storm, I’ll never fade.

For in this castle, worn and old,
I’ve found the key to a heart made bold.
