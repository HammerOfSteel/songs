# Dartmoor

**Labels:** Dancing salamanders, Gills and guts
**Created:** 2024-09-20
**Edited:** 2024-09-20

---

Place: Bodmin Moor

Verse 1
Wanderin’ through the moor at night,
Beneath the stars, so cold, so bright.
The wind whispers secrets in the breeze,
A voice unknown, puts my soul at ease.
The world below, it fades away,
And I stand alone, in the quiet sway.

Pre-Chorus
Endless sky, untamed land,
A boundless space, I understand.
I walk the moor, with hope in sight,
Even in darkness, there’s still light.

Chorus
Dartmoor calls, in the midnight air,
Lost in the vastness, but I don’t despair.
In the wild unknown, I’m free to roam,
Out here in the moor, I’ve found my home.
Stars above, they guide my way,
In the night’s embrace, I’ll never stray.

Verse 2
Each step I take, the earth so wide,
No walls to hold, nowhere to hide.
The heather bends, beneath my feet,
A rhythm slow, a heartbeat deep.
The moor, it echoes thoughts I keep,
Of dreams I’ve sown, and hopes I reap.

Bridge

In every turn, I’ve nothing to hide.

The land is wide, but fear is small,
And in this space, I stand tall.
