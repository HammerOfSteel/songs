# London Mist

**Labels:** Dancing salamanders, Daltokki
**Created:** 2024-09-10
**Edited:** 2024-09-10

---

Cobblestone streets in morning’s haze,
London’s whispers, a timeless maze.
Big Ben chimes, as we stroll hand in hand,
In this ancient city, our hearts expand.

From Tower Bridge to Hyde Park’s green,
In every corner, love is seen.

Whispers in the London mist,
In the city’s heart, we can’t resist.
Through historic streets, our love persists,
In London’s glow, we coexist.

Covent Garden’s lively cheer,
Street performers bring us near.
In cozy pubs, where stories blend,
We find our love, around each bend.

From Camden’s buzz to Notting Hill’s charm,
In every moment, love is warm.

Whispers in the London mist,
In the city’s heart, we can’t resist.
Through historic streets, our love persists,
In London’s glow, we coexist.

Beneath the Eye, the city unfolds,
A panorama of dreams untold.
In museums grand, history’s embrace,
In London’s past, we find our place.

As the fog rolls in on Thames’s shore,
We walk the paths where legends soar.
In every whisper of the misty night,
Our love shines through, pure and bright.

From Westminster’s lights to Soho’s beat,
In every glance, our hearts meet.

Whispers in the London mist,
In the city’s heart, we can’t resist.
Through historic streets, our love persists,
In London’s glow, we coexist.

As the night fades and dawn’s light gleams,
In London’s mist, we chase our dreams.
Whispers in the London mist, forever bound,
In this storied city, love is found.
