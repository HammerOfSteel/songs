# Vodník

**Edited:** 2024-09-12

In the water's cool embrace, I chase the tide,
Climbing higher, pushing on, with nowhere to hide,
My father's debts, they pull me down, like anchors in the deep,
Loan sharks circle, shadows cast, I barely get to sleep.

Vodník, Vodník, watching from the shore,
Is the deal you offer worth what I adore?
Right or wrong, who’s to say, when the line is blurred,
Is it salvation, or am I just unheard?

Vodník, Vodník, z rieky voláš ma,

He sees me struggle, sees my fall, whispers in the night,
“Take my hand, release the pain, and end this endless fight,”
But the waters dark, the price unknown, I hesitate in fear,
Am I drowning in his offer, or is it crystal clear?

The weight lifts, the sharks retreat, but shadows still remain,
What have I lost, what have I gained, in Vodník’s murky game?
The water’s calm, yet ripples spread, beneath the silent moon,
I made my choice, now I must face, the echo of the tune.

Music Style:
Acoustic with subtle electronic elements, deep bass lines, haunting strings, and rhythmic percussion, blending modern and folklore tones.


The story of the swimmer's encounter with Vodník, blending his internal struggle with external pressures, and leaving the moral outcome ambiguous for the listener to ponder.
