# Gills and Guts

**Labels:** Dancing salamanders, Gills and guts
**Created:** 2024-09-20
**Edited:** 2024-09-20

---

Place: Kynance Cove

Verse 1
At the edge of the world, where the cliffs meet the sea,
I stand alone, as broken as can be.
The waves crash wild, like the storm in my soul,
Kynance’s jagged rocks taking their toll.
The sky’s gone grey, the colors have fled,
Gills and guts, the life we once led.
It’s all exposed, torn and raw,
A world of loss, no room for awe.

Pre-Chorus
But in the tide’s pull, I feel the fight,
The world may break, but there’s still light.
Through every wave that tears me down,
I find a strength, though I may drown.

Chorus
Gills and guts, ripped apart,
But there’s beauty in this broken heart.
In the crashing waves, I find my voice,
In the depths of pain, I have no choice.
Gills and guts, bare and true,
I survive this storm, because of you.

Verse 2
The world spins on, though I stand still,
The jagged rocks of Kynance kill.
Every memory, like a tide that pulls,
A world of loss that never dulls.
But in the wreckage, I find my core,
The gills and guts I can’t ignore.
Though torn apart, I start to see,
This brokenness is setting me free.

Pre-Chorus
And in the darkness, I feel the rise,
A whisper of hope, beneath the skies.
The sea may rage, the earth may crack,
But in this pain, I find my track.

Chorus
Gills and guts, ripped apart,
But there’s beauty in this broken heart.
In the crashing waves, I find my voice,
In the depths of pain, I have no choice.
Gills and guts, bare and true,
I survive this storm, because of you.

Bridge
The sea pulls back, but the scars remain,
A testament to all the pain.
But in the echoes, I hear the call,
Even in pieces, I stand tall.

Final Chorus
Gills and guts, torn and frayed,
But through the storm, I’ve found my way.
In the jagged rocks and swirling tides,
I find my soul, where love abides.
Gills and guts, bare and true,
I survive this storm, because of you.

Outro
At Kynance Cove, where the waters roar,
I stand in grief, but I am more.
Gills and guts, torn apart,
But I’ll carry on with this broken heart.
