# Clara färdiga gå

**Labels:** Llwybrau Bywyd
**Created:** 2024-11-30
**Edited:** 2024-11-30

---

Morning sunbeams gently play,
Whispers of a brand-new day.

Clara wakes with sleepy eyes,
Today's the race; nerves arise.
Mom and sister by her side,
Offer hugs, their hearts open wide.

Clara, ready, go!
Let your inner light show.
Leave your worries all behind,
Run with joy, your heart aligned.

At the track, the children cheer,
Clara's heart beats with quiet fear.
She sees the others' anxious eyes,
Realizes she's not alone in sighs.

Lined up now, the whistle's near,
Clara takes a breath to clear.
With a leap, she's in the race,
Determined stride, a steady pace.

Clara, ready, go!
Let your inner light show.
Leave your worries all behind,
Run with joy, your heart aligned.

Midway through, her legs feel weak,
Doubts arise, her outlook bleak.
Then she glimpses, soft and true,
Runners’ tailwinds pushing through.
She feels her own, its gentle song,
Her cadence flows, her steps grow strong.

With every breath, her rhythm's clear,
The tailwind hums, its voice sincere.
Clara lets it freely go,
And finds herself in joyful flow.
The world aligns, her heart takes flight,
Each stride aglow with golden light.

In the race, she finds her flow,
Letting all her worries go.
Smiling wide, her spirit soars,
Light and love unlock new doors.

Across the field, the end in sight,
Her steps a dance, her heart alight.
The wind, her friend, now fades away,
She carries strength from this bright day.
The finish line, her family’s cheer,
Their joy resounds, her path made clear.

Clara, ready, go!
Let your inner light show.
Leave your worries all behind,
Run with joy, your heart aligned.

So, dear Clara, always know,
Life’s a race where love will grow.
With each step and every start,
Run the race that’s in your heart.
