# Wild Apollonian Nets

**Labels:** Koch star
**Created:** 2024-12-03
**Edited:** 2024-12-04

---

Coffee’s cold, the clock’s too loud,
Every face feels like a crowd.
One thought tangles into two,
A web of whispers pulling through.

One thread stretches, one snaps away,
Another one casts shade on the day.
A laugh that lands, a voice unknown,
The sunflowers seed is sown

Wild Apollonian nets, they weave,
Between myself I can’t believe.
Each thread connects, but some divide,
I walk their lines, I cannot hide.

A friend who calls, their words are kind,
But I can’t trust this fractured mind.
Did I forget, or did they stray?
One more thread just slips away.

Wild Apollonian nets, they weave,
Between the selves I can’t retrieve.
Each thread connects, but some divide,
I walk their lines, I cannot hide.

"I sit across from you, my words misaligned,
You see my smile, but not the web behind.
Each strand holds weight, each knot a name,
I hold them all, but not the same."

The threads I feared, they catch my fall,
Each one holds a piece of it all.
Not walls of silk, but ties that bind,
A map that leads through this fractured mind.

Wild Apollonian nets, they weave,
A strength I never could perceive.
Each thread connects, where love resides,
I walk their lines, no need to hide.

Another web will cast again,
A tangle born of loss or friends.
But in this maze, I’ve found my way,
The threads I hold will never fray.

Coffee’s warm, the clock’s still loud,
But I’m okay within the crowd.
Each tangled thread, a bond I’ve grown,
In every line, I find my own.
