# Every Star Deserves to Shine - A Lullaby for Felix the Star-Tinker

**Labels:** childrens books - star sailor
**Created:** 2024-10-07
**Edited:** 2024-10-07

---

Intro:
Through the silent sky where the constellations gleam,
In a workshop of wonders, like a place from a dream,
A small fox works quietly, with a soft, gentle sigh,
As he pieces together the stars in the sky.

His paws dance through stardust, his eyes bright with care,
Though mistakes leave him stumbling, with each fragile tear.
But he keeps on trying, his heart set in line,
For he knows in his soul that each star should shine.

Verse 1:
Felix the fox, with his fur dusted bright,
Sits at his workbench through the long, quiet night.
“Why can’t I get it right?” he sighs in dismay,
As a small, shattered star slips and falls away.

But Star Sailor appears, in her crescent-moon glow,
“You’re still trying, Felix—that’s how stars grow.”
“It’s not about never breaking,” she whispers so kind,
“But how you keep mending, with hope in your mind.”

Chorus:
“Let your paws trace the cracks, let your light weave through,
For stars need the broken to be made anew.”
“Every scar tells a story, every flaw hides a grace,
And in patching each fracture, you find your own place.”

“So don’t fear the pieces that slip from your hold,
For even cracked stars deserve to shine bold.”
“Let your heart guide the way, let your spirit align,”
“For every star, even broken, deserves to shine.”

Verse 2:
They work side by side, with starlight that weaves,
Through fractures and fragments like threads in the leaves.
Felix’s paws steady, as Star Sailor hums,
And the broken star glows, like a new day that comes.

“It’s fixed!” Felix cries, his eyes wide and bright,
“We made it together—it’s glowing with light!”
But Star Sailor shakes her head with a smile so sweet,
“You gave it a chance—its story’s complete.”

Bridge:
“Remember,” she whispers, “when you feel small,”
“That even great stars can sometimes fall.”
“It’s the mending that matters, the courage to try,”
“To give them new wings, to rise through the sky.”

“For flaws don’t define us, and cracks don’t degrade,”
“They show where we stumbled, but also where we stayed.”
“And when stars shine again, through your care and design,”
“It’s your light that’s reflected, and your heart that aligns.”

Chorus (Reprise):
“Let your paws trace the cracks, let your light weave through,
For stars need the broken to be made anew.”
“Every scar tells a story, every flaw hides a grace,
And in patching each fracture, you find your own place.”

“So don’t fear the pieces that slip from your hold,
For even cracked stars deserve to shine bold.”
“Let your heart guide the way, let your spirit align,”
“For every star, even broken, deserves to shine.”

Outro:
As dawn breaks softly, in hues of rose gold,
The workshop glows gently, its stories retold.
Felix stands proudly, with a smile on his face,
For he’s learned that mending is a beautiful grace.

“Thank you,” he whispers, as Star Sailor waves,
Her crescent boat drifting through light-streaked caves.
And she sails through the stars, her glow warm and bright,
Leaving Felix the fox to keep fixing the night.
