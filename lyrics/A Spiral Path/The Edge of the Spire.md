# The Edge of the Spire

**Labels:** A Spiral Path, Dancing salamanders
**Created:** 2024-09-27
**Edited:** 2024-09-27

---

(Verse 1)
At the edge of the spire, I stand alone,
With power in my hands, but a heart turned to stone.
I’ve wandered the world, I’ve learned what I can,
Now the serpent coils tight, testing this man.
Tenjin whispers, from the spire so high,
"Is this the path that will truly satisfy?"
Revenge may be sweet, but at what cost?
Will I find myself, or will I be lost?

(Pre-Chorus)
The weight of vengeance, heavy in my chest,
But a voice inside questions, "Is this truly best?"

(Chorus)
At the edge of the spire, I stand at the brink,
Do I fight, or step back, before I sink?
A second chance waits, like the calm after fire,
But can I walk away, from all my desire?

(Verse 2)
In the shadows of Valia, I see my fate,
A path of blood, or a chance to create.
The face of Tenjin, like a flame in the night,
Shows me the way, but I blur my sight.
Can I let go of the rage I’ve kept near,
Or will I be consumed by the loss and the fear?
The children of Vaelien still taunt me with glee,
But is vengeance truly the key to be free?

(Pre-Chorus)
The weight of vengeance, heavy in my chest,
But the serpent’s gaze, it questions, "What’s best?"

(Chorus)
At the edge of the spire, I stand at the brink,
Do I fight, or step back, before I sink?
A second chance waits, like the calm after fire,
But can I walk away, from all my desire?

(Bridge)
The power I sought, now burns in my soul,
But redemption whispers, a quieter goal.
The spire beneath me, it feels so tall,
One step forward, and I could lose it all.
Yet in the silence, a new voice calls,
A future of peace, beyond these walls.

(Chorus)
At the edge of the spire, I stand at the brink,
Do I fight, or step back, before I sink?
A second chance waits, like the calm after fire,
But can I walk away, from all my desire?

(Outro)
Tenjin’s light, like a beacon in the night,
Shows me the choice, the wrong or the right.
As the winds howl, I make my stand,
Will I seek vengeance, or open my hand?
