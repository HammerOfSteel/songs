# Tides In Memories Arbor - song

**Labels:** Tides In Memorys Arbor
**Created:** 2024-11-02
**Edited:** 2024-11-02

---

Intro
Oh, Little Sun, my Star Sailor bright,
Held by the tides, in memory’s light.
Through worlds unseen, I hold you close,
In every wave, in every coast.

Verse 1
You came like a comet, a star in my arms,
A spark in my hands, safe from all harm.
Your ocean eyes gazed up at me,
All I never knew I needed, set free.

Cradled close, my Little Sun,
With light so pure, our journey begun.
In the cradle of my hands, your light shone true,
My heart forever held by you.

Chorus
Oh, Little Sun, my Star Sailor so wise,
Across the boundless sky you rise.
Through each tide and every shore,
Forever mine, though we walk distant shores.

Verse 2
Curiosity sparked, my Star Sailor bold,
Wandering where the waves unfold.
In each tiny treasure, it’s you I find,
A spellbound list of wonders, pure and kind:

Vegetable soup and marmalade toast,
Sandcastles built by the sea coast.
Green hills and late-night cuddles tight,
Hoppy the Hippo held each night.
Paint like Mom’s, Moomin tales,
Butterflies in sunlit trails.
Stars and sea, laughter and light,
In these moments, pure delight.

Your favorite things, a magic cast,
Moments held, forever last.

Chorus
Oh, Star Sailor, you’re the light of my skies,
In dreams, I see you with wondrous eyes.
Through every wave and whisper, near or far,
You sail beyond, my guiding star.

Verse 3
I’ll hold you forever, my Little Sun,
Though now it’s your mother’s arms you’re held in, one.
As I take the long way around,
Tides memory’s arbor resound.

You are my whole world, my joy, my tide,
Every laugh, every tear I can’t hide.
Someday we’ll meet where stars don’t set,
But until then, my love, I won’t forget.

Chorus
Oh, Little Sun, Star Sailor so free,
Across the vast sky and endless sea.
Through every starlit night, you guide me home,
Until the tides bring us together, never alone.

Outro
Bound by the tides, in memories, we flow,
Star Sailor, Little Sun, wherever you go.
In the whisper of waves, in the silence of night,
You’re the dawn in my heart, the everlasting light
