# Aurum Astrale: The Astrologer’s Gold

**Labels:** Dancing salamanders, Lucerna Obscura
**Created:** 2024-08-21
**Edited:** 2024-08-22

---

"In the heavens’ gold, the stars weave their silent tales. The astrologer’s gaze follows the celestial thread, tracing the path of light."

Verse 1
In the night’s vast sea where the heavens turn,
A Watcher gazes, where the stars burn.
Through the Sphaera Caelestis, his eyes do roam,
Seeking the path to his distant home.

"Stellae Arcanum," whispers from afar,
In the dance of light, where the secrets are.
He reads the signs in the golden threads,
Where fate and time weave their silver heads.

Chorus
Aurum Astrale, the gold of the skies,
In the stars’ embrace, the truth lies.
Through the Celestial Flame, he finds the key,
Unlocking the door to eternity.

The Heavenly Gold, in the stars it gleams,
In the silent night, where the soul dreams.
Aurum Astrale, where wisdom’s told,
In the Watcher’s hand, the stars unfold.

Verse 2
In the depths of night, where shadows breathe,
The Watcher casts his cosmic wreath.
Through the Zodiacus, the wheel turns slow,
Marking the passage of what we know.

"Astra Ignis," the starry fire,
Guides his hand in the ancient choir.
He charts the course of the Ordo, grand,
In the silent sweep of the heavens’ hand.

Bridge
In the Firmamentum, where Lux does spin,
The Watcher sees where fate begins.
"Vitae Auriga," the charioteer’s flight,
Guides the soul through the endless night.

Through the Stellatum, the truth revealed,
In the Aurum’s light, the heart is healed.
The Watcher knows the cosmic tune,
In the stars above, the ancient rune.

Outro
As the dawn breaks, the night’s retreat,
The Watcher’s work in silence complete.
"Aurum Invisibile," the gold unseen,
In the Astral Light, the truth is gleaned.

The stars fade, but their mark remains,
In the Watcher’s heart, the golden veins.
Aurum Astrale, the heavens’ gold,
In the eternal night, the tale is told.
