# Bore da cariad.

**Labels:** Dragons Breath
**Created:** 2025-07-07
**Edited:** 2025-07-07

---

Click-a-clack, goes the railway hum, steam and ghosts.
Sun pulls shadows that sunbath on the coast.
Said it again, 
over your train bed bunk, 
by the tiny sill
Bore da, cariad,
quiet, but still.

Riscan blue in hues of cold.
Tippy tiny tapping feet, just a years old
Catch you waddle over, big bright and bold.
Tucked in the duckie, not even a fuss,
Outside in our garden the bees go buzz.
Giggle’ by the bin, windy train platform stares
You have my heart, the wind has your hair.
Cornwall kipped crooked blue tent,
Sea whisperin’ dreams through nylon bent.
Burn coffee as per, pure tradition.
Ofnadwy, I swear this BnB
Never been better aye, blimey.
This is lush, this is grand, this is life on toast
It's a burnt out Choir, full of ghosts.

Bore da, cariad… bore da, mam,
We still go walkin’ where the bluebells ran.
One mug, two spoons, here I stand,
Bore da, cariad… hold my hand.

Train sounds like heartbeat under the track
London to Swansea, we’d nap back to back
You taught me to love the world in its mess
The breadcrumbs of joy, not chasin’ success
We’d whisper like spies in cathedral pews
Poppin’ into Greggs like it’s fine dining news
Fish n chips at Barry, paper torn by gulls
You say “dwi’n dy garu di,” mouth full,
cheeky skulls
One time in Aber, it rained inside
We just danced in the tent till it steamed with pride
Now I hold those moments in second-hand light
Callin’ to the dawn, but you don’t write.

Bore da, cariad… croeso’r haul
I still hum the tune when the kettle boils
Same cup, same spoon, same old soil
Bore da, cariad… life still toils*

You said: “The morning forgives what the night don’t say”
So I say it back now, in our own way
Your smile’s in the steam, in the jam on my bread
In the little footstep stories we never said.

This cup’s got cracks, but it holds the brew
Like my heart with you stitched in every hue
A ghost ain’t gone when it still gets fed
By habits and laughter and things we said
One sock’s yours, yeah, I still mismatch
One glove, one scarf, but I make it catch
You said “no rush, just sip your tea”
Now every morning feels like poetry
So I wake, and I stretch, and I don’t say much
But the sun through the blinds gives a mother’s touch.

Bore da, cariad… the sky’s still kind
I pour two cups though it’s just one mind
Train of thought always leaves on time
Bore da, cariad… you’re still mine*
