# The Weight of Silence

**Labels:** A Spiral Path, Dancing salamanders
**Created:** 2024-09-25
**Edited:** 2024-09-25

---

Verse 1:
In the shadow of her loss, I stand alone,
A father’s hands, trembling, but not made of stone.
I reached for my sisters, but they turned away,
Fearing the weight of words they couldn’t say.

Pre-Chorus:
In the silence, I hear the unspoken pain,
Their love wrapped in fear, like a heavy chain.
But the words unsaid cut deeper than steel,
A chasm between us I can no longer heal.

Chorus:
The weight of silence, it pulls me under,
Like a storm without sound, like distant thunder.
I walk through the unknown, the unclaimed land,
Genbu’s valor in my heart, though I barely stand.
Where is the voice to speak her name,
To break this curse, this silent shame?

Verse 2:
They think that if they speak, I’ll shatter like glass,
But it’s the silence that makes these moments pass.
In the void between us, I see their fear,
But what I need most is to feel them near.

Pre-Chorus:
The Unclaimed Lands, the wilderness of grief,
I tread alone, seeking the smallest relief.
But the silence is vast, and so is the weight,
As if my sorrow is tied to this fate.

Chorus:
The weight of silence, it pulls me under,
Like a storm without sound, like distant thunder.
I walk through the unknown, the unclaimed land,
Genbu’s valor in my heart, though I barely stand.
Where is the voice to speak her name,
To break this curse, this silent shame?

Bridge:
Can you not see, it’s words I crave,
To lift this weight from an unmarked grave.
Through silence, I bear it all alone,
Like a king without a throne.

Final Chorus:
The weight of silence, it pulls me under,
Like a storm without sound, like distant thunder.
I walk through the unknown, the unclaimed land,
Genbu’s valor in my heart, though I barely stand.
But I will speak her name, let the silence break,
For her memory’s sake, for our love’s sake.

Outro:
In the land unclaimed, I’ll speak her name,
And in the silence, she’ll rise like flame.
