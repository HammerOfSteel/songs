# Paper Planes

**Labels:** Geddon Bird
**Created:** 2024-10-29
**Edited:** 2024-10-29

---

(Intro – Spoken)
[Amelia]
“Alright, last one. What’s this one say?”
[Ella]:
“Something brilliant, obviously.”
[Amelia]:
“Obviously. Burn or fold?”
[Ella]:
“Oh, definitely burn.”

(Verse 1)
[Amelia]:
"Dear Future [Ella], don’t you forget—"
[Ella]:
“Always make time to adopt a pet.”
[Amelia]:
“What does that even mean?”
[Ella]:
“Fifteen-year-old wisdom. Don’t question it, Queen.”

[Amelia]:
We laugh, then fold the edges tight,
A paper plane ready for flight.
[Ella]:
The wind grabs hold, it dips, it spins—
Like every plan that’s ever been.

(Chorus – Together)
Paper planes drift high, drift low,
They soar, they fall, and down they go.
Ashes rise, letters burn bright,
We set them free in flickering light.

(Verse 2 – Dialogue, playful banter)
[Amelia]:
"This one's deep: You’ll figure it out."
[Ella]:
“Wow. How profound—without a doubt.”
[Amelia]:
"Was that me or you?"
[Ella]:
"Does it matter who?
We both wrote nonsense, that much is true."

[Amelia]:
We toss it in—ash takes the page.
Old letters crumble like words with age.
[Ella]:
The flames dance high, the past burns thin—
One less weight we carried within.

(Chorus – Together)
Paper planes drift high, drift low,
They soar, they fall, and down they go.
Ashes rise, letters burn bright,
We set them free in flickering light.

(Bridge – Slower, reflective)
[Ella]:
Do you remember the park in spring,
Climbing trees, pretending to sing?
[Amelia]:
We built worlds out of sticks and sand,
And swore we’d never let go of this land.

[Ella]:
But here we are, paper planes in hand,
Learning to let go—wasn’t part of the plan.
[Amelia]:
It never is, but we carry the song—
The parts we love, they stay all along.

(Chorus – Together, bittersweet)
Paper planes drift high, drift low,
They soar, they fall, and down they go.
Ashes rise, letters burn bright,
We set them free in flickering light.

(Outro – Dialogue, soft and slow)
[Amelia]:
“One last plane, before we head out?”
[Ella]:
“Of course—fold it tight, no room for doubt.”
[Amelia]:
“We burn or we fly?”
[Ella]:
“We do both. Goodbye.”

(Soft guitar fades as they laugh quietly, the crackle of fire lingering.)
