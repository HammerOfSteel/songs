# "Echoes Beyond the Tides" (Welsh: Eilydd Dros Y Tonau)

**Labels:** The Nature of Things, Dancing salamanders
**Created:** 2024-10-24
**Edited:** 2024-10-24

---

[Verse 1]
I cast a bottle into the sea,
A whisper carried far from me.
Words adrift on restless waves,
Seeking shores my heart still craves.

Days and nights blend into one,
Under skies with no rising sun.
A prayer to currents, wild and deep,
For love that distance cannot keep.

[Chorus]
Oh, tides that turn, come back again,
Carry my voice through storm and rain.
To where she waits beyond the shore,
And echoes answer, forevermore.
Each wave that breaks will call her name,
And though I’m lost, love stays the same.

[Verse 2]
The wind has stilled, no stars remain,
But hope still sails on the smallest flame.
The ocean knows, it never lies—
Each wave returns, though one heart dies.

Through shifting tides, I’ll drift alone,
But in my heart, I still hear home.
Her voice, a song, so soft, so clear,
A melody only the sea can hear.

[Chorus]
Oh, tides that turn, come back again,
Carry my voice through storm and rain.
To where she waits beyond the shore,
And echoes answer, forevermore.
Each wave that breaks will call her name,
And though I’m lost, love stays the same.

[Bridge]
If waves can hold the past in time,
Then every ripple is a sign.
Each memory that drifts away
Returns upon the next bright day.

[Outro]
So cast my heart into the deep,
Where love and sorrow learn to sleep.
And though she stands on distant sand,
I’ll reach her still—wave to land.

Rwy’n taflu llestr i’r môr glas,
Gair mud yn hedfan, yn chwilio’r traws.
Ar don gaboledig mae’n teithio’n rhydd,
I gyrraedd glannau fy nghalon sydd.

Dydd a nos yn bleth yn mynd,
O dan awyr heb haul yn codi na mynd.
Gweddi i’r tonnau dwfn a gwyllt,
Am gariad nad yw’r pellter yn fylch.

O, tonnau’n troi, dewch yn ôl,

Pob ton sy’n dorri, ei henw sy’n galw,
Er fy mod ar goll, mae cariad yn dalu.

Os daw’r tonnau â’r gorffennol i’r awr,
Pob gragen ar lan yw hen fflach gawr.

Daw yn ôl gyda’r wawr i’r dŵr.

[Outro]

Lle dysgir cariad a hiraeth i dor.
