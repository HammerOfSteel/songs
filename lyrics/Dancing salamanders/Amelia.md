Verse 1
Born by the sea, where ideas set free,
From children's play, dreams and hopes came to stay.
In the whispers of the waves, she found her name,
In the tides of change, her heart aflame.

Pre-Chorus
Time for bed, with tomes half read,
Dancing starts above her head.
In the stars, she saw her fate,
A journey calling, she couldn’t wait.

Chorus
Amelia, oh Amelia,
In the heart of the storm, you'll find your way.
Through the shadows and the trials,
Your courage lights the darkest day.
