# Sånger

**Labels:** Johan Lind
**Created:** 2025-07-20
**Edited:** 2025-12-22

---

A tabby sleeps on karma slips,
Jesus sips from office cups.
Socks for sages, ghosts in halls,
And Buddha watching shopping malls.

Falukorv becomes divine,
With coffee prayers at half past nine.

In riddim sweet and mid-day sun,
We chant of chores, of saints, of fun.

It’s dub and doctrine, ska and sin,
The sacred weirdness lives within.

---

1. “Jesus åt min lunchlåda” (Jesus Ate My Lunchbox)

Concept: A surreal office breakroom tale where Jesus quietly swaps your falukorv for eternal wisdom.
Tone: Reverent meets ridiculous.
Style: slow dub shuffle, soft melodica, lo-fi snare taps, spoken/sung verses


---

2. “Karmakatten” (karmic cat)

Concept: Sorting out your past lives but a tabby keeps curling up in your karmic receipts.
Tone: Existential domestic comedy.
Style: fun mid tempo reggea groove, offbeat guitar pokes, upright bass, distant synth choir pads


---

3. “Gud på en sparkcykel” (God on a Kick Scooter)

Concept: A divine being rides through a suburb at dusk on an electric kickbike, waving like a postman, but wait.. don't he just pass that curb.. oh no.. it's looping again, let's hope it's not some new junior hire for the ChiestOps team that pushed this to production (play on IT dev office stuff)
Tone: Holy and pedestrian, office, mundane etc
Style: reggae-kvällspromenad, distant reverb guitar, brushed percussion, bassoon cameo


---

4. “Min moster är min moralteolog” (My Aunt is My Moral Theologian)

Concept: A series of questionable life choices narrated with judgmental backing vocals and cookie crumbs.
Tone: Family banter meets inner crisis.
Style: ska-funk shuffle, kitchen utensils as percussion, call-and-response vocals


---

5. “Parkbänksprofeter” (Prophet on a Park Bench)

Concept: A prophet’s wisdom only comes while feeding ducks and complaining about e-scooters.
Tone: Deeply silly and genuinely wise.
Style: acoustic-ska fusion, gentle fingerpicking, flute fills, upright bass


---

6. “Korvstoppning och konfirmationen” (Sausage Stuffing and Confirmation Class)

Concept: A memory of religious instruction that felt more like forced learning – but with hot dogs and harmonium.
Tone: Critical but playful.
Style: old-school ska beat, children’s choir outro, broken organ bridge


---

7. “Amen och andra mynt” (Amen and Other Coins)

Concept: A poetic reflection on faith, vending machines, and gratitude at 3:14 a.m.
Tone: Philosophical, sleepy, humorous.
Style: soft reggae waltz, vibraphone, muted brass, spoken word with echo

---

8. “Sockor till Sokrates” (Socks for Socrates)

Concept: A dreamy monologue about mailing wool socks to philosophers and getting back silence and static.
Vibe: Lo-fi beat, dub bass, dusty attic organ, jazzy blues swing undertones
Tone: Warm, slightly existential


---

9. “kaffet I köket och det heliga spöket” (coffe in the kitchen and the holy ghost)

Concept: A tired soul seeks salvation via espresso, latte liturgies, and divine crema.
Tjena, tjena, yrvakna mittbena.
Solve et och koagula, 
kom vi går innan vi blir sena.
Ah men vänta lite gubben kaffet har inte ens kokat klart, kan ju knappt tänka klart.
Upp på pulkan drar och drar,
Yrvaken snöstormen dånar helt underbar.
Hej hej Godmorgon gamla plugg,
Int har vi saknat dig ett endaste dugg.
Okej okej, dags att säga hej, hejdå
Se så, ha en bra dag,
Bara du och jag och vårat rutin,
Slänger dig en kyss och min vanliga miin.

Klingande klankar skorna av lite snö,
Upp i köket sockret strö,
Strö i kaffet svart som dagen nu är kort,
Eller för själens lilla komfort.
Surrar och skämtar en intre dialog,
Kaffet i köket och det heliga spöket

Vibe: jazzy ska groove, soft horns, steam hiss FX
Tone: Sacred and sleep-deprived


---

10. “Bananskal från Babylon” (Banana Peel from Babylon)

Concept: A journey begins with slipping, not walking — maybe that’s fine.
Vibe: dub-surf fusion, reverb guitar, woozy brass
Tone: Mythic nonsense, poetic drift




---

11. “Ett altare i tvättstugan” (An Altar in the Laundry Room)

Concept: A vision among socks and softener. A holy spin cycle.
Vibe: acoustic-ska with dub undertones, soft harmonies
Tone: Domestic divinity


---

12. “Hej alla Helgon i hallen” (hello all Saints in the Hallway)

Concept: Christmas with "family" this year again, you don't want this, you can't.. you're daugther is gone and they just don't.. the silence, the unberable fucking silence, the unrelenting cheer. You should scream, you should just go.
Vibe: minimal dub-poetry style, footstep FX, echo
Tone: Passive agressive, irony and subtle hints.


---

13. “Horoskop och hushållsost” (Horoscopes and Household Cheese)

Concept: The stars speak... through dairy.
Vibe: reggae shuffle with kazoo solos
Tone: Nonsense meets mysticism


---

14. “Domedagen i Djursholm” (Judgment Day in the Suburbs)

Concept: Trumpets blow. Nothing happens. Mail still comes.
Vibe: funky ska, gospel-choir break, slap bass
Tone: Satirical, calm-panic


---

15. “Buddha på balkongen” (Buddha on the Balcony)

Concept: He’s been there for months, watching the neighbours, mindfully meddling meditating on their life prolithicly profiling. Nobody seems to notice him.. is.. is he only in my mind - let go of your self proclaimed suffering he says
Vibe: mellow dub groove, windchimes, lo-fi brass
Tone: Still, weirdly profound


---

16. “profetter i pyjamas” Prophets in Pyjamas)

Concept: She never leaves the flat but knows exactly when to water the plants.
Vibe: ska-meets-muzak, soft keys, dub delay
Tone: Respectfully ridiculous
