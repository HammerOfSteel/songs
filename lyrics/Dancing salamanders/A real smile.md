# A real smile

**Labels:** Dancing salamanders, Drink deeply, and dream
**Created:** 2024-09-07
**Edited:** 2024-09-07

---

A song about the profound moment of realization, where the weight of life’s trials and the pain of self-doubt are confronted and transformed into a renewed sense of purpose. Through the symbolic journey of a bird and a young girl, the narrative explores the delicate balance between despair and hope, ultimately choosing the path where the real work of healing begins.

Verse 1
Imagine a mountain, ancient and high,
Rising from a plain beneath a starless sky.
A bird descends, its beak it wears,
On stone so vast, untouched by airs.
Every ten thousand years, it comes to rest,
And leaves a mark, its silent quest.
In a hidden grove, a girl stands near,
Watching the bird, her heart unclear.

Chorus
In the shadow of time, where whispers blend,
We walk a path with no clear end.
Through silent winds and echoes lost,
We seek the truth, no matter the cost.
With each step forward, through night’s deep veil,
We chase the dawn for a smile that’s real.

Verse 2
In the heart of dusk, where shadows play,
The serpent coils in the twilight’s sway.
Through veiled mists, where secrets lie,
The girl holds hope, though she knows not why.
The mountain fades as the bird flies on,
In the dance of time, she greets the dawn.
She shapes the clay with gentle hands,
To find the path where the truth stands.

Bridge
As the mountain crumbles, the phoenix stirs,
In the depth of night, a change occurs.
The stars look down, the earth below,
In the silent work, the truths will grow.
Through ash and ember, in the quiet flame,
The hidden light calls her name.

Outro
And as the mountain fades from sight,
The bird returns, through endless night.
To the girl, it brings a subtle sign,
A smile so real, a fleeting line.
A fork in the road, where the work begins,
In that smile, the true journey spins.
