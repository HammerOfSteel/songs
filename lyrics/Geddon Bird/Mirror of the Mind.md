# Mirror of the Mind

**Labels:** Geddon Bird
**Created:** 2024-10-28
**Edited:** 2024-10-28

---

(Intro)
The cup sits there, it's warmth I see.
The bells chime and beckons me.
I take a sip of the tea, the brew
The light, the vine, it shines right through

(Verse 1)
I close my eyes, the world fades away,
Maya’s voice guides where shadows sway.
Doors unfold, one after the next,
Each reflection a question, each step a test.

I walk through a room that feels like my own,
But the mirrors show faces I’ve never known.
Some look at me with anger and fear,
Some whisper truths I don’t want to hear.

(Chorus)
In the mirror of the mind, what’s hidden will rise,
The pieces you bury reflect in your eyes.
Doors within doors, and none lead outside—
There’s nowhere to run from what lives inside.

(Verse 2)
I reach for a handle, the door turns slow,
Behind it, fragments of things I don’t know.
A child’s small voice, a scream in the night,
A version of me that gave up the fight.

Through each frame, the pieces align—
The lies I told, the truths I malign.
The shadow stands close, a mirror of me,
Not a monster, but something I must see.

(Chorus)
In the mirror of the mind, what’s hidden will rise,
The pieces you bury reflect in your eyes.
Doors within doors, and none lead outside—
There’s nowhere to run from what lives inside.

(Bridge)
I press my hand to the mirror’s face,
Fingers meet fingers, no distance, no space.
The shadow and I breathe in the same,
Both of us calling each other’s name.

Maya said, "What you fear, you must meet,
The only way out is through what you keep."
And here, in the glass, I see the design—
The light in the dark, the dark that’s mine.

(Chorus)
In the mirror of the mind, what’s hidden will rise,
The pieces you bury reflect in your eyes.
Doors within doors, and none lead outside—
There’s nowhere to run from what lives inside.

(Outro)
I open my eyes, the dream slips away,
But the shadow and light decide to stay.
And now, when I look, I can finally see—
Every part of the mirror is part of me.
