# Mister Saturn’s Tiny Anthem

**Labels:** The Deep Darkness
**Created:** 2025-04-17
**Edited:** 2025-04-17

---

Mr. Saturn tilted his head and said,
“Boing. Language lesson begin.
Soup is love. Socks are proof.
Okay? Okay.”

Then he tooted his kazoo.
That meant it was time to sing.

“What is hug if you stand on spoon?”
Mr. Saturn asked under the moon.
Ness said, “Uh… love?”
“WRONG!” he beamed. “Hug is boing when socks are moon!”

Paula smiled, “I think he means… be kind for no reason.”
Jeff rubbed his temple. “Or it’s kinetic cohesion.”
Poo just nodded. “This is Mu, but loud.”
And Saturn did a spin that made everyone proud.

Boing means yes!
Zoom means maybe!
Ding means soup — but only if gravy!
Clunk means ‘you are seen,’
And Blip means hug machine!
Say it now, loud and loony:
“Boing, zoom, ding — you are my spoony!”

Mr. Saturn brought tea in a crayon cup,
Declared, “This is dream. Never give up.”
He gave Ness socks with different toes,
“Boing,” he said, “You are many. And it shows.”

He drew a heart in the steam with soup,
Then ran in a circle and yelled, “Friend group!”
Paula laughed till her eyes grew wide,
And Ness whispered, “I feel okay inside.”

Paula sat down, warmed by the firelight.
“I think he says ‘I love you’ with socks.”
Jeff replied, “Or soup.”
Poo looked up at the stars.
“No — I think he means it without meaning it.
Which is the truest way.”

Ness watched the smoke curl like ribbon.

“Boing,” he said softly.
“Means you stayed. Even when you could’ve gone.”

Boing... zoom... ding...
Clunk... blip... sing...
Fuzzy socks and soup at night,
You're still here — and that’s all right.
Say it once, say it true,
Boing... I love you.
