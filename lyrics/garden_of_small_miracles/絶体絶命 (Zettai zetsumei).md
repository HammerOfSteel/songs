# 絶体絶命 (Zettai zetsumei)

**Created:** 2026-02-21

---

[Intro]
[child voice, toddler-like, unaccompanied, close mic]

この道や
行く人なしに
秋の暮

*(On this road — no one else goes — the autumn dusk)*

[shamisen plucks in, slow and deliberate; shakuhachi breathes underneath]
[soft taiko pulse enters, barely audible]

---

[Verse 1]
[indie lead vocal, warm, unhurried, over shamisen and low drum]

The thunder came a'knockin',
it shook the little frame,
and it stole away the syllables
of your precious, perfect name.
Nothing should bear this,
not the burrow of this shame —
now it will never,
it will never be the same.

[bamboo flute curls in, aching, one slow phrase]

---

[Pre-Chorus]
[lead vocal, softer, almost spoken]

But to be a flower upon the wall,
the silent witness of it all —
we patched this tiny sermon
for the preacher and a prayer,
we found a match and struck it
to set alight our despair.

---

[Chorus]
[full vocal, reverent, over shamisen strum and shakuhachi held note]
[child voice joins in harmony on the Japanese lines]

露の世は
露の世ながら
さりながら

*(The world of dew / is only a world of dew — / and yet... and yet...)*

And yet the light still finds a crack,
and yet the small things answer back.
絶体絶命 — no way through —
and yet, and yet, and yet we do.

---

[Verse 2]
[lead vocal, a little more open, taiko pulse steadies]

We thread the eye of ruin,
we carry what we can —
a name, a scent, a fragment
held inside an open hand.
The narrow door is narrow
but the narrow door is real,
what cannot be undone, love,
can still be learned to feel.

[bamboo flute answers the vocal, call and response]

---

[Pre-Chorus]
[harmony joins, close and tender]

And to be a flower upon the wall,
the silent witness of it all —
we lit a candle in the rubble,
laid it where the ashes fall,
found a miracle no bigger
than a breath against the cold —
small enough to cup and carry,
old enough to make you bold.

---

[Chorus]
[full vocal and child voice together, shamisen brighter, taiko lifts]

露の世は
露の世ながら
さりながら

*(The world of dew / is only a world of dew — / and yet... and yet...)*

And yet the light still finds a crack,
and yet the small things answer back.
絶体絶命 — no way through —
and yet, and yet, and yet we do.

---

[Bridge - Instrumental]
[shamisen solo, folk-melodic, over spare taiko and drone]
[shakuhachi weaves above, bamboo flute traces the haiku melody]
[space, breath, silence between phrases]

---

[Final Verse]
[lead vocal alone, quietest point in the song]

The desperate hour does not last,
the narrow door becomes the past.
We did not know we'd make it through —
we only knew we had to move.
One small miracle, one struck match,
one syllable we dared to catch.

[child voice enters, wordless hum, single sustained note]

---

[Outro]
[child voice, solo again, slow and clear]

鐘消えて
花の香は鐘く
夕哉

*(The bell fades out — the scent of flowers rings on — evening falls)*

[shamisen fades to single pluck, then silence]
[shakuhachi holds one last breath, gone]
