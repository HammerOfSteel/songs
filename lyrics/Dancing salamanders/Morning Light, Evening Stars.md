# Morning Light, Evening Stars

**Labels:** Dancing salamanders, Daltokki
**Created:** 2024-09-10
**Edited:** 2024-09-10

---

Morning light through curtains streams,
Awakening softly from our dreams.
In the quiet dawn, we find our place,
In your arms, a warm embrace.

Coffee brews, the day begins,
In these small moments, love wins.

Morning light, evening stars,
In every moment, here you are.
From sunrise’s glow to twilight’s gleam,
You’re my reality and my dream.

City streets and busy days,
In every step, in all the ways.
Lunches shared and secret smiles,
In these moments, love’s compiled.

Through the hustle, through the rush,
In every heartbeat, there’s a hush.

Morning light, evening stars,
In every moment, here you are.
From sunrise’s glow to twilight’s gleam,
You’re my reality and my dream.

Evening falls, the world slows down,
In your eyes, I never drown.
Under starlight, hand in hand,
Together forever, we will stand.

Dinner’s warmth and laughter’s sound,
In our home, love’s profound.
From morning light to evening’s grace,
In every moment, I see your face.

Nighttime whispers, dreams take flight,
In your arms, I find my light.

Morning light, evening stars,
In every moment, here you are.
From sunrise’s glow to twilight’s gleam,
You’re my reality and my dream.

As the night fades and dawn arrives,
In every day, our love thrives.
Morning light, evening stars,
In my heart, that’s where you are.
