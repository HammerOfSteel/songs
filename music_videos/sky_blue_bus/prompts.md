## Production Notes

### Color Palette
- **Real World:** Warm golds, oranges, browns, grey-blues for rain, Welsh stone greys
- **Dream World:** Ethereal blues, starlit purples, golden memory glows
- **The Bus:** Consistent brilliant sky-blue throughout, anchor point

### Visual Transitions
- Use rain/puddle reflections to transition between real and dream
- Fire light to memory light
- Blue of bus to blue of dream space
- Soft dissolves maintaining watercolor aesthetic

### Character Consistency
Narrator always: short dark brown hair, blue eyes, oversized woolen jumper
Tima always: medium-long dark brown hair, blue eyes, small child proportions
Maintain these across all clips - no "same as before" but describe fully each time

### Pacing
The 6-second format creates natural rhythm, mimicking both:
- The walking beat of the early verses
- The dreamlike movement of memory
- Quick cuts for energy, longer single-take feelings when needed

### Emotional Arc
1. Mundane domestic tasks (groundwork)
2. Small moment of wonder (the blue bus)
3. Cozy safety (home and hearth)
4. Dream transformation (threshold)
5. Mythic journey (dream space)
6. Bittersweet return (waking)
7. Love as anchor (Tima's presence)
