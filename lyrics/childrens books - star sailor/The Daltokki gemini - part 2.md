Cover
A magical moonlit forest scene with tall, silvery trees that have shimmering star-shaped leaves. The ground is covered in glowing blue moss, casting a gentle, ethereal light. In the center of the scene are two young moon rabbits (Daltokki siblings) standing side by side—a young boy and girl—with soft, silvery fur and large, expressive eyes. The brother stands slightly forward, his posture eager and curious, while the sister is beside him, looking thoughtful and cautious. A crescent-moon sailboat floats just above them, glowing softly, with a tiny girl (Star Sailor) standing in the boat. Star Sailor is around 2-3 years old, with short brown hair and bright blue eyes, her small figure radiating a gentle light. Above them, the moon is high in the sky, casting its silvery glow over the entire forest. The overall atmosphere should feel magical, enchanting, and filled with wonder, with a mix of dark shadows and glowing lights.
