# Y Goleudy - The Lighthouse

**Labels:** Blodau yn y Lludw
**Created:** 2025-01-17
**Edited:** 2025-01-17

---

"Mae’r môr yn llawn storïau, ac mae’r sêr yn cofio’r cyfan.
(The sea is full of stories, and the stars remember it all.)
Wrth i’r goleudy ddisgleirio, rydym i gyd yn dod adref.”
(As the lighthouse shines, we all come home.)

The air tastes of salt, the twilight grows near,
Footsteps echo softly, yet the path feels unclear.
A child’s shadow dances, her heart drawn to the glow,
Where the tower stands silent, with secrets to show.

Her fingers graze the weathered brick,
The walls breathe whispers, ancient and thick.
She stumbles on a compass, tarnished and old,
A token of journeys that stories never told.

In the keeper’s room, a lantern still stands,
Its glass etched with names by unknown hands.
The logs speak of storms and a sailor’s plea,
Guided by the light through an endless sea.

Mae goleuni’n arwain drwy’r nos heb ddiwedd,
(The light guides through the endless night,)
A thread through time where shadows blend.
Through winds that cry and skies that scar,
She feels the pull of those afar.

The sea whispers softly against the shore,
Each wave a memory, a knock at the door.
The child stands still, the wind calls her name,
A voice from the depths, eternal as flame.

The beacon glows, both fierce and kind,
A bridge to what she hopes to find.

Mae goleuni’n arwain drwy’r nos heb ddiwedd,
(The light guides through the endless night,)
A beacon calling, a soul to mend.
Through winds that howl and tides that mar,
She finds her place beneath the stars.

She climbs the stairs where silence reigns,
Each step a shadow, each step contains
The lives that passed, the dreams they cast,
A flame in the dark, holding fast.

“Yn y goleuni hwn, mae pawb yn dod adref.”
(In this light, everyone comes home.)
The sailor's voice lingers, soft and sure,
The light their anchor, the love their cure.

The lantern fades, but her heart remains,
The compass warm, its story sustains.
She steps into the night, her spirit alight,
Carrying their flame through the starlit flight.

Mae goleuni’n arwain drwy’r nos heb ddiwedd,
(The light guides through the endless night,)
A bridge to shadows, to what love sends.
Through whispers soft and echoes far,
She carries their light beneath the stars.

"Yn y tywyllwch, mae goleuni’n byw, ac mae’r cariad yn para’n dragwyddol."
(In the darkness, the light lives, and love endures eternally.)
The sea fades into silence, the stars gleam high,
The lighthouse stands steadfast, a guide for every sky.
