1. Homebound Horizon
Music Style: Uplifting EDM beats with soothing acoustic guitar interludes, blending energy with nostalgia.

Essence: Captures the emotional journey back to one’s roots, reflecting the mix of familiarity and newness in rediscovering home and heritage.

Lyrics:

Intro:
"Paths stretched, heart led, yn galw fi nôl (calling me back),
The roads whisper tales, in every mile’s call."

Verse 1:
"Each turn a memory, each sign a song,
The horizon calls as I journey along.
Echoes of laughter from fields gold and wide,
A youthful echo in the countryside."

Chorus:
"Homebound horizon, lle mae'r calon yn byw (where the heart lives),
To the hearth where old tales are spun anew.
Through the whispers of winds that sweep and sway,
Homebound horizon, lle mae’r seren yn ddisgleirio (where the star shines)."

Verse 2:
"Beneath the willow’s ancient, watchful eyes,
The stream carries whispers, old lullabies.
Over the bridge, under the archway’s crown,
Each step familiar in this sunset town."

Verse 3:
"Welcoming smiles, the scent of supper’s call,
The porch light glowing, shadows dancing tall.
Every corner, every stone a story told,
Safle ddiguro (unbeaten place), in the heart’s own fold."

Bridge:
"Now the miles shrink under the homeward sky,
Starlight guides me where the old dreams lie.
Through the threshold where yesterday meets today,
In the glow of the hearth, I'm home to stay."

Outro:
"Horizon behind, my journey’s end in sight,
In this cherished embrace, under the star’s light."
