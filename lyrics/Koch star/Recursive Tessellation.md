# Recursive Tessellation

**Labels:** Koch star
**Created:** 2024-12-05
**Edited:** 2024-12-05

---

The lines stretch on forever,
No beginning, no escape.
What should we do with him now?
Besides the answer to the how.

The tessellation shifts and sighs,
Each tile a secret, each crack a lie.
The Keeper’s voice comes low and clear,
“Look at the pieces you left here.”
Eyes that stare, innocent blue,
Searching, panic, look at you.
Thinly bare, a ghostly thread,
Their gaze repeats the words I’ve said.

“Erase the cracks, smooth the lines,
Cover the fragments, leave no sign. 
A perfect shape, no space for blame,
Or live forever in this domain.”
But the tiles hum back, their voice is mine,
“The cracks reveal the hidden time.”

I press my hands to fractured seams,
The edges cut through buried dreams.
“Erase the past,” the Keeper cries,
“Seal the wounds, don’t let them rise.”
But the tiles shift beneath my skin,
Their voices whisper, “Let us in.”

“Erase the cracks, smooth the lines,
Cover the fragments, leave no sign.
A perfect shape, no space for blame,
Or live forever in this domain.”
But the tiles hum back, their voice is mine,
“The cracks reveal the hidden time.”

The cracks are not broken, they’re maps of my soul,
Each jagged edge a story untold.
The Keeper falters, their shadow fades,
The tessellation is not a cage.
What’s scattered can’t be undone,
But the pattern weaves its own new sun.

I see the lines for what they are,
A web of scars beneath the stars.
The Keeper lingers, but their voice is faint,
The tessellation has no saint.
Each crack, a thread of endless art,
A fractured whole, a perfect start.

“Erase the cracks? No, let them shine,
Each fragment holds a part divine.
The lines that break, the wounds that heal,
Are where the truth reveals what’s real.
The tiles hum back, their voice is mine,
The cracks reveal the hidden time.”

The lines stretch on forever,
No beginning, no escape.
But now I walk among them,
And the cracks, they light my way.
