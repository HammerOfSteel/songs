# Home in the Sky – Pen y Fan, Brecon Beacons

**Labels:** Dancing salamanders, Ar Lan y Mor
**Created:** 2024-09-19

Verse 1:
At the peak where the winds meet the sky,
I stand alone as the day waves goodbye.
The sun sinks low, painting the land,
And I feel your touch, as I raise my hand.

With each breath of air, you're all around,
In the quiet dusk, your presence is found.
The twilight calls, as stars awake,
I know you’re here, in every step I take.

Chorus:
Your home is in the sky, where the heavens unfold,
In every star’s shimmer, your story is told.
Though you're far beyond, you're never gone,
In the light of the night, you carry me on.

Verse 2:
Pen y Fan, so high and grand,
I reach for the sky, yet hold your hand.
The valleys stretch beneath my feet,
But your spirit rises in every heartbeat.

The first star shines, a beacon of grace,
And in that light, I see your face.
Though the world is vast, you're near,
Guiding me forward, year by year.

Chorus:
Your home is in the sky, where the heavens unfold,
In every star’s shimmer, your story is told.
Though you're far beyond, you're never gone,
In the light of the night, you carry me on.

Bridge:
From the mountaintop to the distant sea,
I carry your love, it carries me.
In the sky’s embrace, you find your peace,
And in my heart, you'll never cease.

Final Chorus:
Your home is in the sky, where the heavens unfold,
In every star’s shimmer, your story is told.
Though you're far beyond, you're never gone,
In the light of the night, you carry me on.

Outro:
As the stars fill the sky, I feel no more fear,
For I know you’re with me, always near.
The dawn will break, the night will end,
But our love will rise again and again.
