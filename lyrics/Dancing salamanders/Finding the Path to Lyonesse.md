# Finding the Path to Lyonesse

**Labels:** Dancing salamanders, childrens books - star sailor
**Created:** 2024-10-05
**Edited:** 2024-10-05

---

Where the mist meets the ocean, where the waves softly sing,
A small crescent boat glides on a silver moonbeam.
Star Sailor looks down, sees a figure alone,
A girl lost in shadows, far from her home.


---

Verse 1:

“Who are you?” calls the girl, eyes wide with fear,
“I’ve wandered so long, there’s no family near.”
Star Sailor smiles softly, her glow shining bright,
“I’m here to guide you, let’s walk through the night.”

Hand in hand through the ruins, where the ivy walls weep,
Through halls filled with echoes of memories deep.
With each step they take, the stars softly sing,
Bringing warmth to the places where shadows still cling.


---

Pre-Chorus:

“In a land where dreams have faded, where the tide hides every tear,
There’s a path still waiting, even if it’s not clear.”
“Let’s follow the song, where the moonlight does gleam,
And maybe we’ll find the road to your dream.”


---

Chorus:

“Lost in the mist of Lyonesse, we’ll walk where memories dance,
Guided by stars, and a soft, glowing chance.
Even where time’s river has flowed and dreams end,
With light and with hope, the path can mend.”

“For even in the deepest dark, when the way seems gone,
We’ll weave a new thread, and follow the dawn.”


---

Verse 2:

Through the gardens drowned in green, through streams lined with gold,
Star Sailor’s light glows, making paths through the cold.
The girl smiles softly, her eyes wide and clear,
“Maybe home’s closer, when hope draws near.”

They pass statues of kings, old thrones in the shade,
Through doorways of ivy, where lost songs are played.
“Do you hear that?” she whispers, “the song in the air,”
“It’s the sound of a promise, of hearts that still care.”


---

Bridge:

“So take each step slowly, and hold on to me,
We’ll find the path hidden where dreams meet the sea.”
“For love never falters, and light never fades,
If we walk on together, the night will give way.”


---

Chorus (Reprise):

“Lost in the mist of Lyonesse, we’ll walk where memories dance,

“For even in the deepest dark, when the way seems gone,

---

Final Verse:

As dawn’s light breaks softly, painting gold through the trees,
The girl sees her family, beyond the misty seas.
“I’ve found them,” she whispers, “with your light to guide,”
“And here, in this moment, I’ve come home inside.”

Star Sailor smiles gently, her glow fading low,
“Sometimes it just takes a small light to show,”
“That even in lands where dreams seem to end,”
There’s always a way, if you follow the wind.”
