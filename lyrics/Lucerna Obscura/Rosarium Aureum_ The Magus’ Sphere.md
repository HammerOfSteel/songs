# Rosarium Aureum: The Magus’ Sphere

**Labels:** Dancing salamanders, Lucerna Obscura
**Created:** 2024-08-21
**Edited:** 2024-08-22

---

"Within the golden garden, the unseen blooms where the rose and cross entwine. The magus turns the wheel, spinning threads of hidden lore."

Verse 1
In the shadowed grove where whispers weave,
The Magus treads, where few perceive.
Among the Lunae spinae, in twilight’s hold,
Where Canticum sings, in tones untold.

"Sphaera rubra," the sphere in bloom,
Twines the secret in its womb.
The Crux invisibilis and Rosa caeca,
Unveil the path of the anima’s pacta.

Chorus
Rosarium Aureum, where nightshade twines,
In the garden’s hush, the Arcanum’s signs.
The Magus turns the wheel of night,
Where Nox luminis meets inner sight.

Through the Cardines, where Flammae hide,
In the garden’s heart, the soul’s Auriga resides.
The cross and rose, in Solis dance,
The golden sphere in sacred trance.

Verse 2
Among the echoes of the Primus Flos,
The Magus seeks the Aether’s gloss.
In Herba mystica, where shadows play,
The petals breathe in silent sway.

"Flos obscurus," the hidden bloom,
Threads the light in darkness’ womb.
With every step, the Sphaera spins,
In Noctis auras, where life begins.

Bridge
In the Turris Opaca, where secrets blend,
The Magus finds the Aurum’s end.
"Iter per obscura," the path is clear,
Through Labyrinthus, the soul draws near.

In the sphere’s embrace, the Rosae sing,
Of Nox eternis and Veritas ignis’ ring.
The wheel spins on, the night’s refrain,
In the Rosarium, all is one again.
