# seren fach

**Labels:** The Alchemists Estate
**Created:** 2025-11-25
**Edited:** 2025-11-26

---

The algorithm broke at three A.M.
We ran the numbers,
but you didn’t fit in them.
You came quiet as snowfall
on the Hackney Road,
Loud as destiny, shifting the load.
All the theories we argued,
the fears that we kept,
Dissolved in the second
that you finally slept.
You’re a variable
we didn’t know how to write,
A sudden soft physics
in the middle of the night.
I look at your hands,
unravelled and new,
And I don’t know a single thing,
except for you.
The flat is a vessel,
the river’s a hush,
The world outside slowing its terrible rush.

Seren Fach,
little star,
Look at exactly where you are.
Not a sun to burn the sky,
Just a steady light to navigate by.
Welcome home, daffodil,
The world is turning,
but here it is still.
Little golden trumpeter,
bloom in the room,
Chasing the shadows
right out of the gloom.
Seren Fach, Tima,
cariad so true,
The whole of the sky was waiting for you.

Your mum is asleep
with a hand on your sheet,
The bravest army resting their feet.
You’re the gravity holding
her pieces aligned,
The first constellation
that she’s really defined.
May this hallway be galaxies,
may the kitchen be vast, 
May you be the anchor
that holds through the blast.
She whispers your name
like a spell or a prayer,
“Tima,” she breathes, 
and it hangs in the air.
And I see how you look
at the shape of her face,
Like you’ve always
known this was your landing place. Milk-warm breath and a blink of the eye, Teaching us how to say the old life goodbye.

And when it gets loud,
as the world often does,
When the silence is eaten by static and fuzz, Remember the kettle,
the click and the steam,
The ordinary miracles woven in the dream. The washing line dancing,
the rain on the glass,
The promise that
every hard season will pass.
You don’t have to blaze,
you don’t have to roar,
You’re enough
just by breathing right here on the floor.

Seren Fach, little star, 
We love you exactly as you are.
Not a sun to scorch the day,
Just a gentle glow to show the way. Welcome home, daffodil,
The city is racing,
but we are standing still.
Little golden trumpeter,
play your new song,
You’re right where you’ve belonged all along. Seren Fach,
Tima, cariad so new,
The best of us starts
and it ends here with you.
