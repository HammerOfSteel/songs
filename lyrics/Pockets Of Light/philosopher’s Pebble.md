# philosopher’s Pebble

**Labels:** Pockets Of Light
**Created:** 2025-08-28
**Edited:** 2025-08-29

---

"This stone is my stone of stones…"
"I keep it where the pocket goes…"

Tick-tock pebble, plip-plop stream,
Carry me closer to the shimmer of dream.
Flick-flack patience, little hands hold,
Silver from silence, lead into gold.
Mutter and stutter, the magic will tell,
Circle of circles, a wish in the well.

This stone is my stone of stones,
Rattling soft in my bones.
Wait for the weight to be light,
Day drips slow into night.

Pick it up, drop it down, spin it in rings,
Alchemy whispers in everyday things.
Penny in pockets, secrets in sand,
The riddle of riddles sits small in my hand.
Fiddle the middle, the mystery grows,
A pebble remembers what nobody knows.

This stone is my stone of stones,
Changing the marrow of bones.
Patient as patience can be,
The pebble is changing me.

Round as a circle, flat as a page,
Wiser than wisdom, older than age.
It tells me of tumbling, rivers that run,
It whispers of many and sings of the one.
Pocket it, rock it, knock it in time,
It answers in riddles, it rhymes when I rhyme.

This stone is my stone of stones,
Rolling through marrow and tones.
Not just a pebble, you see,
It’s pocketed alchemy.

Pebble, pebble, little spell,
Who will break it, who will tell?
Pebble, pebble, round and small,
It holds the secret after all.

"This stone is my stone of stones…"
"Transmute me slowly… carry me home…"
