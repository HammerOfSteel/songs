# Eternal Sea, Endless Shore

**Labels:** Dancing salamanders, Elara
**Created:** 2024-08-23
**Edited:** 2024-08-23

---

"Eternal Sea, Endless Shore" is a poignant tribute to the lasting bond between Owen and Elara, symbolized by the vastness of the ocean and the constancy of the shore. The song reflects on the memories shared by the sea, where the eternal tides carry the echoes of their time together. Through its serene and reflective melody, the song captures the timeless nature of their love, anchored in the beauty of the natural world.

Verse 1
By the eternal sea, where the waves kiss the shore,
I feel the pull of tides that we walked before.
Your footprints in the sand, washed away by time,
But in the ebb and flow, your heart echoes in mine.
Each wave that crashes down, a memory so clear,
In the whispers of the ocean, I still feel you near.

Chorus
Eternal sea, endless shore, where we once played,
In the rhythm of the tides, our love never fades.
Through the crashing waves and the whispered breeze,
Your spirit dances, bringing my heart to ease.
Eternal sea, endless shore, you’re forever more,
In the timeless waters, our souls explore.

Verse 2
We gathered shells at twilight, where the sky meets the sea,
Each one a tiny treasure, a keepsake for you and me.
But as the tide rose higher, the shells returned to the deep,
Eternal sea, endless shore, secrets the ocean keeps.
The sun sets on the horizon, painting skies with fire,
But even in the dusk, your light will never tire.
