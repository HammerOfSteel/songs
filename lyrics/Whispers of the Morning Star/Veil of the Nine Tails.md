# Veil of the Nine Tails

**Labels:** Whispers of the Morning Star
**Created:** 2024-09-15
**Edited:** 2024-09-15

---

Verse 1
In the forest's shadows, under the pale moonlight,
A fox with nine tails dances through the night.
She wears a smile, so soft and fair,
But beneath the beauty, a truth so rare.
In each flicker of her tail, a secret lies,
Whispers of a life wrapped in disguise.
The mask she wears, so perfectly formed,
Yet within her heart, a storm is born.

Chorus
Veil of the nine tails, a story untold,
A heart that's hidden, a spirit uncontrolled.
In every shadow, the truth is concealed,
But in the light, her soul is revealed.
Who are we, behind the masks we don?
In the dance of life, where have we gone?
Veil of the nine tails, a truth so frail,
A journey to find the self we trail.

Verse 2
She moves with grace, each step a lie,
Her eyes reflect the stars in the sky.
But deep within, where shadows play,
She questions the roles she must portray.
Is she the fox, or is she the flame?
A creature of lore, with no true name.
Her laughter rings, but it's tinged with fear,
For the veil she wears is never clear.

Bridge

Afraid of the truth in the light of day.
Yet, in her heart, she feels the spark,
A flame of truth that ignites the dark.

Outro
As dawn approaches, she stands alone,
The veil now lifted, her true self shown.
No longer bound by the tales of old,
She walks the path with a heart that's bold.
The nine tails fade into the misty morn,
A new day rises, and she is reborn.
Veil of the nine tails, a memory now,
A story of truth, a solemn vow.
