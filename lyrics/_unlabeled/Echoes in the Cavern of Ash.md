# Echoes in the Cavern of Ash

**Created:** 2024-08-18
**Edited:** 2024-08-18

---

In the heart of the cavern, where the shadows breathe,
I walked through the remnants of all that we leave.
Ashes to ashes, where stories have slept,
I felt the presence of a mother I never met.

Your name was a whisper in the tales he’d tell,
Of a love that lingered, where memories dwell.
But in the silence of the cavern’s core,
I knew I had to let go, to find something more.

From the ash, I forge a bond that time can’t sever,
A chain of light that will hold us forever.
Letting go, I find you anew,
In the heart of the flame, where our spirits grew.

As I walked through the echoes, where your voice was faint,
I felt the grief, the love, the joy, and the taint.
Stories faded in the fire’s breath,
But in the letting go, I found life in death.

I see you now, not just as a tale,
But as the strength in the wind, the song in the gale.
In this cavern of ash, where shadows loom,
I release the past and let the future bloom.

In the forge of resolve, where the past is remade,
I see the chain of light in the fire’s cascade.
No longer a memory that flickers and fades,
But a bond of spirit, where no darkness invades.

I feel your presence in the quiet night,
A guiding star, a gentle light.
Through the ashes, I carry you near,
In every step, you are here.

From the ash, I forge a bond that time can’t sever,
A chain of light that will hold us forever.
Letting go, I find you anew,
In the heart of the flame, where our spirits grew.

As I emerge from the cavern, light in my chest,
I know our bond is more than a quest.
You walk beside me, in the quiet and the loud,
A presence eternal, unbroken and proud.

The past has shaped, but no longer confines,
For in the ash, our love now shines.
Together we rise, in the light we’ve found,
An eternal bond, where our spirits are bound.
