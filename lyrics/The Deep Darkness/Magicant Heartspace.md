# Magicant Heartspace

**Labels:** The Deep Darkness
**Created:** 2025-04-17
**Edited:** 2025-04-17

---

You wake up too early for morning but too late for dreams.
Someone’s banging on your door.
The air smells like iron and firecrackers.
Your name hasn’t been spoken yet —
but your story already started.

I stepped out in socks on a street too still,
Crickets playing synths, sky swallowing the hill.
He said, “There’s a light where the ground broke wide,”
So we climbed up slow with fear half-tied.

Pajamas stick to skin, night air like foil,
Mailbox still warm, earth ready to boil.
Buzz Buzz said, “You don’t get to wait,”
And Starman crashed with a glitching fate.

In the heartspace, echoes bloom,
Every first step, every bedroom.
Enemies fade but they leave their shape,
I hold them all like video tape.
(Play, rewind, play, rewind...)

Inside’s weird. I mean deeply weird —
Like finding your own name carved in a beard.
A mirror said, “You ain’t healed that yet.”
And I laughed like, “Bet.” But the floor got wet.

I walked past thoughts like pixel ghosts,
A hallway of “almosts” and “what hurts most.”
You open a present — inside, a cookie.
“Leave it?”
I stood there way too long. That shook me.
Someone said, “You’re brave.” I said, “Define that.”
Then stared at the couch where my joy once sat.

In the heartspace, maps don’t work,
Just paths shaped by old homework.
I lost my shoes to a memory tide,
But found my face on the other side.

Back in town, before the badge or the buzz,
Before warp zones and what Ness was.
There were fences to climb, dogs to outwalk,
And a trail of flashlights whispering talk.

Mom left lights on, even when I left,
My room smelled like baseball and cassette breath.
Magicant isn't made, it’s remembered raw —
The home behind every psychic flaw.

The bug said, “You’re not alone,” before it died.
And that stuck in my heart where rage can’t hide.
I saw stars like codes in a broken machine,
And knew the dream was real — and the real was dream.

I’m stitched from silence and meteors loud,
A flashlight kid in a too-big crowd.
But I keep this beat, this space, this flow —
Where the starmen land and I still grow.

You’ve been here before.
You’ll be here again.
Not because time repeats —
but because some places echo loud enough to pull you back.
Welcome to your heartspace.
The lights are still on.
