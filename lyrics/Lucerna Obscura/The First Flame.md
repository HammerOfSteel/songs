# The First Flame

**Labels:** Dancing salamanders, Lucerna Obscura
**Created:** 2024-08-20
**Edited:** 2024-08-21

---

Verse 1
In the womb of Terra, where salts conspire,
The Crimson Beast stirs, in shadowed pyre.
From Sol’s deep root, where the Serpent coils,
It wakes in silence, where night’s fire toils.

"Rubedo’s veil," where the hidden dance,
The roaring flame in an iron trance.
Through cinder and marrow, the Prima cries,
For within the blaze, the Phoenix flies.

Chorus
León Rubeus, in the core of coal,
Devours the dross, leaving only the soul.
The forge burns fierce, yet wisdom breathes,
In Dragon’s breath, the secret wreathes.

The Beast’s embrace, where shadows wane,
Turns lead to gold, through crucible’s pain.
In the belly’s core, where truths seethe,
The Crimson’s flame, the soul’s new sheath.

Verse 2
The hidden sage whispered to the heart of blaze,
In the Lion’s blood, etched hidden ways.
From dew of earth, where seeds are sown,
He summoned the fire, where the Stone is grown.

"Vermilion’s arc," in the silent gleam,
A dance of flame through a mystic stream.
The Beast’s roar, in the sulphurous air,
Burns through veils to a secret lair.

Chorus
León Rubeus, in the forge concealed,
Crafts gold from black in the silent field.
The Serpent’s eye, where truth abides,
In hidden hands, the secret hides.

Through the Crimson’s flame, the base is crowned,
In the silent work, the path is found.
The first fire burns in the night’s deep,
To awaken the soul from the ancient sleep.

Outro
In the coldest ash, where fire has fed,
The Beast now rests, in the gold and red.
"Solve et Coagula," the circle spins,
As the journey renews, where the dawn begins.

Through the Crimson’s breath, the path is near,
To the heart of stone, the seeker draws near.
In the Lion’s fire, the soul is forged,
In the endless blaze, the truth engorged.


"In the heart of the lion, the primal spark ignites, setting the path ablaze. The journey begins where fire breathes life into the shadows."
