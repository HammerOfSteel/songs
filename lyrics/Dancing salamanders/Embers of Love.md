# Embers of Love

**Labels:** Dancing salamanders, Risca
**Created:** 2024-09-02
**Edited:** 2024-09-02

---

"Embers of Love": A warm, reflective song where the father sits by the fire, feeling the enduring presence of his wife and daughter in the crackling embers. The fire symbolizes their lasting love, with each ember representing a cherished memory that continues to bring solace and light.

Verse 1
By the hearth, I sit alone,
Yet never truly on my own.
The embers glow, mor fwyn, mor fyw (so gentle, so alive),
Their warmth a love that’s ever true.
Each crackle in the fire's light,
A heartbeat in the still of night.
It whispers tales of days gone by,
When we were here, just you and I.

Chorus
Embers of love, glowing bright,
Holding us close in the quiet night.
Their warmth, a comfort, deep and strong,
A bond unbroken, all night long.
Though time has passed, and you’re afar,
You’re with me still, fy seren (my star).

Verse 2
The flames, they dance with memories sweet,
Of moments shared, complete, cyflawn (complete).
I see your smiles in every spark,
Your laughter echoing in the dark.
The love we had, it doesn’t fade,
It lives within this fire we made.
Each ember holds a piece of you,
A love so deep, mor ddwfn, mor wir (so deep, so true).

Bridge
As the night deepens, the world is still,
But here by the fire, mae’n calonau’n llenwi (our hearts fill).
Your presence wraps me, tender and kind,
In every ember, it’s you I find.
The flames may dwindle, but they won’t die,
For in this fire, your love will always lie.

Outro
So as the embers slowly fade,
I hold on to the love we made.
In every spark, there’s a piece of you,
A light that guides, mor ddisglair (so bright), mor wir (so true).
And as the night turns into day,
Your love remains, it will not sway.
For in this fire, your heartbeats stay,
In every ember, trwy’r nos a’r dydd (through the night and day).
