# Strange attractors

**Labels:** Koch star
**Created:** 2024-12-08
**Edited:** 2024-12-09

---

The room hums; the lights spin slow,
A sea of faces I don’t quite know.
I fix my mask, it’s paper-thin,
A smile to greet, a wall within.

“Relax, just take it slow,
We don’t have to stay, we can always go.”
But I pull along, a quiet roar,
"Let's just go and explore"
I step inside, take in the sight,
Masks slip on in the neon light.

I’m spinning circles, a seamless dance,
Caught in the pull of every glance.
The room is loud, the mirrors gleam,
Prisms shift like a waking dream.
You say my name, but who am I?
A strange attractor in the sky.

“Hi, nice to meet you,” I hear her say,
A voice not mine, yun taken the stage today.
Laughter spills like shattered glass,
Each moment splinters, none seem to last.
Yun glance at him; unyielding gaze,
A strange attractor through the haze.

I’m spinning circles, a roudy dance,
Caught in the pull of every glance.
The room is loud, the mirrors gleam,
Perspective shift like a waking dream.
You say my name, but who am I?
A strange attractor in the sky.

“Do they see me?” we ask him low,
He shakes his head, whispers, “No.”
Yun glances across the crowd,
Chattering voices all to loud
“You’re enough,” his voice a thread,
Pulling me back from where I’ve fled.

The party hum fades to a tone,
We see him there, we're not alone.
The mirrors shift, but doesn't fall,
A kaleidoscope, I see them all.
Not the pieces, but a mosaic bright,
Every curve catching the light.

I’m spinning circles, a graceful dance,
But now I see within the glace.
The room still hums, the mirrors gleam,
But I’m alive in this shifting dream.
You say my name, we're right there,
The stars they twinkle everywhere.

The room hums loud; the lights spin slow,
A sea of faces I’m starting to know.
I fix my mask, then let it fall,
We stand right here after all.
