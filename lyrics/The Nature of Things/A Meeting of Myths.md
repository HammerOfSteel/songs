# A Meeting of Myths

**Labels:** The Nature of Things
**Created:** 2024-11-21
**Edited:** 2024-11-22

---

The room hums low, the air feels wide,
Stories bloom where shadows hide.
Folklore weaves through the spoken lines,
Each word a lantern, a thread to find.

"Do they see me here, or someone else?
A borrowed mask, a fractured self."
She glances up, across the space,
His quiet gaze, a steady place.

He speaks of legends, of sea and stone,
His voice a rhythm, a softened tone.
She leans in close, her heart unsure,
A guarded soul, but his words feel pure.


"What do you see in the myths they tell?"

"A world within, where I can dwell.
A place to be more than shadows and seams,
Where stories flow like hidden streams."

A meeting of myths, a path untold,
Two hearts that seek, two souls unfold.
Through ancient tales and whispered skies,
A bond begins where wonder lies.

They walk outside, the air turns cool,
The stars above a timeless school.
He asks her thoughts, she hesitates,
Then finds her words, as the silence waits.

"Do you think we’re made of stories we keep?"

"Perhaps they wake us when we sleep.
Each tale a mirror, a light to guide,
Through the mazes we hold inside."

She feels the walls she’s built so high,
Shift and tremble beneath his sky.
Her voice grows stronger, her heart unsure,
But something stirs, both raw and pure.

"Can he see the pieces that I hide?
Would he walk with me through the tides?"

"The stories we carry make us whole,
They bind the heart, they lift the soul."

A meeting of myths, a sacred thread,
A light that lives where shadows tread.
Through whispered lore and timeless skies,
A bond unfolds where wonder lies.

The stories echo in her mind,
Each word a key, a thread to find.
She feels the parts of herself align,
A fleeting peace, a moment divine.

"Could I be more than I’ve known before?
A keeper of stories, an open door."

The moon hangs low, the stars draw near,
Their words grow soft, their hearts sincere.
She shares a tale of a forest deep,
Where shadows dance and secrets sleep.

"Does the forest hold a truth you seek?"

"Perhaps it whispers what I can’t speak.
A story shared can set roots free,
Like lanterns cast across the sea."

A meeting of myths, a light, a guide,
Two souls that walk through the riverside.
Through ancient tales and whispered skies,
A bond now formed where wonder lies.

The workshop ends, the world feels wide,
Yet something lingers, a spark inside.
She watches him as he walks away,
A fleeting moment she hopes will stay.

"In the threads of myths, I’ve found a part,
Of him, of me, of this fragile heart."

The stories fade, the echoes stay,
A sacred meeting, a fleeting day.
In myths and dreams, their paths entwine,
A quiet spark through endless time.

"A meeting of myths, where wonder lives,
A tale we share, a gift it gives."
