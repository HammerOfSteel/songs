# Cloudberry Blues

**Created:** 2026-02-21

---

[Intro - sparse harmonium swell, soft marimba shimmer, brushed snare enters slowly]
[spoken, hushed, close mic]

Not gonna lie,
small clouds, like breath —
drift to the sky.

[upright bass plucks in, slide guitar hums beneath]

---

[Verse 1]
[warm reedy lead vocal, intimate, slightly ragged]

We stole the last ripe jewel from the mossy edge of ground,
I cupped it like a secret, felt the sugar soft and round.
A taste and all the sour turns to honey on your lips,
brave, orange, tiny, shiny — bitter apocalypse.

Here it hangs in the air between us,
bare feet on old damp moss.
Here it hums among electric sheep,
flutter over rooftops steep.

---

[Pre-Chorus]
[close two-part harmony enters, voices barely a breath apart]

And it's a secret — well known to all,
aye, just a scraggly little crew,
happily composed of this little bird…
me, and you.

---

[Chorus]
[full harmony, brushed snare lifts, harmonium swells]

Do not go gentle into the night,
do not let gentle dim out your light.
Rage, rage against the fear and the fright —
pick up your heart and take up the fight.

---

[Verse 2]
[lead vocal alone again, more tender]

The bog turns gold in August, but the season never stays,
we carry summer's amber through the cold and darker days.
One cloudberry in the palm, one small sweet for two —
taste it, taste it slow — and know the blue.

Here it hangs in the air between us,
bare feet on old damp moss.
Here it glows like a small lit lantern
flicker-warm against the loss.

---

[Pre-Chorus]
[harmony returns, slightly fuller]

And it's a secret — well known to all,
aye, just a scraggly little crew,
happily composed of this little bird…
me, and you.

---

[Chorus]
[full harmony, harmonium swell, marimba twinkles above]

Do not go gentle into the night,
do not let gentle dim out your light.
Rage, rage against the fear and the fright —
pick up your heart and take up the fight.

---

[Bridge - Instrumental]
[slide guitar solo, lyrical and aching, over harmonium drone and brushed snare]
[upright bass walks slowly underneath]
[marimba accents on the turn]

---

[Outro Verse]
[solo voice, barely above a whisper, organ pad sustains beneath]

I'd be content if this was the last of my days,
heart grown in this valley, set in its ways.
One cloudberry in the palm, one small sweet for two —
taste and know the blue.

[close harmony hums, wordless, fades with organ]
[slide guitar trails off, single note held]

---

**Style:** Nordic folk, blues, melancholic, acoustic, slide guitar, harmonium, upright bass, brushed drums, close harmony, marimba, bittersweet, intimate, warm vocal, reedy, slow burn
