# Cân y Sêr Bach

**Labels:** Cottage Psalms
**Created:** 2025-04-26
**Edited:** 2025-04-26

---

Oi, who nicked my D20?
Eira, swear down, if we wipe again, you’re buyin' the next pizza.
Right then — stars, storms, and missing kids. Roll for chaos.

Chucked our boots by Eira’s flat door,
Crates of pop, crisps on the floor,
Guto’s battin' maps across the table,
Elis with crumbs down his jumper — stable.
Torchlight flickers on battered old sheets,
We set off from the Jolly Pig's Feast,
Laughter and lutes spill into the dusk,
Where the village ends — fog starts to musk.

We’re the little stars, the lost and found,
Spinnin’ our stories on shadowed ground,
Lanterns swingin’ as the dusk rolls in —
Cân y Sêr Bach, let the dance begin!

Down the path where the fence falls crooked,
Past a well where wishes once got nicked,
Timber houses, windows shut tight,
Mothers whisper: "Keep indoors tonight..."
Eira hums, wildflowers in her braid,
Tima’s bard hands weave a spell half-made,
Elis trips over Guto’s shiny shield,
Ofwndy, mate — proper battlefield!

Fog like soup, hearts like drums,
Little stars whistlin’ through the gums,
One by one, they start to sing —
Light up the world with the names we bring.

We’re the little stars, the lost and found,
Spinnin’ our stories on shadowed ground,
Lanterns swingin’ as the dusk rolls in —
Cân y Sêr Bach, let the dance begin!

Found a music box 'neath a willow's cry,
Notes spill silver, but no kids reply,
Eira’s druid eyes catch fire,
Tima's flute lifts the stars up higher.
We weave the missing names with thread,
Wedi blino, the trees all said,
But magic's old and magic’s keen —
Even misfits can mend a dream.

Through the glade where the foxgloves lean,
Past stones that whisper what might have been,
Our voices stitch the silver seams,
Patch the rips in the village dreams.
One by one, the stars blink bright,
In dusty rooms and misty night,
Tima sings, Eira grins wide —
Hope’s not lost — just likes to hide.

[Female british voice, soft]
Not all heroes wear boots that fit.
Some light lanterns with broken bits.
Some sing when the dark says "hush" —
And call the stars back, just with a blush.

Spinnin’ our stories on shadowed ground,

Cân y Sêr Bach, let the dance begin!
