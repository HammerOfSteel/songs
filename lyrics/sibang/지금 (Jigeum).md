# 지금 (Jigeum)

**Labels:** sibang, Dancing salamanders
**Created:** 2024-09-27
**Edited:** 2024-09-27

---

[Verse 1]
The top of the mountain, the calm dawn
In the cold air, I'm standing alone
My heart buried in the sea wave
I'm standing there, waiting for you to come up

[Pre-Chorus]
memories of the past in the wind
The past days call me
indelible traces of one's heart
Nevertheless, the darkness disappears and the sun rises

[Chorus
Right now, I'm standing in this moment
The past is in the waves, leaving me
Now, here I am
Looking at the morning, waiting for the future

[Verse 2]
Warm sunshine rises and fills the sky
The sun permeates over my cold heart
Call my name and wave your hand to say hello
A little hope grows in my heart

[Pre-Chorus]
memories of the past that were intertwined like threads
Like healing my soul, the sun rises
The morning comes again now
After dawn, the darkness disappears

[Chorus]
Now, live in this moment
The past is in the waves, leaving me by my side
Now, again, I promise
We'll be with you in the future

[Bridge]
The wind is passing by. Close your eyes
Take a deep breath. I'm here
Lost time. I can't find it again
I'm here. I live in this moment

[Chorus]
Now, live in this moment
The past seeps into the waves
Now, I'm standing here
In the early morning light, I'm about to wake up again

[Outro]
The light of Seongsan shines on me
a journey to find the beginning at the end
Darkness is gone and dawn is coming
I walk down the street again, meet another day
