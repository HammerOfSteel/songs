# Boil and Bubble

**Created:** 2026-02-21

Instrumentation
- Warm acoustic strum, soft marimba accents, low cello drones, light percussion (brushes). Occasional warped kettle sample.

Focus
- Kitchen witchery: simmering as metaphor for slow change, patience, bubbling anxieties transformed by gentle tending.

Hook
- Lyrical refrain: "Boil and bubble, keep the trouble small; skim the glare, keep the simmering warm."

Structure
- Haiku intro (steam image)
- Verse (sensory imagery of simmering pot & inner heat)
- Chorus (mantra about tending)
- Bridge (short spoken list of what we skim away)
- Outro (pot hiss fades into field ambience)

Lyrical seed

Haiku:
Steam ghosts the window,
small storms cluster in the pot,
we tend the slow light.

Verse sketch:
A spoon that traces circles like a palm,
we draw the day into a salted calm.
Each bubble holds a worry and a seed,
and we scoop the scum to keep what we need.

Production note
- Use live kettle/pot recordings; apply low-pass automation to create warmth.
