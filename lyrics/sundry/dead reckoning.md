# dead reckoning

**Labels:** sundry
**Created:** 2026-01-12
**Edited:** 2026-01-12

---

Look at the leaf, look at the green,
beautiful enough, just being seen.

A billion paths the light could take,
To hit the mark for heaven’s sake.

a perfect collapses into one,
The recollection that must be done.

through a forest, no compass, no mark,
Blindfolded running through the dark

haven't hit a single tree,
should be impossible,
aye, believe you me.

ready ready,
steady hand.
just watch as they all land...
perfect and precise...

Oh, we’re dead reckoning, eyes shut tight,
Find the way home in the dead of night.

Far in the north
aye, skipping away.
Thus goes another little day.

ain't nothing to hit,
not ready to quit.
just..
have to allow it..

trusting the wind to blow us right,
striding right along at the edge of the light.

"True Neutral"
ample grace..
everything falls into its proper place

Little sinister suspension about to break,
Into the only choice,
anything could ever make.

Aye, I am ready to try,
can only rely...
on this..

dead reckoning, hands held tight,
Finding the "Home" in the middle of night.

burnt the map,
let go of the plan.
We already have all,
needed to understand.

You are already home.

waves of light,
a cosmic speck.
heck, I don't even want to know.
no matter which way it would go.

Found it's way home.
