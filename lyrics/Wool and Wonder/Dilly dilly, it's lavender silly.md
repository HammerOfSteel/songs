# Dilly dilly, it's lavender silly

**Labels:** Wool and Wonder
**Created:** 2025-05-14
**Edited:** 2025-05-14

---

Lavender’s blue… dilly dilly.
Lavender’s green…
Alright lil’ miss? Ready to spin this dream?

Lavender’s blue, dilly dilly, lavender’s green,
When you are king, dilly dilly, I shall be queen.
Crown on my head, slippers of gold,
I got my palace, my kingdom bold.
Silk on my pillow, satin on my bed,
Tiny tiara when I rest my head.

Who told you so, lil’ miss bossy boots?
Who gave you claim to royal pursuits?
Who said you rule the living room floor?
Who said you’re queen behind that door?
Was it your heart? Dilly dilly, I thought so.
Lil’ queen of the block, yeah, I ought know.

Lavender’s blue, dilly dilly, lavender’s green,
You be the queen, dilly dilly, I be your king.
Lavender’s soft, dilly dilly, lavender’s fine,
We got the dream, dilly dilly, this bedtime’s mine.

Call up my friends, dilly dilly, set ‘em to work,
Some with the plough, some with the fork.
Some with the hay, some threshing corn,
Me and you cozy while it’s stormin’ ‘til morn.
I boss ‘em about, but I do it with love,
Cos a queen’s gotta guide, gotta rise above!

You cheeky queen, dilly dilly, wild little thing,
Calling the shots, but I taught you to swing.
You got that fire, you got that shine,
But when day breaks, that kingdom’s mine.
Until then, lil’ miss, cuddle in tight,
Lavender dreams, queenie. Goodnight.

Lavender’s blue, dilly dilly, lavender’s green,
You be the queen, dilly dilly, I be your king.
Lavender’s soft, dilly dilly, lavender’s fine,
We got the dream, dilly dilly, this bedtime’s mine.

Alright then… Queenie.
Sleep tight.
