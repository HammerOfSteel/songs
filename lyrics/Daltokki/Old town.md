# Old town

**Created:** 2024-09-10
**Edited:** 2024-09-10

---

Snowflakes fall, a silent symphony,
Gamla Stan’s streets, a wintry tapestry.
Cobblestone paths, under lantern glow,
In this old town, our love does grow.

Through alleys narrow, where history breathes,
In the heart of winter, our souls find ease.

Crystal nights in Gamla Stan,
Where love’s warmth melts the frost.
Under the northern stars, hand in hand,
In this winter wonderland, we’re never lost.

Frosted windows and candlelight,
Shadows dance in the Nordic night.
From Riddarholmen to Stortorget square,
Every corner whispers, memories we share.

Through the old town’s charm, so pure and bright,
We find our peace in the snowy night.

Crystal nights in Gamla Stan,
Where love’s warmth melts the frost.
Under the northern stars, hand in hand,
In this winter wonderland, we’re never lost.

Silent nights and the moon’s soft gleam,
Frozen rivers, like a dream.
In the quiet hush of Stockholm’s night,
Our hearts are warmed by love’s own light.

Icy bridges and whispering trees,
In Gamla Stan, time seems to freeze.
From the Royal Palace to narrow lanes,
Love endures through snow and rains.

Through every snowfall, in every breeze,
In this winter world, our hearts find ease.

Crystal nights in Gamla Stan,
Where love’s warmth melts the frost.
Under the northern stars, hand in hand,
In this winter wonderland, we’re never lost.

As dawn breaks and the city wakes,
In Gamla Stan, our love remains.
Crystal nights, forever we’ll recall,
In this ancient town, we found it all.
