# Chiaroscuro

**Labels:** Koch star
**Created:** 2024-12-11
**Edited:** 2024-12-11

---

The world is bright, yet here I stand,
A ghost among shadows, my life unplanned.
The night reveals what light conceals,
A dance of truths, a pact of deals.

Beneath the neon’s fractured glow,
A thousand faces, none I know.
Each voice, each laugh, a fleeting taste,
A hunger stirs, no time to waste.

Their energy hums, a pulse I claim,
An endless thirst, an endless shame.
Each stolen smile, each borrowed grin,
A story lost before it begins.

Chiaroscuro, the light and the dark,
A vampire lost, yet leaves a mark.
A thief of warmth, a vessel drained,
In this shadowed world, I remain chained.

Each fragment calls, a voice inside,
A whisper soft, a plea denied.
"Take what you need, but tread with care,
The light you steal, the wounds you bare."

The city hums with half-told truths,
A siren’s song, uncaptured youth.
I navigate the unseen game,
A soul in parts, yet whole in name.

The darkness speaks, it pulls me near,
But light reveals the scars I fear.
Each fleeting moment, a fragile thread,
Each path I walk, a life misread.

Chiaroscuro, the night and the pain,
A vampire trapped, yet seeks to gain.
A thief of hearts, a shadow’s lore,
Bound to hunger, forevermore.

The stars above, a map unseen,
A guide through tides, through might-have-beens.
Each shadowed step, a story unfolds,
The light within, a truth it holds.

Their kindness burns, their trust feels cold,
But here I stand, their tales retold.
A vampire’s path, a fragile guise,
Yet hope remains where darkness lies.

The shadow and light are both my own,
A balance found, a life unknown.
Through tangled threads, I start to see,
The beauty lies in all of me.

Chiaroscuro, the dark and the light,
A vampire no more, I claim my night.
A thief of nothing, a soul unchained,
In this fractured world, I have remained.

The waves still lap, the city sighs,
A harmony found where shadow lies.
Through chiaroscuro, my story’s told,
A dance of shadows, a heart of gold.
