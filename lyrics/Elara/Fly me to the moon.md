# Fly me to the moon

**Labels:** Elara
**Created:** 2025-06-22
**Edited:** 2025-06-22

---

Fly me to the moon
Let me play among the stars—
Let me feel what spring is like
On Jupiter and Mars.
In other words, take my hand,
In other words, love, help me stand.

Fill this chest with song, my dear,
Let the sky be our refrain.
You’re the thought I chase through seasons,
Through the sunshine and the rain.
In other words, don’t drift far—
In other words, you’re all I are.

You’re all I dream for,
All I cherish and adore.
With every breath, I’ll sing your name,
Forever, evermore.
In other words, please be true—
In other words, I love you.

We’ll picnic on Saturn’s rings,
Kick stars like they’re pebbles by the sea.
You’ll ask what comes after this life,
And I’ll say, “ Tima, You and me.”

So fly me to the moon once more,
Let us dance and never part.
You’re the crown of every morning,
The lullaby inside my heart.
In other words, just us two—
In other words, Elara… I love you.
