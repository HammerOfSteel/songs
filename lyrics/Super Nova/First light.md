# First light

**Labels:** Super Nova
**Created:** 2025-06-06
**Edited:** 2025-09-29

---

I loved you before you were born,  
even when you were just a thought in the vast sea of time.  
I loved you in quiet moments and silent nights

You talkin’ mad again, love, little oracle feet in glitter socks,
Spillin’ juice on my theories while you climb the cardboard box.
Said, “I watched you when you was sad, before I got born,”
Eyes wide like galaxies, wrapped in unicorn corn.

You claim you floated by the window, saw me hummin’ to no one,
Said you picked me 'cause my tea had more sugar than some.
Rumi on the radio, silence in my lap,
"lost her," you said, "but I circled back."

Yeah? That’s sweet, mushy perfect little peas
Who throws ‘em like meteors across the stars and seas.
blink, and I feel it, that look like you know.
Like you held me once before, way before the first snow.

First light, you knew me before
You were sittin’ by the window, I was knockin’ on the door
Out there floatin’, soul just scorin’ a score
Now I’m nappin’ on your chest like I’ve done it before.

I saw you in the dark, with your tea on the floor,
Didn’t like that flat, it was cold by the door.
You talked to the lamp, you whispered her name,
I hovered like a bird, then I jumped in the frame.

You was lonely, I seen it, your heart went all grey,
your laugh was still gold, so I came anyway.
Followed the rabbit down the warren, he gave me a hug,
curious curious, he said with a shrug. 

So I zoomed down, fwoosh, glued in fate,
Kicked in your ribs, yeah, I came in late.
wild wild child, queen of the storm,
Knew your smile, before you were born.

First laugh, and you knew it was me,
I was watchin’ from the stars with a cuppa and a key,
‘what key?’, oh you know, the one to your heart,
‘Cause I chose you, innit?, that's where it starts

You speak in riddles and raisin fingers,
Pullin’ moonlight through my mind like meanderings rivers.
Said, “Rumi knew it, I know it too,”
“Love is the bridge between me and you.”

So I wrote that on the fridge, next to stars you drew,
And I swear some nights I can feel it, see it in you.
The twinkle ember that never let's go,
Some old soul tap dancin’ under our flow.

Tales told bold, spun on rerun,
Some laugh from before this timeline begun.
The more I deny it, the more I believe,
You came with your fists clenched and stardust sleeves.

First light, and the dawn still glows,
In your giggle, in your cries, in your stompin’ toes,
You rewrote my laws with a crayon prose,
Now the cosmos breathes in the way your nose blows.

“Did you come from the stars, little flame?”

I came from the hugs that said your name.

When morning comes in like the tide
you will have reached the other side.

Swim, star, swim, through the midnight sea
when your hair is dry, come back to me!

Little star, so near, so far,
shine on Wick and Zanzibar

on Dundee, Belfast and Armagh,
and shine on my bike’s bent handlebar
