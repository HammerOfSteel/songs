# what a Loop Is Like - ALBUM

**Labels:** what a Loop Is like, Album
**Created:** 2025-09-03
**Edited:** 2025-09-09

---

(Sut mae dolen yn teimlo)
A concept album about living with impossible dissonance—where grief, love, shame, devotion, anger, and the sacred ordinary keep circling back. The loop is: breath/prayer/routine/river. The break is: choice/mercy/listening.

Sonic palette (the “loop kit”)
Core feel: slow to mid-tempo; intimate, raw, no autotune; space for silence.

Instruments: Welsh harp, nylon guitar/mandolin, harmonium, low whistle, bodhrán, brushed snare, handclaps, tape hiss, kettle, footstep/river field recordings.

Structures: rounds/canons, da capo forms, 

palindrome lyrics, tempo cycles (5/4 ↔ 3/4), ostinato pedals, call-and-response.

Refrain device: a mutable line that returns across the album:

 “This is what a loop is like.” → morphs to “This is what love is like,” “…what leaving is like,” “…what returning is like.”




Tracklist.
