# The Light Beyond the Horizon – Worm’s Head, Gower Peninsula

**Labels:** Dancing salamanders, Ar Lan y Mor
**Created:** 2024-09-19
**Edited:** 2024-09-19

---

Verse 1:
At the edge where the cliffs greet the sea,
I stand alone, yet you’re here with me.
The first light breaks beyond Worm’s Head,
In the quiet dawn, where all fears have fled.

The tides retreat, revealing the way,
Just as your love guides me through each day.
I feel your presence, in the morning’s glow,
A silent promise that you’ll never let go.

Chorus:
Beyond the horizon, where the sun will rise,
You watch from the heavens, with knowing eyes.
In the light that breaks through the morning’s veil,
Your voice whispers softly, “All is well.”

Verse 2:
As the tides return, covering the shore,
I know that you’ll guide me, forevermore.
Worm’s Head disappears beneath the waves,
But your light remains, a beacon that saves.

I walk the path we once roamed hand in hand,
Now guided by your light in this distant land.
The cliffs of Gower stand tall and wide,
But it’s your love that keeps me by your side.

Chorus:
Beyond the horizon, where the sun will rise,
You watch from the heavens, with knowing eyes.
In the light that breaks through the morning’s veil,
Your voice whispers softly, “All is well.”

Bridge:
In the shifting tides, I see your grace,
A reminder that love transcends time and space.
The horizon’s glow, a sacred sign,
That you are with me, for all of time.

Chorus (Final):
Beyond the horizon, where the sun will rise,
You watch from the heavens, with knowing eyes.
In the light that breaks through the morning’s veil,
Your voice whispers softly, “All is well.”

Outro:
As Worm’s Head fades into the sea,
I feel your love surrounding me.
In every sunrise, in every wave,
I know you’re near, your spirit brave.
