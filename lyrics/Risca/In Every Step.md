# In Every Step

**Labels:** Dancing salamanders, Risca
**Created:** 2024-09-04
**Edited:** 2024-09-04

---

Verse 1
As I walk this winding road,
With memories heavy, yet they lighten the load.
Pob cam, pob tro (Every step, every turn), I feel you there,
Guiding me with tender care.
The earth beneath, the sky above,
Each step I take is wrapped in love.

Chorus
In every step, I carry you,
Your spirit strong, your love so true.
Ar hyd y ffordd (Along the way), I’m not alone,
In every step, I find my home.
With each stride, your light I keep,
In every step, through valleys deep.

Verse 2
The road is long, with trials and bends,
But I feel your presence, it never ends.
Eich cariad yn fy nghalon (Your love in my heart), it leads me on,
Through the darkest night to the brightest dawn.
You walked before, and now I see,
Each step I take, you walk with me.

Bridge
Though the journey’s hard and the road is steep,
Your whispered words in my heart I keep.
Pob tro, pob cam (Every turn, every step), you guide my way,
Through the night and into day.
Together we walk, in this dance of life,
Through joy, through pain, through every strife.

Outro
So I’ll keep walking, come what may,
With your love to light my way.
In every step, i carry you,
Our love in the cauldron brew.
