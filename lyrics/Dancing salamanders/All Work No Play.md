# All Work No Play

**Labels:** Dancing salamanders, Gills and guts
**Created:** 2024-09-20
**Edited:** 2024-09-20

---

Place: Truro Cathedral, Truro

Verse 1
Tick-tock, the clock’s my chain,
Running fast, I'm stuck in the lane.
Chasing gold, but losing time,
Lost in the grind, no reason, no rhyme.
I step inside, where the echoes fall,
Truro’s tower, so grand, so tall.
But I can’t hear the peace it sings,
Too caught up in the race of things.

Chorus
All work, no play,
The grind is stealing my days away.
In the shadow of the cathedral’s glow,
I’m learning to let go, to take it slow.
All work, no play,
I’ve been lost, but now I’ll find my way.
The bells ring clear, they’re calling me,
To stop, to breathe, to simply be.

Verse 2

But my mind spins, a ceaseless grind,
Chasing tasks that leave me blind.
I kneel beneath the vaulted skies,
Trapped in lists, but I realize…
