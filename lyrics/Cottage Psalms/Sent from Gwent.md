# Sent from Gwent

**Labels:** Cottage Psalms
**Created:** 2025-04-26
**Edited:** 2025-04-26

---

Sent from Gwent…
Never posted, mind.
Just folded, pressed —
and tucked behind the clock on Nan’s mantel.
You know how it is.

Bench by the Co-op, dusk in my coat
Book in my lap, dreams I never wrote
Bus pulled up like a breath I held
But I stayed sat — just hid, just dwelled
Library warm, fiction thick on my knees
Told myself, "You can leave when you please"
But I never did. Not then, not yet
Just wrote out wishes in silhouette

Sent from Gwent, but never signed
Heart in the stamp, name left behind
Words I wrote in corner light
Were all the ways I tried to fight
So if you read this, read it slow —
It’s where I went. It’s what I know.

Rain on the glass like typewriter taps
Clack of the heater, dog-eared maps
I’d trace a line past Pontypool Hill
Then stop at Crosskeys, quiet and still
Chippy smell, my coat all steam
Fingers cold, but ink still keen
Somewhere a future I’d never send
Tucked in a notebook, "Dear almost-friend"

Sent from Gwent, in tea-stained rhyme
Across the page, across the time
I folded love into every line
But kept it tucked in a plastic spine
So if you find it, find it true —
I sent it then. I send it… to you.

Pages rustle like my Nan’s old song
Where the kettle sings, I still belong
And every bench I ever hid on,
was never lonely... just waiting on.

I saw my name carved small in the bark
By the river bend where days turn dark
Left a note in the library wall
About dragons, stars, and trying at all
I don’t live there now, but in some way I do
'Cause Gwent’s the ink I wrote myself through

Sent from Gwent, no return-to-me
Just rain-soaked dreams in minor key
If home is more than place or name
Then I’m still posting just the same
So if you hear me, hold it near —
This little song I sent from here.

Still sendin’ em.
Bit more honest now,
Bit less folded.
