# True colors

**Labels:** The Nature of Things
**Created:** 2024-11-22
**Edited:** 2024-11-23

---

The rain fades soft, the clouds turn thin,
A golden glow begins to spin.
The air is fresh, the world feels wide,
A rainbow arcs where the hills collide.

"Do you see it, love, just over there?"

"The rainbow’s path—it’s in the air!"
"Shall we chase it, Papa? Can we try?"
"Of course, my sun, let’s see where it lies."


They climb the trail where the storm had played,
The leaves still drip, the paths are swayed.
By Twmbarlwm’s crest, the view expands,
A world below in nature’s hands.


"Do you think we’ll find a pot of gold?"

"Or maybe treasures both bright and old!"
"What if we meet a dragon there?"
"Then we’ll bring him flowers and show we care."

True colors dance where the skies align,
A path of wonder, a secret sign.
Through hills and trails, through streams that play,
We follow the rainbow, come what may.

The river winds with a playful hum,
Reflecting hues of the setting sun.
She stops to gather a stone so smooth,
Her tiny hands a world to prove.

"Papa, do fish see rainbows too?"

"They might—they swim where the colors grew!"
"Do you think they know where it ends?"
"Perhaps they follow it with their friends."


Through fern-lined trails where the shadows hide,
The rainbow dances, a fleeting guide.
She spins and laughs as the breeze takes hold,
Her tiny steps so brave, so bold.

"Papa, does the rainbow wait for me?"

"It’s always here, in the sky, you’ll see.
Its colors stay in the world we know,
In every stream, in every glow."


True colors shine where the heart will roam,
A map of magic, a path to home.
Through hills and woods, through skies of gray,
We follow the rainbow, day by day.

The rainbow fades as the evening nears,
But its magic lingers through time and years.
Her voice grows soft, her steps grow slow,
"Papa, do rainbows always go?"

"They drift away, but they never leave,
They live in the colors we believe."


At the hill’s high crest, the world feels clear,
The colors fade, but the magic’s near.
She stands so tall, her eyes so wide,
"Look, Papa, the world is inside!"


"Every hill we climb, every path we trace,
Brings us closer to this sacred place."

The sun dips low, the twilight sings,
Her tiny hands hold wildflower rings.
She hums a tune as the stars appear,
Her heart still dancing, her mind sincere.


"Papa, will we find another one soon?"

"Every storm brings a rainbow’s tune.
We’ll chase it again, through rain or shine,
And every color will always be mine."


True colors live in the skies above,
A song of wonder, a gift of love.
Through storms and trails, through skies of gray,
We follow the rainbow, come what may.


The lanterns glow as the night takes hold,
The colors rest where the tales are told.
Her dreams take flight in a painted sky,
Where rainbows wait and wonders lie.


"True colors shine where the heart will roam,
A path of magic, a place called home."
