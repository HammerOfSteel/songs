# Tad y Seren

**Labels:** Cottage Psalms
**Created:** 2025-05-05
**Edited:** 2025-05-05

---

You were trouble from day one, you know that?
All hair and howl and starlight squintin’...
And I — well, I’ve been orbiting ever since.
Seren fash. My little spark.

Wiggle wiggle, little lass,
Kickin’ clouds with fists of brass.
Night was nappin’ — then you came,
Spilled stardust down the window frame.
You called me Tad, called me home,
From quiet roads I used to roam.
Every breath you stole with ease,
Turned my storms to summer breeze.

Seren fach, oh Seren fach,
Shine where I can't tread or watch.
If I'm wind, you be the flame,
We’ll whisper through the world again.

You asked once: “What’s a star, really?”
Said, “It’s a hug light-years long, silly.”
Told you gold was love, gone still —
Stuff we hold when time stands still.
We brewed constellations from jam and toast,
Named our galaxy: ‘Dad Does The Most’.
I wrote poems in steam on the café glass,
You wiped ’em off with your sticky sass.

Seren fach, oh Seren fach,
Shine where I can't tread or watch.
If I'm breath, you be the tune,
We’ll meet again beneath the moon.

I never left — just stepped aside,
Still kiss your dreams on the sly.
You can’t see me — but I see you.
Every wish, I walk it through.

They said grief comes like rain — nah.
It comes like socks with one gone, yeah?
But love, love's got longer legs.
Love makes tea and scrambles eggs.
You’ll find me in your wildest giggle,
In midnight chats and bookcase wiggle.
I’m the breath behind your swing’s first sway,
The “go on, then” when skies turn grey.

Seren fach, you wore the sky
Like a coat two sizes high.
Even now, you’re bright, you burn,
Waitin’ for the earth to turn.

If you ever feel alone,
look up.
That one twinklin’ like it knows too much?
That’s me.
Still orbitin’. Still yours.
