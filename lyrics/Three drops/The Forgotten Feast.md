# The Forgotten Feast

**Labels:** Three drops
**Created:** 2024-09-17
**Edited:** 2024-09-17

---

Verse 1: Through mist and moor, where paths entwine,
A group of friends, on Samhain's line.
They wander deep where shadows play,
In Cymru's heart, where legends lay.
The night it falls, the stars take flight,
A glow appears in the velvet night.
A feast they find, with ancient grace,
A cauldron’s warmth in a secret place.

Chorus: Ysbrydion dance, around the flame,
The Awen flows, they speak its name.
In every cup, in every bite,
The soul is fed, with ancient light.
Forgotten feast, on Samhain’s eve,
In this old world, they start to believe.
A sip of cwrw, a taste of lore,
Their spirits rise, forever more.

Verse 2: The cauldron boils with wisdom’s brew,
The mead of poets, gold and true.
Each drop a verse, each bite a tale,
From Cymru’s depths, where dreams prevail.
The friends they sit, with hearts so wide,
As old and new worlds now collide.
They drink deep from the cauldron’s well,
As bardd and awenydd stories tell.

Chorus: Ysbrydion dance, around the flame,
The Awen flows, they speak its name.
In every cup, in every bite,
The soul is fed, with ancient light.
Forgotten feast, on Samhain’s eve,

Bridge: The wind it howls, the forest sighs,
As old spirits in the feast arise.
They share a meal with those unseen,
In a place where time has never been.
The cauldron stirs with life’s own brew,
As tales are told, both old and new.
In every laugh, in every cheer,
The past and present draw near.

Chorus: Ysbrydion dance, around the flame,
The Awen flows, they speak its name.
In every cup, in every bite,

Outro: As dawn approaches, the feast fades away,
But in their hearts, the Awen stays.
A forgotten feast, where souls convene,
On Samhain night, where they have been.
