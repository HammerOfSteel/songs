# A Winter Star

**Labels:** Lanterns for the Night
**Created:** 2025-01-09
**Edited:** 2025-01-10

---

"Bore da, cariad"
(Good morning love)

"bore da baba"
(Good morning papa)

"Wel dyna i ti ddylyfiad gên, wyt ti dal yn gysglyd?
Tyrd yma, wna’i dy godi di i fyny."
(What a yawn, are you still sleepy? 
Come here and I’ll pick you up.)

"hihihi"

"Mae hi’n amser codi, seren fach,"
(It’s time to rise, little star,)

"Cân yr eira’n tywynnu’n dawel, yn llachar."
(The song of the snow shines quietly, bright.)

"A heddiw yw’r diwrnod i ddathlu’n fawr,
Fy seren fach yn y byd mawr."
(And today’s the day to celebrate big
My little star in the wide world.)

The frost clings gently to the trees,
A whispered chill in the winter breeze.
Her laughter echoes, soft and bright,
Guiding me home through the velvet night.

Happy birthday, fy seren fowr,
Shining so true, wherever you are.
Through every season, near or far,
You’ll always be my winter star.

We tread the path where shadows play,
Footprints fading, lost in the grey.
A chapel glows with candlelight,
A beacon warm on this frozen night.

Each flicker’s a memory, a wish I’ve made,
For love to linger, never to fade.

Happy birthday, fy seren fach,
Shining so true, wherever you are.
Though time may whisper, though skies turn far,
You’ll always be my winter star.

"Edrych ar y goleuadau yn y ffenestri."
(Look at the lights in the windows.)'

"Wyt ti’n gweld, Tima?"
"Mae’r seren yna fel ti—yn tywynnu’n ddistaw."
(Do you see, Tima? 
That star is like you—shining quietly.)

The fire crackles, stories unfold,
Her tiny hands in mine to hold.
A song she hums, soft as snow,
Her warmth the light that melts the cold.

Mae amser yn araf, ond mae’r cariad yn para,
Each moment a treasure, each heartbeat a star.
The silence speaks what words cannot say,
Forever here, come what may.

Her eyes grow heavy, her dreams take flight,
As stars now glisten in the deep of night.
I whisper softly, “Sleep well, my sun,”
For this journey of ours has just begun.

Happy birthday, Tima, my little sun,
With all of my love, my only one.
For always, forever, near or far
You’ll always be my winter star.

Mae’r eira’n cwympo, distaw a phur,
(The snow falls, silent and pure,)

A’r byd yn araf, ond cariad yn para.
(And the world slows, but love endures.)

O dan y nen, mor eang, mor fawr,
(Under the sky, so vast, so wide,)

Am byth, fy seren yn y byd mawr.
(Forever, my star in the wide world.)
