In the stillness of the night, where the ancient stones lie,
Whispers of the heart, in the echoes of the sky.
Under the sacred oak, where our spirits intertwine,
We speak of the journey, through the hidden sign.

Through the shadows of the grove, where our paths once crossed,
I hear your voice in the leaves, never lost.
Yn y gwyntoedd hynafol, rydw i’n teimlo dy law,
Guiding me gently, through the mysteries we draw.

In the whispers of the heart, we find our light,
Through the labyrinth of dreams, in the quiet night.
In every breath, in every glance,
We dance the steps of the sacred dance.

By the rivers of the soul, where the currents flow deep,
I see the stars reflected, in secrets we keep.
Ar y llwybrau cuddiedig, lle mae’r enaid yn canu,
I find our truth in the dew, where the worlds are one.

In the waters of life, where our mercury lies,
We seek the tree of wisdom, under hidden skies.
Per aspera ad astra, through the veil we tread,
With every word spoken, in the light we are led.

In the whispers of the heart, we find our light,
Through the labyrinth of dreams, in the quiet night.
In every breath, in every glance,
We dance the steps of the sacred dance.

Through the corridors of time, where the old gods rest,
I carry your essence, in the light’s pure quest.
Yn y dyddiau a ddaw, byddaf yn dal y fflam,
In every dawn that breaks, in every sacred name.

In the whispers of the heart, our paths align,
Through the cycles of time, in the alchemical sign.
In the dawn’s gentle glow, where the old worlds blend,
I carry your fire, through the journey without end.
O fewn y fflamau, lle mae’r ser yn dringo,
Our love remains, eternal, in the songs we sing.

"Whispers of the Heart"
A deeply spiritual and emotional dialogue between Mira and Owen, "Whispers of the Heart" explores their connection beyond life and death. Through symbolic references and poetic language, the song captures the eternal bond that transcends the physical, as Owen resolves to carry Mira’s light forward in their daughter’s life.
