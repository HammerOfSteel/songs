# Sdim ots

**Labels:** Dragons Breath
**Created:** 2025-07-05
**Edited:** 2025-07-05

---

You don’t move on, you carry. 
You don’t forget, you adjust the weight.

Went home for three weeks,
Never smiled more than I did there.
Seeing you there,
Being with you there,
Kissing you there,
And leaving you there.
Yes, I went home for three weeks,
And though I was never raised there,
I grew there,
And each day,
I grow to miss it.

Window’s weepin’, drips draw lines,
Like someone’s writin’ grief between the signs.
Same hills, same puddles, but they lie,
They pretend time’s passed, but I know time’s shy.

Pont-y-gwaith, never stopped there, right?
But now it stings like it held her light.
She once said, “Look, Daddy, clouds got legs!”
Now I hear her in wheels and broken pegs.

Don’t come with them phrases all fluff,
"Least you had her", as if that’s enough.
“She’s in a better place now”, go on, say it again,
And I’ll show you what silence wrapped in rage is, my friend.

‘Sdim ots, cariad, let the world roll by
We ride the road, not askin’ why
All them ghosts, they wave then hide
But I still see you in the mist sometimes.

"Least you're young, you'll have more".

what in the world would you say that for?

For me, truly?
Maybe just for yourself.
Mate - those are platitudes, 
meaningless able that carry no weight.

"Everything happens for a reason",

Oh a reason is it, 
pray tell what that then?
Some mysterious deity, 
in his mysterious ways?

Nah mate, 
what you spout is
an empty meaningless phrase.

“God needed another angel”, oh please.
She needed me, not wings and leaves.
Don’t tell me, what you don't know
Little hollow platitudes, what you sow.

If you don’t know this, then don’t try the script.
Just sit in the dark and let silence drip.
Offer a cup, a hug, a coat,
Not answers in phrases you saw on a post.

Bus wheels turn like nothin’ changed,
A world cracked wide, and I rearranged.
Still see her smile in the steamed-up pane,
Still hear her laugh when it starts to rain.

Hirwaun fog and burnt toast days,
A thousand versions of me in the haze...
I don’t mind, I don’t cry, I just ride.
But she’s still sat left side on the 45.

Sdim ots, cariad — let the world roll by
We ride the road, not askin’ why
All them ghosts, they wave then hide
But I still see you in the mist sometimes

Tried writin’ her name on the window glass,
It slipped like a ghost that don’t want to pass.
You can’t write this into form or code,
It just shows up with your shopping load.

She’d be tall now — feel it in me bones,
Voice like her mother’s, eyes like her own.
still pack two lunchbox snacks,
Still fold her coat like she’ll want it back.

Hey, Mr mandolin Man.

Take me on a trip upon your magic swirlin' ship,
My senses have been stripped, my hands can't feel to grip,
My toes too numb to step, wait only for my boot heels
To be wanderin'.

This town looks friendly,
This town looks nice.
Looks are deceiving.
