# Hush heart

**Labels:** Little Heart, Little Spark
**Created:** 2025-06-22
**Edited:** 2025-06-22

---

[Intro – Whispered Hook]
Hush now, heart, don’t you cry,
Papa’s here, just close them eyes.
No gold, no cradle, just a song,
But I’ll hold you when the nights feel long.

[Verse 1]
Hush little baby, keep still, love —
I got no silver, no skies above.
Just hands that shake and dreams I lost,
But I’ll wrap you warm, no matter the cost.
You breathe so soft like nothin’s wrong,
But I ain’t slept proper in so long.
Still hum that tune like it ain’t cracked,
Still whisper hope when the world turns black.

[Verse 2]
They said, “You'll manage,” yeah alright —
But where’s the map through sleepless nights?
The rent's past due, the lights blink dim,
But you giggle once and I forget the grim.
Your fingers curl ‘round mine so tight,
Like you trust me to make this right.
So I rock you slow in the fading dark,
Sing hushheart songs from a spark gone stark.

[Chorus – RnB Vocal Hook]
Hush little heart, don’t you fear,
I’ll keep the shadows off you, dear.
No diamond ring, no lullaby gold,
But I’ll carry your world ‘til the daylight holds.
Hush little heart, let sleep come slow —
I’ll be your ground when the winds all blow.

[Verse 3]
Used to think I’d be the strong one,
Now I’m just beggin’ dawn to come.
You ask me for a tale each night,
And I make 'em up just to hold the light.
Don’t wanna lie, but how do I say
Papa cries when you look away?
Still I hum, still I breathe,
Still I hush the pain beneath.

[Break – Spoken Whisper]
Go on then… close them eyes.
Don’t worry ‘bout the world tonight.
If it falls apart, let it fall on me —
You just rest, you just be.

[Chorus – Final Hook Variation]
Hush little heart, don’t fear the rain,
I’ll sing it back into calm again.
No cradle rich, no royal song,
But I’m here, my love, where you belong.
Hush little heart, sleep safe, sleep true —
Everything I’ve got, I give to you.

[Outro – Fading Hook, music box style]
Hush now, hushheart, don’t you cry...
Papa’s still here… close your eyes.
