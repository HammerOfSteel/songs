Yet the music in her mind was stuck on pause and she couldn’t hear the true magnificence of the lullaby. So softly spoken were his words that weaved her like a quilt through the pain and suffering, she was the thread and he was the needle, pulling, pulling. She retaliated and tried to draw her mind back into a peaceful place but his force and calm lulled her back into his arms. No longer was her mind filled with those distracting emotions or sentiments, just a beautiful vacancy that he longed to fill. His voice sank into her mind, banishing any ideas of free will. Thoughts of security – trust and dependence – filled the head that he now owned. He smiled as the soulless eyes stared directly at him, unknowingly ready to cater to his every wish.
