# Super Nova

**Labels:** Super Nova
**Created:** 2025-06-09
**Edited:** 2025-06-09

---

Time is not a line nor a road,
It doesn't pass by in equal integers:
It grows,
Swells,
Accumulates-
In small moments,
Gets caught in the reefs.
larger pools for more prominent moments.
Boundless depth in a singularity.

The time we spent
Continues to consume,
It swells,
And dwells
In the foreground,
Always.
Time does not pass by,
It is here,
Screaming,
Just as it always has been,
Growing.
Haunting.

I don’t think that I can bare
To accumulate anymore of our time.
My lungs are full,
I have choked on the untameable mass of the lamenting sea.

She weren’t made — she arrived,
a collapse of the maybe into the only.
Like all the stars that could’ve been my life
shrank to a single flash that held me wholly.

Wavefunction girl — she smiled,
and the laws of my world rewrote themselves.
Not born, but remembered.
Like I’d already held her
in dreams I forgot to believe.

She was every timeline burnin’ in my chest,
every almost, every maybe, every rest.
A soul spun from quanta and lullabies,
laughin' at me like she'd always been wise.

I weren’t ready — still ain’t —
but she didn’t ask for my permission.
She just was, like gravity,
pullin’ my orbit into submission.

You think you know love
til it breaks all known laws.
Til it splits you like a nucleus,
no applause.

She shone so bright, it burned the room —
every grief I’d buried bloomed.
She held my finger with starlight hands,
said nothin’, but I finally understood demands.

There were none.

Just fire.

Just presence that shattered the choir
of logic I’d tried to build round loss.
You can’t carry her and live.
But somehow I still do —
and I call that gift.

I walk with her echoes stitched in bone,
talk to the night like it’s her phone.
Swear sometimes she answers —
not in words, but in heat and stone.

Super nova, burst of bone and flame,
you rewrote the stars with your tiny name.
I’m here and not, I breathe and ache —
collapsed to light for your love’s sake.

I see you in her — this little sun.
Not the same, never one-to-one.
But the light refracts when I hold her face,
and for a breath, you're both in the same place.

There’s no replacing.
Just ripples across this space I’m placed in.
You, the gone star; her, the flicker that stays —
I orbit both in unspeakable ways.

Love didn’t end.
It multiplied.

You were the burning, she is the tide.
For me, for you, for us little sun,
and the bridge still burning — undone.

“I carry you in particles and prayer.
In breath. In bread. In bedtime air.”
“I am broken. I am whole.
You were the collapse — she is the soul.”

I stay, I ache to leave.
But I’ve learned how to love through grief.
Fickle flame burning the seams
To be the one who names the dreams.

Sat down on your yellow brick road,
Clicked me heels and kissed a toad.
Rickety chat with queen of hearts,
Searching for a way to start.

Mad as a hatter, cool as a storm,
Of tea and thyme and bee toast,
Laughing with chocolate on your nose.
Take me home away from here, 
up the warren all is clear.

You're gone. But not lost.
She’s here. But not whole.
And I am the in-between.
That singular fold,
Fold in time, time to place
A super Nova born of grace.

Half hung grin on the Cheshire cat,
Velvet stitched the mad mad hat.
Good and kind, none the callous.
This super Nova named Alice.

Super nova, flash of never and now,
you bent my soul and taught me how.
To love like fire that never asks back,
to stay when the sky goes black.

singularity is
when you hold me tight
on a sapphire night,
magic made
twisted under satin sheets
beneath the nonchalant moon
 
your intent gaze,
cuts through mist of illusion,
gently whispering “detachment”
from a banausic existence
 
love and pain
swell deep inside my fragile body,
as i sink
into the infinity of spacetime,
where the gravity of your love alone
bends my aching heart
