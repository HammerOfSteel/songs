# Red dust

**Labels:** Dancing salamanders
**Created:** 2024-08-09
**Edited:** 2024-08-09

---

Alone beneath the endless Martian sky,
Red dust swirling, no comfort in sight,
Footsteps fade in the desolate sand,
A pioneer’s heart, fighting to withstand.

Red dust, red dreams, fading in the light,
Hope clings to the edge, holding on so tight,
A war within the soul, a battle left unseen,
In the wasteland’s grasp, where nothing’s evergreen.

Discovery’s thrill, now buried in despair,
Whispers of Earth, lost in the thinning air,
The horizon stretches, an unyielding line,
As memories of blue skies begin to unwind.

Red dust, red dreams, fading in the light,
Hope clings to the edge, holding on so tight,
A war within the soul, a battle left unseen,
In the wasteland’s grasp, where nothing’s evergreen.

Internal echoes, a dialogue of doubt,
The fight to keep believing, when all light’s gone out,
In the silence, the heart’s quiet plea,
To find some meaning in this vast, empty sea.

Stranded in the red, no stars to guide the way,
Yet in the depth of night, the spirit starts to sway,
A glimmer of resolve, faint as it may be,
To keep pushing forward, in the face of what can’t be.

Red dust, red dreams, fading in the light,
Hope clings to the edge, holding on so tight,
A war within the soul, a battle left unseen,
In the wasteland’s grasp, where nothing’s evergreen.

The Martian winds howl, yet the heart beats on,
In the barren red, where all seems gone,
A pioneer’s vow, to never turn away,
To find hope’s ember, in the dust and decay.

Style:
Acoustic, sparse, haunting strings, melancholic tones, introspective, atmospheric, evocative of desolation and inner conflict.
