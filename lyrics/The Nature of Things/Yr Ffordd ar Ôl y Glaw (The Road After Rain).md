# Yr Ffordd ar Ôl y Glaw (The Road After Rain)

**Labels:** The Nature of Things
**Created:** 2024-11-27
**Edited:** 2024-11-27

---

The rain has passed, the sky feels wide,
The river hums with a softened tide.
He carries her close, her breath so light,
A fragile warmth in the fading night.

"Does the river know how the storm has gone?
How it flows so steady, carrying on?"

The path bends low where the water flows,
The trees arch high, their shadows grow.
Her tiny hand reaches toward the air,
Grasping at moments that linger there.

"The world feels calm after the rain,
Do you see the clouds? Do they feel the same?"
She coos in reply, her gaze so bright,
A piece of her in the soft twilight.

The road after rain, where the light breaks through,
Where the world feels fresh, yet softly true.
Each step a song, each breath a guide,
A love that lingers, that won’t subside.

The river twists where the reeds stand tall,
A place they walked once, he can’t recall.
Her laughter faint in the echoes near,
A ghost of love that feels so clear.

"Would she see us now, would she know this peace?
The rain has gone, but the love won’t cease."
Her tiny fingers tug at his sleeve,
A reminder that it's okay to grieve.

He stops by the shore where the pebbles gleam,
The air feels soft, a waking dream.
She reaches down for a stone so round,
A world of wonder in what she’s found.

"What have you there, my little sun?
A piece of the river, the storm undone?"
Her laughter breaks like the dawn’s first ray,
A light that guides him through the gray.

The road after rain, where the heart finds rest,
Where love remains though put to the test.
Each step a bridge, each thought a flame,
A path that carries her name.

The sky grows soft as the day turns late,
The river hums, the trees await.
He holds her close, her eyes half-closed,
A love so vast, it overflows.

"Through storms we’ve weathered, through grief, through pain,
We’ve found a road after the rain.
Each tear that fell, each step we take,
Is part of the light that love can make."

The cabin glows as the path grows clear,
The scent of earth from the storm lingers near.
She rests in his arms, her tiny face,
A moment of love in a timeless space.

"You’ll never know how strong you are,
You’re my light, my life, my guiding star."
Her breath grows steady, her fingers still,
A quiet peace, a will fulfilled.

The road after rain, where the world feels whole,
Where love pours forth and heals the soul.
Each step a promise, each day a guide,
A love that lingers, that won’t subside.

The river fades as the night takes hold,
The stars appear in hues of gold.
He carries her home, her dreams take flight,
Through the road after rain, into the night.

"Through the road after rain, we’ll always go,
A love that follows, a light that glows."
