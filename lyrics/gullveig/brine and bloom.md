“Instrumental folk-jazz piece titled Brine and Bloom. Warm, organic mood — a gentle reel with fiddle and fingerpicked acoustic guitar interweaving like seaweed in current. Upright bass walking softly beneath, brushed ride cymbal shimmering like light on water, and delicate piano accents echoing slow tides. The music should feel tender and healing, with a rhythm that sways like waves — expressing growth through grief, where salt preserves and life returns. Hints of coastal breeze, evening calm, and quiet resilience. Emotional tone: reflective, hopeful, maritime, alive.”
