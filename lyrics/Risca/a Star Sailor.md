# a Star Sailor

**Labels:** Dancing salamanders, Risca
**Created:** 2024-08-30
**Edited:** 2024-08-30

---

Verse 1
In the whisper of the morning mist,
Where the dewdrops gently cling,
I feel your spirit close to mine,
In every bloom of spring.
Your laughter echoes in the breeze,
Like a song the larks would sing,
You’re the light that guides my way,
Through every darkened thing.

Chorus
Fy Seren Forwr (My Star Sailor), you light the sky,
Even when the world seems dim and dry.
Your love, a beacon, pure and bright,
Leads me through the endless night.
Though time has passed and you’re afar,
You’re with me still, my guiding star.

Verse 2
In the forest where the shadows dance,
I see your face in every tree,
The rustling leaves, they speak your name,
Cariad (Love), you’re here with me.
The waves that crash upon the shore,
Carry whispers from the sea,
Reminding me of days gone by,
When it was just you and me.

Bridge
And in the stillness of the night,
When the world is calm and still,
I feel your touch in every breeze,
A warmth that time can’t kill.
Little Sun, you shine so bright,
Guiding me with gentle light,
I hold you close, in heart and mind,
In every memory I find.

Outro
So here I stand, beneath the stars,
On this day that marks your birth,
I promise to carry your light within,
As long as I walk this earth.
Butterfly, you’re free to soar,
Yet you’re with me evermore,
In every breath, in every sigh,
You’re the star that lights my sky.
