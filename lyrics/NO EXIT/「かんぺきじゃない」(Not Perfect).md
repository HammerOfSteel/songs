「かんぺきじゃない」(Not Perfect)

🎙️ Spoken Intro
だれかの期待ばかり追いかけてた
I kept chasing the expectations of others

気がついたら、自分の声が消えてた
Before I knew it, my own voice had disappeared

わたしは…完璧じゃない。でも、それが悪いこと？
I’m… not perfect. But is that really such a bad thing?




🎤 Verse 1 – Middle School Era
制服のしわを気にしてた朝
Mornings I worried about wrinkles in my uniform

しずかな教室、息を殺したまま
In the quiet classroom, I held my breath

目立たないように、答えも正解
Answered just right, so I wouldn’t stand out

間違える勇気より空気の方が大事
Fitting in was more important than the courage to be wrong

ノートに書いたのは他人の言葉
What I wrote in my notebook were other people’s words

「いい子ですね」それが鎖の言葉
“Such a good girl” — praise that felt like chains

マスクをつけた、いつのまにか
I put on a mask, and didn’t notice when

肌に溶けて、わたしがいなくなった
It melted into my skin — until I was no longer there




🎶 Chorus 1
完璧じゃない、それでいいはずなのに
Not perfect — that should’ve been okay

ずっと「正しい」ふりをしたあの日々
But I spent those days pretending to be “right”

わたしの声、どこで失くしたの？
Where did I lose my voice?

誰の夢を生きてたの？ねぇ、教えてよ
Whose dream was I living? Tell me — I want to know




🎤 Verse 2 – High School into University
模試の点数、偏差値の檻
Test scores, a cage made of numbers

夢より現実、それが「正しい」道
Reality over dreams — that was the “right” path

メイクで隠す目のクマと涙
Hiding tears and dark circles under makeup

「大丈夫です」しか言えない朝
All I could say was “I’m fine” every morning

サークルもバイトも履歴書のため
Clubs and part-time jobs — all for my résumé

本当の自分？今さら聞かないで
My true self? Don’t ask me that now

ビルの谷間で迷子になって
Lost in the shadows between high-rise buildings

笑顔の仮面がヒビ割れてきた
My smiling mask finally started to crack




🎶 Pre-Chorus
このまま続けたら、壊れるだけ
If I keep going like this, I’ll break

逃げることは、弱さじゃない
Running away — that’s not weakness




🎶 Chorus 2
完璧じゃない、やっと気づいた瞬間
Not perfect — and I finally realized that

息ができた、涙が熱くて甘い
I could breathe again — my tears were warm and sweet

わたしの声、少し震えてたけど
My voice trembled at first, but

生きたいって叫んだ、それだけでよかった
I shouted, “I want to live!” — and that was enough




🎤 Verse 3 – The Break, Leaving Everything
駅のホーム、切符も捨てた
On the train platform, I threw away my ticket

行き先はない、でも足は進んでた
No destination — but my feet kept moving

スマホの電源も落として放つ
Turned off my phone — and let it all go

はじめて見た空、こんなにも広かった
The sky I saw for the first time was so wide

コンビニの弁当、知らない森の中
Convenience store bento in an unfamiliar forest

誰にも見つからない、自由な静けさ
No one could find me — it was a peaceful kind of freedom

土に触れた手が震えてたけど
My hands trembled as I touched the earth

わたしはわたしを取り戻しはじめた
But I was starting to reclaim myself



🎶 Chorus 3
完璧じゃない、でも今ここにいる
Not perfect — but I’m still here

傷もシミも、わたしを作る色
Scars and stains — they color who I am

わたしの声、もう怖くないよ
My voice doesn’t scare me anymore

生きるって、そういうことなんだろう
Maybe this is what it means to live




🎤 Verse 4 – Decades Later, with Grandchild
湯気立つ茶碗、孫の笑顔
Steam rising from tea, my grandchild’s smile

「おばあちゃん、どうして山に住んでるの？」
“Grandma, why do you live in the mountains?”

答えは簡単、でも深い話
The answer’s simple — but a deep story

「心がね、土みたいに耕されたの」
“My heart… it was tilled like soil”

陶器のヒビも、絵のにじみも
The cracks in my pottery, the smudges in my paintings

消さずに残す、それがわたしの生き方
I leave them as they are — that’s my way of living

禅が言う無我？それも一理あるけど
Zen says “no-self”? That’s got truth to it

わたしは影を抱いて、光にしたかったの
But I chose to hold my shadow — and turn it into light




🌉 Bridge
影を無視すれば、光も偽る
Ignore your shadow, and your light becomes a lie

弱さも怒りも、ぜんぶ私
My weakness, my rage — they’re all me

ただの器じゃない、火で焼いた魂
Not just a vessel — but a soul fired in flame

欠けても、美しいままに
Even broken, I remain beautiful



🎸 Musical Solo
(instrumental break — no lyrics)




🗣️ Spoken Outro
孫よ…
My dear grandchild...

他人の夢を生きる必要はない
You don’t need to live someone else’s dream

君の声は、君だけの宝物
Your voice is a treasure that only you have

完璧じゃなくても、君は美しい
Even if you’re not perfect, you are beautiful

だから、生きて。君のままで。
So live — just as you are.
