# haul ar fryn

**Labels:** Dragons Breath
**Created:** 2025-07-19
**Edited:** 2025-07-19

---

Hope, socks, tea, light
One kiss, two laughs, sleep tight
Spare change for a sunny mood
A smile or two, and warm food

Trolley wheel squeaks, street hummin’ slow
Weather’s feelin’ unsure, but I still go
Past daft dreams in the windowpane frost
Maps in my mind, of all I’ve lost

But there’s petrol in the gut, fire in the sock
Mum’s note in the coat, with her crooked clock
"Things mend, bach" she wrote by the tear
And I walk on, mate, I’m still here

Clink, clank, tap, tune
Better days come, maybe not soon
Push, pull, walk, spin
But the sun’ll rise, back on that hill again

Crisps in my pocket, past the playground fence
Laughter still echoes like it’s past tense
Bench knows my weight, crack in the wood
I sit and I think, yeah, I reckon I could

Do this again, even if it’s bare
Even if the moon’s just pretendin’ it cares
I plant little joys like seeds on a track
Even pavement cracks get flowers back

Tick, tock, swing, grin
Every loss makes space for a win
Laugh, lean, blink, still
The sun keeps hidin’, but it knows the hill

Skip a beat, skip a fare
Sing to no one, still someone there
Toast crumbs on the bed, tea too cold
But I’m still here, still gettin’ bold

Tie my shoelace, pick a fight with fate
Tell the clouds, "go on then, I’ll wait"
Bit mad, bit brill, bit tired too
But the road hums soft like it always knew

Dum, dee, hum, sway
We keep walkin’, come what may
Bump, jolt, tug, grin
Sun’s not far, just behind that hill again

Close your eyes, count to tree
Sun’ll find you, wait and see
Cwtsh your dreams, soft and still
Haul ar fryn, it always will.
