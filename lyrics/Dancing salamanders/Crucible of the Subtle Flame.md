# Crucible of the Subtle Flame

**Labels:** Dancing salamanders
**Created:** 2024-08-16
**Edited:** 2024-08-16

---

Verse 1
In the athanor of dusk, where shadows transmute,
I step through the furnace, where spirits refute.
The dragon's breath stirs in the argent glow,
As the prima materia whispers secrets below.

Chorus
In the fire’s crucible, where fear is born,
The salt dissolves as the veil is torn.
Bound to the flame, where the soul is unmade,
In the cauldron of self, where the heart is weighed.

Verse 2
The Subtle Fire Being, with eyes of the quicksilver dawn,
Presents a mirror where all masks are gone.
Visions flicker in the alembic's haze,
Where the blackened sun reveals the soul's maze.

Bridge
In the furnace where the sulphur sings,
The black crow takes flight on ashen wings.
In the shadow’s maw, where fears entwine,
The gold of the spirit begins to shine.

Chorus
In the fire’s crucible, where fear is born,
The salt dissolves as the veil is torn.
Bound to the flame, where the soul is unmade,
In the cauldron of self, where the heart is weighed.

Outro
As the vision fades in the fiery tide,
I walk the path where the raven hides.
In the flame’s embrace, I find my core,
Transformed by the secret that the fire bore.
