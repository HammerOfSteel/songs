[Verse 1]
[Male Voice british accent]
In the hush before the kettle hums,
And the morning finds its way,
The light spills soft across the floor,
Like memories at play.

[Childs voice]
[giggle] 
“Papa, chase the butterflies!”
The echo drifts, so kind,
A shimmer caught between the worlds,
Where heart and breath align.

[Pre-Chorus]
Through air and ember, I still hear,
Your laughter weave the dawn,
The wind becomes philosopher,
Teaching me you’re never gone.

[Chorus]
You’re the flutter of wings in the quiet morn,
The gold in the breath I take,
Though time has cooled your tender form,
Love stirs each leaf awake.
Butterfly kisses on the wind,
Alchemy of soul and skin,
You’re the hush between the heart’s two beats,
And where my prayers begin.

[Verse 2]
In the garden by the low stone wall,
Where marigolds still bloom,
Your tiny footprints mark the path,
That leads beyond the gloom.

[soft inhale] 

The petals know your secret name,
And bow as you drift through,
Butterfly kisses on the wind,
Transmute my grief to dew.

[Pre-Chorus]
The world’s a wheel of turning breath,
Where endings fold to start,
And every gust that stirs the leaves
Reshapes the broken heart.

[Chorus]
You’re the flutter of wings in the quiet morn,
The warmth that thaws the frost,
Though I reach for what can’t return,
No step in love is lost.
Butterfly kisses on the wind,
A spell that never ends,
You’re the echo in the morning light,
The pulse that grief transcends.

[Bridge]
[whisper] 
I heard you in the candle flame,

In twilight’s fragile seam,
Where air and memory intertwine,
And mercy feels like dream.
If sorrow is the crucible,
Then love’s the fire divine —
Butterfly kisses on the wind,
Your soul forever mine.

[Final Chorus]
You’re the shimmer of dawn when I lift my gaze,
The sigh when the night withdraws,
Though your hands are gone, your light remains,
The truth behind all laws.
Butterfly kisses on the wind,
Philosopher’s breath, my friend,
Through loss we turn, through turning find,
The gold that will not end.

[Outro]
So when the wind begins to play,
And daisies start to bend,
I’ll close my eyes, you’ll brush my cheek,
In every whisper, I’ll ascend.

[soft laugh] 
“Papa, look! The sky’s awake!”

The moment holds, forever.
