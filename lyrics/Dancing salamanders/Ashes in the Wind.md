# Ashes in the Wind

**Labels:** A Spiral Path, Dancing salamanders
**Created:** 2024-09-27
**Edited:** 2024-09-27

---

Verse 1:
The ground is cold where I lay her down,
In the silence of grief, no one around.
The winds from the Unclaimed Lands blow through,
Whispering secrets of places unknown, but nothing true.
I called to my family, but no one came,
Now I bury her alone, lost in pain.
The unknown spire looms, a shadowed crest,
Yet I’m stuck here, with her to rest.

Chorus:
Ashes in the wind, carried far away,
The pieces of her soul, slipping day by day.
The Unclaimed Lands, a mirror to my heart,
Empty and forsaken, where do I start?
No whispers of comfort, no hands to hold,
Just ashes in the wind, growing cold.

Verse 2:
I gaze to the distance, where the spires touch the sky,
Wondering if beyond them, her spirit could fly.
But all I see is the unknown that lies ahead,
And the hollow feeling inside where once hope bled.
They say the unclaimed spire is unreachable, lost,
But I’m already there, having paid the ultimate cost.
Her life was my beacon, now all is dark,
In this desolate place, I’ve lost my spark.

Chorus:
Ashes in the wind, carried far away,
The pieces of her soul, slipping day by day.
The Unclaimed Lands, a mirror to my heart,

Bridge:
Beyond the spires, where the unknown hides,
Are the answers I seek, or just more lies?
I stand at the edge, staring into the abyss,
Wondering if I’ll ever feel more than this.
The wind takes her ashes, as the world spins,
I wonder if my journey here begins or ends.

Final Chorus:
Ashes in the wind, carried far away,
The pieces of her soul, slipping day by day.
The Unclaimed Lands, a mirror to my heart,

No whispers of comfort, no hands to hold,
Just ashes in the wind, growing cold.

Outro:
Alone, I stand where the unknown begins,
With nothing but silence, and ashes in the wind.
