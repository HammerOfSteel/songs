# Fflam Wen - The White Flame

**Labels:** Blodau yn y Lludw
**Created:** 2025-01-29
**Edited:** 2025-01-29

---

Mae’r fflam yn byw, ond heb ddistrywio.
(The flame lives, but it does not destroy.)
Pa liw yw hiraeth? A yw’n wyn?
(What color is longing? Is it white?)

¨'The streets are quiet, yet full of sound,
Footsteps echo where none are found.
Rhwng cysgodion a goleuni, rwyt ti’n aros.
(Between shadows and light, you remain.)
A shopfront window, a child's handprint,
A faded name on a weathered bench.

'You carry me, but do you see?
Am I a beacon, or do I bind thee?
Cariad yw cysgod nad yw’n mynd yn ddu,
(Love is a shadow that never turns black,)
A whisper in the dark, a spark in the blue.

I hear you burning in my chest,
A warmth, a weight, a hollow nest.
Ai cariad yw hwn, ai carchar?
(Is this love, or is it a prison?)
Tima, fy machgen haul, fy seren fach,
Your name still lingers on my path.

But if I let go, will you take her too?
Is loss the keeper, or is it just you?
Mae’r gwynt yn canu, ond mae’n dawel hefyd.
(The wind sings, but it’s quiet too.)

You carry me, but do you see?
I am not prison, only memory.
Mae hiraeth yn fflam nad yw’n llosgi’n lân,
(Longing is a flame that never burns away,)
A ghost of warmth, a love that stays.

The world turns, the years slip by,
Still, the stars align in the midnight sky.
Mae’n creu pont o olau, rhwng y cysgod a’r hedd,
(It builds a bridge of light, between shadow and peace.)
I speak her name, and the night sighs back,
Not silence, but something intact.

The streets are quiet, yet full of sound,
My steps still echo, but I am unbound.
Mae’r fflam yn byw, ond heb ddistrywio.
(The flame lives, but it does not destroy.)
Not an ending, not a loss,
Just a path we always cross.

You carry me, but do you see?
I am not pain—I am memory.
