# Psalm of Small Things

**Labels:** Heartbound
**Created:** 2025-08-17
**Edited:** 2025-08-17

---

En flod flyter sakta fram, sakta likt människan,
En flod ger vad livet gav tillbaks till sitt hav.
Ge mig styrka, ge mig mod, att värna om denna jord
Ge mig styrka, ge mig mod och kraft som en flod.

Steam from a mug on a winter’s day,
cat by the grate where the coals still play.
Rain on slate with its silver tongue,
a hymn half-written, a song half-sung.
The hush of a room when the fire is low,
the softest footprints in falling snow.
A candle stub, a lullaby thread,
the quiet blessing of a loaf of bread.

Small things, bright things, whispering true,
psalms in the shadows, carried for you.
Little things, hidden things, stitched in the seams,
the world remade in the smallest dreams.

First cry breaking the hush of night,
your mother’s smile in the candlelight.
Tiny fists and a breath so thin,
a universe folded where life begins.
Your first words stumbling, soft but clear,
the world made wide just by hearing you near.
The games, the walks, the tumble, the climb,
the way the stars bent low in time.

Small things, bright things, glowing in streams,
lanterns of memory, carried in dreams.
Little things, fleeting things, still they remain,
written in whispers, spoken in rain.

The sun he is shining rising and shining
The sun he is shining to brighten our way
Father sun shine over me your child I will always be
Father sun shine over me and brighten our way

Hugs that mended what words could not,
summer fields where the sunlight caught.
The taste of berries, the scrape of knees,
the stories read beneath leaning trees.
The tilt of her head when she held you near,
the laugh that scattered the weight of fear.
All that I miss, all that I know,
is the psalm of small things that made us whole.

Small things, bright things, gathered in flame,
sing them, remember, call them by name.
Little things, endless things, carved in the soul,
the psalm of small things makes the heart whole.

The river is flowing flowing and growing
The river is flowing back to the sea
Mother Earth is carrying me her child I will always be
Mother Earth carry me back to the sea
