# Hej

**Labels:** Dancing salamanders, Gills and guts
**Created:** 2024-09-20
**Edited:** 2024-09-20

---

Place: St. Ives

Verse 1
I step off the bus, cobbled streets ahead,
New town, new faces, thoughts fill my head.
St. Ives’ beauty, but I feel so small,
Artist’s dreams on the gallery walls.
I got my guard up, don't know what to say,
Just a stranger trying to find my way.
They say “Hej,” I just smile and nod,
Words in my throat, but they never get caught.

Chorus
Hej, it’s hard to say,
When the world feels miles away.
But in this town, I’ll find my voice,
Make a move, it’s my choice.
Hej, it’s strange but true,
Connection’s hard, but I’ll break through.
Like a canvas clean and new,
Gotta paint it, me and you.

Verse 2
They’re out there laughing, but I’m locked in tight,
Afraid of the awkward, afraid of the light.
Walk these streets, galleries in bloom,
But I feel like the odd one out in the room.
Brushstrokes on canvas, I watch them flow,
Wishing I could speak like they let go.
Maybe I’m art that’s yet to be found,
Gotta open my heart, let it make a sound.

Bridge
I’m walking the line between fear and hope,
Stuck on the outside, trying to cope.
But these streets, they whisper soft and low,
“Every masterpiece started slow.”
So I’ll take a step, then one more,
Unlock the door, can’t ignore
That the world outside’s waiting for me,
Hej, maybe that’s the key.

St. Ives, your colors, they set me free,
In this canvas of life, I’ll find me.
