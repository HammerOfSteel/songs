# A Letter Unread

**Labels:** A Spiral Path, Dancing salamanders
**Created:** 2024-09-27
**Edited:** 2024-09-27

---

(Verse 1)
The letter came, untouched by time,
A paper folded, creased with lines.
Her name in ink, their hands so frail,
After years of silence, now a tale.
They ask of her, my brightest star,
The memory of her, never far.
Tears blur the words, but I hold them tight,
In this moment, the wrong feels right.

(Pre-Chorus)
How long have I waited, for this sign?
But now the pain and hope entwine.

(Chorus)
A letter unread, now trembling in my hands,
From those I lost, through broken strands.
They ask me of her, the light we all knew,
In their words, I find something new.

(Verse 2)
The years of silence, cut deep inside,
But now I read their hearts, open wide.
The pain of absence, the hurt they caused,
Yet I feel the love beneath the flaws.
They ask me for a memory sweet,
A moment of joy, something complete.
But all I can think of are the days she smiled,
A fleeting memory of my precious child.

(Bridge)
Can we find our way back from this?
Years of sorrow, of moments missed.
But in this letter, I see a start,
A chance to mend, to heal the heart.
Her memory binds us, even now,
Perhaps it’s time to learn just how.

(Outro)
With tears in my eyes, I start to write,
A memory shared in the fading light.
In this letter, a bridge I’ll send,
To begin again, and maybe mend.
