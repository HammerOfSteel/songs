# Det vackraste

**Labels:** Elara
**Created:** 2025-06-16
**Edited:** 2025-06-16

---

[Verse 1]
Y peth mwyaf prydferth,
Yw dy weld di’n cysgu,
Yn y tawelwch pur,
Anghofiaf amser, lle a sŵn.
Rwyt ti fan hyn gerllaw,
A gallaf deimlo’th anadl,
Cael dy gariad di,
Yw’r peth mwyaf prydferth i mi.


---

[Verse 2]
Mor hudol yw’r bore,
Pan mae niwl y nos yn cilio,
Mae’r golau’n dawnsio’n dawel,
Wrth i’r wawr ddod nôl i’r bryniau.
Awel oer yn chwythu,
Trwy’r ystafell fel breuddwyd,
Mi grogaf wrthy’th ochr,
Yn llawen fy mod ti yma.


---

[Chorus]
Amser o dawelwch,
Eiliad o heddwch llwyr,
Eiliad o lawenydd,
Dim ond fi a’th loer.
Cael caru a chael fy ngharu,
Seren fach yn fy mynwes,
Ein cariad gonest ni,
Yw’r peth mwyaf prydferth i mi.


---

[Verse 3]
Y peth mwyaf annwyl,
Yw bod yn agos at rywun,
Pan fo’r lleuad lawn
Yn gwenu dros y mynyddoedd.
Rwyt ti’n dal gerllaw,
A gallaf deimlo’th anadl,
Cael dy gariad di,
Yw’r peth mwyaf prydferth i mi.

[Chorus – repeat with variation]
Amser o dawelwch,

Eiliad o lawenydd,
Dim ond ni dan y sêr.

The loveliest thing I know,
Is watching you while you’re sleeping,
The whole world soft and still,
And time just slips away and stills.
You’re right here by my side,
I feel your breath so steady,
To be loved by you, little star,
Is the loveliest thing by far.

It’s magic in the morn,
When the mist begins to scatter,
And all the hills wake up,
With golden light on stone and heather.
A breeze rolls through the room,
Like whispers through the valleys,
I pull you close to me,
So lucky just to let you be.

Moments of quiet,
A little peace to call our own,
Snuggled up proper,
Just us, no need to roam.
To love and be loved back,
My little star held close to me,
This gentle kind of love—
It’s the loveliest thing there’ll be.

The best thing in the world,
Is being close to someone truly,
When the moon hangs up above,
Over Risca shining bluely.
You’re lying by my side,
I feel your breath beyond measure,
To be yours and you be mine,
Is love beyond all treasure.

Moments of quiet,
A little peace to call our own,
Snuggled up proper,
Safe, and never alone.
To love and be loved back,
My little star held close to me,
Your love, so warm and true—
That’s the loveliest thing to me.
