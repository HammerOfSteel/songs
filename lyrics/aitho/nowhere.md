# nowhere

**Labels:** aitho
**Created:** 2025-12-30
**Edited:** 2025-12-30

---

The house is full of sulfur and the smell of old regrets,
Of words we haven’t spoken, things the heart forgets.
The moment that we’re moving, the wheels begin to spin, 
Starts trickle down, flicker underneath your tired skin.

Little toast ghost, made of a million brekkies past,
strech it, 
hold it,
have it last. 

Seen it
Lived it,
wore that hat, 
There’s no use looking backward,
no, there’s no time for that.

The city is a furnace,
the kitchen is a pyre,
Out there in the moss and the myre,
Breathing in the fule,
breathing out the fire.

Don't stop,
please do not,
it's like you've already forgot.

Remember what happened at the light back there?
Freeze up like that at the first smell of fear.

If we don’t keep going,
there’s no telling nor knowing.
Knowing where we'll end up, at the end,
aye, please, just...
let's not pretend...

Stay here,
let's just dissolve..
let it involve, Just me and you..
just... only...
us two.

Great Solvent, river,
meandering through the night,
Washing off the history, dim little car light.

It’s dissolving all sensible, the forms we used to wear,
It’s pulling all the static out of every strand of hair.

Rancid,
Principle,
the only thing the acid cannot touch,
The part of us that’s "kindled",
to little,
much too much.

Every mile is a blessing,
every exit is a sin,
We’re scrubbing out the "person" just to let the "spirit" in.

So keep your eyes on the horizon,
and your foot upon the floor,
Until we don’t remember what we're even fighting for.

Do you see it too?
In the passanger seat.
All snuggled up, clutching its knees.

Nothing but the friction,
Nothing but the heat,
A chemical reaction,
bitter,
and..
sweet... 

Let it take the names,
Let it take the dates,
Let it take the anger,
Lift these rusted weights.

We’re finally becoming what we were always meant to be,
A single drop of you,
of me,
in a slow moving sea.

Just me and you...
(Just me and you)

Just... only us two.
(Let it dissolve)

Don't stop...
please do not...
