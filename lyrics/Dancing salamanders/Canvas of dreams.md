# Canvas of dreams

**Created:** 2024-08-14
**Edited:** 2024-08-14

On a canvas of fire, where dreams take flight,
In the quiet of night, I find my light.

Brush in hand, heart full of fire,
I paint the world, my own desire.
Far from the shadows, far from the pain,
In every stroke, I break the chain.

On a canvas of dreams, I draw my fate,
Through colors and shades, I navigate.
In the silence of night, I paint the sky,
With every brush, I learn to fly.

Under the moon, I find my way,
Through the whispers of night, I seize the day.
Far from the past, in the light’s embrace,
I find my truth, I find my place.

Sous le ciel de Paris, je peins ma vie,
In every stroke, I find the key.
In the heart of the city, my dreams unfold,
A story of fire, a story untold.

On a canvas of dreams, I draw my fate,
Through colors and shades, I navigate.
In the silence of night, I paint the sky,
With every brush, I learn to fly.

With every line, with every hue,
I paint the world, I make it new.
In fjarlægðinni, where dreams reside,
I find my truth, I cannot hide.

On a canvas of fire, my spirit soars,
Through the darkness, through the doors.

"Canvas of dreams"
A reflection of Mira’s burning passion for art, "Canvas of Fire" delves into the conflict between tradition and self-expression. The song paints a vivid picture of her struggle to create her own identity, where every brushstroke is a step toward freedom.
