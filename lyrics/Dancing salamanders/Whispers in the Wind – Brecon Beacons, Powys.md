# Whispers in the Wind – Brecon Beacons, Powys

**Labels:** Dancing salamanders, Ar Lan y Mor
**Created:** 2024-09-19
**Edited:** 2024-09-19

---

Verse 1:
On the hillside, where the winds do roam,
I stand alone, yet never on my own.
The grasses dance, the sky so wide,
But it’s your voice I hear beside.

A whisper soft, through the breeze you call,
A fleeting touch, yet I feel it all.
In the rustle of leaves, in the sway of trees,
Your spirit lingers, carried on the breeze.

Chorus:
Whispers in the wind, you are near,
In every gust, your presence clear.
Though I walk this path alone,
Your love is the breeze, guiding me home.

Verse 2:
The hills stretch far, the clouds roll by,
But it’s your voice that lifts me high.
Through the fields of green, the valleys deep,
Your memory is a song I keep.

In the quiet hum of nature’s breath,
You speak to me, beyond life and death.
In every gust, in every sigh,
Your love flows with the wind on high.

Chorus:
Whispers in the wind, you are near,
In every gust, your presence clear.
Though I walk this path alone,
Your love is the breeze, guiding me home.

Bridge:
The winds may change, the seasons turn,
But your flame inside will always burn.
Through the storm and through the calm,
Your whispers are my healing balm.

Final Chorus:
Whispers in the wind, you are near,
In every gust, your presence clear.
Though I walk this path alone,
Your love is the breeze, guiding me home.

Outro:
As I stand beneath the sky so wide,
I know you're here, right by my side.
In the wind’s embrace, soft and kind,
Your love, forever, in the air I find.



"Whispers in the Wind" – Brecon Beacons, Powys
