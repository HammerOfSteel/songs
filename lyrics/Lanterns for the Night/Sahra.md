# Sahra

**Labels:** Lanterns for the Night
**Created:** 2025-01-04
**Edited:** 2025-01-04

---

[Intro - Soft, mystical strings and desert wind sounds]
He whispers her name, softly, like the wind,
“Tima,” he calls, “the night has secrets to send.”
Through the tent’s thin walls, the moonlight bends,
The desert awaits where the stars descend.

[Verse 1]
The sand is cool beneath their feet,
The camels rest, their breaths soft and sweet.
“بیٹی، آؤ” (Beti, come), her father says,
“There’s a story here that the moon conveys.”

[Chorus 1]
The desert speaks in whispers and rhymes,
Telling tales from forgotten times.
تیما، میری روشنی (Tima, my light), my sahra so vast,
A name like a song, a bridge to the past.

[Verse 2]
By the oasis, they crouch and hide,
Invisible hands weave blooms in the tide.
Her father murmurs, “Do you دیکھو؟ (Dekho? Do you see?)
Djinn of the sands, in the moonlight’s spree.”

[Pre-Chorus]
“They whispered your name, before you were born,
A gift in the night, from hearts that adorn.
ایلارا (Elara), they called you, the starry embrace,
For the miracle they sensed, your shining grace.”

[Chorus 2]
The desert winds carry names so true,
تیما، میری دنیا (Tima, my world), the djinn honored you.
ایلارا (Elara), they said, your soul would ignite,
A beacon of love in the endless night.

[Verse 3]
The djinn’s forms shimmer, soft and bright,
Dancing through shadows, bathed in light.
Her father kneels, offers تحائف (tuhaf, gifts) of thanks,
The desert hums with celestial ranks.

[Bridge]
“یا تیما، یا ایلارا” (Ya Tima, ya Elara), the djinn softly sing,
Their voices blend with the stars they bring.
A timeless exchange, no words to convey,
But the love in their hearts guides the way.

[Musical Solo - Delicate oud and flute melodies echoing the desert’s rhythm, evoking the dance of the djinn and the serenity of the night.]

[Verse 4]
Hand in hand, they return to their tent,
The night’s mystery lingering, softly spent.
Tima’s eyes heavy with خواب (khwab, dreams) untold,
Of djinn and stars, of courage bold.

[Chorus 3]
The desert winds carry stories so near,
تیما، میری محبت (Tima, my love), forever sincere.
ایلارا (Elara), a name the heavens bestowed,
A treasure of light in the paths we’ve trod.

[Verse 5]
He whispers, “بیٹی، آسمان کے خواب دیکھو (Beti, dream of the sky),
Of djinn who dance and stars that cry.
The desert knows, it holds you close,
My little one, my heart’s repose.”

[Outro - Soft desert wind sounds, fading strings and flute]
Her name lingers in the night’s embrace,
A story of love time cannot erase.

[End of song]
