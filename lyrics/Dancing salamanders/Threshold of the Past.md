# Threshold of the Past

**Labels:** Dancing salamanders, Risca
**Created:** 2024-08-31
**Edited:** 2024-08-31

---

Verse 1
I stand before the ancient door,
Where echoes linger, still and deep,
The weight of time upon my chest,
A wound that never sleeps.
This place, a sanctuary lost,
Where memories are steeped in dust,
I fear the shadows that await,
Yet know this step is just.

Chorus
On the threshold of the past, I stand,
A sacred line drawn in the sand.
The air is thick with whispered names,
And every breath ignites old flames.
Do I dare to cross the line,
And face the ghosts I left behind?

Verse 2
The creak of wood beneath my feet,
A sound that stirs the years gone by,
I taste the sorrow in the air,
And feel the tears I’ve yet to cry.
The walls hold secrets, softly kept,
Each one a tale of love and pain,
To step inside is to relive,
The moments lost, the endless rain.

Bridge
With trembling hands, I touch the door,
A portal to what was before,
I close my eyes, take one deep breath,
And step into the space of death.
Yet as I cross, a warmth remains,
A pulse of life within the pain,
For in this place where shadows fall,
I find the love beneath it all.

Here, where my lost love resides.
