# The cult of ur

**Labels:** Skrumt Magi
**Created:** 2024-11-13
**Edited:** 2024-11-13

---

Three friends together at dawn’s first light,
With laughter and stories, their spirits bright,
They pack the car with well-worn gear,
A lantern, a blanket, a memory near.

To Urkult’s call, where shadows sway,
A fire’s song and spirits play,
Where earth and stars in rhythm meet,
And hearts are bound with timeless beat.

In the backseat rides a little girl,
With eyes wide open, a world to unfurl,
Her first time joining this ancient path,
She watches the forest and holds back a laugh.

“Daddy, where are we going?” she asks,
He smiles and says, “To a land of masks,
A place of songs, of warmth and peace,
Where hearts find joy and fears release.”

They reach the grounds, the tent goes high,
Under the open northern sky,
A place where laughter and tales are told,
And every year, new memories hold.

To Urkult’s call, where shadows sway,
A fire’s song and spirits play,
Where earth and stars in rhythm meet,
And hearts are bound with timeless beat.

The girl runs free with children small,
Dancing ‘round as the shadows fall,
Hand in hand, they spin and leap,
In laughter’s warmth, in joy’s deep keep.

To Urkult’s light, where shadows sway,
In childlike steps and night’s array,
A circle cast, where dreams are found,
And ancient voices wrap around.

As night descends, the fire roars high,
A blazing circle beneath the sky,
The friends stand close, their memories near,
Reflecting on years that brought them here.

“I remember when we were young,” one says,
“Coming here each year to rest,
And now we bring her, she’ll keep it strong,
Carrying on as the nights grow long.”

The child sleeps softly on her dad’s chest,
The glow of the fire, a warmth in rest,
He holds her close, feeling peace,
In the quiet knowing that moments cease.

To Urkult’s bond, where shadows dwell,
With songs like stars in ancient spell,
A path well-traced, where souls are freed,
And time and heart are all we need.

They leave the forest, but the spirit remains,
In stars, in fire, in songs and refrains,
A circle of friends, a dance, a spark,
The road to Urkult, a light in the dark.
