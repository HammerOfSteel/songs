# "The Dew of Three"

**Labels:** Dancing salamanders, Three drops
**Created:** 2024-09-17
**Edited:** 2024-09-17

---

Verse 1: Underneath the full moon's light,
A magi wanders through the night.
A butterfly with wings so bright,
Guides her steps in silvered flight.
Her heart, it aches with a mother’s grief,
For a child lost, with no relief.
Yet whispers of the ancient tongue,
Lead her where old songs are sung.

Chorus: Three drops of dew, from Ceridwen's llyn,
Burn the tongue, and cleanse the sin.
In visions clear, beyond the veil,
She sees her child, where spirits sail.
Cariad fy nghalon, we will meet once more,
In the light of dawn, on a distant shore.

Verse 2: Through coedwig dark, she finds the way,
To a hidden cave where shadows play.
Ceridwen stirs the cauldron deep,
Where secrets of the Awen sleep.
She’s welcomed there, with gentle grace,
To stir the brew, in this sacred place.
As midnight peaks, with hands so sure,
She drinks the drops, both fierce and pure.

Chorus: Three drops of dew, from Ceridwen's llyn,
Burn the tongue, and cleanse the sin.
In visions clear, beyond the veil,
She sees her child, where spirits sail.
Cariad fy nghalon, we will meet once more,
In the light of dawn, on a distant shore.

Bridge: The fire within, it fiercely glows,
As wisdom ancient swiftly flows.
Her child’s voice, a song so sweet,
Reaches through the brenin's seat.
With tears of joy and heart so light,
She’s led by Ceridwen to the dawn’s first light.
Together they rise, with morning's breath,
Renewed in spirit, conquering death.

Chorus: Three drops of dew, from Ceridwen's llyn,

Outro: As the sun’s first rays kiss the land,
Ceridwen guides her by the hand.
With hope renewed and love reborn,
They step together into the dawn.
