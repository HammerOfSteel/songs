# Am I Me

**Labels:** Dancing salamanders, Gills and guts
**Created:** 2024-09-20
**Edited:** 2024-09-20

---

Place: Golitha Falls

Verse 1
By the falls, I sit alone,
Wondering where the time has gone.
The river flows, yet stays the same,
But who am I beneath my name?
I watch the water twist and turn,
Each ripple something new to learn.
Am I the current, or the stone?
Am I still me, or have I grown?

Pre-Chorus
The rushing water speaks in rhyme,
Of shifting sands and changing time.
Who was I then, and who am I now?
I search for answers in the how.

Chorus
Am I me, or just a ghost?
A fleeting thought, a distant coast?
Like the falls, I bend and break,
But in the flow, my soul awakes.
I’m the river and the rain,
The constant change, the joy, the pain.

Verse 2
The trees above, they bend and bow,
As if to ask me, “Who are you now?”
I feel the earth beneath my feet,
But my reflection’s incomplete.
Each drop of water tells a tale,
Of who I was when I set sail.
But now I’m drifting far from shore,
Unsure of who I was before.

Bridge
In every drop, a piece of me,
Lost in the river, wild and free.
I am not who I used to be,
But somewhere in the flow, I see.
That I am more than flesh and bone,
A story yet to be my own.

Final Chorus
Am I me, or something new?
A soul that’s searching for what’s true?
Like the falls, I twist and turn,
But in the flow, I start to learn.
I’m the river and the rain,
The constant change, the joy, the pain.

Outro
The water flows, it doesn’t end,
And neither does the self I tend.
In the falls, I see it clear,
I am me, though never near.
