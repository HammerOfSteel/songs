# If you see me now

**Labels:** Heartbound
**Created:** 2025-08-20
**Edited:** 2025-08-21

---

So they took the blossoms of the oak, and the blossoms of the broom, and the blossoms of the meadow-sweet, and produced from them a maiden, the fairest and most graceful that man ever saw.
In every beating pulsing strand of samsara.
Breaths my love for you my Tima Elara.

I walk roads laced in frost, my breath in the air,
Asking the silence, is anyone there?
Crow calls over hedges, reels of the past,
Will I ever see you again at last?

Every cottage window, every slate roofed street,
Feels like a hymn where our echoes meet.
I’ve prayed at doors that never opened their gate,
Still I keep walking, though heavy with weight.

The fire dies down but the embers still glow,
I shovel the silence, I carry it slow.
But I still keep lighting the candle, you know,
Just for us, in the river’s soft flow.

If you see me now,
I am here, I am here.
If you see me now,
Don’t let me disappear.
If you see me now,
Dad, open your eyes.
If you see me now,
I am here, I survive.

Papa, you’re staring through glass and stone,
But I’ve been right here, you were never alone.
Every laugh of the brook, every reed in the breeze,
That’s me, still weaving through branches and leaves.

The clouds may be heavy, the rain may fall,
But I’m in the hush, in the night bird’s call.
I’m in the silver of the frost on the lane,
I’m in the hymn of the wind and the rain.

Your questions, they echo, your heart cracks the ground,
But love, dad, it circles, it always comes round.
You don’t need proof in the ashes of night,
I’m the flame in your chest, I’m the soft little light.

Do you think of me now?

Every step that you tread.

Do you carry me still?

In the words you’ve said.

Will the road bend us closer, or is it the end?

The reel has no ending, it only bends.

Can I hold you again?

You already do.

Then why’s the silence so heavy, so true?

Silence just sings in a different key,

And how do I know that you’re near to me?

Open your eyes, Papa, you’ll see.

If you see me now,
I am here, I am here.
If you see me now,
Don’t let me disappear.
If you see me now,
Dad, open your eyes.
If you see me now,
Love never dies.

Roads are long, and grief is wide.

But love’s the tide, it will not hide.

Clouds will part, and rivers bend.
The reel keeps playing, there is no end.

I will not let her go, between me and God

Do you still see me now?

I never lost view.

The reel turns, the stars vow,
We are together, if you see me now.
