# Heighway dragon

**Labels:** Koch star
**Created:** 2024-12-04
**Edited:** 2024-12-06

---

Beneath the void, let there be light,
A path of curves, our mercury bright.
The guardian waits with veiled command,
Between the chaos and the land.

A brush in hand, the canvas bare,
Each color whispers truths I dare.
A serpent coils within my sight,
“Do you seek the void or light?”

Imagine curves plotted through night,
Imagine the dragon grinning brigth.
In its gleaming eye I see
The abyss is staring back at me

The void consumes what dares to stray,
Speaking words I dare not say.
Its eyes like stars, its voice like rain,
“Cross the line and bear this pain.”

The canvas breathes, the colors ache,
Each stroke a choice I cannot shake.
Beyond the threshold lies the key,
The mandala I place to be

Imagine stars and sattelites,
Magnolias in bare moonlight.
The serpent speaks, a voice I know,
I ken the things I cannot do.

“In the void, the amber stands,
A guardian born of broken hands.
Not an enemy, but a fickle flame,
Burning falsehoods, naming names.”

The guardian bows, the path unspools,
Each line now bends to different rules.
The brush moves free, the colors soar,
Each curve a truth, I've known before.

Curves that move, but never yeild,
Understanding once concealed.
The breaking of the dragons shell,
My voice resounds, a ringing bell.

In the void, in colors bright,
The guardian fades into the night.
The curves remain, the map unrolled,
A story shaped, a life retold.
