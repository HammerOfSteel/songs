# Colors of the Heart - song

**Labels:** childrens books - star sailor
**Created:** 2024-10-06
**Edited:** 2024-10-06

---

Verse 1:
Through the mist-covered peaks, where the colors dance free,
Star Sailor floats softly, her light like a key.
She sees in the distance two foxes so fair,
Their paws painting colors in the cool mountain air.

With each step and turn, the seasons take flight,
Pinks bloom in springtime, reds blaze through the night.
The forest glows brightly, alive in their play,
As Star Sailor watches, in quiet dismay.

Chorus:
“Let your heart be the brush, let your spirit sing true,
For art is the voice that shines deep within you.”
“Don’t worry ‘bout patterns, don’t think of the form,
Just follow your light where your feelings are born.”

“For colors aren’t perfect, and lines sometimes stray,
But what makes them beautiful is what they convey.”
“So let your heart be the brush, let your spirit sing true,
For art is the voice that comes shining through you.”

Verse 2:
Star Sailor steps closer, her light shy and small,
“Can I try?” she whispers, afraid she might fall.
The foxes nod gently, their eyes soft and kind,
“Just follow your feelings, and see what you find.”

But her light scatters wild, bright sparks with no rhyme,
It twists in confusion, no rhythm, no time.
She sighs in frustration, her heart sinking low,
“It’s nothing like yours… it doesn’t even glow.”

Bridge:
The vixen steps forward, her gaze warm and wise,
“Art is not what you see—it’s what’s felt deep inside.”
“Close your eyes, little one, and let your light flow,”
“Let your spirit take over, and your true colors show.”

Star Sailor breathes slowly, lets go of the fight,
And soft, gentle hues start to shimmer and light.
Pale blue for hope, and soft pink for love,
And gold for the joy that comes down from above.

Outro:
As dawn softly rises, the mountain aglow,
Star Sailor waves gently, her heart full and whole.
“Thank you,” she whispers, “for showing me how,”
“To let my light wander, to paint without bounds.”

The foxes nod slowly, their fur bright with hues,
“Remember, dear star, that art is your truth.”
“Just follow your spirit, let colors unfold,”
“And life will be painted in stories untold.”

And so Star Sailor sails on, through mist and through day,
Her light dancing softly, like bright shades of May.
The mountain foxes linger, in colors so grand,
Their spirits entwined, like brushstrokes on land.
