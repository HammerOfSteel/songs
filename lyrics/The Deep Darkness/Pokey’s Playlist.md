# Pokey’s Playlist

**Labels:** The Deep Darkness
**Created:** 2025-04-18
**Edited:** 2025-04-18

---

“Welcome to Pokey’s Playlist.”
“Track one: petty. Track two: permanent damage.”
“Let’s go.”

They said I was weak — look at me now,
Spider mech dreams in a static crown.
Downloaded rage, booted up pain,
Glitched through timelines, rewrote the game.

Y’all had moms who packed lunch and love,
I had silence and a push from above.
Giygas said, “Be the weapon, not the wound.”
So I learned how to smile in a locked room.

Ness? Please. He swings like a kid.
I control galaxies with a casual fib.
You leveled up love — I upgraded war.
You knocked, I shattered the door.

King of the glitch, Lord of the loop,
Broke the code, now I rule the soup.
Static crown, blue light veins,
I’m the end you didn’t plan to name.
Pokey’s Playlist: full of scars,
No one clapped, so I built the stars.

Giygas never hugged me, but he called me son,
Said, “Don’t need friends when you ruin everyone.”
I got cronies now — kinda. Not real.
They follow cause I pay, not how I feel.

I built an empire on ignored tears,
Coded loyalty, hacked my fears.
You think I like this throne of chrome?
Nah. But power’s louder than being alone.

I watched you all grow — happy and soft,
While I read fate from a terminal cough.
I wasn’t the hero, wasn’t the joke.
I’m the error you whisper before you choke.

King of the glitch, Lord of the loop,
Broke the code, now I rule the soup.
Static crown, blue light veins,
I’m the end you didn’t plan to name.
Pokey’s Playlist: fear on blast,
Skip to track twelve — I outlast the past.

Remember my room? That echo box?
Mom yelled at the door like it made me tough.
Ness got sleepovers and cookies and sun.
I got cold air and “You’re no one.”

I wanted the bike. I wanted the cape.
I wanted one person who didn’t escape.
So I built my fortress from backup saves,
Wrapped in jokes and ignored graves.

Yeah, I’m the villain. Yeah, I bite.
Yeah, I broke the stars just to feel right.
Final boss, final breath,
Still a kid…
Just scared of death.

“Pokey’s Playlist...
maybe it wasn’t enough.”
