# Always My Love, Always

**Labels:** The Nature of Things
**Created:** 2024-11-17
**Edited:** 2024-11-17

---

[Intro]
The wind speaks low through the rustling trees,
Scarlet leaves drift on the autumn breeze.
The mountain stands in its quiet grace,
I start to climb, your voice takes its place.


"Little sun, it’s been years, yet here I stand,
Seeking your light in this foreign land."

"Papa, I’m here, just follow the air,
Each step you take, I’ll meet you there."


[Verse 1]
The path is steep, the stones feel cold,
Each breath I take feels heavy, old.
I hear your laugh in the whispering leaves,
A memory clings, a heart that grieves.

[Chorus 1]
Always, my love, always,
Your light will guide me through these days.
Though time pulls far, though shadows fall,
Your love still echoes through it all.


[Verse 2]
The autumn gold paints the forest floor,
A fragile warmth I can’t ignore.
I stop to rest by an ancient tree,
Its roots run deep, like you in me.

"Do you remember those summer skies?"

"I see them now, through your weary eyes."


[Verse 3]
The air grows thin as the climb ascends,
Each turn a memory that never ends.
Your hand I feel in the rushing wind,
A tethered bond that won’t rescind.

[Chorus 2] (evolves)
Always, my love, always,
Through every season, in every phase.
Though mountains rise, though time moves fast,
Our love remains, it will outlast.


[Bridge]
The summit nears, the sky turns wide,
The sea below reflects the tide.
Your voice grows soft, a distant song,
Yet in my heart, you still belong.


"Papa, you’ve climbed so far, so high,
But I’m with you, in every sky."


[Instrumental Solo | Strings and Piano]


[Verse 4]
The summit greets me with open arms,
A peaceful vastness, its quiet charms.
The islands stretch where the sea meets sky,
I feel you here, though you’re not nearby.

"Tima, I see you in the waves,
In the light that dances and gently saves."

[Verse 5]
The wind wraps close, your laughter fades,
But your love lingers in golden shades.
I whisper low, as the world turns still,
"You’re with me always, and always will."

[Chorus 3] (final chorus)
Always, my love, always,
Through life’s hard climb and autumn’s haze.
Though time may pull and memories fade,
Your light remains, it won’t evade.


[Verse 6]
I start the path back down the way,
The leaves drift soft where shadows lay.
But lighter now, my steps descend,
Your love a compass, my guiding friend.

"Papa, you’ll find me in the breeze so,
In the stars, the trees, wherever you go."


[Outro]
The sea hums low, the mountain sighs,
The autumn fades, but never goodbyes.
Always, my love, always near,
Through every step, I’ll feel you here.


"Always, my love, always."
