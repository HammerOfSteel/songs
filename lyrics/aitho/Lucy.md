# Lucy

**Labels:** aitho
**Created:** 2025-12-30
**Edited:** 2025-12-30

---

The fluorescent is humming a low-frequency hymn,
In a circle of cushions where the lights are going dim.
I’m looking at the stickers on the sweaters and the fleece,
A collection looking for a piece of peace.

"Hello my name is... Lucy,"
I read on that little sign,
Scribbled four letters on a neatly drawn line.

She’s got a look in her eye like a storm in a jar,
A jagged little light like a neutron star.

"Tell me... what are you doing here?"

She says
"Same as you lot,"
with a sneer.

Om Mani Padme Hum, 
Om Mani Padme Hum,
Swim, Star, swim,
swim and become.

Perfect, precise, and polite,
Aye, this is going to be a long night.

Trust, trust, and let it show,
Observe, see, let it go.

Regulus,
right,
enter the room.
A cold spark,
in the heat will it bloom.

He stands up with a rattle and a bowl,
Strikes it on the edge and lets it toll.

"Welcome travelers,
welcome one and all,
Ladies,
gentlemen,
all you tall,
all you small.

Welcome you who came out of fear,
Welcome you who came to make it clear.

Welcome to this circle round,
May it bring you...
what you’ll find."

A thunderbolt waiting for the first little sip.
The Vajra holds you snug in its little grip.

I look over at Lucy,
she’s staring at the sky, 
can already tell that,
this lot are pretty high.

Time ticks forward,
a second at a time.
Until I too lose my grip on the line.

Now the walls are breathing and the "aíthō" is high,
And Lucy’s looking up like she’s seeing the sky.
That sneer is gone,
replaced by a jagged kind of grace,
The Star Regulus glowing right there on her face.

It’s not a soft light,
it’s a cold, but bright,
A bit like a diamond...
yeah, that’s what it’s like...

The strangers are dissolving,
the "I" is getting thin,
Just the sound of the rattle underneath the skin.
The "Star" is the sign that the smelting is done,
In the middle of the circle,
all, all one.

Swim, Star, swim...
(Hello, my name is Lucy)

Observe, see, let it go... 
(Aye, it’s a long night)

Becoming, Become the dark...
