Time is not a straight line, nor a road, it does not fold, 
In equal integers or patterns, no, it holds.
Well, or so I am told.
as it beckons and calls,
forming small cracks upon the walls.

Manifold operates outside of rhyme or law,
cannot unsee, what we saw.

Close the godamn book, you say,
the lightning finds a path and comes turning for our way.

Strikes bold, strikes true.
Eating through the manifold,
just to get a glimpse of you.

It’s not a straight line,
it's not a line at all.
not a shape to be drawn upon the wall.

You look back, so far back,
you have absolute zero, no clue,
Just the utter nonsense you spew.
Standing in your giant shadow,
am I one of the few?

One of the few who knew...
who really knew you?

I like you,
I love you,
and all that...
staring at your eyes,
Cold, blue, motionless,
I confess,
I cannot guess.
what they express.

On this side of the chair am I sat,
waiting for nothing, anything at all,
another tiny small, barly a sigh
While the minutes and the seconds,
enlessly roll by.

Regrets?
Do you have any regrets?
Your closed lips,
lets out a sigh,
Could you just try..

"It’s your fault,"
it escapes your tongue,
it strips me to the bone
It holds me stung,
shivering.
Just...
leave me alone...

And what do you know?
My heart, it finally agrees,
It sees your restless argument and,
it falls upon its knees...

She's not coming back, she's not"
She's not coming back, she's not" 

It relentless stole the doubt,
it stole any reason small,
Does it really even matter,
after all?

After all,
She’s not coming back, 
she’s not,
the sentence strikes the spot.

storm out, anger, hate, self-doubt,
this time she did'nt even shout.

Full of something even I... 
even I do not know, 
As the colors start to fade, 
as they fade...
the rainbow.
