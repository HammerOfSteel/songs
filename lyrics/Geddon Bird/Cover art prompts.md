# Cover art prompts

**Labels:** Geddon Bird
**Created:** 2024-10-25
**Edited:** 2024-10-25

---

1. Mindful Meddling
Prompt:
An oil painting of a whimsical coastal town at twilight, lanterns flickering along narrow streets with waves crashing softly in the background. A feather floats mid-air near a winding path, with keys scattered across the cobblestones. In the distance, two figures stroll, trailing light as they move. The scene evokes synchronicity, playful disruptions, and hidden meaning beneath the surface.

2. Hawthorne
Prompt:
An oil painting of ancient stone ruins surrounded by wild hawthorn trees, some in bloom with white flowers, others bristling with thorns. A narrow path winds through the ruins under a stormy sky breaking into light. A breeze stirs petals and leaves, hinting at both love and loss. The mood is melancholic, with symbols of transformation and endurance in the landscape.

3. Two Birds
Prompt:
An oil painting of two birds in flight over Snowdonia’s misty mountains at sunrise. One bird soars toward the horizon bathed in golden light, while the other glides closer to a winding river below, surrounded by shadow. In the distance, the landscape shifts between sunlight and twilight, representing divergent yet connected paths. The scene captures both freedom and memory.

4. Living on the Moon
Prompt:
An oil painting of a solitary figure standing on the edge of a cliff overlooking the ocean under a vast night sky. A silver moon hangs low, its light shimmering across the waves like scattered stars. Letters drift in the air, some crumpled, some glowing faintly, symbolizing longing and connection. The scene feels ethereal, with subtle tension between isolation and hope.

5. Bare with Me
Prompt:
An oil painting of a winter landscape in Plymouth, with bare trees standing along quiet streets. A small café glows warmly in the distance, contrasting the cold outside. A figure sits inside by the window, looking out at the gray sky. Hands almost touch through the glass, separated by space and emotion. The mood is reflective, balancing grief, connection, and healing.

6. The Work
Prompt:
An oil painting of an abstract landscape blending dream and reality. A ladder stretches into a misty abyss, while a mirror reflects not what’s before it, but distant hills and an endless sky. In the foreground, a winding path leads toward the cliffs of Cornwall, symbolizing initiation and inner work. Subtle figures move in the background, hinting at unseen guides.

7. Kindling Kin
Prompt:
An oil painting of a garden in soft afternoon light, marigolds and wildflowers blooming under a sky shifting between sun and rain. A mother and child sit together, their hands in the soil, planting flowers. The child’s expression is joyful, while the mother’s gaze drifts toward the horizon, her smile tinged with sadness. The scene is tender yet bittersweet, capturing love, loss, and memory.

8. Familiar Strangers
Prompt:
An oil painting of two figures crossing paths in a university hallway, their faces turned away but their shadows overlapping. Books lie scattered on the ground, and in the background, rain streaks across a tall window. The light is muted, with subtle tension in the air. The scene suggests hidden burdens and the quiet complexities of human connection.

9. The Weight of Words
Prompt:
An oil painting of an old desk scattered with open books and unwritten letters, illuminated by the dim light of a nearby window. Reflections flicker on the glass, showing faint, overlapping silhouettes of two figures deep in conversation. In the background, words seem to float in the air like whispers, blending with the soft breeze from outside. The scene reflects thought, language, and emotional connection.

10. Echoes in the Garden
Prompt:
An oil painting of a moonlit garden within Saltram Park, where silver leaves and soft petals catch the night’s glow. Faint echoes swirl through the air like drifting mist, suggesting whispered secrets from long ago. Two figures walk along the path, their forms softened by moonlight. The scene captures nature's quiet wisdom and the interconnectedness of life.

11. The Long Way Home
Prompt:
An oil painting of quiet streets in Plymouth at night, with streetlights casting golden pools of light on wet cobblestones. A distant figure walks under an umbrella, following a familiar path toward a glowing window in the distance. The fog wraps gently around the scene, blurring edges and evoking the search for belonging and the redefinition of home.

12. Circles in the Sand
Prompt:
An oil painting of a secluded beach, where a lone figure draws circles in the sand, each line washed away by the ebbing tide. The sun sets low on the horizon, casting long shadows over the beach. The patterns left behind hint at repeating cycles, both fleeting and eternal. The scene reflects the search for meaning and the patterns that shape life.

13. Mirror of the Mind
Prompt:
An oil painting of a dreamscape where doorways open into each other endlessly, and mirrors reflect fragments of unseen places. A lone figure stands between two mirrors, caught in an infinite reflection. The scene feels surreal, with soft shadows shifting unpredictably. The painting suggests self-exploration and the confrontation with hidden aspects of the self.

14. Between the Veil
Prompt:
An oil painting of a vast, starry sky merging with a river of mist that flows through a forest at night. Figures drift within the mist, their outlines blurred and dreamlike. In the distance, a thin veil of light separates the earthly forest from something otherworldly. The scene evokes mythology, dreams, and spiritual exploration.

15. Paper Planes and Ashes
Prompt:
An oil painting of two figures sitting on the edge of a quiet park, folding paper planes that fly across a sunset sky. Some planes soar high, while others fall into a small fire burning nearby. Ashes drift softly through the air, mingling with the fading light. The scene suggests nostalgia, release, and transformation.

16. Lighthouse in the Fog
Prompt:
An oil painting of a lighthouse emerging from thick coastal fog, its beam cutting softly through the mist. A figure walks along the shoreline, drawn toward the light in the distance. The ocean waves lap gently at the rocks, reflecting the glow from the lighthouse. The scene conveys a sense of searching, guidance, and emotional navigation.

17. The Quiet of Morning
Prompt:
An oil painting of Plymouth at dawn, with the first light touching rooftops and birds beginning to stir. A figure sits by an open window, a cup of tea in hand, gazing out at the peaceful morning. The streets are still empty, bathed in soft, golden light. The scene captures the quiet acceptance of a new beginning, filled with subtle hope.
