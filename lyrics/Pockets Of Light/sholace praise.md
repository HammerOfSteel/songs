# sholace praise

**Labels:** Pockets Of Light
**Created:** 2025-08-30
**Edited:** 2025-08-30

---

you took my hat off and wore it

it looked better on you

you took my shoelaces and
tied them around your wrists like bracelets

i wanted to hold your hands

 Morning light spills through the window pane,
 we sit on the floor where the shoes still remain.
 Little hands fumble, steady the lace,
 your laughter comes easy, it softens this place.
 Knots in the morning, prayers in guise,
 bows like small halos that shine in your eyes.

 Shoelace praise, simple and true,
 every knot a hymn I tie for you.
 Shoelace praise, gentle and small,
 holding our steps all through it all.

 loops of lace like rabbit’s ears,
 stains of salt and sifting tears.
 laugh of knots comes loose again,
 I show you the trick, round once, then ten.
 up and at it, out the door, 
 aye, done this dance many time before.

reckon there's some magic in those knots, bach.

oooooh,
sing sing, my magic string!

 Shoelace praise, steady and near,
 loops of devotion that hold us here.
 Shoelace praise, humble and kind,
 binding our hearts as we walk in time.

 All through the day those laces stay,
 through puddles and pavements, hopscotch play.
 Tiny ritual thread us close,
 the smallest things are what I love most.
 Shoelaces fray, the fabric wears thin,
 but the bow is a promise of where we’ve been.

 Shoelace praise, worn but strong,
 a tether that hums like a quiet song.
 Shoelace praise, dusk to dawn,
 a knot in the world that keeps us on.

 Evening comes, the day lets go,
 I kneel once more, and I loosen slow.
 The laces fall open, the bows release,
 knots undone into quiet peace.
 Sweet little yawn,  you carry it on.

With my shoelaces untied
I'm always falling short
Short of you
Short of yesterday
Short of tomorrow 

Papa… tomorrow, I’ll tie them myself.

 Aye, cariad… that's my lass.
