# Radnor the red

**Labels:** En Sol
**Created:** 2025-01-27
**Edited:** 2025-01-27

---

In the dim glow of a pub corner, the old storyteller leaned forward.
"They say, under Radnor Forest, the last dragon sleeps.
And on Samhain night, when the veil is thin, it’s said the brave can find their way to the heart of the forest.
But be warned: the dragon guards the bridge to the stars, and it tests those who seek passage. Only the devoted may walk where the starlight bends."
I whispered back, "What happens if I make it?"
He smiled, "Then you’ll find the one you lost."

Under the autumn moon, the forest calls my name,
Leaves crunch beneath my feet, but the shadows don’t feel the same.
A dragon slumbers deep, beneath the earth’s embrace,
Tonight, I’ll face the legend, to see your shining face.

The wind carries whispers, like your laughter on the breeze,
I walk where no man dares, past ancient, shivering trees.
The stories told in pubs don’t hold the weight of fear,
When you face the sleeping dragon, your purpose must be clear.

The churches stand like sentinels, their stones are cold and gray,
But they hold the dragon’s barrier, a spell to keep it at bay.
I step into the circle; the air is sharp with frost,
And I hear its breath below, like a rhythm from the lost.

Through Radnor’s dark, where the dragon lies,
I seek the bridge beneath starry skies.
Let me see the one I hold so dear,
On Samhain’s night, bring her near.

Its voice booms low, shaking the ground,
"Why have you come to where shadows abound?"
"For my daughter," I say, my voice a raw plea,
"I’ve come to the forest; she’s waiting for me."

The dragon’s eyes glimmer, like stars split apart,
"You carry sorrow heavy; does love still fill your heart?"
"It burns like a fire," I answer through tears,
"Her light guides my soul, even through these years."

The dragon exhales, its breath a luminous haze,
"Step forward, seeker; your devotion sets ablaze.
But the bridge you cross demands more than might,
You’ll need to release her to embrace her light."

Through Radnor’s dark, where the dragon lies,
I cross the bridge beneath moonlit skies.
Her voice calls softly, I feel her near,
Through the veil of stars, she appears.

And there she stands, in silver glow,
Her eyes like the stars, the love I know.
"Papa," she whispers, her voice like a stream,
"You don’t have to carry me, I live in your dreams."
"But I miss you," I say, my knees hit the ground,
She smiles, "I’m the stars, I’m the night all around."

The dragon speaks, its voice a steady hum,
"You found what you sought; now let it become.
Her light is eternal; it’s etched in your soul,
The bond is unbroken, even when you’re not whole."

Through Radnor’s dark, where the dragon lies,
I found her light beneath endless skies.
Her love is the fire that keeps me strong,
In the stars, she lives; in my heart, her song.

The forest fades, the dawn is near,
The dragon slumbers, but she’s always here.
In every breath, in every star,
She’s my guide, my hope, my eternal spark.
