# Autumn’s Lanterns

**Labels:** The Nature of Things
**Created:** 2024-11-19
**Edited:** 2024-11-19

---

[Intro]
The leaves hang low, the air feels clear,
The whisper of autumn draws us near.
Lanterns of gold light up the trees,
A patchwork sky through the autumn breeze.


"Come now, love, the path awaits,
Let’s find where autumn decorates."

"Look, Papa, the leaves are stars,
Falling down from where they are!"

[Verse 1]
Through Twmbarlwm’s trails, the colors gleam,
A canopy glowing, a golden dream.
She skips ahead, her arms spread wide,
Collecting leaves from the woodland side.


"What will you do with the leaves you take?"

"I’ll make a crown for the squirrel to wear,
And give him acorns for his lair!"


[Chorus 1]
Autumn’s lanterns, shining bright,
Guiding us through golden light.
The world turns soft, the season sings,
A magic found in simple things.


[Verse 2]
By the Sirhowy, the river flows,
Reflecting the trees where the lanterns glow.
She kneels to watch the ripples play,
A mirrored dance in the autumn’s sway.


"Papa, do fish see the sky so high?"

"They see their world in the water’s eye,
A piece of heaven where they lie."


[Verse 3]
The path winds further through Cwmcarn’s shade,
Where ferns and moss in green cascade.
A fox’s track in the mud appears,
She gasps in wonder, her voice sincere.


"Is he hiding, Papa, watching us here?"

"He might be near, in the trees unseen,
A quiet guardian of this autumn scene."


[Chorus 2]
Autumn’s lanterns, warm and kind,
Lighting the way for hearts to find.
Through winding trails and woodland strings,
We gather joy from simple things.


[Bridge]
As the sun dips low, the air grows chill,
The town ahead feels soft and still.
She tugs my hand with a playful cheer,
"Papa, chippies! Let’s go from here!"

"Chippies it is, my little sun,
We’ll end our day with laughter spun."


[Verse 4]
At the chippy shop, her eyes grow wide,
The smell of salt and sea collide.
She holds her cone like a precious prize,
Her laughter rising to the skies.

"Do squirrels like chips, Papa, do you think?"

"Maybe they’d try, here have a drink!"


[Verse 5]
The stars come out as we head back home,
Her tiny steps where lanterns roam.
She holds her crown of leaves so tight,
Her little world feels big tonight.


"Papa, will the lanterns wait for me?"

"They’ll glow each autumn, in every tree."


[Chorus 3]
Autumn’s lanterns, soft and true,
Guiding our hearts in all we do.
Through golden woods and starry swings,
We find our love in simple things.


[Verse 6]
The fire burns low, the house feels warm,
Her eyes grow heavy, safe from harm.
The leaves she gathered rest on the floor,
A memory kept forevermore.


"Goodnight, my love, my little star,
No matter the season, I’m never far."

[Outro]
The lanterns fade as the night turns still,
The golden woods, the autumn’s thrill.
Her dreams take flight, her heart serene,
In the glow of lanterns, soft and green.

[Whisper]
"Autumn’s lanterns, my guiding light,
Forever shining, soft and bright."
