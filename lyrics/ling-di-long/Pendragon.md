# Pendragon

**Labels:** ling-di-long
**Created:** 2025-04-15
**Edited:** 2025-04-15

---

🗣️ [Spoken Intro – At the Wake]
Teema asked,
[Young girl, British accent] “Was she really a dragon?”

I said,
[Man, British accent] “Only when the winds were wrong.”

Peter poured tea like it weighed a spell,
Mam struck the match like she knew it well.

[Woman, Welsh accent, soft] “Dim galar,”
[Woman, British accent] “No grief tonight.”
Just jumpers, mist, and quiet light.

It wasn’t a wake — more like a rhyme,
A fire, a name, a fold in time.

🎤 [Verse 1 – The Hearth, Rhymed]
Four mugs steaming in golden hush,
A book left open, its spine still plush.

Her marker a leaf — crisp, curled, and thin,
Held in place like breath held in.

Teema drew dragons, scales like steam,
Said,
[Young girl, British accent] “She must’ve had wings like a kettle dream.”

Peter smiled — no punchline near,
Mam whispered,
[Woman, British accent] “She's with us, love. Just not too clear.”

I looked where frost met fir and tree,
And felt her breath return to me.

🎶 [Chorus 1 – Pendragon’s Name]
Though I am small, I carry a song,
Pendragon’s name where dreams belong.
I have been a book in the origin,
A fire that hums beneath the skin.

🎤 [Verse 2 – The Telling, Rhymed]
Peter recalled how she grew things wrong —
Sunflowers in snow, “Just because — it’s song.”

Mam said,
[Woman, British accent] “She kept her truths like feathers kept —
Close to chest, and never wept.”

Teema asked,
[Young girl] “Did she cry a lot?”

I said, “Only for stars and songs she forgot.”

She told me once, when I was small,
That dragons don’t break — they rise, they crawl
Through grief like ash until they gleam —
And light the road we thought was dream.

🎶 [Chorus 2 – Echoed and Lifted]
Though I am weak, I still remain,
Pendragon’s breath in bone and name.
She left her mark in wind and flame,
A book still open in the rain.

🎤 [Verse 3 – The Glimpse Beyond, Rhymed]
Teema curled up near the hearth’s warm lip,
Her drawing clutched in a sleepy grip.

Her dragon had wings like thorn and star,
Mam sang a hum from a place afar.

I blinked as the candle danced then stilled,
The air grew deep, the silence filled.

A shadow moved where the fog sits low —
And I knew, without knowing, she’d come to show
Not young, not old, not ghost, not flame —
But something more: a spoken name.

🌿 [Bridge – Voice of Taliesin]
I have been a word among the brave,
A book before it ever gave.

A tear in the air, a silent hymn,
A breath that burns beyond the brim.

I have been the dullest of stars,
And still I rise from broken jars.

🎶 [Final Chorus – All Voices Together]
Though I am small, I still remain,
Pendragon’s light in smoke and rain.
Though she has gone, her spell is spun —
In every hush, in every sun.

🗣️ [Spoken Outro – Closing the Door]
Peter raised his cup and said,
[Man, British accent] “She never liked endings.”

I whispered,
[Man, softer] “Then we’ll tell it like a beginning.”
