# patchwork gnosis

**Labels:** sundry
**Created:** 2026-01-07
**Edited:** 2026-01-07

---

I looked for a pillar, I looked for a spine,
To hold up this "Sundry" existence of mine.

I wanted a blueprint, a masterly plan,
To prove I was more than a flickering man.

But the stitches are showing, the edges are frayed,
In this curious garment that life has displayed.

A scrap of a sorrow, a ribbon of grace,
All sewn in a tangle across this place.

And strangely, the "Gnosis" is finding the thread,
That keeps all these pieces from filling with dread.

aye, patchwork of spirit, sundering mess,
when to say no,
when to say yes.

To the bits and the bobs and the fragments of soul,
That don’t need a logic to make themselves whole.

surrender
"Why"
give
"How,"
rudimentary tao.

various and wide, 
pieces of pisces in the tide.

beaten, broken,
all new, 
glued back together,
sundry and askew.

fortune,
drawn from the stack,
no way home,
no way back.
faith, faith,
figure of eigth.
take a leap,
sundry all the secrets we keep.

pisky, pine,
shimmering state,
all left hungering on the plate.

entrence fee to,
a cathedral of scraps,
all these figures running laps,
around the altar.

don't need a signal,
don't need a screen,
a need to be shown,
to be seen.

hi, gnosis blistering blessed
among all the fragments,
correctly guessed.

To those who's written fate,
already broken the stone,
in the tappestry sewn,
grown in the bone.

surrender
"Why"
give
"How,"
rudimentary tao.

various and wide, 
pieces of pisces in the tide.

Gnosis is no knowing...
slow seed in chaos growing.
not seen,
not heard,
not even known.
in you alone,
shall it be sown.

patchwork of pisces...
luminous soul...
