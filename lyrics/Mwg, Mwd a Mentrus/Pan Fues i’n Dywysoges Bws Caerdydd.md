# Pan Fues i’n Dywysoges Bws Caerdydd

**Labels:** Mwg, Mwd a Mentrus
**Created:** 2025-05-05
**Edited:** 2025-05-05

---

Nôl pan o’n i’n dywysoges bws Caerdydd...
Dim coron, dim ceffyl — ond tebyg iawn, mun.

Rîp yn y sanau, gliter ar y llaw,
Tacsi’n rhy ddrud, felly bws yw’r brâu.
Rhif 8 o’r pentre i’r castell mawr,
Teimlo fel brenhines, ond yn trio peidio â sbarduno sgwâr.
“Ti pwy?” medd Mam, “ti’n hanner cartŵn!”
Ond fi’n gwasgu coron i’m pen gyda phinnau bŵn.

Fi’n neud fy orymdaith o’r pentre i’r plaza,
Croen papur wal, calon fel NASA.
Dim angen ceffyl pan mae bws yn dyfod,
Fi yw’r Dywysoges, ti’n gwybod?

Crown o ffyrc sglodion, tŷ yn y twll,
Gwallt mewn ddau bobl, hoodie yn dynn fel hull.
Y castell? Ikea lamp a throw cotwm,
Ond pan dwi’n eistedd y tu ôl, fi’n teimlo fel lot fyd enwogion.
Dynion yn chwerthin, fi’n rhoi iddyn nhw smyg,
Trwy’r ffenest: “Ti’n gwybod, mun — fi’n edrych fel hug!”

Fi yw’r frenhines o’r safle bws pen draw,
Dwi’n codi fy llygaid, nid dim byd yn islaw.
Pob sbwriel, pob chwerthin, pob tag ar wal,
Yn rhan o’m coron, fi’n eu caru nhw i gyd.

Symudais i ffwrdd ond y bws dal yn mynd,
Goleuadau yn fflachio, fel atgofau’n llyn.
Dydy’r gliter ddim yno, ond ysbryd dal fan hyn,
Yn y sedd rownd y cefn lle dechreuodd y syn.
Dwi’n dal i fod hi, dim mwy o show,
Ond fi’n dal i fynd heibio’r safle bws, yn araf, isel, slow.

Fi oedd y Dywysoges pan oedd pawb yn chwerthin,
Wedi addurno’r bws fel llys mewn carthen.
Does dim byd yn fach pan ti’n teimlo’n fawr,
Fi’n dal i fod hi — ond jest heb y fawrder swnllyd.

🌍 English Translation

[Spoken Intro]
Back when I was the Cardiff bus princess...
No crown, no horse — but pretty close, yeah.

[Verse 1]
Rip in my tights, glitter on my hand,
Taxi’s too dear, so the bus is grand.
Number 8 from the estate to the castle gates,
Felt like a queen — trying not to get rinsed at the crates.
“Who are you?” said Mam, “you’re half a cartoon!”
But I pinned that crown to my head with five bobbles, soon.

[Chorus 1]
I parade from the estate to the plaza,
Wallpaper skin, but a heart like NASA.
No need for a horse, the bus shows up,
I’m the Princess — look it up.

[Verse 2]
Crown made of chip forks, house in a hole,
Hair in two bobbles, hoodie tight as a scroll.
The castle? Ikea lamp and a cotton throw,
But sat at the back, I felt like I was on the show.
Lads laughing, I give ’em a wink,
Out the window: “You know, love — I look good in pink!”

[Chorus 2]
I’m the queen of the bus stop’s end,
Eyes up high, never need to pretend.
Every bin, every laugh, every tag on the wall,
Part of my crown — I loved it all.

[Instrumental Break – Casio solo + “Is this one coming?” sample]

[Verse 3]
I moved away but that bus still runs,
Lights flashing like old reruns.
The glitter’s gone but the soul is here,
On the seat at the back where it all felt clear.
Still her, just not so loud,
But I pass that stop, heart slow and proud.

[Chorus 3]
I was the Princess when they all laughed,
Turned the bus into a palace, wrapped in a scarf.
Nothing’s small when you feel it big,
I’m still her — but now I whisper it.
