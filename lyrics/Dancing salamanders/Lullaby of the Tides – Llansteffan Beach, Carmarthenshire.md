# Lullaby of the Tides – Llansteffan Beach, Carmarthenshire

**Labels:** Dancing salamanders, Ar Lan y Mor
**Created:** 2024-09-19
**Edited:** 2024-09-19

---

Verse 1:
On the shores where the castle stands,
I walk alone, my feet in the sands.
The tides roll in, then slip away,
But my love for you will always stay.

The wind hums soft, the sea sings low,
A melody only we could know.
In every wave, I hear your name,
Whispered back, like an ancient flame.

Chorus:
Sleep now, my love, where the tides embrace,
In the arms of the sea, in a timeless place.
Though the waves may rise, and the moon may fall,
You’re here with me, through it all.

Verse 2:
The castle’s shadow on the shore,
Reminds me of the days before.
When laughter echoed in the breeze,
And we chased the light beneath the trees.

Now, though the world feels far apart,
I carry you inside my heart.
The tides may shift, the seasons turn,
But for your touch, I still yearn.

Chorus:
Sleep now, my love, where the tides embrace,
In the arms of the sea, in a timeless place.
Though the waves may rise, and the moon may fall,
You’re here with me, through it all.

Bridge:
In the lull of the ocean’s sway,
I feel your spirit guide my way.
The tides speak of love’s eternal flow,
A bond that time will never throw.

Chorus:
Sleep now, my love, where the tides embrace,
In the arms of the sea, in a timeless place.
Though the waves may rise, and the moon may fall,
You’re here with me, through it all.

Outro:
As the moonlight fades and the stars grow dim,
I’ll always find you within the hymn,
Of the ocean’s song, of the tide’s soft call,
Forever with me, through it all.
