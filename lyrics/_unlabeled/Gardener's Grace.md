# Gardener's Grace

**Created:** 2024-08-31
**Edited:** 2024-08-31

---

Verse 1
In the morning light, we walked the rows,
Where every bloom and leaf bestows,
A quiet peace, a whispered grace,
In the garden, our sacred place.
Your tiny hands in soil deep,
Planting dreams for us to keep,
Each flower, a memory, tender and true,
A bond we nurtured, just me and you.

Chorus
In the garden’s gentle embrace,
We sowed our love with tender grace.
Every seed, a hope, a dream,
In the sunlight’s golden beam.
Through every season, every year,
Our garden grew with love sincere.

Verse 2
The roses red, the daisies white,
Each petal soft, a pure delight,
We spoke in whispers, soft and low,
Of stories only we would know.
The butterflies would dance above,
In this haven filled with love,
Each plant a promise we would tend,
A love that knows no end.

Bridge
And when the winds of autumn blew,
We gathered leaves, a golden hue,
With every fallen leaf, we’d say,
We’ll plant new dreams another day.
In the garden’s quiet peace,
We found a love that would not cease,
A gardener’s grace, a sacred bond,
In every bloom, our love lived on.

Outro
Now, in this garden, I still stand,
With every flower, I touch your hand,
Though you’re gone, your spirit stays,
In the garden, our love’s bouquet.
A gardener’s grace, forevermore,
In every bloom, our hearts explore.
