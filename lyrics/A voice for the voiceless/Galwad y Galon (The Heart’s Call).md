# Galwad y Galon (The Heart’s Call)

**Labels:** A voice for the voiceless
**Created:** 2025-03-05
**Edited:** 2025-03-05

---

A song about the balance of joy and sorrow, and the wisdom in listening to one’s heart.

"There is a song that only your heart can hear. It hums beneath the weight of heavy days, it whispers when the world feels quiet. Some say joy and sorrow are opposites, but they are not. They walk together, hand in hand, like rain and sunshine painting the same sky. If you listen closely… you’ll hear it too—the call of the heart."

Soft as a breath, strong as the sea,
A voice inside keeps calling me.
Through light and dark, through rise and fall,
The heart still sings, it sings for all.

Oh, the rain will fall, but the sun will rise,
Tears and laughter share the skies.
So listen close, and hear the sound,
The heart still beats, so strong, so loud.

Fel y gwynt drwy’r coed yn drwm,
Fel tonnau’n dawnsio yn y grym.
Ni waeth pa nos, ni waeth pa ddŵr,
Mae calon wan yn dal i ganu’n glir.

Oh, the rain will fall, but the sun will rise,
Tears and laughter share the skies.
So trust the wind, so trust the tide,
The heart still sings, it won’t subside.

A tear may fall where flowers grow,
A shadow moves where bright lights glow.
But even in the quiet deep,
A heart still calls, it never sleeps.

Listen now, the hush, the drum,
The beat of where you came from.
Not just sorrow, not just light,
Both together make it right.

Clyw y galon, clyw ei sain,
Yn y distawrwydd mae’n parhau.
Ni chewch golled heb ei heulwen,
Na serch heb un gollwng deigryn.

Hiraeth, llawenydd, dwylo’n un,
Fel y glaw ar wawr y dydd.
Peidiwch ofni, peidiwch mynd,
Mae’r galon dal i ganu’n fyw.

"So when the night feels long, when the weight is heavy—listen. The heart still sings, through storm and sun, through loss and love. It sings because you are here. And you are never alone."
