🌑 Week 1: The Disconnection
“Silver Cord” – The moment of separation from the body.

“Weightless” – A strange liberation: free of pain, but confused.

“Taciturnity” – The soul’s cries go unheard; denial.

“AWOL” – Seeing loved ones grieve, watching the funeral.

“Real-time trench” – The soul walks through a misty mirror of the world.

“Lodestar” – First glimpses of guides and presences.

“Sung songs” – The power of prayers from the living.

🌒 Week 2: The Inner Reckoning
“Astral axiom” – Life review begins.

“Egg” – Feeling every pain and joy caused.

“Observe” – Seeing patterns, pride, guilt.

“Compassion” – Small kindnesses remembered in full.

“Duality” – Self-accusation or acceptance.

“Facilitators of Forgiveness” – Spiritual beings help with compassion.

“Lethe” – The decision to release the past.

🌓 Week 3: The Threshold
“Y Draig” – The invisible vibrational border.

“Labyrinth of Self” – Fears, guilt, addictions take form.

“Fragments” – The valley of shadows, made of the soul itself.

“The Silent Companions” – Guides can walk beside, not through.

“Wayfarer” – The soul must choose to cross.

“A River or a Bridge” – Every soul sees the threshold differently.

“Green stone” – The traumas left behind now rise.

🌔 Week 4: Reunion and Realization
“Ancient Hands” – Contact with ancestors.

“There for, I am” – Vibrational recognition of who you are.

“Archetypical” – Your spiritual lineage appears.

“Everywhere, always” – Closure from relationships across lifetimes.

“Sholder of giants” – Direction from those who came before.

“Riding the lightwaves” – Realization of belonging to something vast.

“Dancing in the sky” – This reunion only appears when you're balanced.

🌕 Final Days: Integration and Beyond
“In the begining” – Higher plains, return to Earth, or remain in-between.

“All of us” – Revisiting the places of life.

“Let there be life” – Final letting go. A door opens.

☀️ Album Outro: Optional “Hidden Track” (Track 32)
“The Next Beginning” – Not life after death, but something deeper. A continuation.
