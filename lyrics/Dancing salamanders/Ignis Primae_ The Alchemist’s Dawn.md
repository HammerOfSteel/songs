# Ignis Primae: The Alchemist’s Dawn

**Labels:** Dancing salamanders, Lucerna Obscura
**Created:** 2024-08-21
**Edited:** 2024-08-22

---

"In the first fire, the dawn of knowledge awakens. The ancient flame burns anew, igniting the path where wisdom sleeps."

Verse 1
Under the desert’s silent breath,
She walks alone, through sands of death.
The stars above, in cold embrace,
Guide her steps through time’s erased.

"Sepulchrum vetus," where shadows dwell,
She finds the tomb where secrets swell.
The ruins speak in whispers low,
Of fires lost in the ancient glow.

Chorus
Ignis Primae, the dawn’s first flame,
In the darkness, she calls its name.
Through the tears, the truth unfolds,
In the desert night, the light beholds.

The pages blank, now slowly burn,
As knowledge lost begins to turn.
From grief’s embrace, the fire’s drawn,
Ignis Primae, the Alchemist’s dawn.

Verse 2
Her hands trace lines in forgotten stone,
Where echoes of the past are sown.
In the silent tomb, the air is thick,
With secrets deep and knowledge quick.

"Lacrima ignis," the tears descend,
On the blank pages, where grief does bend.
As each drop falls, the words ignite,
Revealing the fire, in the dead of night.

Bridge
In the Flamma Antiqua, the fire’s birth,
She finds the secrets of the earth.
"Mater Ignis," the mother’s hand,
Guides the flames across the land.

The first fire, in tears revealed,
The ancient knowledge long concealed.
From grief’s forge, the light is born,
In the heart of night, a new dawn is sworn.

Outro
Under the stars, where silence weeps,
The Alchemist’s flame forever keeps.
"Lux in obscuro," the light in shade,
In the tomb of night, the fire’s laid.

Through the sands of time, her steps are cast,
In the Ignis Primae, the knowledge lasts.
The dawn’s first light, through tears was born,
And in her hands, the flame is sworn.
