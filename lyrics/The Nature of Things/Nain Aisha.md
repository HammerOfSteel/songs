# Nain Aisha

**Labels:** The Nature of Things
**Created:** 2024-11-15
**Edited:** 2024-11-15

---

[Intro]
The morning hums, the kettle sings,
The suitcase packed with little things.
Her father smiles as he kneels down low,
"Are you ready, love? It’s time to go."

The train winds through the misty hills,
With whispered dreams, the air is still.
From Risca’s tracks to Heathrow’s flight,
A world unfolds, so vast, so bright.

"We’ll fly through clouds, cross the skies so high,
To a place where family waits nearby."
She nods but clutches boe so tight,
Her voice unsure, a fickle fright.

[Verse 1]
At Arlanda’s gates, the world feels new,
With towering trees and skies of blue.
The train to Stockholm hums with care,
Her father whispers, "We’re almost there."

The platform buzzes, the city’s awake,
Through the glass, her Nain awaits.
Tima steps close, her courage small,
She hides behind him, barely tall.

"Oh, who is this small one,
Smiling like a little sun"
"I've heard many tales of you my dear,
and now you are finally here"

[Chorus]
Nain Aisha, a gentle voice,
A quiet warmth, a steady choice.
Through city streets and skies of blue,
A bond of love begins anew.

[Verse 2]
The cobblestones echo beneath her feet,
She peeks around at the bustling street.
Hand in hand, they wander slow,
Her father smiles as her courage grows.

At Skansen’s gates, the animals play,
Through forest paths, they spend the day.
Tima’s hand lingers, still unsure,
But her Nain’s patience makes her heart secure.

"Do you see the fox with her clever eyes?
She takes her time, but love still flies."

"Do they stay close, even when they roam?"
"Always, my dear, their hearts stay home."

[Verse 3]
By the bear’s enclosure, her laughter breaks,
The shyness softens, the moment takes.
She runs ahead, her steps grow free,
Her Nain follows, as proud as can be.

"You remind me, love, of someone here,
You father just like you you my dear"

[Bridge]
As the sun dips low, the city glows,
The lilac scents where the river flows.
Through quiet streets, their laughter rings,
A day of joy in the little things.

[Instrumental Solo | Violin and Piano]

[Verse 4]
Back at Nain’s, the candles glow,
A table set with care to show.
Tima climbs into her Nain’s lap,
a love so clear just like that.

"This home is yours, my little star,
A space of love, no matter how far."

"Nain, can we do this all again?"
"Always, love, and even then."

[Verse 5]
Under the stars, her eyes grow dim,
Her Nain reads softly, her voice a hymn.
Tima rests, her breath serene,
A day of love, a bond unseen.


"Goodnight, my sweet, my heart’s delight,
Through day and night, my little light."

"Today was the best, Goodnight, Nain."
"Goodnight sweethear, treassure mine."

[Chorus]
Nain Aisha, a gentle voice,
A quiet warmth, a steady choice.
Through city streets and skies of blue,
A bond of love begins anew.

[Outro]
The stars shine bright, the city fades,
Through dreams and love, their hearts cascade.
And when she wakes, there she'll be
Among sweet loving family.
