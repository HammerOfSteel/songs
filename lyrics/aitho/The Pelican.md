# The Pelican

**Labels:** Album, aitho
**Created:** 2025-12-29
**Edited:** 2025-12-29

The hatchback’s a furnace where I’m curing my bones, 
Tracking the silence and the way the wind groans. 
My sleeping bag is tangled in the XLR leads, 
I’m planting all my hunger like they’re mustard seeds.
The dashboard is a clutter of the things I’ve outgrown,
ridiculous Athanor, all on its own.

The condensation’s dripping like a slow-moving clock,
finding a rhythm in a steering-wheel knock.

See this little light of mine,
I’ll never forget the way it shines.
See this little spark untethered and fed,
all of this for its daily bread.
Kindle, kindle,
shimmer and shine,
this little light of mine.
satiated hollow,
beckon me on,
come,
come follow.

I’m scratching out the truth in that Iron Gall stain,
It’s eating through the notebook a slow-moving rain.
The ink is made of vitriol and a piece of my pride,
It’s corrosive and it’s bitter, but I’ve got nothing to hide.
The more I write it down, the more the pages disappear,
I’m recording every shadow, every inch of what I fear.
the song eats the singer, well, at least it’s been fed,
At least there’s something still living,
in the space under the bed.

See this little light of mine,
I’ll never forget the way it shines.
See this little spark untethered and fed,
all of this for its daily bread.
Kindle, kindle,
shimmer and shine,
this little light of mine.
Give me one more reason,
give me one more sign.

Give me another reason to record,
Triumphant little wayward sword.
Tell me,
tell me little bird,
Tell me of the far wide world.
The engine’s getting cold but the embers are bright,
We’re distilling the ghosts in the pale dashboard light.
The friction of the pen on the page,
A tiny little riot in a very small cage.

Do not fly,
do not turn back home,
Stay,
stay in the storm,
and read me your poem.

The Pelican is plucking at the center of its chest,
Just to keep the little melodies warm in the nest.
Stay in the storm...
Stay in the storm...
