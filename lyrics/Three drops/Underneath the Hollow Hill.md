Verse 1
Underneath the hollow hill, where shadows dance and secrets fill,
The earth it breathes, the ground it sighs,
A whispering wind, a thousand eyes.
A doorway carved in roots and stone,
Leads the Seeker far from home.
Into a world where time stands still,
Underneath the hollow hill.

Chorus
Underneath the hollow hill, where twilight bends the light,
The Hill King reigns, with silver crown, in robes of starless night.
A court of echoes, voices low, where ancient spirits trill,
The Seeker finds a world unseen, underneath the hollow hill.

Verse 2
There stands the King, with eyes like coal,
A gaze that pierces through the soul.
He speaks in riddles, old as time,
In words that weave and twist like rhyme.
“Why do you come, what do you seek?
This is the realm where shadows speak.”
The Seeker trembles, but dares to say,
“To find the truth, I've lost my way.”

Bridge
The Hill King laughs, his voice like frost,
“In Annwn’s depth, all paths are crossed.
The world above is but a dream,
A surface gloss, a fleeting gleam.”
The dancers twirl, the fires blaze,
In endless nights, in endless days.
“Stay, if you will, where the earth is deep,
Or leave, if you dare, the price you’ll keep.”

Outro
The Seeker turns, with heart unsure,
One step to stay, one step to cure.
The path is long, the road is steep,
Where truth and shadow ever meet.
Beneath the hill, where the old ways dwell,
The Seeker knows there's more to tell.
For wisdom waits where shadows spill,
Underneath the hollow hill.

Song Description: "Underneath the Hollow Hill"
"Underneath the Hollow Hill" follows the Seeker's journey into the Otherworld, or Annwn, a mythical realm from Arthurian and Celtic lore. Guided by a mysterious hill that seems to breathe, the Seeker crosses into a hidden world beneath the surface, ruled by the enigmatic Hill King, a figure resembling Gwyn ap Nudd. This song paints a vivid picture of a subterranean court where time and reality blur, challenging the Seeker to confront their fears and desires in a realm beyond the known. It explores themes of courage, choice, and the search for truth in a world filled with shadows and secrets.
