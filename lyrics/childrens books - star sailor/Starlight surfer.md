# Starlight surfer

**Labels:** childrens books - star sailor
**Created:** 2024-10-14
**Edited:** 2024-10-14

---

Verse 1:
Across the endless astral sea,
A sailor drifts on waves of light,
Her crescent-boat sails silently,
Through stars that shine so distant, bright.

The galaxies whisper secrets old,
Of journeys long and hearts that ache,
But through the silence, bold and cold,
A voice of love, a bond won’t break.

Pre-Chorus:
For though the chasm lies so wide,
Between the stars, between the tides,
There’s a bridge no loss can sever—
A light that sails on, forever.

Chorus:
Starlight surfer, riding the waves,
Through the echoes of time and the love that saves.
Though the distance is vast, and the stars shine far,
I feel your light like a guiding star.
Across the void, beyond the night,
You carry my love, shining so bright.

Verse 2:
The satellites spin in their steady flight,
Like beacons of hope in the cosmic dark,
Each one a promise, a silver light,
That love’s connection leaves a mark.

She sails the waves of stardust wide,
Her father’s love her compass still,
No void too deep, no space too wide,
Her light will always guide his will.

Bridge:
Between the planets and endless skies,
Where the constellations softly rise,
A father waits, his heart aglow,
For the starlight surfer he will always know.

Chorus (Reprise):
Starlight surfer, riding the waves,
Through the echoes of time and the love that saves.
Though the distance is vast, and the stars shine far,
I feel your light like a guiding star.
Across the void, beyond the night,
You carry my love, shining so bright.

Outro:
So sail on, my Star Sailor, across the sky,
Through the stars, through the waves, where love cannot die.
Though the ocean of space may lie in between,
You’re my brightest light in this endless dream.
