# Star of Pryderi

**Labels:** The Nature of Things
**Created:** 2024-11-16
**Edited:** 2024-11-16

---

[Intro]
The night feels still, the world slows down,
Stars hum softly, no one around.
The clock ticks low, the shadows stay,
Waiting for dawn to break the gray.

"You’re quiet tonight. Are you okay?"
She shifts her gaze, her hands turn away.

"It’s strange to feel a life inside,
To know this body holds more than mine."

"You’re stronger than you think, my star.
She’s lucky to have you, whoever you are."
Her laugh is soft, her fear remains,
But love begins to ease the strain.


[Verse 1]
The hours stretch long, the moments fade,
Yet hope grows bright through time’s cascade.
He holds her hand, his words are few,
But his touch says more than he ever knew.


"Do you think she’ll look like you or me?"

"She’ll be herself, as she’s meant to be."
Her voice is soft, a quiet spark,
A beacon bright in the looming dark.


[Verse 2]
The night breaks slow, a light takes hold,
A fragile dawn, a story untold.
Her breath is deep, her grip is tight,
The world begins to fill with light.

"She’s coming now; I feel her near,
A tiny star in a world unclear."

"Then hold on, love, we’re almost there,
Her light will guide us anywhere."


[Instrumental Solo | Piano and Strings]


[Verse 3]
A cry breaks through the morning’s haze,
Their hearts ignite, their eyes both blaze.
Her tiny hands, her quiet sigh,
A star descends from the endless sky.


"She’s so small, so perfect, so bright,
A spark of love in the newborn light."

"She’s our beginning, our guiding flame,
Our little star with a radiant name."


[Verse 4]
Her mother’s arms, a gentle place,
Her father’s gaze, a steady grace.
Their fears dissolve, their hearts entwine,
Two souls made whole by the gift of time.


"I never thought I’d feel this way,
To hold a life, to watch it stay."

"She’s proof, my love, that we can be,
A family born from love’s decree."


[Bridge]
The stars outside seem to burn less bright,
Compared to the glow of her quiet light.
Through tears and joy, they whisper low,
"You’re our star, and we’ll help you grow."


[Verse 5]
The sun sets low as they bring her home,
A fragile bond, a world unknown.
Her tiny breath, her quiet dreams,
A life begins, like flowing streams.


"Do you think we’ll do this right?"

"With her, love, we’ll find the light."
The room feels full, though the world is wide,
Their hearts now whole, their fears subside.


[Verse 6]
The cradle sways, the shadows fall,
Her tiny form feels vast, yet small.
Her parents watch, their love is clear,
A life so bright, so pure, so near.


"She’s ours to guide, our star to see,
The world reborn through her, through me."

"Her name will echo, her light will shine,
Through every step, through every time."


[Chorus]
Star of Pryderi, so bright, so new,
A beacon of love to guide us through.
In the quiet morn where the world feels wide,
You’re the light that burns where dreams abide.


[Outro]
The city hums, the world turns slow,
Through every path, through all we’ll know.
Our Star of Pryderi, pure and true,
A life begun with me and you.

"You’re our star, our light, our guide,
Through endless skies where dreams reside."
