# Hessendal lights

**Labels:** Dancing salamanders
**Created:** 2024-08-09
**Edited:** 2024-08-09

---

Among the montains, where the reindeer roam,
Beneath the Northern Crown, trolls call it home,
In the halls of Dovre, under midnight’s gleam,
Young hearts weave verses, in an ancient dream.

Hessendale lights, dancing through the pines,
Flickering like embers, in the winter’s signs,
Whispers of longing, where the rivers wind,
A tapestry of love, in the mountain’s mind.

By the birch and yew, under skies so vast,
They speak of Nøkken's song, and winds that cast,
Tales of hulder maidens, in the mossy glade,
In each woven word, their true love is laid.

Hessendale lights, dancing through the pines,
Flickering like embers, in the winter’s signs,
Whispers of longing, where the rivers wind,
A tapestry of love, in the mountain’s mind.

In the glow of the aurora, their spirits rise,
Yearning like the fjell, reaching for the skies,
With each verse they cast, like nets in the fjord,
Hoping to catch her, in every tender word.

When the moon is full, and the foxes cry,
They gather by the fjord, under starry sky,
Hessendale lights, as the forests sigh,
Reflecting the love, where the eagles fly.

Hessendale lights, dancing through the pines,
Flickering like embers, in the winter’s signs,
Whispers of longing, where the rivers wind,
A tapestry of love, in the mountain’s mind.

As the dawn breaks gently over Trollheim’s crest,
The words they've spoken, in her heart find rest,
The mountain keeps their secrets, ‘til the next fortnight,
When the Hessendale lights will again take flight.
