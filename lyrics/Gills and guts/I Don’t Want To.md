# I Don’t Want To

**Labels:** Dancing salamanders, Gills and guts
**Created:** 2024-09-20
**Edited:** 2024-09-20

---

Place: St. Nectan’s Glen

Verse 1
I stand beneath the ancient trees,
The weight of life brings me to my knees.
In the stillness, I feel the strain,
A quiet voice that echoes pain.
The world is heavy, the light is dim,
My heart’s grown cold, the fire within.
I don’t want to fight, I don’t want to stay,
But the water calls, not far away.

Pre-Chorus
Through the mist, I see the fall,
Its steady song, I hear its call.
A gentle voice, yet strong and clear,
It whispers hope I cannot hear.

Chorus
I don’t want to, but I will,
Like the river flows, I’ll climb this hill.
The water knows the weight I bear,
And through the flow, I find a prayer.
In the glen, where shadows play,
A spark of light guides my way.

Verse 2
The waterfall, it never sleeps,
Through the silence, its promise keeps.
It tumbles down through rock and stone,
A reminder that I’m not alone.
Though the world feels far too wide,
The water flows, it doesn’t hide.
I don’t want to, but still I stand,
With the glen’s soft touch, it takes my hand.

Pre-Chorus
Through the mist, I see the fall,
Its steady song, I hear its call.
A gentle voice, yet strong and clear,
It whispers hope I can now hear.

Chorus
I don’t want to, but I will,
Like the river flows, I’ll climb this hill.
The water knows the weight I bear,
And through the flow, I find a prayer.
In the glen, where shadows play,
A spark of light guides my way.

Bridge
In the darkest place, the seed is sown,
Beneath the surface, life has grown.
The waterfall, a sacred rhyme,
It teaches me to wait for time.
I don’t want to, but I’ll remain,
To find the peace within the rain.

Final Chorus
I don’t want to, but I will,
Like the river flows, I’ll climb this hill.
The water knows the weight I bear,
And through the flow, I find a prayer.
In the glen, where shadows play,
I find the strength to walk away.

Outro
The fall keeps flowing, gentle and true,
And in its song, I start anew.
Though I didn’t want to stay and fight,
The glen has shown me through the night.
