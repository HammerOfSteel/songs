# The Flame Within

**Labels:** Dancing salamanders, Risca
**Created:** 2024-09-04
**Edited:** 2024-09-04

---

Verse 1
Deep in the heart where shadows lie,
There burns a flame that will not die.
Achos eich cariad (Because of your love), it flickers bright,
Guiding me through the darkest night.
This fire, kindled by your touch,
Reminds me of a love that’s much too much.

Chorus
The flame within, it never fades,
Through every storm, through every shade.
With every breath, with every sin,
I feel your strength, the flame within.
It lights my path, it guides my hand,
In every step, I firmly stand.

Verse 2
In the stillness of the quiet morn,
Where hope is renewed, where dreams are born,
Yr aelwyd hon (This hearth), it warms my soul,
A testament to a love that makes me whole.
Though time may pass and seasons change,
The flame within will not estrange.

Bridge
When the world feels cold and the night is long,
I draw from you, and I am strong.
Mae'r fflam yn tywynnu (The flame shines bright), deep inside,
A light that never will subside.
For in your love, I find my will,
To keep on moving, to climb each hill.

Outro
So I’ll keep this flame, close to my heart,
A beacon of love that will never part.
In your memory, I’ll live and fight,
Fueled by the flame that burns so bright.
