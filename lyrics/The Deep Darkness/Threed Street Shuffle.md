# Threed Street Shuffle

**Labels:** The Deep Darkness
**Created:** 2025-04-17
**Edited:** 2025-04-17

---

There’s a skeleton sleeping in booth nineteen,
A fog rolls in that don’t stay clean.
Mailbox twitchin’, streetlight grins,
Someone’s got ketchup stains on their shins.

The nurse says, “Just a seasonal flu,”
But the trash can bit her left shoe.
Paula lights candles, Jeff wires the gate —
I swear that bus stop tried to levitate.

You feel it in your laces, in your teeth, in your bed,
It’s crawling in your cereal and whisperin’ in red.
Don’t scream too loud, don’t trip that beat —
Just shuffle on down your Threed Street feet.

This is the Threed Street Shuffle — uh huh!
Back up now, bring the funk and hustle — oh yeah!
If they rise, we rise too — swing low, swing through.
This is the shuffle, baby, what else is new?

Gravekeeper’s smoking behind the inn,
Says, “The dead get bored — let 'em in.”
Someone danced with a ghoul in the church,
Now he’s dating a ghost with a fog research.

We don’t panic, we print a plan,
Jeff rigged zombie paper with his bare hands.
Circus tent glows like a trap in disguise,
And boom — undead caught like flies.

Smells like teeth and bubblegum soup,
Hear that bassline dig its loop.
When the eyes go yellow, when the shoes walk wrong,
You don’t run.
You dance strong.

This is the Threed Street Shuffle — uh huh!
Don’t blink now, get the team to huddle — come on!
If they growl, we growl too — rhythm’s the glue.
This is the shuffle, baby, what else is new?

"They come at night, sure.
But they come slow.
Stiff knees. No rhythm. No soul.
That’s why we always win.
‘Cause we swing in time."

"When they groan, we groove.
When they lunge, we lunge back — with style.
You don’t survive Threed.
You perform it."

There’s fog in the fridge and ghosts in the drain,
But we strut on through like it's part of the train.
One girl’s got a yo-yo blessed by monks,
Jeff’s got a slingshot tuned to funk.

I’m just a kid with bat and grit,
Zombies rise, but I don’t quit.
Threed ain’t safe, but it’s ours at least,
And we dance every night with the groaning beast.

This is the Threed Street Shuffle — oh yes!
They moan, we move, no time to stress —
If they bite, we bite too — all funk, all true.
