# Felix the Star-Tinker - Story prompts

**Labels:** childrens books - star sailor
**Created:** 2024-10-07
**Edited:** 2024-10-07

---

1. Setting the Scene: The Constellation Workshop
Prompt:
An ethereal, star-filled workshop floating in space, filled with glowing gears, delicate tools, and large workbenches covered in shimmering stardust. The workshop is surrounded by vast, dark space dotted with stars and constellations. There are broken stars of varying sizes hovering softly in the air, some cracked and dim, others shattered into small glowing pieces. The windows of the workshop look out onto the starry expanse, where constellations twinkle faintly in the distance. The atmosphere should feel magical, intricate, and slightly chaotic, with a sense of wonder and the delicate beauty of the night sky.

2. Felix the Star-Tinker
Prompt:
A small, reddish-brown fox with bright, clever eyes and soft fur, standing on a large workbench covered in stardust and glowing star fragments. Felix’s fur is speckled with tiny flecks of stardust, and his paws have faint, glowing scratches from working with the delicate shards of broken stars. He looks determined but a little weary, his tail twitching slightly as he holds a small, glowing piece of a broken star between his nimble paws. The background should show a portion of the workshop filled with floating tools, shimmering threads of light, and scattered pieces of stars, capturing the atmosphere of a magical, starlit workspace.

3. Star Sailor’s Arrival
Prompt:
Star Sailor, a tiny girl around 2-3 years old with short brown hair and bright blue eyes, stands in her crescent-moon sailboat, glowing softly. She’s dressed in a robe made of shimmering starlight, and her expression is curious and gentle. The sailboat hovers lightly in the air, surrounded by the soft glow of the workshop, with the faint outlines of broken stars hovering in the background. Her small form radiates warmth and wonder, contrasting with the chaotic, slightly cluttered environment of the workshop. Include soft, glowing light effects around her, emphasizing her magical presence in this ethereal space.

4. Felix Looking at the Fixed Stars
Prompt:
Felix, the reddish-brown fox, stands quietly in a corner of the workshop, gazing up at a small cluster of fixed stars. These stars are patched together with shimmering threads of silver stardust, their surfaces marked by tiny, delicate repairs that glimmer like silver scars. Felix’s eyes are wide with a mixture of awe and pride, his tail held still as he takes in the beauty of what he’s created. The stars are small but glow brightly, casting soft light around Felix and illuminating his thoughtful, slightly surprised expression. The background should show other, broken stars hovering faintly in the shadows, creating a contrast between what’s been fixed and what’s still waiting.

5. Star Sailor Helping Mend a Star
Prompt:
Star Sailor stands beside a broken star hovering in the air, her tiny hands glowing softly as she weaves delicate trails of light through the cracks. She’s focused, her brow slightly furrowed, as she carefully guides the glowing threads to fit the shattered pieces together. The star’s light is dim but growing steadily brighter under her touch. The background should show Felix watching quietly from a distance, his form blurred to keep the focus on Star Sailor’s gentle, intricate work. The atmosphere should feel calm and hopeful, with the soft glow of the star casting a warm light around Star Sailor’s small, radiant figure.

6. Felix and Star Sailor’s Final Creation
Prompt:
Felix, the reddish-brown fox, stands beside Star Sailor, both of them gazing up at a fully restored star glowing brightly in the center of the workshop. Felix’s eyes are filled with joy and pride, his tail wagging slightly as he stands tall beside the small girl. Star Sailor, glowing softly, smiles up at the star, her face filled with quiet wonder. The star shines brightly, its light pure and steady, casting a soft, golden glow around them. The background should show the workshop filled with other broken stars, their dim lights contrasting with the brilliance of the star they’ve just mended together.
