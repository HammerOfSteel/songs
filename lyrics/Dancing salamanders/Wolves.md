# Wolves

**Labels:** Dancing salamanders, Gills and guts
**Created:** 2024-09-20
**Edited:** 2024-09-20

---

Place: Lanhydrock House

Verse 1
The walls stand tall, but the night is near,
I hear the wolves, they circle here.
Beneath the moon, their eyes they gleam,
I’m caught between the howl and dream.
In shadows deep, I feel their breath,
A creeping fear, a whispered death.
But in these halls, where once was peace,
The wolves won’t let their hunger cease.

Pre-Chorus
Fight or run, what should I do?
The wolves are near, they’re coming through.
I hold my ground, but will I fall?
Or face the fear and stand up tall?

Chorus
The wolves are howling at my door,
I’ve never felt this fear before.
But I’ll protect what’s mine tonight,
I’ll face the dark, I’ll fight the fight.
The wolves may come, but they won’t win,
I’ll stand my ground, I won’t give in.

Verse 2
Lanhydrock’s halls once held such grace,
But now the wolves have found this place.
Their shadows stretch across the floor,
I hear them scratching at the door.
They test my heart, they test my will,
With every breath, the air grows still.
But in the quiet, I find my fire,
To stand my ground, no more to tire.

Pre-Chorus

Bridge
In every shadow, I see their eyes,
They test my soul, they hear my cries.
But deep within, I find the strength,
To face the wolves at any length.
No longer will I hide or run,
I’ll face the pack till night is done.

Final Chorus
The wolves are howling at my door,
I’ve never felt this fear before.
But I’ll protect what’s mine tonight,

Outro
The dawn will break, the wolves will fade,
But in my heart, the fire’s stayed.
I’ll stand my ground, no need to flee,
For in the end, I’m wild and free.
