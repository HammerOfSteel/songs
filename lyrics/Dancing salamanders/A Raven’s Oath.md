# A Raven’s Oath

**Created:** 2024-08-28

Verse 1
On a barren branch, beneath the moon,
A raven sits with eyes like runes,
Feathers dark as a midnight sea,
A keeper of secrets, a mystery.
It caws in tones both sharp and soft,
A voice that carries worlds aloft.
It speaks in riddles, half-truths and lore,
"Away, away, to the lake’s hidden shore."

Chorus
Cloaked in shadow, perched in night,
A raven’s oath, a seeker’s flight.
Between two worlds, it straddles the line,
Whispering words, ancient and divine.
Through realms of mist, where truths lay deep,
Follow its call, through the veil of sleep.

Verse 2
It speaks of paths where none have tread,
Of souls entwined, the living and the dead.
“Seek the water where shadows dwell,
Find the lake where secrets swell.”
But the raven’s eyes are stormy grey,
A fractured soul, it cannot stay.
It flutters, it pauses, then offers a grin,
"To find your truth, you must look within."

Bridge
The raven flits, from branch to branch,
In its beak, a sprig, a chance.
It drops it near the Seeker’s feet,
A sign, a guide, the soul to greet.
“Not all is seen with mortal eyes,
Some paths are traced in cloudy skies.
Remember, Seeker, what is sworn,
In death’s embrace, new life is born.”

Outro
It takes to wing with a mournful croak,
A promise kept, a raven’s oath.
“Find the lake, where silence sings,
Where shadows speak, and the soul has wings.”
The Seeker stands, the wind grows cold,
With only the raven’s words to hold.
Bound to wander, ‘til truth is found,
By the lake where lost souls are bound.

Song Description: "A Raven’s Oath"
"A Raven’s Oath" follows the Seeker’s mysterious encounter with a raven who exists between worlds, carrying the wisdom and the weight of both life and death. Drawing from Norse and Celtic myths, the raven offers cryptic guidance, pointing the Seeker towards a hidden lake, a place where secrets lie submerged and truths await discovery. The raven speaks in riddles and fragmented phrases, embodying the thin veil between the living and the spirit world. This song embodies the duality of existence, the mysteries of fate, and the journey to uncover what is concealed within.
