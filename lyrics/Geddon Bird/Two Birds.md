# Two Birds

**Labels:** Geddon Bird
**Created:** 2024-10-23
**Edited:** 2024-10-23

---

(Verse 1)
We climbed the hills where the wind always sings,
Tracing the trails where we once spread our wings.
You carried the past like a stone in your hand,
While I chased the future across open land.

Two birds on a wire, one flies ahead,
The other stays near, where old stories are bred.
We dance through the years, though the rhythms may change,
Somehow our hearts always beat the same.

(Chorus)
Two birds on the wind, one soars, one stays,
One chasing the dawn, the other delays.
But through every distance, our spirits entwine—
Your memories carry, and dreams still are mine.

(Verse 2)
Snowdonia's sky holds both rain and light,
Moments of sorrow and joy taking flight.
You held my hand when the winds tried to break,
And I gave you hope when your heart would ache.

Though time pulls us down two different roads,
The weight of your love still lightens my load.
No matter how far the horizon may run,
We’ll always share the same rising sun.

(Chorus)
Two birds on the wind, one soars, one stays,
One chasing the dawn, the other delays.
But through every distance, our spirits entwine—
Your memories carry, and dreams still are mine.

(Bridge)
We don’t need to walk the same path to know,
Our roots are entwined in the places we grow.
The future is wide, but the past never dies,
It lives in the moments we hold in our eyes.

(Chorus)
Two birds on the wind, one soars, one stays,
One chasing the dawn, the other delays.
But through every distance, our spirits entwine—
Your memories carry, and dreams still are mine.

(Outro)
So fly where you will, and I’ll do the same,
We'll meet in the skies beyond fortune and fame.
Two birds forever, through all that we find,
One heart in two places, forever aligned.
