# The Veil Between Worlds

**Labels:** Dancing salamanders, Risca
**Created:** 2024-09-04
**Edited:** 2024-09-04

---

Verse 1
In the quiet of the night, when the moon is high,
The walls of the cabin breathe a sacred sigh.
Lloches sanctaidd (Sacred shelter), where shadows dance,
A place where the spirit finds its chance.
To reach across the bounds of time,
To touch the heart in whispered rhyme.

Chorus
Through the veil, so thin, so clear,
I feel your presence drawing near.
Yn y byd rhwng (In the world between), we meet again,
In this space where love transcends pain.
Here, the past and present blend,
In the veil between, where hearts mend.

Verse 2
The fire’s glow, a beacon bright,
Guides the soul through endless night.
Here, the air is thick with grace,
A doorway to another place.
You’re here, I know, in every breath,
A bond that even time can’t test.

Bridge
The wind whispers your name, so sweet,
A melody that’s incomplete.
But in this cabin, sacred, still,
Your voice lingers, your love fulfills.
And though the world outside moves fast,
Here, we find a love that lasts.

Outro
In the stillness, I know you’re near,
Through the veil, you calm my fear.
Lloches sanctaidd (Sacred shelter), where we’re not alone,
In this cabin, you’ve found your home.
Through the veil, we’ll always be,
Together, for eternity.
