# The Artist’s Legacy

**Labels:** Dancing salamanders, Risca
**Created:** 2024-09-04
**Edited:** 2024-09-04

---

Verse 1
In the quiet brush of dawn’s first light,
I see your colors, bold and bright.
Mewn pob lliw (In every color), you’re here with me,
Your love, a masterpiece, for all to see.
Through every stroke, your spirit’s near,
Guiding my hand, wiping each tear.

Chorus
This is your legacy, a life in art,
Every creation, a piece of my heart.
Cynllun gobeithio (A design of hope), in all I do,
I carry your love, pure and true.
Though you’ve gone, your light remains,
In every line, in every frame.

Verse 2
Your dreams live on in every form,

A beacon strong, where dreams reside.
I build this life in honor of you,
A legacy of love that’s ever true.

Bridge
The artist’s hand, the mother’s grace,
Lives on in every sacred space.
Through every challenge, every strife,
I find the strength to honor your life.
Mewn pob siâp (In every shape), you’re there to see,
A guiding star, setting me free.

Outro
With every breath, I keep you near,
Your love, a voice I always hear.
This legacy, it’s yours and mine,
A testament to love, divine.
Through art, through life, we’ll always be,
Bound together, eternally.
