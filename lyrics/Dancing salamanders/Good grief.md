# Good grief

**Labels:** Dancing salamanders
**Created:** 2024-08-05
**Edited:** 2024-08-05

---

Verse 1
In the stillness of the night, shadows softly fall,
Whispers of the past, echoes in the hall.
Grief, a companion, ever by my side,
In the depths of sorrow, we learn to confide.

Chorus
Good grief, a paradox we hold,
Fire tests gold, and suffering molds.
Not all tears are evil, they cleanse the soul,
In the mosaic of pain, we find our whole.

Verse 2
In the heart of darkness, a light still remains,
Amidst danger and guilt, through life's endless rains.
Within our bosom's bound, a sky so bright,
Warm with rays of hope, dispelling the night.

Chorus
Good grief, a paradox we hold,
Fire tests gold, and suffering molds.
Not all tears are evil, they cleanse the soul,
In the mosaic of pain, we find our whole.

Bridge
The whole is greater, than each sorrow's part,
In the tapestry of loss, we mend our heart.
Gestalt of grief, shaping who we are,
Through the trials we face, we travel far.

Verse 3
Grief waxes and wanes, like the moon's gentle glow,
Teaching us the balance, in ebb and flow.
It lives within, like another part of us,
A testament to love, and the ones we trust.

Chorus
Good grief, a paradox we hold,
Fire tests gold, and suffering molds.
Not all tears are evil, they cleanse the soul,
In the mosaic of pain, we find our whole.

Outro
What matters it, that darkness lies around,
If within, a bright sky can be found?
In grief and loss, we find life's tune,
A melody of sorrow, a resilient rune.
