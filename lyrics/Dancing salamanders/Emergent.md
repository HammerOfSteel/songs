# Emergent

**Labels:** Dancing salamanders
**Created:** 2024-08-04
**Edited:** 2024-08-04

---

In the schoolyard, sitting by myself,
Time drips slow, like rain on stone.
Waiting for the bell to free me,
From a day that's never my own.

The bell chimes, but what does it bring?
Another day gone, still wandering.
All these changes, can't understand,
A stranger in my own land.

Days turn to weeks, still nothing's clear,
Summer whispers in my ear.
Gaze at the horizon, a dawn breaking through,
A spark of something new.

Chaos within, a storm in my chest,
Restless nights, no time to rest.
Searching for meaning, seeking the way,
Lost in the shadows of yesterday.

But as I stand beneath the summer sky,
A feeling grows, I can't deny.
Purpose rising from the pain,
A seed of hope in the rain.

Days turn to weeks, still nothing's clear,
Summer whispers in my ear.
Gaze at the horizon, a dawn breaking through,
A spark of something new.

Clarity comes, like dawn's first light,
Breaking the shell, taking flight.
Solace found in stillness deep,
A path unfolds, in dreams I sleep.

No longer trembled by the storm inside,
Peace within, a calmer tide.
A way forward through the woods,
Eyes closed, I understand.

Days turn to weeks, still nothing's clear,
Summer whispers in my ear.
Gaze at the horizon, a dawn breaking through,
A spark of something new.

From chaos to calm, the journey long,
Found my voice, found my song.
In the silence, I found me,
Emergent, finally free.
