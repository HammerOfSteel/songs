# Sophia

**Labels:** Dancing salamanders
**Created:** 2024-08-06
**Edited:** 2024-08-06

---

Verse 1
In the heart of winter, where the cold winds blow,
A wanderer lost, adrift in the snow.
Her path obscured, in the storm's embrace,
Seeking a foothold, in this sacred space.

Chorus
Sophia, seeker of light,
Through the darkness of night.
To see the truth from afar.
Searching for a guiding star.

Verse 2
In the quiet whispers of the frozen air,
She reflects on life, in silent prayer.
The world a labyrinth, so vast and wide,
Her spirit wanders, with no place to hide.

Chorus
Sophia, seeker of light,
Through the darkness of night.
To see the truth from afar.
Searching for a guiding star.

Bridge
From the depths of despair, meaning must rise,
A journey within, beneath the skies.
In the gnostic wisdom, she finds her way,
Through the shadows, to the break of day.

Verse 3
Each step she takes, a question posed,
In the search for purpose, her heart exposed.
The snowstorm fades, as dawn draws near,
Her family’s warmth, she holds dear.

Chorus
Sophia, seeker of light,
Through the darkness of night.
Finding truth in every scar,
Searching for a guiding star.

Outro
Back to her home, where love prevails,
In the warmth of family, her journey entails.
Sophia's search, a tale of old,
Finding meaning, in the cold.
