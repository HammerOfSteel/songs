# Pont y glaw

**Labels:** Dragons Breath
**Created:** 2025-07-04
**Edited:** 2025-07-04

---

“You remember that Tuesday?
Where we both forgot our coats?
But still stood out in it like two daft daffs…
Talkin’ ‘bout ghost dogs and… toast preferences?”

“Anyway. This one's yours, innit.”

You ain't Welsh, but you move like you are,
Got that "tut at the kettle" then hug like a star.
Talk with your hands, sigh with your brows,
Switch moods quicker than the weather in town.

You say “I’m fine” and the room gets colder,
Then you chuckle, and I’m back on your shoulder.
Paint peels off when you feel too much,
But you hold pain like a prayer, tender touch.

You’ll feed a stray with your last bite o' bap,
Then swear blind you’re heartless, liar and that.
Got a mouth like a crow and soul like a bell,
I swear you’ve got valleys in you, you just fell elsewhere.

Met you under Pont y Glaw,
Hair all soaked, hoodie all wrong.
You said “Oi, you gonna kiss me or what?”
I laughed, but then, I did. Right on the spot.

You talk like laughs a lad from town,
Seen too much, but still comes round.
I see it in the way you fold your tees,
Like tidyin’ makes room for peace.

You brew like Taid, strong and wrong,
Say “just one cuppa”, four hours gone.
You got war in your shoulders, lullabies in your lap,
And that pout? It’s fatal. Makes logic collapse.

You can’t fake calm, so you hum instead,

Met you under Pont y Glaw,
Trainers soaked, heart ajar.

You said “You’re a mood and a mess,”

But I still asked, “You cold?” You said, “Yes.”

We laughed. We stayed. Rain like a vow. Met you under Pont y Glaw. Still there now.

“You said I was like four people in a coat.”

“Yeah, moods changin’ like radio on scan.”

“Still held the brolly over me, though.”

“’Cause you’re rain, init. Can’t help lovin’ the weather.”

You bite your pen when you overthink,

You forget your tea’s gone cold, don’t blink.

You send memes when you can’t say why,

You play sad tunes just to cry in disguise.

You hold me like a swear you won’t break,

You hold back words but give me the ache.

We’re not neat, but we’re true as hell,

Built a home in the rain, and we wear it well.

Pont y Glaw, bridge of grey,
Where we found love in the strangest way.
All of you, all at once, too much, too sweet,
Still the only storm I ever hoped to meet.

“Don’t need sunshine when you’ve got heart, innit.”

“Still moody as hell, though. You’re lucky you’re cute.”
