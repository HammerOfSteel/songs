# The Dream

**Labels:** Dancing salamanders, Drink deeply, and dream
**Created:** 2024-09-10
**Edited:** 2024-09-10

---

"The Dream": An alchemist dreams of transforming sorrow into joy, wandering through a surreal landscape filled with cryptic symbols. The song is a metaphor for the pursuit of inner transformation, where true wisdom is found not in gold but in the endurance of life’s trials.

Verse 1
In the quiet of night, where shadows play,
I dreamt of a path, both lost and stray.
An alchemist’s quest, with hands so worn,
To turn the lead of life into golden dawn.
Through valleys deep and mountains tall,
I chased the echoes of a silent call.
In each symbol, a truth half-told,
Of sorrow’s alchemy turning pain to gold.

Chorus
In the dream, the world unfolds,
Where lead is light and gold is bold.
Through cryptic signs and whispered lore,
The alchemist’s heart seeks evermore.
For in the dream, the truth does lie,
That within the stone, our spirits fly.

Verse 2
I wandered through fields where nothing’s clear,
Where the air is thick with whispered fear.
Each step I took, a question raised,
In a world where logic is never praised.
The symbols danced in patterns strange,
Promising change in an endless range.
Yet in their dance, a lesson found,
That in the earth, the heart is bound.

Chorus
In the dream, the world unfolds,
Where lead is light and gold is bold.
Through cryptic signs and whispered lore,
The alchemist’s heart seeks evermore.
For in the dream, the truth does lie,
That within the stone, our spirits fly.

Bridge
The dreamscape fades, but the vision stays,
A silent echo of life’s ways.
The Stone, it seems, is not to hold,
But a journey made, where souls grow bold.
The alchemist’s task, a sacred art,
Is to forge the gold within the heart.

Outro
As the dream dissolves into morning light,
I wake to find the world still bright.
For the Philosopher’s Stone, I now can see,
Is the wisdom gained through life’s decree.
In every trial, in every tear,
The alchemist’s dream becomes clear.
