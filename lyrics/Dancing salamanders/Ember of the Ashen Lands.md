# Ember of the Ashen Lands

**Labels:** Dancing salamanders
**Created:** 2024-08-18
**Edited:** 2024-08-18

---

Verse 1
In the twilight where shadows bend and blend,
I met a spark, neither he nor she, but a flame without end.
Ember of the Ashen Lands, a spirit unbound,
We found each other where no roots touch the ground.

"In the heart of the fire, all souls are the same,"
They whispered soft, as we walked without name.
I brought tales of a world beyond their sight,
And in return, they shared the secrets of the night.

Chorus
Together we burn, together we learn,
In the dance of the flame, where the old ways turn.
Through the ashes and dust, we forge something new,
In the bond of the fire, where the many become the few.

Verse 2
We wandered through the silence, where the wind has no voice,
In a land where the stars and the flames have no choice.
Ember taught me the dance of the desert’s breath,
I showed them the rhythm that defies even death.

"Your world is strange, but your fire is true,"
They said, as the dawn broke the night into two.
We wove together the old and the unknown,
Creating a flame that was ours alone.

Bridge
In the space where tradition and mystery meet,
We walked the line between the cold and the heat.
"You have the spark of a world beyond,"
They said, and I felt our bond grow strong.

Chorus
Together we burn, together we learn,
In the dance of the flame, where the old ways turn.
Through the ashes and dust, we forge something new,
In the bond of the fire, where the many become the few.

Outro
As we prepared for the trial that lay ahead,
Our flames intertwined, where no words were said.
We knew that together, we were more than apart,
For the fire we shared was the fire of heart.

"The Spire awaits, but the journey’s begun,"
Said Ember, as the first light touched the sun.
And so, with a bond as bright as the day,
We walked side by side, toward the flame’s last ray.
