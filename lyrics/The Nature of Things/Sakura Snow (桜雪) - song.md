# Sakura Snow (桜雪) - song

**Labels:** The Nature of Things
**Created:** 2024-10-24
**Edited:** 2024-10-24

---

[Verse 1]
Beneath a sky of blossoms bright,
Petals drift in morning light.
Each flower falls, a silent sigh,
Moments bloom, then say goodbye.

千の夢が消えてゆく
約束も誓いも永くはない

[Chorus]
桜雪、静かに舞う
Petals falling with the flow.
Seasons turn, and rivers glide,
Time moves on; we can't abide.
But in each step, each breath, each sky,
The meaning lives before goodbye.

[Verse 2]
Cherry blossoms in the air, so fleeting
Time’s a thief, and life’s still beating.
Mujo says: Nothing will last—
The future’s now, the past’s the past.

These petals fall like stars in flight,
Glowing once, then lost from sight.
We chase the bloom but lose the day,
Trying to hold what slips away.

[Chorus]
桜雪、静かに舞う
季節は巡り、川が流れる
一歩ごとに、息ごとに、空の中に

[Bridge]
花は散る、それでいい。
命は流れる川のように。

[Verse 3]
Ride the wave, don’t fight the stream,
Hold the moment, not the dream.
Spring will pass, and summer too,
But autumn leaves are something new.

終わりごとに新たな空が呼ぶ
木はまた来年咲くだろう
And love returns—so never fear.

[Chorus]

[Outro]
桜が咲く空の下
Petals drift like falling snow.
A fleeting bloom, a sight so bright,
一瞬の花、眩しい光
Gone with the dusk, but full of light.
