# How Are You
**Album:** Okay
**Track:** 1
**Created:** 2026-03-16

---

## Style
- Indie UK rap, upbeat and quick — think Rizzle Kicks: warm, witty, bouncy, British
- Two male British voices trading lines, cutting in, finishing each other — same person's two sides, or two mates both carrying the same unspeakable thing
- Sentence-to-sentence rhyme throughout, rap cadence for verses, slightly sung on chorus
- Upbeat feel right up until the moment it isn't — and then the upbeat returns immediately, because that's the whole point

## Instrumentation
- Upbeat indie-pop/rap backing: live drums with a bright snappy snare, clean electric guitar, walking bassline
- Brass stab on the chorus (very Rizzle Kicks — one trumpet, punchy)
- Hand claps on the beat in the chorus
- The pre-chorus strips back to bass and voice only — the contrast does the work
- Bridge: near-bare, half-spoken, something fragile underneath; then snaps back

## Production Notes
- Both voices close-mic'd, warm, very British — accents kept, not ironed out
- V1 and V2 trade mid-line sometimes — fast, easy, like two people who've had this internal argument a hundred times
- The outro rhythm stays exactly as bouncy as the opener — right up until V1 trails off mid-word
- That mid-word cut is the last sound before silence, then Track 2 begins
- No fade — hard cut to silence when V1 doesn't finish
