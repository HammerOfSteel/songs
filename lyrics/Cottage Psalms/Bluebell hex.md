# Bluebell hex

**Labels:** Cottage Psalms
**Created:** 2025-04-26
**Edited:** 2025-04-29

---

"By bloom and breeze, by thorn and dew,
I tie this song to dreams and you..."

Under the oak where the mistwinds turn,
Lay down the name you ache to learn.
Under the yew where the river bends,
Whisper the wishes you never send.

Bluebell bless, bluebell bind,
Thread of the heart, weave through the mind.
Star of the moss, song of the rain,
Hold what was lost without sorrow or stain.

Fold the fern and the foxglove fair,
Braid them in dreams with the morning air.
Gather the dew in the palm of your hand,
Drink to the ones who forgot how to stand.

Bluebell bless, bluebell bind,
Thread of the heart, weave through the mind.
Star of the moss, song of the rain,
Hold what was lost without sorrow or stain.

One for the river, two for the rain,
Three for the thorn, four for the flame.
Five for the breath, six for the tide,
Seven for the dream that still survives.

Bluebell bless... bluebell bind...
Bluebell bless... bluebell bind...
