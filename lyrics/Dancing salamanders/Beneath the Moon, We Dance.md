# Beneath the Moon, We Dance

**Labels:** Dancing salamanders
**Created:** 2024-08-09
**Edited:** 2024-08-09

---

In the quiet village, under Okinawan skies,
A love forbidden, where truth often lies,
Turu, with eyes like the ocean’s deep,
Found a heart that the village wouldn’t keep.

ウムイガマ、タチカナシ, whispered in the night,
Beneath the moon, where shadows hold tight,
アカマタ、ワンニンギラ, a love so pure,
Shunned by the world, but ever secure.


In the shadows, he came, neither beast nor man,
A spirit of the night, with a tender hand,
Their love bloomed in secret, away from the sun,
But in the light, their story was undone.


ウムイガマ、タチカナシ, whispered in the night,
Beneath the moon, where shadows hold tight,
アカマタ、ワンニンギラ, a love so pure,
Shunned by the world, but ever secure.


タチカナシ、ヤシガイナ, they sighed in the dark,
But their hearts knew no guilt, no fear, no mark,
In a cave by the sea, where the spirits dwell,
Their love found its place, beyond the village's spell.


Under the stars, they danced in the sand,
Defying the world, hand in hand,
Turu’s heart, bound by a thread so fine,
Led her to him, where their souls entwined.


ウムイガマ、タチカナシ, whispered in the night,
Beneath the moon, where shadows hold tight,
アカマタ、ワンニンギラ, a love so pure,
Shunned by the world, but ever secure.


As the tides rose high, and the night turned grey,
Their love remained, though the world slipped away,
ウムイガマ、カナサ, they sang as they swayed,
In the deep of the sea, where their spirits stayed.

style:
Acoustic Okinawan folk, traditional sanshin, haunting flute, tender strings, mystical, poetic, weaving ancient and modern elements.
