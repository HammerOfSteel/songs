# Butterfly kisses on the wind

**Labels:** Dancing salamanders, Elara
**Created:** 2024-08-23
**Edited:** 2024-08-23

---

"Butterfly Kisses on the Wind" captures the ephemeral yet powerful presence of Elara, symbolized by the delicate touch of butterfly wings carried on the breeze. The song is a tender reflection on how her spirit continues to touch those she left behind, offering comfort and connection in the smallest, gentlest moments. It beautifully expresses the idea that love transcends physical boundaries, remaining ever-present in the natural world.

Verse 1
In the whisper of the morning breeze,
Where the daisies softly sway,
I feel your gentle presence,
As the night turns into day.
Butterfly kisses on the wind,
They brush against my soul,
A tender touch, a fleeting smile,
That makes the broken whole.

Chorus
You’re the flutter of wings in the quiet dawn,
The sunlight that melts the frost.
Though your body’s gone, your spirit sings,
In every breeze, I’m never lost.
Butterfly kisses on the wind,
A dance of love that never ends,
You’re the echo in the morning light,
In every breeze, my heart transcends.

A promise held in sleep.
