# Under the Lotus

**Labels:** Whispers of the Morning Star
**Created:** 2024-09-15
**Edited:** 2024-09-15

---

Verse 1
In the stillness of the dawn's first light,
Where the world is hushed, and shadows take flight,
Beneath the surface, in waters deep,
The lotus waits, in quiet sleep.
Through murky depths where darkness hides,
It reaches up where hope abides,
A journey slow, yet sure and bright,
Toward the kiss of morning light.

Chorus
Under the lotus, where silence grows,
In the heart of stillness, the spirit knows.
Rising above the mud and the mire,
Seeking the truth, a soul’s desire.
In every petal, a story told,
Of battles fought, and peace behold.
Under the lotus, in quiet grace,
The soul ascends to its rightful place.

Verse 2
Through meditation, the mind does drift,
In the current's pull, it starts to lift.
Reflections dance on waters clear,
Echoes of the self draw near.
In the lotus bloom, wisdom’s found,
Roots in earth, but not bound.
Each breath a step, each thought a guide,
Through the lotus, the soul does glide.

Bridge
As petals open, one by one,
The journey ends, yet just begun.
For in the light, the lotus shines,
A beacon in these troubled times.
Its roots remain, in earth below,
Yet its beauty’s all the world will know.
In its bloom, we find our way,
Through night’s end to the break of day.

Outro
In the lotus, we find our peace,
A quiet place where struggles cease.
Through the mire, through the strife,
We rise, we bloom, we embrace life.
Under the lotus, calm and true,
We find ourselves, we find the new.
In its petals, in its light,
We awaken to the endless night.
