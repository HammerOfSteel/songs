# Four Paws and Two Hearts

**Labels:** Dancing salamanders, Daltokki
**Created:** 2024-09-10
**Edited:** 2024-09-10

---

In the morning light, with tails that wag,
Four little paws, and hearts that never lag.
From the first bark to the evening’s play,
They bring us joy in every way.

Through the parks and city streets,
Their love is pure, their joy complete.

Four paws and two hearts, a family’s delight,
In their playful eyes, the world is bright.
Together we walk, through life's little parts,
Bound by the love of four paws and two hearts.

In Seoul’s embrace, they roam so free,
Every corner sniffed, with boundless glee.
From cozy naps to spirited runs,
They light up our lives, like twin little suns.

Through the seasons, rain or shine,
Their loyal hearts, forever entwined.

Four paws and two hearts, a family’s delight,
In their playful eyes, the world is bright.
Together we walk, through life's little parts,
Bound by the love of four paws and two hearts.

In their barks and in their sighs,
In their loving, soulful eyes,
We find a bond, so strong and true,
In every wag, a love renewed.

From morning cuddles to midnight dreams,
Their love is pure, like moonlight beams.
In their presence, we find our peace,
With every paw print, our joys increase.

Four paws and two hearts, a family’s delight,
In their playful eyes, the world is bright.
Together we walk, through life's little parts,
Bound by the love of four paws and two hearts.

As the day fades and night descends,
In their company, our hearts mend.
Four paws and two hearts, forever we’ll be,
A family bound by love, wild and free.
