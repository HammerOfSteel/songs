# Breakbeat Kyrie

**Labels:** what a Loop Is like
**Created:** 2025-09-09
**Edited:** 2025-09-09

---

count me in on the offbeat, love. one, two… lean.

When you came around I found my ground,
heart held steady, safe and sound.
Sound bound, like a prayer-cord wound,
devotional extension, current found.
Beautiful, bountiful, double-edged sword,
cuts what’s false, leaves what’s adored.
Palm on the table, cheek to the light,
I learned how to bow instead of bite.
Name me a sinner learning to sing,
set down the stone, let mercy ring.

I breathe, unclench, release the floor,
if love is law, I need no more.

Kyrie, Kyrie

Lord have mercy

Arglwydd, trugarha

hold this heart, hold this hour

teach my fear to flower

Lay my sword down, lower

I was many in one in a skin so thin,
kept doors ajar so the small could win.
Stayed by splitting, stayed by seam,
braided the edges to carry the dream.
I wanted to stay, I wanted to mend,
to be the beginning and still be the friend.
Now I hold her where bells run clear,
I fold her laughter into the year.
If you listen at night to the hush on the stair,
that’s me with our daffodil, brushing her hair.

I breathe you home through every pane,
mercy in marrow, light in the vein.

Kyrie, Kyrie

Lord have mercy

Arglwydd, trugarha

bind my breaking, make me brave

tide me over, teach me save

lift the hand I could not raise

Then you left, hearts in hand at the cleft,
over the span, no map, no heft.
No chance, no stance to know the why,
only the weather and a wordless sky.
What made you so afraid to let go
of my hand and her small glow.
She needed you for more than a mile,
more than a bus stop, more than a while.
Sure as hell I still do too,
truth like salt, clean and true.
Yet somehow I know, in a field of sun,
our daffodil holds you, and you hold mum.

Until I come home, keep watch tonight,
keep them warm in that milk-blue light.

Kyrie, Kyrie

Lord have mercy

Arglwydd, trugarha

stitch this hour to what’s above

as below, so learn to love

teach my stubborn hands to unclove

When you came around I found my ground,
sound bound to a brighter sound.

When I had to go, I stayed by thread,
I’m in the breath around your bed.

If rage is red, I steep it gold.

If grief is cold, let comfort hold.

Mercy, mercy, mercy, be the mold.

I am many in one and I lift her high,
teach her the names of the kindest sky.
I whisper your jokes in a greener tongue,
she laughs like a bell that was never unstrung.
I wanted to stay; I keep staying now,
just different doors, the same soft vow.
When you stumble and the day feels late,
lean your ear to the figure-eight.

I will meet you on the offbeat land,
sync my step to an open hand.
If the world is a drum and the bar runs long,
I’ll wait for the drop and enter the song.

Kyrie, Kyrie

Lord have mercy

Arglwydd, trugarha

loosen the knot, let kindness lead

meaningful meddling, mercy’s creed

pour me the courage my bones still need

Kyrie, Kyrie

Lord have mercy

Arglwydd, trugarha

you girls are never alone.

until I come home.

Kyrie… Lord have mercy… Arglwydd, trugarha…
