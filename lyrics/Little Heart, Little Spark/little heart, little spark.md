# little heart, little spark

**Labels:** Little Heart, Little Spark
**Created:** 2025-08-10
**Edited:** 2025-08-10

---

Little heart, little spark,
light my world in the dark,
still carry the flame,
always, always, in your name.

Got crumbs on the table, cocoa in the mug,
rain tap-tappin’ on the sill, wrapped up snug.
Little laugh in the corner, hummin’ some tune,
moon peekin’ in like it’s peek-a-boo with the room.

Got tales in my pocket, got sky in my hand,
drawin’ worlds in the steam on the glass where I stand.
Sock-feet shufflin’, the kettle’s first song,
like the clock on the wall, you’ve been tickin’ along.

Little heart, little spark,
light my world in the dark,
still carry the flame,
always, always, in your name.

Cupboard-door creak, night-light glow,
whisper like the river where the willows grow.
Weave your dreams in a blanket fort’s keep,
where the shadows play gentle and the stars don’t sleep.

Half-rhyme hums and a “mind the gap,”
train-track dreams in a midnight nap.
Little spark in the pocket of a cloudless thought,
like the hope that stays when the storms get caught.

And it’s always been here, see,
right where you left it.
Not lost, not gone…
just waitin’ in the quiet.

Little heart, little spark,
light my world in the dark,
still carry the flame,
always, always, in your name.

Oi, pass the jam, mind the crumbs on the plate,
every small bit counts when the night runs late.
Spin a yarn ‘bout the fox and the fairground light,
bout the rain-boot race in the puddles last night.

Lil’ daffodil grin in the frost-bit air,
mornin’ smells like toast and your mum’s old chair.
And we keep on hummin’ that “ring-a-ring-rose,”
‘cos the world still turns while the heart still knows.

Keep the spark in your mitten, keep the flame in your song,
keep the name on your lips where it’s safe, where it’s strong.

Little heart, little spark,
light my world in the dark,
still carry the flame,
always, always, in your name.
