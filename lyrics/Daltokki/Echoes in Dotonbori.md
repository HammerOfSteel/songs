# Echoes in Dotonbori

**Labels:** Dancing salamanders, Daltokki
**Created:** 2024-09-10
**Edited:** 2024-09-10

---

Neon lights reflect on the river’s skin,
In Dotonbori’s glow, where our night begins.
Takoyaki stands and okonomiyaki dreams,
In Osaka’s heart, where love’s light gleams.

Echoes in Dotonbori, where the city sings,
In the rhythm of the night, our love takes wing.
Through the pulse of neon lights, we find our way,
In the heart of Osaka, we’ll forever stay.

Signs that flicker, lanterns in the night,
Street performers under the moon’s soft light.
Kushikatsu bites and tempura treats,
In every taste, our love repeats.

Echoes in Dotonbori, where the city sings,
In the rhythm of the night, our love takes wing.
Through the pulse of neon lights, we find our way,
In the heart of Osaka, we’ll forever stay.

Walking under Glico Man’s glowing stride,
We find our love, where cultures collide.
Through laughter and tears, day and night,
In Dotonbori’s echoes, our love shines bright.

In the maze of Shinsaibashi’s arcades,
We find treasures in each other’s gaze.
Savoring fugu and sweet taiyaki,
Our love’s flavor, so rich and lucky.

Echoes in Dotonbori, where the city sings,
In the rhythm of the night, our love takes wing.
Through the pulse of neon lights, we find our way,
In the heart of Osaka, we’ll forever stay.

As the night fades and dawn draws near,
In the echoes of Dotonbori, love is clear.
With every step, in every light,
We find forever in Osaka’s night.
