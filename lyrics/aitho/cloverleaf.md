# cloverleaf

**Labels:** aitho
**Created:** 2026-01-01
**Edited:** 2026-01-01

---

Betrothed, beware, bothered, butchered and betrayed, 
The scales of the heart are all sitting here weighed. 
All at once,
a bunch of great fortune and great despair,
Will you share?
Do you dare?
Is this what you let me live through?
It’s anything, anything but fair.

You hold me in the palm of your collective hands,
you know,
While I wear my heart on my sleeve and let the whole thing show.

Thinking,
"Aye, this is the only way that I could ever go,"
You are my circle,
you’re my bone,
you’re my very skin,
And even when bleed me dry,
I’ll always let you back in.

I've heard told of constellations consisting of pure love,
Blessed and good and together as the push came to the shove.
I’ve seen the olive branch extended,
splendid,
mended,
Surrendered,
amended,
and defended.

Cloverleaf, many of its kind,
I am sure you'll find
this a suitable metaphor,
for this war.
raging in the quiet hour,
good,
bad,
all the same,
tricky little game.

I too have encountered the sting;
let it ring,
let it sing.

I’ve seen and I’ve grasped the the two-edged sword.
Tricked myself to stay onboard,
adored all you say and swear,
Long, long silence that’s brought me here.

Do you dare?
Do I dare?
Would it even be fair?

To me,
to you,
just to let this between us go?

Aye, you talk so sweet,
as if you lot really, truly care,
Maybe you really believe you do,
it’s a lovely little show.

But the truth is,
you do not know,
you don’t know me at all.

Because if you did,
you lot could have sworn,
To stand with me in the storm!

You would actually take my fucking hand,
Somehow you’d try,
you’d try to understand!

You’d at least try and keep it warm,
Hold it steady as it shifts form.

You would not run,
you would not hide,
You would at the very least have tried...

You would have cried...
When she died.

No... 
some things...
they should not...
They could not be forgiven.

So let me go.
Please stop pretending.
Let this be okay.
Let there be an ending.

To the suffering,
this constellation shine,
Let me take the bread,
let me take the wine.

Just leave me alone...
Let me...
go home...
