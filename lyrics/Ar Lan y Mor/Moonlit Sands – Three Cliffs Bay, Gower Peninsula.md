# Moonlit Sands – Three Cliffs Bay, Gower Peninsula

**Labels:** Dancing salamanders, Ar Lan y Mor
**Created:** 2024-09-19
**Edited:** 2024-09-19

---

Verse 1:
On the moonlit sands of Three Cliffs Bay,
I see your face as clear as day.
We walk beneath the stars so high,
As the waves softly sing a lullaby.

The cliffs rise tall, a shadowed gate,
But here with you, there is no weight.
We laugh and talk, as we once did,
In this dream, where nothing’s hid.

Chorus:
On moonlit sands where the tide meets the shore,
We find each other once more.
Across the veil, beyond the night,
Our hearts are one in the silver light.

Verse 2:
The tide kisses our feet, so cold,
But your hand in mine feels warm, feels bold.
You tell me tales of where you’ve been,
Of distant lands and worlds unseen.

Three Cliffs stand guard, but we pass through,
In this world where dreams come true.
No distance keeps us far apart,
For in this dream, I hold your heart.

Chorus:
On moonlit sands where the tide meets the shore,
We find each other once more.
Across the veil, beyond the night,
Our hearts are one in the silver light.

Bridge:
In the quiet, as the waves retreat,
I feel your presence, so pure, so sweet.
Though waking calls with morning’s grace,
I carry with me your smile, your face.

Final Chorus:
On moonlit sands where the tide meets the shore,
We find each other once more.
Across the veil, beyond the night,
Our hearts are one in the silver light.

Outro:
Three Cliffs Bay, beneath the moon’s soft glow,
A place where time has no place to go.
We walk together, hand in hand,
Forever bound by moonlit sand.
