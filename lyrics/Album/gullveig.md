🌊 ALBUM CONCEPT — Gullveig

Core theme:
Rebirth through fire, sea, and time — where gold, salt, and sorrow meet.

Tone & mood:
Maritime folk with soft jazz undertones — upright bass, brushed drums, warm Rhodes, acoustic guitar, and layered harmonies. Think tides and lamplight, smoke and brass — intimate, poetic, alive.

Language & lyrical tone:
Modern rhyming poetic language — not ancient retelling, but echoes of myth in everyday metaphor. Like the sea narrating Gullveig’s reincarnations through human lives.

Symbolic framework:
Each song as both a coastal phenomenon and a state of alchemical/mystical transformation — the water cycle as mirror to reincarnation.


---

🔱 THEMATIC TRIAD (three layers)

1. The Sea — Renewal & Memory

The ocean as crucible of emotion; the salt preserving and healing.
→ Natural phenomena: longshore drift, tidepool, harbor light, undertow.
→ Emotional tone: grief, healing, love that moves through loss.

2. The Fire — Transformation & Alchemy

Gullveig’s burnings as metaphors for breakdown, purification, and return.
→ Each burn = a stage of transmutation (Nigredo, Albedo, Rubedo).
→ Emotional tone: rebirth, rediscovery, self-illumination.

3. The Gold — Desire & Divinity

“Gull” as both literal and inner gold — longing, art, love, value.
→ Gold as sunlight on water, laughter after grief, the warmth that remains.
→ Emotional tone: luminous acceptance, wonder.
