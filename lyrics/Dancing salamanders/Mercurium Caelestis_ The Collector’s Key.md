# Mercurium Caelestis: The Collector’s Key

**Labels:** Dancing salamanders, Lucerna Obscura
**Created:** 2024-08-21
**Edited:** 2024-08-22

---

"Heaven’s mercury flows in the hands of the keeper, unlocking the gates of the cosmos. The liquid key turns, revealing the stars’ silent song."

Verse 1
On the desolate world where stars weep cold,
A lone Seeker stands, with secrets untold.
In the dust of ages, where shadows coil,
He toils in silence, with Mercurium’s oil.

"Planetum mortis," where life once fled,
The Seeker stirs the ashes of the dead.
With the Key of Aether, he turns the wheel,
To unlock the essence, where cosmos kneel.

Chorus
Mercurium Caelestis, in the starry drift,
Where Vitae’s pulse begins to lift.
Through the Sphaera’s arc, the veil does part,
The Seeker holds the cosmos’ heart.

In the black of space, where shadows dance,
The Nox and light entwine in trance.
Through the battle of dark and light,
The Key of Mercury births the night.

Verse 2
From the Nebula Tenebris, a shadow grows,
An ancient force, where Obscurum flows.
The Seeker knows this darkened flame,
A mirror of the light, yet not the same.

"Pugna mentium," where thoughts collide,
The Seeker and shadow in cosmic stride.
Through the blackened night, their wits entwine,
A dance of shadow and light divine.
