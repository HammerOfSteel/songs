# Rainfall in Reverse

**Labels:** eras-imos
**Created:** 2024-12-01
**Edited:** 2024-12-01

---

The story starts as shadows fall,
The lamp casts warmth upon the wall.
His voice is soft; his eyes grow dim,
A world unfolds just for him.

The tale begins, the room grows still,
The quiet hum bends time and will.
A breath is held; the air takes flight,
We step together into night.

The stars are bright; the sky feels near,
A whisper sounds both far and clear.
The rain moves up; it starts to glow,
A dream unfolds where love will grow.

Rainfall in reverse takes flight,
Raindrops rise to meet the night.
Clouds of gold, their soft refrain,
Guide us through this dreamlike rain.

The raindrops lift us to the sky,
A pathway formed where dreams can lie.
Each step reveals a hidden door,
To places we've been once before.

A swing sways lightly in the breeze,
Laughter hums beneath the trees.
"Who is this?" his voice so small,
A little girl who knows it all.

Rainfall in reverse takes flight,
Guided by the starry light.
In glowing clouds, the threads entwine,
Her hand in his, the dream aligns.

She leads him through a quiet stream,
Its waters shimmer, lit by dreams.
Her voice, a melody of grace,
"I’ve been here too; this is our place."

A bridge of light connects the skies,
"Come on!" he calls, and off she flies.
They run through fields of endless hue,
The world feels whole, alive, and new.

Rainfall in reverse cascades,
Through lands where light and love are made.
A sister’s smile, a brother’s song,
The dream weaves bonds where hearts belong.

Together now, we climb the hill,
The rain has stopped; the world grows still.
Memories paint the sky in gold,
Each one a story gently told.

The little girl fades in the glow,
She smiles and whispers, “Now you know.”
A tear falls soft, but hearts feel light,
As dawn begins to chase the night.

Rainfall in reverse has flown,
Guided us where dreams are sown.
In skies of love, their laughter stays,
A bond that time cannot erase.

The blinds glow faint with morning’s grace,
The dream has left its quiet trace.
Our bodies rest, but we still know,
The rain rose up; it let us grow.

Beside him now, his breath is slow,
A tiny smile begins to show.
And in my heart, her hand remains,
A sister’s love through dreamt-up rains.

Rainfall in reverse, it flows,
Through dreamlit paths where wonder grows.
Though morning calls, the stars remain,
A silent bond through lucid rain.

The morning breaks, the room is still,
Our bodies rest, our hearts fulfilled.
The rain, the dream, the night’s embrace,
Leaves gentle smiles upon our face.
