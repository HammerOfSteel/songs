# static altar

**Labels:** sundry
**Created:** 2026-01-05
**Edited:** 2026-01-06

---

Sat on the rug with your knees to your chin,
Watching the watcher, where the static begins.

The box in the corner humming a tune,
A pixles blur the little room.

watching the ants in their endless fight,
doesn't seem right,
does it.

it stirs the silver dream,
sets the theme,
supreme it moves,
up the bloodstream.
such self-esteem.
gone, given for a ride,
bubbling "Sundry",
swirling inside.

Oh, hither,
to the altar,
to the ring
To the all-encompassing-everything,

Great Un-Said, Great Un-Known
garden of pixels,
finally grown.

God of silence, of small words,
of the holiest such,
of migrating birds.

weary, stir the waking dream.
burst with colors at the seam.

up-end,
take and mend,
let it not be just pretend.

Would you open your eyes,
put down that disguise.
Could this just come to be,
no longer something to forsee.

no longer at the ends of the rope,
given gospel..
song...
given hope.

I swear these ants are about to win,
seeing the way things have been.
looking at the way things are going,
really there is no telling,
nor knowing.

Maybe we won't figure on this climb.
I’ll give you my hand if you’ll give me your time.

Come away,
to the altar,
to the bell,
To the breaking of the shell.

Shall it encompass,
all you know.
all though,
could it bestow.
a small seed to grow.

Saint of noise, given a voice,
no small choice.
You've now to make.

do not shake,
that which is at stake.

gone the signal is gone,
gone weary, all gone to bed. 
all wonderful and stuck in the head.

It’s not a mistake,
it’s not a defect,
it's a tressure you could protect.

Accepting the blur and the buzz and the hum,
Until we are finally, gracefully numb.
