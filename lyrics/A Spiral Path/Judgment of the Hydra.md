# Judgment of the Hydra

**Labels:** A Spiral Path
**Created:** 2024-09-23
**Edited:** 2024-09-23

---

Style: Acoustic guitar, melodic, poetic, and powerful

Verse 1
The Hydra’s shadow looms above, its eyes like burning flame,
I stand before the ancient stone, unsure, but not the same.
The path I’ve walked to reach this place, with every step I’ve grown,
Yet now the weight of who I am is heavier than stone.

Pre-Chorus
Beneath the sky, beneath their gaze, I face the storm within,
The judgment of the Hydra’s call, the trial to begin.

Chorus
Will I rise, will I fall, in the depths of the Hydra's might?
Will I find the truth of me in the shadow of its light?
Through the fear, through the flame, through the breaking of my pride,
I stand before the Hydra's gaze, with no more need to hide.

Verse 2
The faces of my family, they linger in my mind,
Their distant eyes, their silent words, the comfort I can’t find.
The mark I seek is not just power, it’s something far more deep,
To prove that I am worth their love, a promise I must keep.

Pre-Chorus
Beneath the stars, beneath the stone, I face the test alone,
The Hydra’s roar within my heart, as cold as chiseled bone.

Chorus
Will I rise, will I fall, in the depths of the Hydra's might?
Will I find the truth of me in the shadow of its light?
Through the fear, through the flame, through the breaking of my pride,
I stand before the Hydra's gaze, with no more need to hide.

Bridge
Each head of fear, each doubt that coils, I fight to break them free,
But in the struggle, I find myself, a stronger, clearer me.
The Hydra’s eyes reflect my soul, as fragile as it’s bright,
And in the darkness, I find strength, a fire that burns in light.

Chorus
I will rise, I will stand, in the heart of the Hydra’s flame,
I’ll find the truth inside of me, and I will own my name.
Through the pain, through the storm, through the breaking of my pride,
I stand before the Hydra’s gaze, with nothing left to hide.

Outro
The mark is carved upon my skin, but deeper in my soul,
I leave the Spire, not just a man, but someone who is whole.
