# Whispers in the Wood

**Labels:** Dancing salamanders, Risca
**Created:** 2024-09-04
**Edited:** 2024-09-04

---

Verse 1
In the stillness of the ancient trees,
The wind carries secrets on a breeze.
Clywaf eich llais (I hear your voice) in the rustling leaves,
A soft echo that never deceives.
The woods remember, they hold your song,
In every branch where you belong.

Chorus
Whispers in the wood, stories untold,
Voices of love, both gentle and bold.
Though you're gone, your spirit is near,
In every whisper, I can hear.
בְּרוּחַ (B'ruach, in the spirit), you linger still,
In the forest’s quiet, I feel your will.

Verse 2
The sun filters through the canopy high,
Painting memories in the sky.
Dros y bryniau (Over the hills) and through the glen,
I walk with you, time and again.
Your laughter dances with the streams,
Filling my heart with forgotten dreams.

Bridge
In the twilight’s glow, the shadows play,
Reminders of you in the fading day.
Each step I take, I’m not alone,
The forest’s whispers guide me home.
Cuddled in nature’s warm embrace,
I find your love in this sacred place.

Outro
So I’ll listen close, to the wind and trees,
For in their whispers, I find my peace.
The woods will always carry your grace,
In every leaf, I see your face.
