# Bumblebee Boe

**Labels:** The Nature of Things
**Created:** 2024-11-15
**Edited:** 2024-11-15

---

[Intro]
In fields of green by Mynydd Machen’s side,
Where wildflowers bloom in gentle pride.
Tima runs free, her laughter flows,
In summer’s warmth, her wonder grows.


"Look, love, the bees are dancing near."
She claps her hands with joy and cheer,
Her eyes alight, her spirit bold,
As bees buzz close in wings of gold.


[Verse 1]
Bare feet brush against the grass,
By Twmbarlwm where breezes pass.
She spins around, her arms out wide,
A summer breeze, her laughter’s guide.

With tiny hands, she picks a bloom,
The meadow filled with sweet perfume.
A simple day, the world her stage,
In fields that bloom beyond her age.


[Chorus]
Bumblebee Boe, come dance with me,
In Cwmcarn’s fields by flowers free.
A teddy bear and buzzing friends,
In summer’s light, the joy won’t end.
Oh, Bumblebee Boe, our laughter rings,
In fields alive where summer sings.


[Verse 2]
From behind his back, her father shows
A bear with eyes as brown as oaks.
With fuzzy paws and gentle grin,
He hands her Boe, her new friend kin.


"For you, my love, on summer’s breeze."

"I’ll call him Boe, like the buzzing bees!"
She hugs him tight, a friend so true,
And laughs as bees dance close on cue.


[Chorus]
Bumblebee Boe, come dance with me,
In Cwmcarn’s fields by flowers free.
A teddy bear and buzzing friends,
In summer’s light, the joy won’t end.
Oh, Bumblebee Boe, our laughter rings,
In fields alive where summer sings.


[Verse 3]
She twirls and skips, Boe in hand,
Together they explore the land.
Through blooms of red and daisies bright,
They chase the bees in golden flight.

Her father watches, heart aglow,
As she and Boe find all to know.
The bees hum soft, a meadow song,
A lullaby where they belong.


[Verse 4]
The sun dips low, the shadows grow,
But still she clings to Bumblebee Boe.
Her eyelids droop, her steps grow slow,
Yet still her heart in meadow’s glow.

"Come, my love, let’s rest awhile."
She nods and gives a sleepy smile,
With Boe held close, her faithful friend,
As summer’s day draws to an end.

[Chorus]
Bumblebee Boe, come dance with me,
In Cwmcarn’s fields by flowers free.
A teddy bear and buzzing friends,

Oh, Bumblebee Boe, our laughter rings,

[Outro]
In fields of green by Mynydd Machen’s side,
Where Tima dreams, her spirit wide.
With Bumblebee Boe, she’ll find her way,
In every warm, sunlit day.

A meadow dance, a summer’s cheer,
In memories she’ll hold so dear.
With Boe by her side and bees that hum,
The magic of summer will always come.
