# Five fingers

**Labels:** Elara
**Created:** 2025-08-01
**Edited:** 2025-08-01

---

I know you well, I know your soul inside,
Your fears and aching sorrows I too hide.
You are my flesh, my blood, my name,
My love for you, a steadfast flame.
I do what I can, I give all I am,
So tall by day, but night makes you so small again.
When no one sees, when none can tell,
Your laughter breaks and tears will swell.

Five fingers will shelter you.
One is the moon, one is the sun,
One the North Star when the clouds have won.
One is hope, and one is true.
The hand’s five fingers cradle you.

Darkness looms so wide,
The moon is split in two tonight,
But night must fall before the day can rise,
And you, my light, are morning in my eyes.
And though this world spins round and round,
None bring despair like you, so deep, profound.
But none are half as real,
Or make me feel the way you make me feel.
You're scared of shadows looming near,
Of threats that never dare come here.
So I will sing you promise-deep,
A vow I know your heart will keep.

Five fingers will shelter you.
One is the moon, one is the sun,
One the North Star when the clouds have won.
One is hope, and one is true.
The hand’s five fingers cradle you.

Now picture that forest road we roam,
Where quiet trees have made their home.
We walk the path down to the shore,
Your small hand in mine once more.
See the lake so deep and still,
Look below, beyond the hill,
A silver treasure shining bright,
It waits for you, my love, tonight.

Five fingers will shelter you.
One is the moon, one is the sun,
One the North Star when the clouds have won.
One is hope, and one is true.
The hand’s five fingers cradle you.
The hand’s five fingers cradle you.
